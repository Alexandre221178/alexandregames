The Autumn Festival is a limited-time event in Hero Wars: Dominion Era, running for 3 exciting days in October. It brings unique missions, special tasks, and exclusive rewards, making it one of the most anticipated events of the season.

Whether you’re a casual player or a dedicated hero, this event is designed to keep everyone engaged with daily goals, resource management, and rewarding milestones.

What is the Autumn Festival Event?

The Autumn Festival is a special 3-day event held in October in Hero Wars: Dominion Era. It celebrates the season with unique tasks, exciting missions, and exclusive rewards, making it a favorite among both new and veteran players.

By completing event tasks, players earn Harunian Brews, a limited-time currency that can be exchanged for valuable resources. The event design encourages daily activity, energy management, and strategic spending, rewarding players at every level of engagement.

Autumn Festival Rewards and Harunian Brew Chests

Throughout the Autumn Festival event in Hero Wars: Dominion Era, players can collect Harunian Brews, which serve as the event currency. These special chests contain a variety of valuable rewards, including shop coins, exclusive currencies, and equipment pieces that help heroes level up faster. Opening Harunian Brew chests is one of the best ways to boost your account’s progression during this 3-day event.


🎉 Conclusion - Autumn Festival

The Autumn Festival event in Hero Wars: Dominion Era offers a wide variety of tasks that reward players with Harunian Brews, a special event currency. By completing missions such as logging in daily, spending Emeralds, earning VIP points, opening Tower chests, gathering Soul Stones, summoning pets, and spending Energy, players can steadily accumulate large amounts of Brews.

The event design ensures that both casual and hardcore players can participate:

  • Casual players can focus on simple tasks like logging in, pet summons, and Tower chests.
  • Active spenders can take advantage of Emerald and Energy spending tasks for much larger rewards.

In total, completing all stages across the 8 missions provides hundreds of Harunian Brews, along with an exclusive Avatar. This makes the Autumn Festival a great opportunity to strengthen your account, progress faster, and collect event-exclusive rewards.
