Event Rewards

Item Description
Ancient Titan Artifact Chest
Contains fragments of Seals, Crowns, and Weapons for Fire, Water, and Earth Titans, as well as Elemental Artifact Essence, Elemental Tournament Coins, and Gold.
Lucky Coin
Use these coins in the Lucky Road event. You can win various rewards such as Gold, Titan Skin Stones, Energy, Titan Potions, Titan Artifact Spheres, Emeralds, Artifact Seal Chests, Elemental Tournament Coins, Essence of the Elements, and Titan Summoning Spheres.

Conclusion

The Elemental Synergy event offers exciting opportunities to earn valuable rewards by engaging in diverse challenges. Whether you’re logging in daily, reaching VIP milestones, or spending resources wisely, every task brings you closer to powerful artifacts and trophies. Stay active, strategize your efforts, and make the most of this event to strengthen your heroes and dominate the Dominion Era battlefield!
