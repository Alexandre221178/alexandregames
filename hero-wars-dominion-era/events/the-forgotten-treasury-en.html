Deep beneath the sands of time, a hidden trove of ancient vases awaits discovery. The Forgotten Treasury event invites you to take the role of a daring archaeologist, unearthing secrets long lost and treasures imbued with mystic energy.

In this thrilling limited-time event, players will gather Archaeologist’s Tools, break open forgotten relics, and claim powerful rewards. It’s more than just treasure it’s a journey into the heart of Dominion’s hidden history.

How the Forgotten Treasury Event Works

To participate, you’ll need Archaeologist’s Tools. These can be earned by completing event quests or purchased through the in-game shop. Once acquired, you can use these tools to inspect Ancient Vases scattered throughout the event screen.

Each vase contains random rewards ranging from Pet Potions and Chaos Particles to rare artifact resources and more. The thrill lies in the unknown you never know what treasure your next excavation will bring!

The Forgotten Treasury: Using the Archaeologist's Tool

In this exciting event, you will use the Archaeologist's Tool to inspect numerous ancient vases. Each vase holds valuable rewards, and you have the chance to open either the main reward or multiple items inside. Explore the mystery behind every vase and claim your treasures!

Conclusion

The Forgotten Treasury event offers a thrilling adventure filled with mystery and valuable rewards. Using the Archaeologist's Tool to uncover ancient vases lets you dive deep into the secrets of Hero Wars: Dominion Era. Be strategic, open your vases wisely, and claim the treasures that await you. Every discovery brings you closer to powerful artifacts and boosts your journey in the game. Don't miss out on this exciting opportunity to enhance your heroes and dominate the battlefield!
