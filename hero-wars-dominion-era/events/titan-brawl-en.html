How Titan Brawl Really Works

Although your calendar only shows Titan Brawl, there are actually two parallel event tracks running: Guardian of Balance and Titan Brawl: [Titan of the Event]. Both are crucial to maximizing your rewards.

In Guardian of Balance, you complete specific missions to earn Event Points. These points can be exchanged for Brawl Coins, which are essential for event purchases.

Simultaneously, Titan Brawl: [Titan of the Event] gives you 5 lives per day to fight other players. You must use the featured Titan in both your offense and defense setups. Winning and defending battles earns you additional Brawl Coins.

How Event Points Work

Event Points

Score points by completing event quests to earn valuable Event Points. Each time you earn Event Points, they are converted into Titan Brawl Coin, which can be spent in the event shop to purchase exclusive items and upgrades.

Titan Brawl Coin

Exchange these Coins for rare goods in the Brawl Shop Event.

Titan Brawl Event and Exclusive Shop Rewards

The Titan Brawl is a limited-time event in Hero Wars Dominion Era where players can battle using Titans in a special mode. By participating, you earn Titan Brawl Coins that can be exchanged in the event shop for valuable rewards like skins, resources, and upgrade materials. Make sure to plan your battles strategically and spend your coins wisely to get the best value from the shop.

Titan Brawl Shop Rewards

The event shop offers a wide variety of rewards, including elemental Titan Skins (based on the featured Titan), Titan Potions, Artifacts, Essence of the Elements, hero equipment, and much more.
