The Ascension to Asgard event in Hero Wars: Dominion Era is a powerful opportunity for players to collect valuable upgrade resources and strengthen their heroes for endgame content. Running for only three days, this event demands strategic energy and emerald spending to maximize rewards such as Random Crystals, Wish Crystals, Radiant and Vibrant Crystals, Insignias, Eternal Seeds, and precious emeralds.

Whether you're an experienced player seeking to refine your Ascension strategy or a newcomer aiming to catch up, this detailed guide will walk you through every part of the event, covering quest chains, efficient spending tips, and how to maximize your returns from each day.

Where to Find the Event

You can locate the Ascension to Asgard event by navigating to the Special Events window. There, the event will appear under a unique tab containing all the related quests and rewards. Keep in mind, all progress is tracked liverewards are distributed instantly upon completing each quest.

Best Ways to Prepare

Here are a few key strategies you can follow to get the most from the Ascension to Asgard event:

  • Stockpile Energy: Save your energy potions and daily energy rewards ahead of time to maximize spending during Challenges Await.
  • Coordinate with Your Guild: Ensure full participation in Asgard boss fights, especially if the event lands on a weekend.
  • Manage Resources: Delay glyph upgrades and Astral Providence pulls until the event is live to knock out multiple quests in one go.
  • Spend Smart: Emeralds are most effective when spent during the event for double rewards. Use them for chest openings, potion purchases, and raid skips.

Conclusion

The Ascension to Asgard event is a short but rewarding period in Hero Wars: Dominion Era that allows players to gather a substantial amount of high-end upgrade materials in just three days. With multiple quest chains covering almost every game mechanicfrom spending emeralds and energy to battling bosses and unlocking glyphsthere's something for every type of player to benefit from.

Maximizing this event comes down to smart planning: hold onto your energy potions, glyph upgrades, and emeralds until the event begins. Then focus on clearing each quest chain methodically to earn as many Crystals, Insignias, Seeds, and Emeralds as possible. Team coordination and daily participation are the key to turning a good event result into a great one.

Whether you‚Äôre ascending your main team to new heights or collecting rare materials for future upgrades, Ascension to Asgard is one of the most vital events in the game calendar. Don‚Äôt miss your chance to rise above the competition!
