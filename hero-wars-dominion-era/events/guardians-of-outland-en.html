How to Get the Most Out of the Guardians of Outland Event

This limited-time event is packed with opportunities to strengthen your heroes. You'll face the fiercest monsters from Outland while collecting Outland Coins that can be exchanged for powerful rewards.

Whether you're a beginner looking to unlock new skins or a veteran aiming for maximum efficiency, this guide will help you understand quest priorities, optimal energy usage, and how to farm Skin Stones effectively.

Event Quests Guide – Guardians of Outland

The Guardians of Outland event is structured around several themed quest chains. Each one offers specific rewards that will help you boost your heroes and progress faster. Here's a breakdown of all quest paths and what you can earn:

🛡️ The Path of Tenacity

Log in daily to receive Energy. Simple, free, and easy progress don’t miss a day!

🏅 The Path of Glory

Reach certain VIP levels to earn Outland Coins and Skin Stones Chests. A great bonus if you've invested in the game!

💎 Going Forward

Spend Emeralds to get Outland Coins and Skin Stones Chests. Plan your emerald use during this event for maximum value.

📘 The Path of Wisdom

Spend Energy to earn Outland Coins and Intelligence Skin Stones. Great for mages and healers!

🏹 The Path of Agility

Collect Hero Soul Stones to receive Outland Coins and Agility Skin Stones. Ideal for boosting your agile damage dealers.

💪 The Path of Strength

Open Outland chests to claim Outland Coins and Strength Skin Stones. Focuses on tanks and warriors!

🔥 The Path of Power

Complete other Guardians of Outland event quests to receive even more Outland Coins. Keep progressing for bonus rewards!

Completing each path allows you to gather a wide range of valuable resources. Whether you're building a balanced team or focusing on specific roles, this event offers something for everyone. Time your actions and make the most of every quest!

Tips to Prepare for the Guardians of Outland Event

The Guardians of Outland event lasts only three days, so preparation is key! Here are some expert tips to help you get the most out of every quest and reward:

  • 💡 Save Energy: Stockpile your energy bottles in advance. You’ll need a lot for The Path of Wisdom quests.
  • 💎 Reserve Emeralds: Avoid spending emeralds right before the event starts. Use them during the event to complete Going Forward quests and maximize rewards.
  • 📦 Don’t Open Outland Chests Early: Save your daily Outland fights and chests until the event begins so you can complete The Path of Strength quickly.
  • 🧠 Focus on Daily Login: Logging in every day will automatically complete The Path of Tenacity. Set a reminder!
  • ⭐ Track VIP Progress: If you’re close to leveling up VIP, consider doing it during the event to claim The Path of Glory rewards.
  • 🧬 Farm Hero Soul Stones: Use Campaign or shops to farm soul stones efficiently for The Path of Agility.
  • 🧮 Plan Your Energy Use: Don’t run out of energy too early. Spread your energy usage across the event days to complete multiple quests.

With smart planning and resource management, you can breeze through each quest path and walk away with tons of Skin Stones, Outland Coins, and more. Prepare wisely your heroes are counting on you!

Conclusion

The Guardians of Outland event is one of the best opportunities to strengthen your heroes through targeted quests and valuable rewards. Whether you're aiming to collect Skin Stones, stockpile Outland Coins, or optimize your emerald spending, every quest path offers something that can boost your progress in Hero Wars: Dominion Era.

With just three days to complete the event, preparation and smart resource management are essential. Follow the tips, plan ahead, and don’t forget to log in daily. Your efforts will pay off with stronger heroes and a more powerful team ready to take on any challenge.

Good luck, Guardian the Dominion needs you!
