Tournament Rewards and Leaderboard

The game configures the tasks and rewards for each group separately, depending on the group's team level. There are two types of tournament rewards: stage rewards and final rewards.

You can get stage rewards daily for gaining tournament points and thus reaching specific thresholds in the progress bar:

Note: The stage progress bar refreshes daily at 2:00 AM UTC. Thus, you need to fill the bar from scratch the next day. After refreshing, all unlocked but unclaimed prizes are automatically sent to your in-game mailbox. Letters expire in 3 days, so do not forget to collect the rewards.

To open the leaderboard and see the final rewards, click Leaderboard:

After the active phase of the tournament ends, the results will be counted. This process might take some time.

Note: If players have the same number of points by the end of the tournament, the player who reached this number earlier takes a higher ranking position.

When the results are ready, players may collect the final reward in the tournament window.

Note: If you do not collect the final reward, it will be sent to your in-game mailbox after the results menu closes. Letters expire in 3 days, so do not forget to collect the rewards.

Conclusion

The Tournament of Titan Power is an exciting and rewarding event that challenges you to upgrade your titans and compete with players of similar strength. By actively participating in quests, upgrading your titans, and collecting both daily stage rewards and final leaderboard prizes, you can maximize your progress and gather valuable resources to strengthen your team. Remember to claim your rewards promptly from the mailbox to avoid losing them. Stay focused, upgrade smartly, and dominate the leaderboard in Hero Wars: Dominion Era!
