The Great Storm is an exciting three-day event in Hero Wars: Dominion Era that challenges players to win battles, find hidden treasures, and defeat a powerful boss in the Eye of the Storm.

This survival guide will walk you through everything you need to know to succeed, from exploring the map and maximizing buffs to managing your Action Points and rewards.

Event Map Overview

The event map contains 57 locations, including enemies, hidden chests, Buff Towers, and the final Boss at the center in the Eye of the Storm. Your goal is to strategically move across the map, collect resources, and unlock every possible reward before time runs out.

Destroying Harbinger’s Steles

To weaken the Eye of the Storm and unlock the final boss battle, you must first destroy three Harbinger’s Steles scattered across the map. These are key objectives in progressing through the event.

Buff Towers

There are three Buff Towers placed randomly on the map. When you move your team to one of them, you’ll be able to choose one of three buffs. These enhancements will help your team significantly during battle. Plan your route to collect all three buffs, and make sure to select the ones that best match your team’s strengths.

Hidden Treasure Chests

Hidden throughout the map are Treasure Chests containing valuable rewards. You will receive an A chest is nearby! notification when your team is close to one. To reveal the chest, defeat enemies in adjacent locations. The more areas you explore, the more treasure you’ll uncover.

Understanding Event Elements

  • Storm Resistance: Boosts your team’s armor during event battles.
  • Merchant: Spend Storm Coins to purchase pet resources like Soul Stones and Potions.
  • Log: Displays your battle history across the map.
  • Rules: Read the full event mechanics and details.
  • Timer: Countdown to the event’s end.
  • Chest Tracker: Shows which hidden chests have been opened and which remain locked.
  • Storm Coins: Your current currency earned from quests and battles.
  • Action Points: Used to move across the map. You’ll need them for every step!

Tips for the Great Storm Shop

The event shop in The Great Storm offers valuable resources to strengthen your pets and progress faster in Hero Wars: Dominion Era. Here are some key items you’ll find and tips on how to prioritize your purchases:

  • Pet Soul Chest: Each chest contains 5 pet soul stones, which are essential to increase a pet’s star level. This is one of the best investments if you want to unlock or evolve pets like Axel, Cain, or Khorus. Focus on these if you haven’t maxed out your core pets yet.
  • Storm Legacy: A mystery box that can contain Chaos Particles, Pet Potions, and other useful rewards. It’s a good secondary option if you need a variety of pet upgrade materials, but it’s random so consider it after grabbing specific items you need.
  • Pet Equipment (Green to Violet+3): The shop includes gear for every evolution level. Prioritize equipment based on your current bottlenecks. For example:
    • Buy Violet+1 and +2 gear if your pets are stuck at high levels due to missing rare components.
    • Skip green or blue equipment if you already have a large stockpile or can farm them easily.
    • Check your top 5 patron pets and focus on their promotion path first.

In short, if you're limited on Storm Coins, prioritize Pet Soul Chests for star level progression, then specific pet gear for key pets, and lastly consider Storm Legacy for a chance at extra upgrade materials.

What Happens After the Event Ends?

Once the Great Storm concludes, all unused Action Points and Storm Coins are automatically converted into Storm Legacy Chests. These chests can contain:

  • Chaos Particles
  • Pet Potions
  • Pet Soul Stones
  • And other helpful pet-related rewards

Final Tips for Success

  • Plan your route to collect all buffs and destroy all three Steles before heading to the final boss.
  • Use your Action Points wisel don’t waste them on unnecessary locations early in the event.
  • Log in daily to collect free Action Points and progress through quest chains efficiently.
  • Keep an eye on hidden chests you don’t want to miss free loot!

With strategic planning, daily logins, and smart movement, you’ll conquer the Eye of the Storm and walk away with amazing pet rewards. Good luck!
