The "Power of the Titans" event brings a surge of cosmic energy to Dominion! Whether you're a seasoned player or a newcomer, this guide will help you navigate the event’s tasks and maximize your Titan rewards.

Complete missions, earn event points, and use your hard-earned coins in the exclusive event shop. Unleash the strength of water, fire, earth, light, and darkness as powerful Titans like Solaris and Tenebris join your ranks.

Unleash the Might of the Titans!

In this limited-time event, every completed mission earns you event points. Exchange those points for coins and dive into the event shop, where a trove of Titan-focused rewards awaits.

From Titan soul stones including rare ones for Super Titans like Solaris and Tenebris to essential Titan Potions and Artifacts, the shop is your path to strengthening your army. Whether you prefer the fury of fire or the serenity of water, there's a Titan waiting to grow stronger by your side.

How Event Points Work

Event Points

Score points by completing event quests to earn valuable Event Points. Each time you earn Event Points, they are converted into Emerald Coins, which can be spent in the event shop to purchase exclusive items and upgrades.

Conclusion

The Power of the Titans event offers a fantastic opportunity to strengthen your heroes and collect exclusive rewards. By completing the event missions and earning Event Points, you can unlock powerful titan items and enhance your gameplay experience. Stay dedicated, plan your progress wisely, and enjoy dominating the battlefield with the mighty Titans at your side!
