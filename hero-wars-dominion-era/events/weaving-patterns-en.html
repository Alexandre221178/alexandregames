Event Overview: What is Weaving Patterns?

The Weaving Patterns event in Hero Wars: Dominion Era is a time-limited celebration of persistence and strategy. Players can earn Golden Threads, Chaos Cores, Soul Crystals, and many more resources by completing specific missions across multiple gameplay areas like summoning, spending emeralds, opening tower chests, and more.

Each task you complete contributes to your total progress and unlocks higher-tier rewards, including the exclusive Chest of Patterns. The event rewards your activity throughout the Dominion, encouraging both daily logins and deeper gameplay engagement.

Weaving Patterns Event Rewards

Item Description

Golden Thread 		You need Golden Thread to upgrade war flag patterns. It can be obtained by unraveling patterns.

Emerald Doll III 		Open Emerald Dolls to get Emeralds and higher level Dolls! Contains from 300 to 500 Emeralds.

Chaos Core 		Use this to evolve Artifacts.

Soul Crystal 		Required for summoning heroes. Refills daily.

Small Skin Stone Chest 		Contains 10 Strength, Agility, and Intelligence Skin Stones. You can choose the reward when you open the chest.

Equipment Fragment Chest 		Contains fragments of Orange and Violet equipment for Heroes.

Wish Crystal 		Transforms into 4 Common / 2 Vibrant / 1 Radiant Crystal(s), selected at random.

Chest of Patterns 		Contains war flag patterns. You can choose the reward when you open the chest.
