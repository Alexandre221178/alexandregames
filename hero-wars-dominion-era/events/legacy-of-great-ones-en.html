The Legacy of the Great Ones is a time-limited event in Hero Wars: Dominion Era that focuses on developing your heroes’ artifacts and maximizing your progression with strategic resource spending.

Lasting only three days, this event rewards you for completing specific tasks by offering event points and valuable items essential for strengthening your team.

📅 Event Duration and Access

The event is available for three days and can be found under the Special Events tab in the game interface. Once the event is active, players will see a special icon in the top right of the screen, leading directly to the Legacy of the Great Ones menu.

There’s no need to claim event points manually they are automatically added to your progress bar the moment you complete a task. A brief notification reading “Quest complete!” will also pop up at the top of the screen.

🎯 Event Objectives: Quest Chains Overview

The event is divided into five distinct quest chains. Each chain focuses on a different aspect of the game, giving you multiple ways to earn points and progress. Here are the quest types:

  • Level up any artifact
  • Get VIP points
  • Spend emeralds
  • Spend energy
  • Open the Artifact Chest

Each task has multiple tiers, and completing higher tiers grants more points and better rewards.

Artifacts are key components for hero strength. In this quest chain, you gain progress by using Artifact Resources to level up the three parts of any hero’s artifact:

  • Weapon (red icon)
  • Book (blue icon)
  • Ring (green icon)

Every level upgrade counts toward your event task progress. The more upgrades you do, the more rewards you unlock. This is a great time to plan ahead and use your Artifact Scrolls, Essence, and Metal for maximum return.

Tip: Try to avoid leveling artifacts until the event starts so you can dump resources efficiently and complete as many stages as possible on day one.

Emeralds are a premium currency in Hero Wars, and spending them during the event grants you points in this chain. You can spend emeralds in many ways, but some give better return than others:

  • Opening Artifact Chests (also counts for quest #5)
  • Buying Energy refills (counts for quest #4)
  • Refreshing shops or purchasing Arena attempts

Tip: Focus your emerald spending on actions that contribute to multiple quests at once such as spending emeralds to open Artifact Chests so you can double dip on rewards.

Artifact Chests are located in the “Artifacts” section of the game and contain a random assortment of artifact resources. You can open them using Artifact Keys or spend emeralds to open more.

Opening these chests contributes to this final quest chain. Since this also counts as a way to spend emeralds, it ties in perfectly with quests #3 and #1.

Tip: Hoard your Artifact Keys and open them during this event. If you have extra emeralds, this is one of the most efficient ways to spend them.

🎁 Event Rewards

Each completed quest stage gives you Event Points. As you accumulate points, you fill the event progress bar and unlock milestone rewards. These typically include:

  • Absolute Artifact Essence
  • Absolute Artifact Scroll
  • Absolute Artifact Metal
  • Artifact Coins
  • Flawless Artifact
  • Chaos Core

The more quests you complete across all chains, the faster you’ll reach the top-tier rewards. There are usually 6–9 milestones, each offering increasingly valuable loot.

💡 Best Practices and Tips

  • Synchronize quests: Spend emeralds on Artifact Chests to hit three chains: emeralds, chests, and artifact level-ups.
  • Plan energy spending: Use all free energy, bottled energy, and emerald refills for mission farming.
  • Use resources wisely: Don’t waste artifact materials before the event. Hoard them for maximum progression.
  • Keep an eye on bundles: During this event, exclusive bundles are often available that include Artifact Keys and emeralds. Consider buying them if you're aiming to reach top milestones.

🤔 Is the Legacy of the Great Ones Worth It?

Absolutely especially if you're actively building your heroes. Artifact development is a critical factor in battle power, and this event is one of the best times to focus on it. By timing your emerald spending, energy use, and artifact upgrades, you can unlock excellent rewards that directly contribute to your team's long-term growth.

Free-to-play players can still benefit greatly by saving up keys, energy bottles, and artifact resources beforehand. While you might not complete every quest chain, you can still reach several milestones and receive meaningful upgrades.

🏁 Final Thoughts

The Legacy of the Great Ones is a high-impact, short-duration event that rewards strategic play and resource management. It gives players an ideal opportunity to strengthen their hero artifacts, spend emeralds wisely, and push for powerful progress rewards. Whether you're a spender or a free-to-play strategist, with good preparation and planning, you can make the most out of this event and come out stronger on the other side.

Prepare ahead, spend smart, and let the legacy of your heroes grow stronger with each day!
