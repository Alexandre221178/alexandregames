Road to the Top ‚Äì Seasonal Rewards Progression

Leveling up your rewards in the Area of Conquest event is simple and exciting! Complete seasonal quests to earn season experience, which increases your reward level. At each level, you can claim exclusive rewards, including Superior Coins, Health Potions, Speed Boosters, and Hero Upgrade Chests. The more quests you complete, the higher your reward level, so make sure to participate daily and maximize your gains!

Battle for Territories ‚Äì Guild Ranking Rewards

In the Area of Conquest event, your guild‚Äôs ranking determines the rewards every member receives. Gather resources and earn Superior Coins on the map to climb the rankings! Remember: only Coins collected on the map count towards your guild‚Äôs position.

The higher your guild ranks, the better the rewards. Top guilds can earn Chest of Patterns, which contains war flag patterns. You can choose the specific reward when opening the chest. Additionally, Golden Thread is awarded, which is used to upgrade war flag patterns. Obtain Golden Thread by unraveling patterns and maximize your guild‚Äôs strategic advantage!

Conclusion ‚Äì Master the Area of Conquest

The Area of Conquest event in Hero Wars: Dominion Era is a unique opportunity for both new and experienced players to earn incredible rewards while enjoying exciting cross-server PvP battles.
