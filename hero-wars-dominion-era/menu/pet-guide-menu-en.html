🐾 Pet Skills and Patronage Guide – Hero Wars: Dominion Era

In Hero Wars, pets play a crucial role by providing powerful skills and support to your heroes. Each pet has two main functionalities: active skills used during battles and patronage that boosts heroes' stats even outside of battle.

🔥 Active Skills

Pets can only use their active abilities when they are actually deployed in battle. Each pet comes with two distinct active skills:

  • First Active Skill: This is available from the start. It becomes usable once the pet's energy bar is full. You can trigger it manually or let it auto-activate as soon as it's ready.
  • Second Active Skill: Unlocked at green rank, this skill activates automatically based on its cooldown timer and does not require energy.

Note: Only one pet can be active in a battle at any given time.

🛡️ Patronage

Once a pet reaches blue rank, it gains the ability to serve as a patron to a specific hero. Patronage gives bonus stats to the chosen hero in most battle modes (excluding max power test battles).

At violet rank, the pet unlocks an additional patronage skill that further boosts its assigned hero.

  • The pet does not need to be in battle for the patronage effects to apply.
  • Each pet can only be a patron for a limited list of heroes. To view the list, go to the Sanctuary, select a pet, and open the "Patronage" tab.
  • You can assign five different patrons to a team, one per hero, even though only one pet enters the battlefield.

📊 Pet Stats

Pets have three important stats that influence their performance:

  • Skill Power: Affects the strength of the pet’s active skills.
  • Armor or Magic Penetration: Depending on the pet, this determines how much physical or magical defense is bypassed.
  • Patronage Power: Defines the impact of a pet’s support when acting as a patron, including its patronage skill.

Mastering pet mechanics is key to building an elite team in Hero Wars: Dominion Era. Choose wisely and upgrade strategically!
