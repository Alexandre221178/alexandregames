Master every Pet Adventure in Hero Wars: Dominion Era with our guide. Learn strategies, buffs, and tips to beat each map with your guildmates.

Minimum Level Required

Adventures become accessible once your team reaches level 40. Upon first entering the Sanctuary, you'll be introduced to the system through a tutorial Adventure.

Overview

Adventures are a cooperative mode where you team up with two other Guild members. You can create either a public group, open to all eligible Guildmates, or a private group, accessible only via invitation. To join, a Guild member must not be currently in another Adventure and must meet the level requirement for the selected map.

There are 13 unique Adventures available, each offering different levels of difficulty and requiring varied strategies for success.

Daily Buffs

Before launching an Adventure, you can preview the required team level, potential rewards, and the daily buffs that affect gameplay. These buffs impact your team, enemies, and bosses, enhancing certain hero roles such as healers or supports. Buffs are assigned randomly and reset automatically at 02:00 AM UTC each day.

Thanks to these buffs, every Adventure can offer a slightly different challenge, making it important to adjust your team composition accordingly.

Portal Charges

To begin any Adventure, you must use one Portal Charge. These cannot be purchased directly. You receive one free Portal Charge daily at 02:00 AM UTC. You can store up to three this way once full, new daily charges will not accumulate.

Additional Portal Charges may be obtained through:

  • Special in-game events
  • Guild Quest rewards
  • Occasional gifts from the official Hero Wars social media channels

Note: Portal Charges gained from these extra sources can exceed the standard three-charge limit.

Adventure Action Points

After starting an Adventure, you’ll be given a set amount of Action Points, used to move across the map. Each movement costs one Action Point.

Note: You cannot purchase or regenerate Action Points, so use them wisely.

Battle Mechanics

Enemy squads are generally random, though some heroes are fixed and always appear in specific locations. If you lose a battle, the enemy’s remaining health and energy will persist into the next attempt.

  • You cannot exit a fight once started, but you may skip the battle, which will then play out automatically.
  • You cannot attack an enemy that another teammate is currently battling but you can spectate the fight.

Adventure Chests

Each Adventure rewards players with up to four chests. Three smaller chests are unlocked by collecting Victory Points, which are earned by defeating enemy squads (20 points per win). A final large chest becomes available after defeating the boss.

Adventure Raid

Once you have completed an Adventure and opened all four chests at least once, you unlock the ability to raid it. Raiding lets you instantly complete that Adventure and claim the rewards without replaying it.

Raids are available until the daily reset at 02:00 AM UTC and consume one Portal Charge.

How to Exit an Adventure

After defeating the boss or once all Action Points are spent by every player, you can end the session by clicking the “End the Adventure” button.

Alternatively, you can leave an Adventure at any time via the Log menu:

  1. Click the Log button in the lower-left corner of the game screen.
  2. Select Leave Adventure.
