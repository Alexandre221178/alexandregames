Vex is one of the most powerful pets in Hero Wars: Dominion Era, providing both direct and supportive bonuses for physical attackers. This guide covers Vex’s maximum stats, patronage effects, and battle utility so you can fully understand its impact on your team’s performance.

With deep synergy around Physical Attack and Armor Penetration, Vex becomes an essential companion for heroes focused on raw damage output. Keep reading to explore its skills, stats, and best uses in battle.

Who Is Vex?

Vex is a pet in Hero Wars: Dominion Era designed to strengthen physical damage dealers. Its unique ability, Deep Wounds, stacks extra damage on enemy targets, allowing allied heroes to shred through defenses faster.

  • Class: Pet
  • Position: Support (Physical Damage Focus)
  • Main Stat: Patronage Power

Vex empowers its owner with Physical Attack and Armor Penetration boosts, depending on Patronage Power. This makes it an excellent choice for heroes that rely on critical strikes and raw damage output like Yasmine, Lara Croft, Ishmael and others..

Its bonus skill applies Deep Wounds stacks on enemies, amplifying incoming physical damage from all allies. With up to 5 stacks active, your team gains a powerful edge in both PvP and PvE battles.

Vex Pros and Cons - Hero Wars: Web and Facebook

✅ Pros

  • Provides strong Armor Penetration bonus, enhancing physical damage dealers.
  • Deep Wounds skill increases team-wide physical damage against marked enemies.
  • Works well with heroes that rely heavily on physical critical hits and burst damage.
  • Good synergy with multiple marksman heroes in Dominion Era.
  • Effective in both PvP and PvE when paired with the right team composition.

❌ Cons

  • Limited to supporting only physical damage heroes, making it situational.
  • Deep Wounds stacks disappear after 8 seconds, reducing effectiveness in long fights without consistent DPS.
  • Less effective against magic-based teams or heroes with high dodge.
  • Requires strong Patronage Power to maximize bonus damage and utility.
  • Other pets may provide more versatile buffs depending on the matchup.

Vex Skills Upgrade Priority - Hero Wars: Dominion Era

Vex focuses on weakening enemies and boosting allies' damage with clouds, curses, and wounds for maximum physical impact.

Vex Active Skills

Noxious Cloud

Vex releases a poisonous cloud in the middle of the enemy team. This cloud damages enemies every half second and lowers their armor. It lasts for 5 seconds, which means enemies inside will take continuous damage and also become weaker against your team’s attacks. In simple terms, the longer they stay in the cloud, the more they suffer, and the easier it is for your heroes to hurt them.

Tips: Its area damage combined with armor reduction makes it devastating against multiple enemies, proving highly effective in both PvP and PvE battles.

Curse of the Flesh

Vex puts a curse on the weakest enemy (the one with the least health). For 7 seconds, that enemy and their nearby allies take extra pure damage every time a basic attack hits them. Pure damage ignores defenses, which means they cannot block or reduce this extra damage. It’s like Vex makes them extremely fragile for a short period of time.

Tips: Best used against strong enemies with low health. Your team’s basic attacks become deadly while this curse is active.

Vex Patronage Skill

Deep Wounds

When Vex supports a hero through Patronage, every physical attack that hero makes puts a “Deep Wound” on the enemy. Wounds stack up to 5 times and last for 8 seconds. Each stack makes all future physical attacks from your whole team deal extra damage. Imagine it like poking holes in enemy armor – the more stacks, the more vulnerable they are to every strike.

Tips: Place Vex on a strong physical damage hero, preferably one with area attacks, to quickly apply stacks. This boosts the overall team’s damage, not just the patron hero.

Conclusion

Vex is a powerful pet for boosting physical damage teams in Hero Wars: Dominion Era, especially on Web and Facebook versions.

With his Armor Penetration bonus and the Deep Wounds effect, he significantly enhances the performance of marksmen and other physical DPS heroes.

While he is not the most versatile pet and works best in specific team setups, Vex becomes an invaluable asset when used alongside the right physical attackers. If your strategy relies on consistent physical burst damage, Vex should be a top priority in your pet roster.
