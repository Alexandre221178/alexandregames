Who Is Pet Merlin?

Pet Merlin is one of the most powerful magical companions in Hero Wars: Dominion Era, known for his exceptional Magic Penetration and Skill Power that can turn the tide of battle.

  • Class: Magic Pet
  • Position: Backline Support
  • Main Stat: Magic Penetration (47,911 at max)

With a maximum power level of 181,943, Merlin stands as one of the top-tier pets in the game. His impressive Skill Power (12,360) and Patronage Power (11,064) make him particularly valuable for magic-based teams.

This guide will explore Merlin's abilities, optimal hero pairings, and strategies to maximize his potential in both PvE and PvP battles.

Pet Merlin Pros and Cons - Hero Wars: Web and Facebook

✅ Pros

  • Massive Magic Boost: With 47,911 Magic Penetration and 12,360 Skill Power at max level, Merlin shreds through enemy defenses
  • Area Control: His Magnus Magica! creates a persistent damage zone that continues hurting enemies even after casting
  • Magic Team Synergy: The Phobia de Magica! skill reduces enemy magic defense, amplifying damage from all magic allies
  • Speed Buff: Tempus Magica! significantly increases skill activation speed for patronaged magic heroes
  • Versatile Patronage: Works with 18 different magic heroes including top-tier choices like Orion, Satori, and Lars/Krista
  • Backline Safety: As a support pet, Merlin stays protected behind your tank while dealing damage

❌ Cons

  • Magic-Only: Useless with physical damage dealers, limiting team composition options
  • Position Dependent: Magnus Magica! only hits frontline enemies, missing backline threats
  • High Investment: Requires significant resources to reach his powerful max stats (181,943 power)
  • Timing Sensitive: The distortion zone lasts only 5 seconds - enemies can move out of it
  • Counterable: Magic defense teams or heroes like Cornelius can neutralize his effectiveness
  • Patronage Limitation: While versatile, only benefits specific magic heroes (not all magic users)

Pet Merlin Skills Explained - Hero Wars: Dominion Era

Learn how Merlin's magic skills work in battle and how to use them effectively to defeat your enemies.

Pet Merlin Active Skills

Magnus Magica!

Merlin's main attack creates a purple magic circle on the ground that lasts for 5 seconds. Enemies standing in this circle take continuous extra damage.

Tips: This skill works best against groups of enemies. For maximum effect, pair Merlin with heroes who attack enemies in frontline (like Aurora or Orion) to keep more targets in the damage zone.

Phobia de Magica!

Merlin weakens the closest enemy's magic defense, making them take more damage from all magic attacks (not just Merlin's).

Tips: This is especially powerful when paired with other magic heroes. The enemy tank (usually in front) will often be the target, making your whole team's magic attacks stronger against them.

Pet Merlin Patronage Skill

Tempus Magica!

When assigned to a hero, Merlin makes their magic skills activate faster. This means your hero can attack more frequently in battle.

Tips: Best used with heroes who have powerful but slow magic attacks. The speed boost can help them get their big attacks off before the enemy can react.

Conclusion: Pet Merlin in Hero Wars: Dominion Era (Web & Facebook)

Pet Merlin stands as one of the most powerful magic-oriented companions in Hero Wars: Dominion Era, particularly valuable for players who focus on magic-based team compositions. His unique combination of area damage, magic penetration, and skill acceleration makes him a game-changer against physical defense teams. The ability to boost multiple top-tier magic heroes through his patronage system adds tremendous versatility to various end-game strategies.

However, Merlin's effectiveness comes with notable limitations that players should consider before investing heavily. His magic-exclusive focus means he provides no value to physical damage teams, and his abilities can be countered by magic defense specialists. The high resource requirement to maximize his potential (reaching 181,943 power) also makes him a long-term investment rather than an early-game solution.

For magic team users playing the Web and Facebook version, Merlin is undoubtedly a top-tier pet worth acquiring and upgrading, especially when paired with heroes like Orion, Satori, or the twins Lars/Krista. While not universally applicable like some pets, his specialized magic amplification creates some of the most devastating offensive combinations in the game when used correctly. Strategic players will find Merlin's unique skill set can dominate many battle scenarios, particularly in Guild Wars and Arena offense.
