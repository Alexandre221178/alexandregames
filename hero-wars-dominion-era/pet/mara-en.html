Mara is a formidable pet in Hero Wars: Dominion Era, known for boosting magic attack and defense stats. She enhances your hero's control effects, making battles easier.

Who Is Mara?

Mara is a magical pet that excels in enhancing your hero's power through increased magic attack and defense stats, along with powerful control effects.

  • Class: Magical
  • Position: Support
  • Main Stat: Magic Attack & Magic Defense

Mara's unique skill, Deadly Weakness, increases the duration of stun, blinding, silence, slow, charm, and mind control effects inflicted by the master. This control boost depends on Mara's Patronage Power.

With maximum stats of 181,943 Power, 47,911 Magic Penetration, 12,360 Skill Power, and 11,064 Patronage Power, Mara is an essential pet for strategic play in Hero Wars: Dominion Era.

Mara Pros and Cons - Hero Wars: Web and Facebook

✅ Pros

  • Strong magic damage with Darkguard, especially against controlled enemies (triple damage).
  • Mind Control skill temporarily turns an enemy to your side, disrupting opponent’s strategy.
  • Patronage skill Deadly Weakness extends the duration of control effects, increasing overall team crowd control.
  • Patronage works even when Mara is not in battle, providing passive support to your hero.
  • Good synergy with heroes who rely on control effects to maximize damage and disable enemies.

❌ Cons

  • Mind Control and Darkguard effectiveness drop against enemies above level 130.
  • Requires careful timing or luck for maximum effect since some skills rely on enemy status effects.
  • Patronage bonuses only affect specific heroes, limiting versatility.
  • Active skills depend on Mara being alive and present in battle to activate.
  • Lower impact if your team lacks control effect abilities to trigger Mara’s damage multiplier.

Mara Skills Upgrade Priority - Hero Wars: Dominion Era

Learn how Mara’s skills work in battle and how they help you control enemies and boost your hero’s magic power effectively.

Mara Active Skills

Darkguard

Darkguard is Mara’s first active skill and deals magic damage to all enemies. It is especially powerful when enemies are already affected by control effects like stun, blind, silence, slow, or charm — they take triple damage!

Tips: Use this skill when enemies are under control effects for maximum damage. It activates automatically when Mara's energy bar is full or can be used manually.

Mind Control

Mind Control is Mara’s second active skill, unlocked at green rank. This skill charms one enemy, forcing them to fight on your side for 3 seconds. It can turn the tide of battle by temporarily removing a strong enemy from the opponent's team.

Tips: This skill works best to disable a key enemy hero. Since it activates automatically based on cooldown, it’s important to keep Mara disrupting foes.

Mara Patronage Skill

Deadly Weakness

Deadly Weakness is Mara’s patronage skill, unlocked at violet rank. This skill increases the duration of control effects like stun, blind, silence, slow, charm, and mind control that your hero inflicts on enemies. The boost depends on Mara’s Patronage Power, meaning the higher her patronage stats, the longer enemies stay controlled.

Tips: This skill works boosting your hero’s control power consistently. It’s great for players who want to maximize control effects in fights.

Mara Guide Conclusion

Mara is a valuable pet in Hero Wars: Dominion Era, especially for players who want to enhance their team’s control and magic damage capabilities. Her active skills provide strong area magic damage and the ability to charm enemies, which can turn the tide of battle in your favor.

Her patronage skill, Deadly Weakness, is particularly useful because it boosts the duration of crowd control effects, giving your heroes more time to disable opponents. This makes Mara a great choice for players who rely on control-heavy strategies and want to maximize their heroes' effectiveness.

While Mara’s skills have some limitations against high-level enemies and depend on proper timing and team composition, her overall utility as both an active fighter and a patron makes her a strong addition to any lineup. Players using Mara will benefit from her unique ability to disrupt enemies and support their team consistently in battles across Web and Facebook versions.
