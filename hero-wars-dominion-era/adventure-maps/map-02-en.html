PET Adventure Map #2 – Valley of the Elements

  • Minimum Hero Level: 45+
  • Recommended Total Power: 22,000+

Valley of the Elements is the second Pet Adventure map in Hero Wars: Dominion Era. In this map, three players must coordinate their movements, plan their routes, and defeat the enemy squads and towers strategically to weaken the final boss. The map is recommended for players with a hero level of 45 and a minimum team power of 21,000.

The key to success on this map is teamwork. Each player should choose a specific route (Blue, Yellow, or Green) and wait at buff points before advancing. It's critical to destroy towers before dealing with their corresponding squads, as the towers grant powerful buffs to enemies.

  • Blue Path: 03 → 06 → 09 → Boss (x2)
  • Yellow Path: 02 → 05 → 08 → 09 → Boss
  • Green Path: 04 → 07 → 10 → 09 → Boss

The final boss in Valley of the Elements is heavily buffed and should only be attacked once all supporting towers and squads are cleared. Make sure all players reach the buff zones before progressing to maximize your team's power.

Boss Boost Effects:

  • +15% damage dealt
  • -50% damage taken
  • Health multiplied by 10
  • +100% energy gain
  • -80% debuff duration
  • No single attack can deal more than 1% of max HP
  • Immune to energy reduction skills

These buffs make the boss extremely hard to take down without proper preparation. That’s why clearing the entire map and destroying towers is essential before the final battle.

Squad 10 - Eye of Harbinger:

This squad grants the boss the "Damage Resist" buff, reducing all incoming damage by 50%. You must defeat Squad 10 before facing the boss to remove this effect. Failing to do so will drastically prolong the boss fight.

Another tip: If your team composition includes heroes that inflict debuffs or reduce energy, these will be mostly ineffective due to the boss's immunity. Focus on heroes that deal consistent damage and those who can survive for extended periods.

Try building your team around the Buff of the Day. Some days will favor tanks, others will increase energy gain, and some will boost control effects. Adapt your squad to match the buffs for best performance.

Plan your route efficiently. Every movement costs action points, and they do not regenerate. Make sure you and your teammates discuss strategies beforehand, and stick to your assigned path.

Conclusion

Pet Adventure Map 2 – Valley of the Elements requires strong coordination, smart routing, and communication with your guildmates. Destroying towers and clearing squads before fighting the boss is the key to victory. Remember to wait at buff points and defeat Squad 10 to remove the boss’s damage reduction buff. Good luck in your adventure!

Don’t forget to collect your loot after defeating the boss: Pet Potions, Chaos Particles, Summoning Espheres, and rare stones await!
