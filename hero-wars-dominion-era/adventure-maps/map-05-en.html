Map 5 offers a balanced challenge that rewards coordination, timing, and correct path choices. The battle takes place in Strongford, and its defenses are under siege. Your team must break through empowered enemy squads, defeat tower buffs, and take down a boss strengthened by multiple enhancements. Choose your path wisely and trust your guildmates.

The Siege of Strongford is a 3-player map, and every player has a critical role. There are powerful towers enhancing enemy squads, and only by defeating these towers can you weaken your foes. Coordinate your movement so you and your allies destroy the correct towers in time. Donâ€™t rush wait for others to reach buff points so you can advance strategically.

To defeat the boss, you must remove its buffs by defeating the squads labeled as Eye of the Harbinger. These buffs drastically increase the bossâ€™s power, so they are your top priority. Remember: more towers destroyed means an easier final fight.

Understanding the boss mechanics is essential. This boss begins the fight with several powerful boosts that make it almost impossible to defeat unless handled correctly. These include:

Boss Boost: Level 70
Increases damage dealt by 100%
Decreases damage taken by 25%
Multiplies Health by 10
Increases energy gain by 100%
Decreases debuff duration by 80%
No attack can damage the boss for more than 1% of max Health
Immune to energy reduction skills

Additional Buffs:
Damage Resist: Decreases damage taken by 65%.
Defeat Squad 20 (Eye of the Harbinger) to remove this buff.

Damage Increase: Increases boss damage dealt by 130%.
Defeat Squad 27 (Eye of the Harbinger) to remove this buff.

Another Damage Resist: Decreases damage taken by another 65%.
Defeat Squad 25 (Eye of the Harbinger) to remove this buff.

Defeating the Eye of the Harbinger squads at 20, 25, and 27 is vital. Without this, the boss remains nearly invincible. Once these squads are cleared, the boss becomes manageable, even under the harsh map buffs.

Bring heroes that deal consistent damage and survive long fights. Pets like Biscuit and Axel are excellent for their healing reduction and protection. Khorus can help resist control effects, while Fenris or Cain boost physical damage output.

Communicate with your guildmates. Use voice or text chat to coordinate tower takedowns, buff grabs, and final boss strategy. Timing is everything.

Conclusion

Siege of Strongford, Adventure Map 5, is where teamwork makes all the difference. Its difficulty lies not in individual strength, but in synchronized execution. You win or lose based on how well your guild acts as one.

Always destroy towers before engaging major squads. Wait for allies at buff points. And most importantly, weaken the boss by eliminating its supporting squads. Doing these steps makes victory and valuable loot inevitable.

With 229 Pet Potions, 72 Chaos Particles, and valuable Espheres and stones at stake, Siege of Strongford is worth the effort. Prepare your pets, rally your guild, and let your legacy unfold!
