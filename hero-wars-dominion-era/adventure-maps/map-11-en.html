Ghirwil City Depths presents a challenging adventure that requires careful coordination with your guildmates. This guide will help you navigate the map efficiently and defeat the powerful boss.

Key Strategy Points

CRUCIAL: Towers 36, 38, and 40 MUST be destroyed before facing the boss to reduce his strength. Wait for your teammates to destroy these towers.

Buff Strategy: Before advancing, get the buff at Squad 2, and wait for your friends to enter to also get the Squad 2 buff. This way you advance with all 3 buffs from squad 2. Patience is key to victory.

All three players will pass through squads 2 and 6 on the map where the buffs are located, making everyone stronger.

Recommended Sequence & Tactics

  1. Initial wave: Start together, clear the opening nodes to preserve Action Points.
  2. Split to assigned lanes: Follow your lane but stay close enough to regroup at major buff points.
  3. Secure buff points: Squad 2 and Squad 6 contain long-lasting buff flowers â€” pass through both so all three players can claim buffs.
  4. Destroy priority towers: Take down Towers 36 â†’ 38 â†’ 40 before touching the boss.
  5. Regroup and boss: After those towers are down, heal, buff and then engage the boss together. Save major cooldowns for boss phases.

Buff Point Etiquette

Each player picks one buff at buff points. Coordinate who takes what (damage increase vs resist vs healing). Never pick a buff alone â€” wait for the whole team to arrive to maximize the benefit.

Boss Mechanics

Boss Boost

  • Increases damage dealt by 25%

  • Communicate with simple pings or short voice cues â€” announce buffs and tower dives.
  • Save a major defensive/healing cooldown for the boss phase after towers are down.
  • If more than one player dies on a tower dive, reset and retry â€” small mistakes compound on this map.
  • Make sure at least one player builds survivability â€” runs fail quickly without sustain.

Conclusion

Success in Ghirwil City Depths requires careful coordination with your guildmates. The key points to remember are:

  1. All three players must collect buffs from Squads 2 and 6
  2. Towers 36, 38, and 40 MUST be destroyed before facing the boss
  3. Wait for your teammates to complete their objectives before advancing
  4. Choose buffs that complement your team composition
  5. Plan your path carefully to maximize efficiency with limited Action Points

With proper coordination and execution of this strategy, your team will be able to conquer Ghirwil City Depths and claim its valuable rewards.
