PET Adventure Map 8 – Required

  • Minimum Hero Level: 100+
  • Recommended Total Power: 160k+

The moment you step into the ruins of the Celestial City, you can feel the weight of what was lost. Crumbled towers, echoes of ancient magic, and the remnants of battles long past this map isn’t just a challenge, it’s a story. A story of bravery, sacrifice, and camaraderie. If you’ve made it to Adventure Map 8, you’re no beginner. You know the stakes. But here, everything is elevated: the tension, the rewards, and the reliance on your guildmates. This is where legends are born, or broken.

The player who takes the yellow path will not face the boss directly. Instead, their vital role is to bring down Towers 29 and 30, which are essential to weakening the boss. This player also helps clear the center of the map, creating a safer path for their allies and ensuring the final battle is more manageable for the team.

Tips for Map #8 Boss

In Fall of the Celestial City, three players must work in perfect sync to secure buffs, destroy towers, and dismantle the defenses of a terrifying boss. Timing is everything don’t rush ahead. Reach buff locations together to ensure your team gains the full advantage. And never forget: every tower that falls weakens your enemies.

Before rushing to the boss, your team must dismantle specific towers and squads to strip the boss of powerful buffs. Mistimed moves can waste valuable action points and make the final encounter near impossible.

Defeating the boss is no small feat. Before you even reach the final fight, you must disable two specific squads: 30 and 29 both Eyes of the Harbinger. These squads hold the key to lowering the boss’s immense defenses.

Boss Boost Map 8

  • Increases damage dealt by 100%
  • Decreases damage taken by 25%
  • Multiplies Health by 10
  • Increases energy gain by 100%
  • Decreases debuff duration by 80%
  • No attack can damage the boss for more than 1% of max Health
  • Immune to energy reduction skills

Additional Buffs:

  • Damage Increase: Increases overall boss damage
  • Damage Resist (x2): Decreases damage taken by 80% defeat Squads 30 and 29 to remove this

Recommended heroes: prioritize Sebastian, Nebula, Axel, and strong single-target DPS like Keira or Dante. Use Martha for healing and consider pets like Fenris or Albus for extra pressure.

Loot from Map 8:

  • Pet Potion ×838
  • Chaos Particle ×124
  • Pet Summoning Sphere ×9
  • Excellent Duckstone ×1
  • Excellent Dawnstone ×1

The Excellent Duckstone is a valuable resource used to evolve the pet Khorus. Known for his strong magic defense support and aura against control effects, Khorus plays a vital role in battles against teams that rely on crowd control and magic burst. Obtaining this stone is a major step toward boosting your team's resilience and counterplay capabilities in both PvP and PvE situations.

The Excellent Dawnstone, on the other hand, is key for upgrading Axel, one of the most versatile and powerful pets in Hero Wars. Axel’s ability to redirect damage and support healers makes him a top-tier choice for many teams. This reward allows players to push Axel to higher ranks, making him even more effective in protecting vulnerable heroes and enabling strategic survival in tough fights.

Conclusion:

Map 8 – Fall of the Celestial City is a journey of trust and tactical depth. You will need your guildmates not just to survive, but to win with style. This map rewards communication, timing, and patience above all else. Every step, every tower, and every decision matters.

So rally your team, mark your paths, and dive into the ruins. The Celestial City is waiting to be reclaimed. Will you rise with it?
