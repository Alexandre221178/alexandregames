PET Adventure Map #10 – Valley of the Elements

  • Minimum Hero Level: 110+
  • Recommended Total Power: 300k+

Valley of the Elements is one of the toughest maps in the Pet Adventure mode. Unlike earlier maps, this one requires complete coordination among three guild members. The boss here is protected by powerful buffs and fortified towers, and enemies hit hard. Players must follow designated paths, time their attacks together, and remove boss buffs before the final encounter.

Strategic Pathing: Divide and Conquer

Map 10 requires three players, each following one of the predetermined paths: Yellow, Blue, or Green. These paths are color-coded on the Alexandre Games map for ease. Choosing the correct path is essential not just for your own success, but for the success of your allies.

Before advancing to any buff towers or Eye of the Harbinger squads, always coordinate with your guildmates. All three of you should arrive at key points simultaneously. If you rush ahead, you may trigger buffs that make enemy squads harder for your allies to defeat.

Important: This map is extremely challenging. If your team power is only slightly above the minimum or just meets the required level 110, it’s best to wait and power up more. Entering too early can jeopardize your team’s entire run.

How to Defeat the Boss in Valley of the Elements

Now comes the toughest part the boss battle. The boss in Map 10 is not only powerful but heavily buffed. These buffs must be removed before engaging or your attacks will do virtually no damage.

Boss Boost Overview:

  • +100% Damage Dealt
  • −80% Damage Taken
  • 10x Health Multiplier
  • +100% Energy Gain
  • −80% Debuff Duration
  • Immune to Energy Reduction
  • Damage Cap: 1% of Max HP per Hit

These boosts make it nearly impossible to kill the boss unless you first destroy the buffing squads: the Eye of the Harbinger towers.

Buff Removal Strategy:

Each Eye of the Harbinger controls a specific boss buff. Coordinate with your team so the Green path player reaches and defeats the following squads:

  • Squad 43: Removes Damage Resist (90%)
  • Squad 45: Removes another layer of Damage Resist (90%)
  • Squad 49: Removes Damage Increase (+225%)
  • Squad 51: Removes final Damage Resist (90%)

Only after removing these buffs can you engage the boss and stand a real chance at victory.

Tips for Team Coordination

Pet Adventures are about more than just brute force. Communication is key. Use your guild chat or voice chat to coordinate movement, timing, and which player takes which path. If one player runs ahead and triggers buffs or towers too early, it weakens the entire team’s chances.

Before starting:

  • Assign paths: Blue, Yellow, Green
  • Wait at buff points until everyone arrives
  • Don’t attack towers or Eye of the Harbinger squads until ready
  • Plan who will target squads 43, 45, 49, and 51

Remember: Teamwork over ego. No single player can win this map alone.

Conclusion

Valley of the Elements is where good players become great. It’s more than just another Pet Adventure it’s a true test of power, patience, and partnership. Many will try it, few will beat it on their first attempt. But with this guide, you now have the roadmap to success.

So before you hit “Start Adventure,” gather your guildmates, discuss your strategy, and ensure everyone is prepared. Follow the paths, take down the buffs, and claim victory in one of Hero Wars: Dominion Era’s most challenging maps yet.
