PET Adventure Map #3 – Ghirwil City Depths

  • Minimum Hero Level: 49+
  • Recommended Total Power: 40,000+

Ghirwil City Depths is one of the early Adventure Maps in Hero Wars: Dominion Era, designed for pet teams starting around level 45. It’s important that you coordinate with your guildmates, as the map requires three players to complete. Each one should follow a designated path and wait at buff towers so your entire team benefits before proceeding. The more towers you defeat, the weaker the enemies become especially the boss.

Timing and teamwork are key. Only proceed after everyone is ready to eliminate buffs and reach the final boss area. Always use the shortest and most efficient routes to minimize Action Point costs.

How to Beat the Boss

To defeat the boss in Ghirwil City Depths, your team must first eliminate the Eye of the Harbinger squads located at points 10 and 13. These squads apply powerful buffs to the boss, such as massive damage resistance and increased damage output. Without removing them, the boss will be nearly impossible to defeat. Once the buffs are cleared, focus on burst damage and sustain use pets and heroes that deal percentage-based damage or reduce armor/magic defense. Coordination is key, as even with buffs removed, the boss remains highly resistant to single-target nukes.

Boss Boost:

Increases damage dealt by 25%

Decreases damage taken by 75%

Multiplies Health by 10

Increases energy gain by 100%

Decreases debuff duration by 80%

No attack can damage the boss for more than 1% of max Health

Immune to energy reduction skills

Boss Damage Increase:

Damage Resist: Decreases damage taken by 55%
Defeat Squad 13 - Eye of Harbinger to remove this buff from BOSS

Damage Increase: Increases damage dealt by 110%
Defeat Squad 10 - Eye of Harbinger to remove this buff from BOSS

Conclusion

Ghirwil City Depths is a strong test of coordination, power scaling, and route strategy. Make sure to follow the recommended power levels, clear towers strategically, and eliminate Eye of the Harbinger squads before fighting the boss. Use this guide and the copyable routes to coordinate more efficiently with your guild.

Timing is everything in this adventure. Advancing too early without waiting for your teammates at buff towers can lead to unnecessary losses and wasted Action Points. Communicate clearly with your guildmates and make sure all three players are synced, especially before removing buffs that affect the boss fight. The map is short but punishing if tackled carelessly.

As your team grows stronger and your pets rank up, you'll be able to complete this map faster and with more confidence. Ghirwil City Depths also provides useful loot like Chaos Particles, Summoning Espheres, and pet stones for Fenris, Cain, Albus, and others making it worth repeating. Mastering this early map is a great step toward conquering harder adventures in Hero Wars: Dominion Era.
