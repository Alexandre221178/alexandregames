Keira, a ghostly marksman who fights from the middle line, was once a brave royal guard. After falling in battle against Morth Chrone, she was resurrected as a restless spirit, doomed to wander forever.

In Hero Wars: Dominion Era, Keira stands out as a powerful agility-based hero capable of dealing high physical damage and piercing enemy defenses. This guide will help you master her stats, strengths, and ideal team synergies to dominate your enemies.

Keira Skills Guide and Evolution Priority for Hero Wars Web and Facebook

Learn the ideal skill upgrade order for Keira in Hero Wars: Dominion Era to maximize her damage, speed, and overall combat efficiency.

Blade Whirlwind

Keira throws spinning blades that travel to the farthest enemy and come back, hitting all enemies in their path. When the blades return, they silence enemies for 4 seconds, which stops them from using their skills temporarily. This is her strongest skill, dealing damage to multiple enemies and helping control the battle.

Ascension skill: Raging Blade Whirlwind – After the blades return, Keira gains the Enraged Soul buff for 2 seconds, greatly boosting her speed.

Evolution Priority: High – This is Keira’s main damage and control skill. It affects multiple enemies and gains a powerful buff after ascension.

Enraged Soul

This skill increases Keira’s attack and skill speed for 5 seconds, up to 300%. This makes her deal more damage very quickly, especially after she uses Blade Whirlwind. It’s great for fast-paced attacks during a fight.

Ascension skill: Undying Enraged Soul – Keira revives once per battle with some health and becomes Undead, continuing to fight until the battle ends. This gives her a second chance if she is defeated.

Evolution Priority: Medium – The speed boost improves her overall damage. After ascension, it also adds survivability with resurrection.

Dancing Blades

This passive skill lets Keira’s normal attacks bounce between enemies. Each bounce deals 25% of the original damage. This helps her hit more enemies at once, but the damage is smaller compared to her main skills.

Evolution Priority: Low – It adds some extra damage, but it’s not as impactful as her active skills, especially early in the game.

Piercing Pain

This passive skill gives Keira extra damage when the enemy's armor is lower than her armor penetration. It helps her hit harder against weaker targets, but it depends on the enemy’s defense and doesn't work well against all heroes.

Evolution Priority: Lowest – The extra damage only works in certain cases and doesn’t affect all enemies. It’s not reliable early on.

Best Patronage for Keira – Hero Wars: Dominion Era

Discover the best Patronage pets for Keira in Hero Wars: Dominion Era to boost her damage and support her skills effectively in battle.

1st Place: Fenris

Fenris is the best pet for Keira. It increases her physical attack and armor penetration, which boosts her main skills. Its patronage skill gives Keira a chance to blind enemies with her basic attacks for 2 seconds, making them miss their attacks. This adds crowd control and helps her stay safe while dealing damage.

2nd Place: Vex

Vex is also a strong choice. It increases physical attack and armor penetration too, like Fenris. Its patronage skill adds Deep Wounds to enemies, which increases physical damage taken by the target. This helps the whole team deal more damage to enemies Keira is attacking. It’s especially good in teams with more physical attackers.

3rd Place: Albus

Albus boosts both magic and physical attack, and increases the pure damage dealt by Keira. This can help her cut through enemies with high armor. However, Keira relies more on raw physical damage and effects like silence and speed, so Albus is not as reliable as Fenris or Vex for her.

Best Skin for Keira – Hero Wars: Dominion Era

Find out the best skin upgrade order for Keira in Hero Wars: Dominion Era to enhance her stats and improve performance in every battle.

Winter Skin

This skin adds +10,665 Armor Penetration, which helps Keira bypass enemy defenses and deal more damage with every hit. It's her best skin overall, as it greatly increases her effectiveness against tanky heroes with high armor.

Evolution Priority: High

Demonic Skin

This skin gives +7,095 Physical Attack, directly increasing the power of Keira’s skills and basic attacks. It’s great when you need raw damage boost, especially combined with armor penetration from the Winter Skin.

Evolution Priority: Medium

Blooming Skin

This skin also grants +7,095 Physical Attack. It’s similar to Demonic Skin, so it comes after it in priority if you already have that one maxed. It still improves Keira’s output but offers no utility or survivability.

Evolution Priority: Medium

Default Skin

This skin increases Agility by +1,365. Since Keira’s main stat is Agility, this also grants bonus armor and physical attack. It's a balanced option and useful as a base, but less impactful than the skins that focus on offensive stats directly.

Evolution Priority: Low

Romantic Skin

This skin adds +106,645 Health. While it improves survivability, Keira is mainly a damage dealer and benefits more from offensive stats. Use this if she dies too early in fights, but it’s the last to upgrade for most players.

Evolution Priority: Lowest

Keira Artifact Evolution Priority

Learn the optimal artifact upgrade order for Keira in Hero Wars: Dominion Era to maximize her physical damage and overall battle power.

1st - Octavia's Blades:

This weapon artifact boosts Physical Attack by +33,459 and, when activated, increases the Physical Attack of the entire team. Since Keira is a physical damage dealer, this boosts her overall power and benefits her entire team, making it the most important artifact to evolve first.

Evolution Priority: High

2nd - Alchemist’s Folio:

This artifact increases Armor Penetration by +16,731 and Physical Attack by +5,577. Armor Penetration helps Keira deal more damage to high-defense enemies. While this boosts her personal damage, it doesn’t affect the whole team like her weapon, so it's second in priority.

Evolution Priority: Medium

3rd - Ring of Agility:

This artifact gives +6,249 Agility. Since Agility is Keira’s main stat, this indirectly boosts her Physical Attack and some defense. While it’s helpful, the boost is smaller and more passive, making it the least urgent artifact to prioritize early on.

Evolution Priority: Low

Keira Glyph Evolution Priority

Discover the best glyph upgrade order for Keira in Hero Wars: Dominion Era to boost her damage, survivability, and battle efficiency.

1st - Physical Attack:

This glyph increases Keira’s overall damage, which directly improves all her skills. Since Keira is a pure physical damage dealer, upgrading this glyph has the highest impact on her effectiveness in battle.

Evolution Priority: High

2nd - Armor Penetration:

This glyph helps Keira deal more damage to heavily armored enemies, which is crucial against frontline tanks and other defensive heroes. It complements her high physical attack and boosts her total DPS.

Evolution Priority: Very High

3rd - Agility:

Agility is Keira’s main stat. Every point of Agility adds more physical attack and armor. While this boosts her damage and survivability, it has slightly less direct impact than the first two glyphs.

Evolution Priority: Medium

4th - Armor:

This glyph increases Keira’s defense against physical attacks. It’s helpful in survivability, but since Keira's strength is offense, it ranks lower in priority.

Evolution Priority: Low

5th - Health:

This glyph increases Keira’s total HP, which improves her survivability slightly. However, it doesn’t contribute to her damage, which is her primary role, so it’s the least urgent to evolve.

Evolution Priority: Lowest

Pros and Cons of Keira Based on Her Skills

  • Pros:
    • High physical damage output thanks to strong offensive skills.
    • Blade Whirlwind deals wide-range damage and applies Silence, great for disabling enemies.
    • Enraged Soul boosts attack and skill speed up to 300%, making her a rapid damage dealer.
    • Passive skills add ricochet and armor-piercing effects, boosting overall DPS.
    • Ascension upgrades grant her a second life, increasing survivability.
  • Cons:
    • Relies heavily on Armor Penetration to be fully effective against tanky heroes.
    • Low base survivability without the Undying Enraged Soul from Ascension.
    • Silence chance and ricochet effectiveness drop against higher-level enemies.
    • Positioned in the middle line, making her vulnerable to burst and control skills.

How to Counter Keira in Hero Wars

Keira struggles against heroes who can disrupt or reflect area-of-effect damage, such as Corvus and Lian. Her performance also drops when facing heroes with high Dodge stats like Dante, Aurora, Heidi, Yasmine, and Qing Mao, making it harder for her to land consistent hits.

Since Keira has low durability, she relies heavily on protection from tank allies like Astaroth. Additionally, she is vulnerable to high single-target burst damage, especially from heroes like Phobos and Yasmine, who can eliminate her before she builds momentum.

Conclusion

Keira remains one of the strongest physical damage dealers in Hero Wars: Dominion Era when properly supported by the right team and setup. Her high area damage potential, especially when paired with heroes like Corvus, Morrigan, or Faceless, can quickly wipe out enemy lines. However, her low survivability and vulnerability to dodge-based heroes make careful team building and timing essential.

To maximize Keira’s effectiveness, prioritize her main skills and artifacts that boost physical attack and armor penetration. The right patron, such as Fenris, and the correct skin and glyph enhancements can make her devastating on the battlefield. Whether in campaign, arena, or guild wars, a fully optimized Keira is a formidable force in 2025.
