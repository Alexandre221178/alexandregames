GUUS is an unconventional Hero that focuses on keeping allies alive with the help of an army of Geese!

This old man and his golden goose protect the entire team from burst damage and damage dealt over time. All the while gray geese are rushing the enemy, flustering and keeping them away!

Who Is Guus?

Guus might look like just an old man with a goose, but in Dominion Era, he's a lifesaving strategist. His quirky skills are surprisingly powerful, blending healing, protection, and disruption all in one feathery package.

  • Class: Healer
  • Position: Middle Line
  • Main Stat: Strength

When enemies strike hard, Guus answers with golden goose magic that shields your team from devastating blows. His healing scales well and keeps your frontline strong.

Meanwhile, his gray geese swarm enemies, breaking their rhythm and causing mayhem. He's not just a support he's a feathered force of control and survival.

Guus Pros and Cons - Hero Wars: Web and Facebook

✅ Pros

  • Unique hybrid healer with both healing and enemy disruption capabilities.
  • Golden Feathers provide strong healing-over-time and life-saving effects.
  • Ultimate skill offers team-wide sustain and area control with geese summoning.
  • Passive protection prevents allies from dropping below 25% HP, ideal for burst-heavy fights.
  • Useful in long battles thanks to consistent healing and utility.
  • Easy to integrate into defensive and support-heavy teams.

❌ Cons

  • Lower direct healing output compared to pure healers like Maya or Thea.
  • Relies heavily on uptime of Golden Feathers for full effectiveness.
  • Slow ramp-up healing and disruption effects are gradual, not immediate.
  • Basic attack healing is weak and requires proper feather management.
  • Artifact stats like Armor Penetration don’t contribute to his role.
  • Can struggle in very fast-paced matches or burst-focused enemy comps.

Guus Skills Upgrade Priority - Hero Wars: Dominion Era

Learn which of Guus’s skills you should level up first to protect and heal your team most effectively in Dominion Era!

March of the Geese

This is Guus's ultimate skill. It constantly summons geese: golden ones heal allies, and gray ones attack enemies and push them away. It’s both healing and crowd control in one ability.

Evolution Priority: Very High – This skill should be maxed first. It’s the foundation of Guus’s playstyle, offering consistent healing and damage while disrupting enemies.

Feathers of Gold

This ability gives Golden Feathers to all allies, which gradually heal them over time. It also boosts the effect of Guus’s main skill by giving feathers during March of the Geese.

Evolution Priority: High – Great support skill that strengthens the main skill and provides healing over time. Level this after March of the Geese.

Guardian Goose

This passive ability prevents allies with a Golden Feather from dying by restoring a portion of their health when they drop below 25%. The more time left on the feather, the stronger the healing.

Evolution Priority: Medium-High – Excellent emergency heal and protection. It’s passive, so it’s always useful, especially when team survivability matters.

Lay on Feathers

This skill changes Guus’s basic attack into healing. He targets front-line allies who don't have feathers yet, healing them and giving a new Golden Feather.

Evolution Priority: Medium – Useful to keep feather uptime going, but its healing is slower and less impactful compared to the other skills. Prioritize last.

Guus Artifact Evolution Priority Hero Wars: Dominion Era

Learn the best order to upgrade Guus’s artifacts to maximize his healing and survivability power in Hero Wars: Dominion Era!

Weapon: Archgoose Feather

This artifact boosts Guus’s Armor and activates in battle with 100% chance. The high Armor bonus helps protect the entire team by increasing Guus’s survivability, which is crucial for a healer.

Evolution Priority: High – It ensures Guus stays alive longer in battle, letting him keep healing. However, it doesn’t boost his healing output directly, so it’s not the very top priority.

Book: Alchemist's Folio

This artifact provides Armor Penetration and Physical Attack, but Guus is a healer whose skills do not rely on damage. These stats do not benefit his healing or survivability directly.

Evolution Priority: Low – These stats are not very useful for Guus’s role. It can be upgraded last unless you want small extra damage from gray geese.

Ring: Ring of Strength

This artifact increases Strength, which is Guus’s main stat. More Strength means more health and physical attack, which indirectly boosts his healing via skills like “Lay on Feathers” and survivability.

Evolution Priority: Very High – Boosts both healing output and durability. This is the most important artifact to level up early for Guus’s performance as a healer.

Conclusion

Guus is a charming yet powerful addition to your Hero Wars: Dominion Era roster. While he may look like a quirky old man with a flock of geese, his ability to sustain your team, prevent lethal damage, and disrupt enemies makes him a valuable support hero. If you're looking for a healer who brings more than just raw healing someone who can protect allies over time and break enemy rhythmthen Guus is the perfect fit.

With the right skill and artifact investments, Guus becomes a resilient, feathered wall between your team and defeat. Don’t underestimate the power of the goose!
