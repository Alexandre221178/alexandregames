Pros and Cons of Orion Skills

Pros:

  • High Magic Damage Output: Orionâ€™s Total Destruction skill rapidly fires multiple magic missiles, making him one of the strongest damage dealers in the game.
  • Fast Energy Gain: His passive skill Full Charge allows Orion to activate his ultimate much more frequently than most heroes.
  • Area Control: Magnetic Field adds crowd control by slowing backline enemies, disrupting healers and damage dealers.
  • Synergy with Armor Penetration: Works exceptionally well with heroes or artifacts that reduce magic defense, such as Augustus, making his magic attacks even more lethal.

Cons:

  • Low Survivability: Orion has weak defenses and can be quickly eliminated if not protected by tanks or support heroes.
  • Relies on Energy: Without constant energy generation (like through Full Charge or supports like Augustus or Nebula), his damage output drops significantly.
  • Limited Crowd Control: Aside from one single-target stun, he lacks consistent control abilities, making him vulnerable to enemy casters and crowd-control heroes.
  • Needs Good Positioning: If enemy teams target Orion early or bypass your tank line, he may not survive long enough to make an impact.
