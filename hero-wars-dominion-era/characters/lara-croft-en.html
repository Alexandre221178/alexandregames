Who Is Lara Croft?

The legendary Tomb Raider brings her exceptional combat skills and archaeological cunning to Dominion. As a deadly back-line marksman, she eliminates foes with precision before they can reach your team.

  • Class: Marksman
  • Position: Back Line
  • Main Stat: Agility

With a staggering 191,025 Power and 106,264 Physical Attack, Lara's critical hits devastate opponents. Her high Armor Penetration ensures even the toughest defenders fall before her relentless assault.

Beyond raw damage, her impressive Agility stat makes her exceptionally elusive, allowing her to maintain sustained damage output throughout extended battles.

Best Patronage for Lara Croft

Discover the perfect pet companion for Lara Croft that maximizes her damage potential and enhances her crowd control abilities in battle.

1st Place:

Fenris

Fenris is the ultimate pet for Lara Croft, providing Physical Attack and Armor Penetration - both essential stats for maximizing her damage output. The chance to blind enemies with basic attacks synergizes perfectly with Lara's role as a back-line marksman, reducing incoming damage by making enemies miss their attacks.

2nd Place:

Vex

Vex offers similar offensive stats to Fenris (Physical Attack and Armor Penetration) but focuses on amplifying physical damage through Deep Wounds stacks. This works well with Lara's multi-shot basic attacks, allowing her to quickly apply multiple stacks that increase damage from all allies' physical attacks.

3rd Place:

Mara

Mara provides the least synergy for Lara Croft, offering Magic Attack and Magic Defense stats that don't benefit her physical damage kit. While the increased duration for control effects could help with her Steady Shot stun, the primary stats are largely wasted on her, making this a suboptimal choice compared to offensive pets.

How to Counter Lara Croft Hero Wars Dominion Era

Counters to Lara Croft in Hero Wars: Dominion Era work by neutralizing her critical damage focus and agility scaling. These heroes exploit her reliance on crits and physical burst to shut down her effectiveness in battle.

Amira

Amira punishes Lara Croft‚Äôs dependence on critical hits. With her Desperate Fury skill, she grants Lara a ‚Äúwish‚Äù that boosts crit chance but causes every critical hit to miss. This completely nullifies Lara‚Äôs main damage output, turning her burst potential into wasted attacks.

Helios

Helios counters Lara by punishing her crit-based attacks. His Flaming Retribution activates whenever allies receive a critical hit, firing powerful beams back at Lara. Since Lara relies heavily on crits for maximum damage, she ends up destroying herself against Helios‚Äôs retaliation.

Lara Croft Best War Flag Hero Wars

The best War Flags for Lara Croft in Hero Wars: Dominion Era are those that amplify her physical burst, disrupt enemies, or boost overall team synergy. These flags maximize her skill efficiency and survivability in key battles.

War Flag of Frost:

This flag reduces enemy skill levels every 18 seconds, weakening their effectiveness and giving Lara‚Äôs team a stronger advantage in prolonged fights. Lowered enemy skill power means reduced damage and control effects, letting Lara survive longer and output more damage.

Lara Croft and Team Benefit: Strong utility in PvP and Guild Wars, as it makes enemies less threatening and helps Lara‚Äôs crit-based damage shine against weakened opponents.

War Flag of Swift Warriors:

Speeds up cooldown recovery for all Warriors, allowing them to cycle skills faster. If Lara is used alongside Warrior allies, this flag increases team synergy and lets frontline allies enable Lara‚Äôs damage more consistently.

Lara Croft and Team Benefit: Best when paired with Warrior-heavy comps, ensuring her team rotates skills faster and applies pressure before opponents can counter.

War Flag of Decline:

This flag reduces enemy team healing by 10%, countering defensive teams with strong healers. Since Lara relies on burst and crits to eliminate enemies quickly, cutting down healing prevents enemies from recovering and prolonging battles.

Lara Croft and Team Benefit: Great against healer-heavy defenses, making Lara‚Äôs crit-based burst far more lethal.

Conclusion

Lara Croft is a powerful agility-based damage dealer in Hero Wars: Dominion Era, excelling in critical hits and team-wide physical attack boosts. When built with the right artifact and glyph priorities, she becomes a consistent source of pressure capable of carrying both attack and defense lineups.

However, her reliance on critical hits makes her vulnerable to specific counters like Helios and Amira, meaning team composition and war flags are crucial to unlock her full potential. By investing in her strengths and covering her weaknesses with proper allies and support, Lara Croft can be a reliable hero for both Web and Facebook versions of the game.
