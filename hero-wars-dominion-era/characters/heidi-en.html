Heidi might look harmless at first glance, but don’t be fooled behind that charming smile lies one of the deadliest mages in Dominion Era. Known for her swift strikes and nature’s fury, Heidi can turn the tide of battle with pure damage and unpredictable dodges.

Whether you’re building a strong magic team or looking for a counter against physical attackers, this guide will show you why Heidi remains a top-tier hero. Let’s uncover her skills, best allies, and secrets to making her unstoppable in Hero Wars: Dominion Era.

Who Is Heidi?

Heidi is a cunning mage who fights from the middle line, using her intelligence and agility to outmaneuver enemies and strike with devastating poison magic. While her high Dodge makes her hard to hit, what truly sets her apart is her pure damage it completely ignores Dodge and Armor effects.

This makes Heidi exceptionally effective against popular evasive heroes like Dante and Aurora, whose main defense relies on dodging attacks. Against them, Heidi’s toxins always land their mark, turning dodge-based teams into easy prey.

  • Class: Mage
  • Position: Middle Line
  • Main Stat: Intelligence

Heidi’s first artifact boosts Dodge for the entire team, making her a perfect partner for heroes like Aurora who thrive on evasion-based defense. With allies such as Nebula and Celeste, Heidi can unleash amplified magic attacks capable of melting even the toughest opponents.

Her synergy with Dorian enhances her damage output even further, allowing her poison strikes to drain enemy health while staying safely behind the front lines. Agile, deadly, and mesmerizing — Heidi is the silent storm your enemies never see coming.

Heidi Pros and Cons - Hero Wars: Web & Facebook

✅ Pros

  • High pure damage output from skills like Deathflower and Toxic Spit, effective against teams with high dodge or physical defense.
  • Strong synergy with heroes like Dorian, Aurora, Nebula, and Celeste, boosting both poison and pure damage potential.
  • Excellent survivability through dodge and crowd control avoidance, especially when combined with the Spy Camo skill.
  • First artifact increases dodge for the whole team, providing team-wide defensive benefits.
  • Can target multiple enemies with high pure damage, making her ideal for clearing waves or controlling backline threats.

❌ Cons

  • Highly reliant on skill upgrades and proper glyph/artifact prioritization to maximize damage and survivability.
  • Vulnerable to energy-drain or charm effects that prevent skill activation (e.g., Jorgen or Lian counters).
  • Struggles against magic-heavy teams that bypass dodge, such as enemies with pure or magic damage focus.
  • Ultimate skill timing is crucial; without energy generation support, her damage output may be delayed.
  • Lower physical defense makes her susceptible to strong physical burst when not properly protected.

Heidi Skills Upgrade Priority - Hero Wars: Dominion Era

Heidi’s skills focus on dealing pure damage that ignores defenses and Dodge, making her extremely effective against evasive heroes like Dante and Aurora. Understanding how each ability scales helps you decide which ones to prioritize first for the best performance in battle.

1 - Deathflower

Heidi summons a large poisonous mushroom in the center of the enemy team. The spores continuously poison all nearby enemies, dealing pure damage over time. This ability hits multiple enemies at once, which makes it her strongest and most reliable source of damage in long fights.

Formula: (55% Magic Attack + Ivl * 70) Pure Damage every 3s.

Evolution Priority: Very High – This is Heidi’s main damage-dealing skill. It affects multiple enemies and scales well with her Magic Attack, making it crucial to level first for maximum effectiveness.

1 - Suffocating Deathflower - Ascension Skill

The Ascension upgrade makes Deathflower even more dangerous by increasing the poison damage every 0.5 seconds. This causes the damage to stack rapidly, burning through even tanky enemies.

Formula: +10% more Pure Damage every 0.5s.

Evolution Priority: High – Unlocking this makes Heidi’s main skill scale much faster, but since Ascension takes time to unlock, it’s a secondary upgrade after her core skills are leveled.

2 - Toxic Spit

Heidi fires a poisoned dart at the nearest enemy, blinding them and dealing pure damage over time. The blind effect is short but can interrupt dangerous attackers like K’arkh or Dante, giving your team a survival edge.

Formula: 51369 Pure Damage + (60% Magic Attack + Ivl * 75) Pure Damage over 5s.

Evolution Priority: High – The consistent poison and blind effect make it an excellent follow-up skill. It’s single-target but provides both damage and control, useful in every fight.

3 - Snake Charmer

Heidi throws a snake at the enemy with the lowest health, instantly biting them for massive pure damage. This skill is perfect for finishing off low-HP enemies who survive her poison effects.

Formula: (70% Magic Attack + Ivl * 100 + 2000) Pure Damage.

Evolution Priority: Medium High – Great for securing kills and increasing overall burst damage. It’s less consistent than her AoE poison but still powerful when maxed later in progression.

4 - Spy Camo

When Heidi avoids damage for 2 seconds, she becomes camouflaged, increasing her chance to Dodge any type of attack. Each successful Dodge restores Energy, helping her use skills faster. However, since her main defense already comes from Dodge and she’s often safe in the middle line, this skill isn’t as crucial early on.

Formula: Dodge increases from 20% to (Ivl * 0.5 + 40%).

Evolution Priority: Medium – Provides good survivability but doesn’t directly increase her damage. Upgrade it after her main offensive skills.

4 - Deadly Spy Camo - Ascension Skill

After Ascension, Spy Camo gains an additional offensive effect. While camouflaged, Heidi deals extra skill damage that increases the longer she stays hidden, turning her defensive skill into a hybrid offensive tool.

Formula: Bonus skill damage from 5% up to 20%.

Evolution Priority: Low – This is useful later in the game, but since Ascension unlocks late and relies on staying hidden, it’s not a priority for beginners.

Heidi Glyph Evolution Priority

Heidi’s glyphs improve her poison strength, survivability, and dodge efficiency. Focusing on the right ones helps her strike harder while staying untouchable against physical and evasive teams like Dante and Aurora.

1st Glyph - Magic Attack:

This glyph directly boosts the power of all Heidi’s poison skills. Every skill scales with Magic Attack, which increases both her magic and pure damage effects.

Stats gain: +6,500 Magic Attack

Evolution Priority: Very High – Magic Attack increases all poison and pure damage formulas, making this the most valuable glyph to level first.

2nd Glyph - Armor:

Armor helps Heidi resist physical hits. Since she already has good Dodge, this glyph works as an extra layer of defense, protecting her from stray hits that bypass evasion.

Stats gain: +6,500 Armor

Evolution Priority: Medium High – Useful to stay alive longer against strong physical teams when Dodge fails to trigger.

3rd Glyph - Magic Defense:

Magic Defense reduces incoming damage from enemy mages. While not her main threat type, this stat can help Heidi survive longer against control-heavy mage teams.

Stats gain: +6,500 Magic Defense

Evolution Priority: Medium – Helpful but situational, as Heidi usually excels against physical and dodge-based teams rather than pure mage setups.

4th Glyph - Dodge:

Dodge is one of Heidi’s signature strengths. It lets her avoid physical attacks entirely and build energy through Spy Camo whenever she successfully dodges.

Stats gain: +1,995 Dodge

Evolution Priority: High – Enhances Heidi’s ability to evade damage and charge skills faster. Strong synergy with her Spy Camo skill and Aurora-led teams.

5th Glyph - Intelligence:

As Heidi’s main stat, Intelligence increases Magic Attack and Magic Defense strengthening both her damage and survivability.

Stats gain: +1,135 Intelligence

  • - Magic Attack from Intelligence: +3,405
  • - Magic Defense from Intelligence: +1,135
  • - Physical Attack from Intelligence: +1,135

Evolution Priority: High – Boosts offense and defense at once; valuable for long-term growth and better performance against both magic and physical damage.

Best Patronage for Heidi

Heidi benefits from pets that boost her pure damage, ultimate uptime, and survivability. Prioritizing pets correctly maximizes her effectiveness in both PvE and PvP battles.

1st Place (PvE):

Albus

Albus Maximizes Heidi’s pure damage output, enhancing all poison skills like Deathflower and Toxic Spit. Patronage also boosts Magic Attack and Physical Attack, making him ideal for raids, adventures, and any PvE scenario.

2nd Place (PvP / Energy Focus):

Cain

Cain Provides bonus energy whenever Heidi successfully dodges an attack. With her naturally high Dodge, this significantly speeds up her ultimate skill activation, making her a constant threat in PvP and against dodge-heavy teams.

3rd Place:

Axel

Axel Protects Heidi from powerful single-target attacks, preventing damage from exceeding her max health. Also increases Magic Attack and Armor, giving a mix of offense and survivability, useful in burst-heavy fights.

4th Place:

Biscuit

Biscuit Reduces all healing received by enemies for 5 seconds when Heidi attacks, while boosting her Magic Attack and Armor. Useful against high-heal teams but less impactful than Albus or Cain for raw damage or ultimate uptime.

How to Counter Heidi - Hero Wars: Dominion Era

These heroes are effective counters to Heidi, using their unique skills to limit her damage, control her abilities, and reduce her survivability in battle.

Cornelius

Why Cornelius counters Heidi: Heavy Wisdom hits enemies with the highest Intelligence, dealing damage proportional to Heidi’s stat. Rune of Suppression lowers her Magic Attack for 12 seconds, reducing her poison and pure damage output.

Corvus

Why Corvus counters Heidi: His Altar of Souls deals pure damage whenever enemies attack allied heroes. This punishes Heidi for attacking and reduces her effectiveness over time, especially in prolonged fights.

Jorgen

Why Jorgen counters Heidi: Torment of Powerlessness summons a Skull that prevents enemies from gaining energy for 9 seconds. This delays Heidi’s ultimate skills, limiting her burst potential and control in battle.

Lian

Why Lian counters Heidi: Enchantment puts all enemies to sleep for 7 seconds, disrupting Heidi’s attacks. Her Conciliation charms attackers who damage her, potentially controlling Heidi and neutralizing her poison damage.

Rufus

Why Rufus counters Heidi: With Rakashi’s Oath, Rufus can only be killed by physical damage. Magic and pure damage from Heidi cannot permanently eliminate him, forcing her to rely on physical attacks and reducing her overall efficiency.

Heidi Best War Flags - Hero Wars: Dominion Era

Heidi excels at pure and poison damage, making the right War Flags crucial to boost her skills, energy generation, and team effectiveness in battles.

War Flag of Decline:

Heidi and Team Benefit: Reduces enemy healing by 10%, ensuring her poison and pure damage over time are more effective. This counters regenerative teams and maximizes her offensive potential.

War Flag of Frost:

Heidi and Team Benefit: Every 18 seconds, casts Frost on enemies, reducing their skill levels by 2 for 8 seconds. This slows enemy skill usage, helping Heidi survive longer and apply continuous damage.

War Flag of Recovery:

Heidi and Team Benefit: Increases all healing by 10%. While not directly boosting Heidi’s damage, it improves team sustainability, allowing her to maintain DPS longer in extended battles.

Conclusion

Heidi is a versatile and evasive hero whose strength lies in her ability to avoid attacks and generate energy quickly, enabling her ultimate skill more often. By prioritizing the right glyphs, selecting the best pets, and using effective War Flags, players can maximize her damage and survivability. Whether in PvE or PvP, building a team that complements her evasive playstyle ensures consistent performance and makes her a formidable asset in Dominion Era battles.

Remember to adjust team composition depending on the enemies faced, and always focus on enhancing Heidi’s evasion and energy gain potential. With proper setup, she can dominate battles and provide both offensive and strategic advantages to your team.
