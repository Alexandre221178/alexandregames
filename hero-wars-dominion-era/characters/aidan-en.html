Aidan isn't just a healer he's a survivor of tragedy, bound by flame and fate to his sister Kayla. In Hero Wars: Dominion Era, Aidan brings both powerful healing and searing magic to the battlefield. But what makes him truly unique is the life he shares with his sister, forming one of the game’s most emotionally complex duos. Whether you’re here to master his mechanics or understand his story, you’re in for something special.

In this guide, you’ll discover Aidan’s strengths, his optimal roles in battle, and how to unleash his full potential as both a Healer and a Fire Herald Mage. Let’s explore why Aidan’s flame burns brightest at the heart of your team.

Who Is Aidan?

Aidan is a back line support hero whose main role is healing, empowered by his bond with his sister Kayla. As a member of the Fire Heralds, his unique connection allows him to channel devastating fire magic while sustaining allies. His origin is marked by tragedy, but his resolve to protect others is unwavering.

  • Function: Healer/Mage
  • Class: Fire Heralds
  • Position: Back Line
  • Main Stat: Intelligence

From a scorched land long lost, Aidan learned young the harsh truth of fire. This trauma forged not only his strength, but an inseparable link with Kayla. They don’t just fight together they live as one.

Healers in Hero Wars often fall into the background, but Aidan demands attention. His synergy with Kayla, his potent heals, and his ability to penetrate magic defense make him a core part of many advanced PvE and PvP team compositions.

Aidan Pros and Cons - Hero Wars: Web and Facebook

✅ Pros

  • Versatile support: Combines healing, shielding, and area damage to protect and sustain the team.
  • Strong synergy with Kayla: Shared health mechanic can create powerful and durable back-line combos.
  • Back-line placement: Safely positioned away from the front, making him harder to reach in balanced matchups.
  • Burn and AoE damage: Can weaken multiple enemies at once with Glyphs and Ignite effects.
  • Scales well with Magic Attack: Easy to enhance via Book, Glyphs, Skins, and Pets.

❌ Cons

  • Vulnerable to back-line assassins: Easily countered by heroes like Cleaver, Jhu, and Kayla.
  • Dependent on energy: Needs to cast his ultimate often to be effective energy control or denial hurts him badly.
  • No crowd control: Offers no stuns, silences, or slows purely reactive support.
  • Shared health risk: If Kayla or a bonded ally dies, Aidan can fall quickly due to the damage feedback.
  • Low base durability: Without scaling defense, he relies entirely on shields and positioning to survive.

Aidan’s Skills Upgrade Priority - Hero Wars: Dominion Era

Understand which of Aidan’s powerful skills to upgrade first to boost healing, survivability, and magical impact in Hero Wars: Dominion Era.

Phoenix’s Embrace

This is Aidan’s ultimate ability. It creates a strong shield on both himself and the farthest ally, and when the ally's shield ends, it explodes, damaging enemies and applying a Phoenix Glyph (which enables his other skills).

Evolution Priority: Very High – This skill boosts both survivability and offense. The shield prevents damage and sets up combos with his Glyph mechanic. Upgrade this first for max impact.

Ignite

Applies the Phoenix Glyph to an enemy, or if all enemies already have it, sends out a damaging spark to nearby enemies. Glyphs burn enemies over time, dealing pure damage that ignores defenses.

Evolution Priority: High – This skill greatly boosts Aidan’s damage potential, especially in longer fights. It's essential for applying burn effects and enhancing overall DPS.

Inner Fire

Heals all allies with a wave of fire and grants self-healing every 2 seconds. The more Aidan heals himself, the more effective his fire wave becomes. When he’s low on health, he ignites and doubles self-healing.

Evolution Priority: Medium-High – This skill improves overall team sustain. It's especially powerful in drawn-out battles, but doesn’t contribute as much early game or if Aidan is controlled or burst down.

Bonds of Flame

Links Aidan to the farthest ally, or Kayla if present. They share damage and healing, making one a proxy for the other. Both gain bonus Armor and Magic Defense. The bond is active from the start.

Evolution Priority: Medium – While strong with Kayla, this skill doesn’t scale as much with level increases. The bond mechanic is useful but doesn’t require fast maxing compared to others.

Best Patronage for Aidan

Discover which pet boosts Aidan’s healing and magic output best in Hero Wars: Dominion Era. Patronage synergy is key to maximize his role.

1st Place:

Merlin

Merlin enhances Aidan’s core strengths: magic penetration and magic attack. The skill speed boost improves his Glyph application and healing wave timing, which is critical for maximizing damage and support output. This is his most consistent and impactful patron.

2nd Place:

Albus

Albus boosts pure damage dealt which works well with Aidan’s Phoenix Glyph burns. This makes Aidan deadlier in boss fights or prolonged battles, especially if you focus on his offensive role. Not as helpful for healing, but excellent for damage-focused builds.

3rd Place:

Khorus

Khorus grants shields based on magic damage and missing health. While interesting in theory, the shield effect is harder to control and less reliable than Merlin’s burst or Albus’ burn bonus. Better in niche defensive setups, especially if using Aidan for sustained shielding.

Aidan Artifact Evolution Priority Hero Wars: Dominion Era

Learn which artifacts to level first for Aidan to maximize his healing, magic damage, and team support effectiveness in Hero Wars: Dominion Era.

Weapon Artifact:

Phoenix Sigil – Activates when Aidan uses his ultimate (Phoenix’s Embrace), granting bonus Armor to the entire team for 9 seconds. This helps improve team survivability, especially against physical teams.

Evolution Priority: Medium – While it provides valuable protection, especially in PvP, it doesn’t boost Aidan’s healing or damage. Upgrade it after boosting core stats.

Book Artifact:

Tome of Arcane Knowledge – Grants +16,731 Magic Attack and +83,649 Health. Magic Attack directly improves all of Aidan’s core skills, including healing, burn effects, shields, and explosion damage. The Health boost helps him stay alive longer in battle, supporting more sustained healing and team utility.

Evolution Priority: Very High – This is Aidan’s most important artifact. The Magic Attack bonus powers up every one of his abilities, while the Health boost enhances his survivability on the battlefield.

Ring Artifact:

Ring of Intelligence – Grants +6,249 Intelligence, which converts into +18,747 Magic Attack and +6,249 Magic Defense. It passively strengthens all his skills and defenses.

Evolution Priority: High – Intelligence boosts both Magic Attack and Magic Defense. This artifact enhances Aidan’s healing and resilience, making it the second best to level up.

How to Counter Aidan in Hero Wars: Dominion Era

Cleaver

Cleaver pulls Aidan to the front line with his Hook skill, instantly exposing him to burst damage and interrupting his ability to protect allies or heal safely.

Corvus

Corvus punishes Aidan’s area damage and healing over time with his Altar of Souls, reflecting damage back and disrupting Aidan’s support effectiveness.

Jhu

Jhu targets the most distant enemy, often Aidan due to his back-line placement and mid-tier survivability. Jhu quickly eliminates Aidan before he can fully cycle his healing abilities.

Jorgen

Jorgen can block Aidan’s energy gain and redirect attacks to the backline, delaying his ultimate and interrupting his support timing, making him far less effective during critical team moments.

Kayla

Kayla jumps to the back line and immediately targets the farthest enemy, often Aidan. Her rapid physical attacks and burn effects quickly bypass his shield and overwhelm his healing, making her a deadly threat that can eliminate him early in the battle.

Luther

Luther can jump to the back line at the start of combat, immediately putting pressure on Aidan and preventing him from safely setting up shields or healing allies.

Aidan Best War Flag Hero Wars

Discover which War Flags boost Aidan’s healing, magic utility, and overall team support performance in Hero Wars: Dominion Era.

War Flag of Recovery:

This flag boosts all healing by 10%, significantly amplifying Aidan's Inner Fire healing waves and regeneration. Since his primary role is to heal and protect his team, this flag enhances his overall impact in long fights.

Aidan and Team Benefit: Aidan’s healing becomes more effective across the board, helping his team survive burst damage and sustain through prolonged battles.

War Flag of Decline:

This flag reduces the enemy team's healing by 10%, which helps counter enemy healers and outlast defensive compositions. When combined with Aidan’s burn damage and sustained healing, it gives your team a strategic edge.

Aidan and Team Benefit: Weakens the opposing team's ability to recover while Aidan continues to support his own, giving your side the advantage in attrition fights.

War Flag of Pet Strength:

Increases pet Skill Power by 10%, indirectly enhancing Aidan's performance when supported by pets like Merlin or Khorus. These pets boost Aidan’s Magic Attack and shield potential through patronage and aura effects.

Aidan and Team Benefit: Improves the effectiveness of support pets that boost Aidan’s skills and survivability, adding synergy and extra team utility.

Conclusion – Aidan in Hero Wars: Dominion Era

Aidan stands out as one of the most versatile support heroes in Hero Wars: Dominion Era, blending sustained healing, protective shielding, and area burn damage. His role is vital in teams that rely on long-term survivability and magic-based synergy.

While vulnerable to back-line assassins and energy control, Aidan excels when properly positioned and supported by allies that amplify his potential. Investing wisely in his artifacts, war flags, and team compositions can turn Aidan into a defensive anchor and a consistent source of pressure.

In short, if you're looking for a hero that keeps your team alive while wearing down enemies over time, Aidan is an excellent addition to your roster in the Dominion Era.
