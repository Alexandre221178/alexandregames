Astaroth is a versatile tank and support hero in Hero Wars: Dominion Era, known for his ability to protect allies and disrupt enemies. This guide provides an in-depth look at his skills, optimal builds, and strategies to maximize his effectiveness on the battlefield. His versatility lies in his strong defensive abilities and his power to resurrect allies, making him one of the best choices for new and experienced players.

Astaroth can serve as a frontline guardian who absorbs massive amounts of damage while providing invaluable support through his passive and active skills. Understanding how to build and position Astaroth correctly can make a huge difference in both PvE and PvP scenarios.

Why Astaroth is a Must-Have Tank

  • Astaroth
    • Tank / Support
    • Fights at the front line
    • Strength

Astaroth isn't just another meat shield - he's arguably the best all-around tank in Hero Wars. What makes him special? Three words: resurrection, damage sharing, and team shielding. While other tanks just soak up damage, Astaroth actively prevents your team from dying while occasionally bringing them back to life. That's why you'll see him in nearly every top-tier team composition.

How Astaroth's Skills Work in Hero Wars Web and Facebook

Understanding Astaroth's skills is crucial because leveling them in the wrong order can seriously hurt your team's performance. Each ability serves a specific purpose in battle, and some are far more valuable than others.

Flame Veil

Casts a shield that blocks physical damage on the entire team. The shield's capacity is shared between all team members.

Evolution Priority: 1 (Highest)

Devastation

Astaroth spews a flare of hellfire, burning up some of the energy accumulated by the furthest enemy.

Evolution Priority: 4 (Lowest)

Demon's Allegiance

Passive skill. Astaroth maintains a connection with the ally who has the least health left and takes on a part of the physical damage inflicted against that ally.

Evolution Priority: 3

Last Word

Once per battle, Astaroth resurrects a fallen ally (or himself).

Evolution Priority: 2

Best Patronage for Astaroth

Choosing the right pet for Astaroth can dramatically improve his performance. Here are the top options ranked by usefulness:

1st Place: Axel

Axel provides magic attack (boosting Astaroth's shields) and armor while protecting him from burst damage.

2nd Place: Oliver

Oliver gives health and armor with healing when Astaroth drops below 50% HP.

3rd Place: Biscuit

Biscuit offers health/armor and reduces enemy healing, good against certain matchups.

Astaroth Artifact Priority

Prioritizing Astaroth's artifacts correctly is crucial - armor first (Scythe), then magic defense (Covenant), finally health (Ring). This order maximizes his survivability to trigger resurrection while blocking key damage types. Never upgrade randomly!

1st - Scythe of Redemption

Provides massive armor boost with 100% activation chance.

Evolution Priority: 1

2nd - Defender's Covenant

Boosts both armor and magic defense.

Evolution Priority: 2

3rd - Ring of Strength

Increases strength for more health.

Evolution Priority: 3

Pros and Cons of Astaroth

Pros:

  • Best resurrection ability in the game
  • Team-wide physical damage shield
  • Protects weakest ally automatically
  • Works in nearly any team composition
  • Excellent against physical damage teams

Cons:

  • Weak against magic-heavy teams
  • Resurrection can be countered
  • Low personal damage output
  • Energy burn skill isn't very impactful

How to Beat Astaroth in Hero Wars

Astaroth may be sturdy, but he isn’t unstoppable. Heroes like Amira can bait his resurrection on a fake illusion, wasting it. Others like Celeste, Jet, or Yasmine apply anti-healing effects, preventing a freshly resurrected hero from recovering health. Morrigan is also a top counter thanks to her ability to shut down resurrection entirely.

Final Thoughts on Astaroth

Astaroth remains one of the most versatile and powerful tanks in Hero Wars for good reason. His combination of shielding, damage mitigation, and resurrection makes him valuable in nearly all game modes. While he has some counters, proper team building can minimize these weaknesses. Focus on his Flame Veil and Last Word skills first, pair him with Axel or Oliver, and watch as your team's survivability soars through the roof!
