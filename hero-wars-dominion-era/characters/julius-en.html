Looking for a tank that's both adorable and deadly? Julius, the mech-suited space cat, might just be your team's ultimate front-line defender.

In this guide, we’ll break down Julius’s skills, stats, and synergies to help you master this cosmic feline and bring fear to your enemies one purr at a time.

Who Is Julius?

Julius is a front-line tank hero in Hero Wars: Dominion Era, known for his mechanical armor, protective skills, and surprising utility.

  • Class: Tank
  • Position: Front Line
  • Main Stat: Strength

Originally from a distant planet whose star is invisible from Dominion’s skies, Julius crash-landed into this world with courage, brains, and a battle mech he built himself.

His adorable looks hide a combat genius: Julius can cleanse debuffs, boost team speed, and even heal. He pairs especially well with shielders like Aidan and Fafnir, and works wonders when empowered by Nebula’s Physical Attack buff.

Julius Pros and Cons - Hero Wars: Web and Facebook

✅ Pros

  • Excellent tank with powerful shields that absorb full damage for the entire team.
  • Scales well with Physical Attack, making his shields and healing stronger over time.
  • Removes debuffs and boosts armor and magic defense after shields are broken.
  • Great synergy with heroes that apply shields (Fafnir, Jorgen, Aidan) and with Nebula for extra damage scaling.
  • Resistant to control effects when shields are up, improving team stability.

❌ Cons

  • Vulnerable before the first shield is activated can be burst down early in battle.
  • Does not deal significant damage on his own; relies heavily on team support.
  • Weak against energy denial heroes like Jorgen who delay his skill usage.
  • Requires investment in Physical Attack and survivability stats to be effective.
  • Shield mechanics are less effective against enemies with true damage or anti-shield skills.

Julius Skills Upgrade Priority - Hero Wars: Dominion Era

Understand which of Julius's skills to upgrade first so you can protect your team and maximize his shield-based support in battle.

Defense Meowtrix

This is Julius’s main skill. It creates a huge team-wide shield that absorbs damage and then heals all allies after it’s broken. The more physical attack Julius has, the stronger this shield and heal become.

Evolution Priority: Very High – It protects the whole team and heals them, so upgrading this boosts survivability for everyone.

Copycat Device

This skill shields Julius, and if the shield survives for 5 seconds, it copies the remaining amount to all allies. Every time Julius gets a shield, he gains permanent physical attack power for the rest of the fight.

Evolution Priority: High – Strong synergy skill that improves team protection and boosts Julius's damage over time.

Nine-Lives Engine

This passive ability activates whenever an ally loses a shield. It removes negative effects and temporarily increases their armor and magic defense. It helps keep the team safe even after shields break.

Evolution Priority: Medium-High – Helps cleanse debuffs and adds a small defense boost, but it's not as impactful as his main shield skills.

Purrfect Reflexes

This skill gives your whole team bonus speed for each active shield on the battlefield. Faster attacks and skills can help you win faster, but the boost is small unless many shields are active.

Evolution Priority: Medium – Useful when there are lots of shields in play, but doesn’t work well in every situation. Upgrade it last.

Best Patronage for Julius

Discover which pet gives Julius the strongest protection and synergy for his shields and team support abilities in Hero Wars: Dominion Era.

1st Place:

Axel

Axel offers exceptional protection by spreading out incoming damage. For Julius, this makes his shield-based tanking far more effective, especially since Axel prevents large burst attacks from overwhelming his defenses. Combined with armor and magic attack boosts, Axel keeps Julius alive longer and enhances the strength of his team-wide shields.

2nd Place:

Oliver

Oliver boosts Julius’s survivability by healing him when he drops below 50% HP. Since Julius often takes frontline damage while shielding allies, Oliver ensures he stays alive longer to keep supporting the team. The bonus health and armor further reinforce his role as a durable front-line tank.

3rd Place:

Biscuit

Biscuit adds utility by reducing enemy healing. While not Julius’s top choice for personal survival or shielding, it’s useful in specific matchups against heavy healing teams. The magic attack and armor bonus are still helpful, but Biscuit shines more in support/control setups than pure tank synergy.

Best Skin for Julius Hero Wars: Dominion Era

Find out which Julius skins provide the most effective stat boosts for his shields, healing, and tanking role in Hero Wars: Dominion Era.

Default Skin – Strength +1,365

This skin boosts Strength, Julius’s main stat. Strength increases both his health and physical attack, which directly powers up his shield capacity and healing amount. It improves all of Julius's skills passively.

Evolution Priority: Very High – Best all-around boost. Increases shield strength, healing, survivability, and physical attack through main stat scaling.

Romantic Skin – Physical Attack +7,095

This skin boosts Julius's physical attack directly, which enhances the strength of his shields, healing, and some skill effects. It’s ideal if you’ve already boosted his main stats and want a big bump in his shield performance.

Evolution Priority: High – Great for improving skill effects directly. Best used after the main stat skin is leveled.

Demonic Skin – Health +106,645

This skin gives Julius a massive health increase. It improves his survivability but doesn’t directly impact his shield or healing strength. It’s helpful in very long battles where staying alive is the top priority.

Evolution Priority: Medium-High – Adds tankiness, but less impactful for skill effectiveness compared to Strength or Physical Attack boosts.

Masquerade Skin – Magic Defense +10,650

This skin increases Julius’s magic defense. While it can be useful situationally against magic-heavy teams, it doesn’t boost his shields or skills. It’s the least impactful for most general team setups.

Evolution Priority: Low – Situational. Only useful against magic damage teams and doesn't affect skill performance.

Julius Artifact Evolution Priority Hero Wars: Dominion Era

Discover the best artifact upgrade order for Julius to maximize his survivability, shield strength, and team support in Hero Wars: Dominion Era.

Weapon: Cattelite Dish

This weapon activates Julius’s artifact skill at the start of battle and provides Critical Hit Chance. While Julius doesn’t rely on crits, the activation benefits the entire team assuming Julius is still alive when it triggers.

Evolution Priority: Medium-High – Valuable for team support, but less useful if Julius lacks durability. Upgrade after his survivability is secured.

Book: Warrior's Code

This artifact grants Julius Physical Attack and Critical Hit Chance. While helpful for enhancing shield and healing effects, it does not improve his health, making it the least critical for his frontline tank role.

Evolution Priority: Medium – Slightly boosts skill output, but offers no defense. Upgrade only after the Ring and Weapon.

Ring: Ring of Strength

This artifact increases Julius’s main stat, Strength, granting him both Physical Attack and a large boost to Health (+249,960 HP). This is essential for a front-line tank, helping him survive longer while powering up all his shield and healing abilities.

Evolution Priority: Very High – Boosts both survivability and skill power. If Julius dies, he can't protect the team start here.

Julius Glyph Evolution Priority

Explore the best glyph upgrade path for Julius to boost his shield power, survivability, and frontline effectiveness in Hero Wars: Dominion Era.

1st Glyph – Physical Attack

This glyph directly increases the strength of Julius's shields and healing skills, as all of his main abilities scale with Physical Attack. It has immediate and visible impact on his performance in battle.

Evolution Priority: Very High – Improves all shield and support skills. Top priority for boosting utility and protection.

2nd Glyph – Health

Health makes Julius more durable as a front-line tank. The more HP he has, the longer he survives to protect allies and keep activating shields.

Evolution Priority: High – Strong survivability boost. Helps Julius stay alive to fulfill his role in long battles.

3rd Glyph – Crit Hit Chance

Although Julius can occasionally deal critical hits, his skills do not depend on Crit Chance. This glyph is mostly useful if you're building an offensive or hybrid comp with crit-scaling buffs.

Evolution Priority: Medium – Optional boost for aggressive team setups, but not necessary for shield-focused builds.

4th Glyph – Armor

Armor reduces physical damage taken. While helpful, Julius already has high natural defenses, and this glyph adds less than raw Health or Physical Attack in most situations.

Evolution Priority: Medium-High – Defensive value is solid but less impactful than health or attack for shield scaling.

5th Glyph – Strength +1135

Strength is Julius’s main stat, which increases both Health and Physical Attack, but at a slower rate than the direct glyphs. It's a balanced but less urgent upgrade.

Evolution Priority: Low – Improves both HP and damage slightly, but should be upgraded after more impactful glyphs are maxed.

How to Counter Julius – Hero Wars: Dominion Era

Julius depends heavily on energy gain and shield uptime. These heroes can shut him down or eliminate him before he becomes effective.

Dante

Dante reduces Julius’s main stat, Strength, which lowers his physical attack, health, and the power of his shields. Combined with his early burst damage, Dante can cripple Julius before he becomes effective in battle.

Iris

Iris punishes tanks with pure damage.

Ishmael

Ishmael excels at quickly eliminating frontliners with critical strikes. His fast burst can remove Julius before he has a chance to scale his shields or receive support buffs.

Jorgen

Jorgen blocks Julius's energy gain, delaying his first skill – the most crucial part of his kit. This denies early shield activation and slows his overall utility.

K’arkh

K'arkh lifts Julius out of position and deals high burst damage before shields can trigger, exploiting his brief early vulnerability.

Yasmine

Yasmine bypasses tanks with high single-target burst and poison damage. She can eliminate Julius early, especially when paired with armor penetration buffs.

Julius Best War Flags Hero Wars

Choosing the right War Flags boosts Julius’s tanking and team support, enhancing shield strength, survivability, and energy gain for a frontline advantage.

War Flag of Fervor:

Julius and Team Benefit: Enhances Julius’s ability to sustain shields and buffs faster, which is vital for frontline tanking and keeping allies protected longer.

War Flag of Recovery:

This flag increases all healing by 10%, boosting the effectiveness of Julius’s shield healing and any allied healers, improving overall team sustain in battle.

Julius and Team Benefit: Amplifies healing effects, making Julius’s post-shield heals and team survivability stronger during prolonged fights.

War Flag of Swift Warriors:

Speeds up skill cooldown for Warriors by 5%, which helps Julius and other Warriors use their skills more often for better control and defense.

Julius and Team Benefit: Faster skill cooldown means Julius can refresh his shields and buffs more frequently, increasing frontline durability and team control.

Julius Guide Conclusion

Julius is a powerful frontline tank whose main strength lies in his durability and ability to protect the team with strong shields. His skill set makes him ideal for absorbing damage and controlling the flow of battle, especially when paired with the right pets, artifacts, and war flags that enhance his survivability and team support. Prioritizing defense and sustainability over pure damage output is key to maximizing Julius’s effectiveness.

While he may lack burst damage, his resilience and utility in prolonging fights provide valuable opportunities for damage dealers to shine. Building a team that complements Julius’s strengths will ensure you dominate both attack and defense battles in Hero Wars: Dominion Era.
