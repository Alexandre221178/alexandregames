Meet Ziri, the desert guardian who channels the power of Akhreb Ummi to protect her allies! This powerful tank hero is more than just a wall she’s a fierce frontliner with unmatched endurance and the mystical ability to reflect damage and dodge death itself.

Whether you're new to Hero Wars: Dominion Era or seeking to upgrade your tank lineup, this guide will reveal why Ziri is one of the most resilient and strategic heroes you can add to your team. Ready to dive into the sands of power?

Who Is Ziri?

Ziri is a tank hero who fights on the front line with unshakable faith and brutal resilience. She draws her strength from the divine scorpion goddess Akhreb Ummi, protecting her allies by taunting enemies and deflecting their fiercest blows.

  • Class: Tank
  • Position: Front Line
  • Main Stat: Strength

“Akhreb Ummi, oh great Mother of Scorpios, help your devoted child...” This invocation is more than a phrase it’s the spirit of Ziri’s defense. She digs into the ground when under heavy attack, healing while staying hidden from harm.

With the ability to fully reflect physical damage and draw enemy skills to herself, Ziri stands as a perfect counter to heroes like K’arch and Amira. Her unique skill set and massive survivability make her ideal for stalling and protecting fragile backline allies.

Ziri Pros and Cons - Hero Wars: Web and Facebook

✅ Pros

  • Can fully reflect physical damage back to attackers, punishing melee heroes.
  • Taunts enemies, protecting allies from targeted abilities like K’arkh’s and Amira’s first skills.
  • High survivability thanks to her passive: she burrows underground when taking heavy damage and heals over time.

❌ Cons

  • Less effective against teams that focus on magic damage, which bypasses her reflection.
  • Vulnerable to control effects like stun, freeze, or blind, which can interrupt her taunt or healing cycle.
  • When underground, the frontline is left exposed requiring another tank or protector to maintain defense.

Ziri Skills Upgrade Priority - Hero Wars: Dominion Era

Learn which Ziri skills to level up first in Hero Wars: Dominion Era and why each one impacts her tanking power in your team.

Focus of Hatred

This skill makes Ziri shout and taunt all enemies, forcing them to attack her instead of her allies for 8 seconds. While taunting, she gets a big bonus to both Armor and Magic Defense, reducing the damage she takes.

Evolution Priority: Very High – This is Ziri’s core tanking tool. The stronger the skill, the better she absorbs damage and protects the team. It should be your top priority.

Reflection of Pain

This skill reflects all physical damage Ziri takes back at the attacker for 8 seconds. It's like a magical mirror great against physical heroes like Ishmael or Dante.

Evolution Priority: High – Very effective in physical matchups. Upgrading this skill boosts Ziri’s threat level and punishes damage dealers who try to burst her down.

Shelter of Sands

When Ziri’s health drops below 30%, she burrows underground, avoiding all attacks and healing herself for 7 seconds. It happens automatically once every 15 seconds.

Evolution Priority: Medium-High – This is great for survival, especially in longer fights. Boost it to make Ziri last longer when focused by enemies.

Fury of Akhreb Ummi

When Ziri comes out of hiding, she stuns and knocks back nearby enemies and instantly activates her reflection skill. Great for disrupting front lines.

Evolution Priority: Medium – While useful, this passive relies on her going underground first. Upgrade it later once your other skills are stronger.

Best Patronage for Ziri

Discover the most effective pets to support Ziri in Hero Wars: Dominion Era based on battle utility and synergy with her tanking skills.

1st Place:

Oliver

Oliver is the best match for Ziri. His patronage offers bonus health and armor, which are core stats for tanks. More importantly, when Ziri's health drops below 50%, Oliver heals her continuously, helping her stay alive even when her own self-burrow skill is on cooldown. This creates a powerful synergy that extends her frontline presence.

2nd Place:

Biscuit

Biscuit supports Ziri defensively by reducing healing on enemies she hits. This helps her team secure kills faster by weakening enemies' sustainability. He also boosts armor, complementing her high durability, though he offers no direct healing or survivability to Ziri herself.

3rd Place:

Fenris

Fenris increases Ziri’s physical attack and grants her a chance to blind enemies with basic attacks. While Ziri isn’t a damage-dealer, blinding enemies can reduce incoming physical damage. However, this pet is more suited for offensive tanks or bruisers, making him the weakest synergy for Ziri in most cases.

Ziri Artifact Evolution Priority Hero Wars: Dominion Era

Discover the optimal artifact upgrade order for Ziri in Hero Wars: Dominion Era. Boost her tank potential and survivability effectively in battles.

Weapon: Akhreb Ummi’s Sting

This artifact activates every time Ziri uses her ultimate skill, providing a strong armor bonus (+50,190) to all allies. It boosts team-wide physical defense, especially useful when Ziri taunts enemies and draws their attacks.

Evolution Priority: Very High – This is Ziri’s most important artifact because it enhances her role as a tank and directly benefits the entire team’s survivability. Upgrade this first to make her ultimate skill truly impactful.

Book: Defender’s Covenant

This artifact passively increases both armor (+12,546) and magic defense (+12,546), helping Ziri resist all types of damage even when her active skills are on cooldown.

Evolution Priority: High – Important for consistent damage reduction and survivability. It supports her tanking passively, especially when she's not using skills like Shelter of Sands.

Ring: Ring of Strength

This artifact increases Ziri’s Strength stat (+6,249), which gives her more health and slightly improves her physical attack power.

Evolution Priority: Medium-High – While Strength does help increase Ziri's health pool, its benefit is less impactful than the armor/magic defense provided by her other artifacts. Upgrade this last unless you need a quick HP boost.

Ziri Glyph Evolution Priority

Discover the best glyph upgrade order for Ziri in Hero Wars: Dominion Era to boost her tanking potential and battle survivability.

1st Glyph – Physical Attack:

Increases Ziri's physical damage (+4,340). While she does deal some damage, her primary role is tanking. This stat doesn't significantly boost her utility or survivability.

Evolution Priority: Low – Physical Attack has the least impact on Ziri's effectiveness as a tank. It can be leveled much later or even ignored if you're focusing on survivability.

2nd Glyph – Health:

Boosts Ziri's maximum health by +62,200. This directly improves her survivability, especially when she uses Shelter of Sands to heal a percentage of her total health.

Evolution Priority: Very High – Health is Ziri’s most important stat. It enhances her self-healing, tankiness, and synergy with pets like Oliver. Always prioritize this glyph first.

3rd Glyph – Magic Defense:

Increases Ziri's resistance to magic damage by +6,500. Combined with her taunt skill, this helps her tank better against mages like Orion, Amira, and Celeste.

Evolution Priority: High – This glyph is crucial against magic-heavy teams. It works well with Focus of Hatred and her artifact that boosts magic defense passively.

4th Glyph – Armor:

Raises her armor by +6,500. Great against physical teams, especially since Ziri often taunts enemies and reflects physical damage using her second skill.

Evolution Priority: High – Very useful when facing strong physical damage dealers. Level this along with magic defense depending on your enemies.

5th Glyph – Strength:

Increases Strength by +1,135, which boosts Ziri’s health and physical attack. Since she scales directly from Strength, it adds durability and slight offensive benefit.

Evolution Priority: Medium-High – Not as vital as flat health or resistances, but still important for overall tank growth. Prioritize after Health, Armor, and Magic Defense.

How to Counter Ziri – Hero Wars: Dominion Era

Amira

Amira counters Ziri by disrupting Strength-based heroes. Her debuffs greatly reduce Ziri’s effectiveness, and her magic damage bypasses Ziri’s physical damage reflection.

Dante

Dante deals high physical damage and applies debuffs that reduce Ziri’s main stat, Strength directly weakening her health and damage output. His ranged attacks avoid her taunt, and his knock-ups exploit the moment she burrows underground, leaving the frontline exposed.

Mushy

Mushy and Shroom overwhelm Ziri with consistent magic damage and control effects. Their summons distract her taunt, and they can pressure her while she’s underground.

Polaris

Polaris can freeze Ziri, interrupting her taunt and slowing her skill cycle. Her magic burst punishes Ziri’s low magic resistance windows between skill activations.

Yasmine

Yasmine bypasses Ziri’s front-line tanking by jumping to the backline. This reduces the effectiveness of Ziri’s taunt and weakens her team’s protection while Ziri is underground.

Ziri Best War Flag Hero Wars

Discover the best War Flags to pair with Ziri in Hero Wars: Dominion Era, boosting her survivability and supporting her tanking abilities in team battles.

War Flag of Fervor:

This flag increases Energy Gain for Tanks by 10%, allowing Ziri to activate her skills more frequently especially her ultimate, which triggers her armor-boosting artifact and crowd control combo.

Ziri and Team Benefit: Ziri heavily relies on skill timing to taunt enemies and protect allies. With faster energy gain, she becomes a more consistent frontliner, offering better protection and reflection uptime for the whole team.

War Flag of Recovery:

This flag increases all healing by 10%, which directly benefits Ziri’s passive healing from Shelter of Sands and synergizes well with healer pets like Oliver.

Ziri and Team Benefit: Since Ziri heals herself when burrowed, the additional healing increases her survivability window. It also enhances team healing effects if you pair her with supports like Thea or Martha.

War Flag of Pet Strength:

This flag increases Pet Skill Power by 10%, boosting the effectiveness of pet-linked healing, protection, and patronage skills. Especially useful when using Oliver or Biscuit with Ziri.

Ziri and Team Benefit: Ziri benefits greatly from supportive pets. This flag strengthens those pet abilities, ensuring stronger shields, healing, or debuffs to assist Ziri’s tank role.

Conclusion – Is Ziri Worth Using?

Ziri is a resilient frontline tank who thrives under pressure, turning enemy aggression into team protection. With her unique ability to reflect physical damage, absorb deadly hits, and burrow underground to survive, she brings incredible durability and control to any lineup. While she struggles against magic-heavy or high-control teams, the right combination of supports, pets, and war flags can transform her into an unbreakable guardian.

Whether you're building a defensive stronghold or a counter-punch strategy, Ziri rewards players who understand timing, synergy, and positioning. If you invest in her properly and build around her strengths, Ziri can be a game-changing asset in both offense and defense in Hero Wars: Dominion Era.
