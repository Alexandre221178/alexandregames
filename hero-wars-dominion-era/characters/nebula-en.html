Who Is Nebula?

Nebula is a versatile support hero who excels in boosting your team‚Äôs overall damage. Her ability to balance energy between two nearby allies can significantly enhance the performance of your main damage dealers or healers.

  • Class: Support / Healer
  • Position: Middle Line

She shines best when paired with strong DPS heroes like Dante, Orion, Keira, or Sebastian, increasing their effectiveness in critical fights. Her buffs make her an indispensable ally in high-level content and Guild War strategies.

In addition to empowering allies, Nebula can remove debuffs and drain energy from enemies, offering utility and control. These features make her a flexible pick for various team compositions across PvE and PvP modes.

Skills Priority and Explanation for Nebula

Understand how each of Nebula's skills works and which to upgrade first to boost your team in Hero Wars: Dominion Era.

Nebula is a support hero who strengthens allies with her basic attacks and helps the team survive and deal more damage.

Astral Projection

Nebula fires a slow energy ball that explodes either when it hits the last enemy or is activated manually. It splits damage and drains energy from enemies in the area. However, because Nebula turns her basic attacks into buffs through Equilibrium, it takes a long time for her to activate this skill (she needs full energy).

Evolution Priority: Low - It‚Äôs her ultimate, but she rarely activates it due to her support playstyle and low energy gain.

Ascension skill: Astral Balance Projection ‚Äì When activated, this skill also grants 2 allies a boost to both physical and magical attack for 5 seconds. While helpful, it still depends on her activating the ultimate, which takes time for beginners.

Note: Nebula's Astral Projection can be manually activated. During battle, her icon will flash when the skill is ready, tap it to activate the skill instantly. This allows you to strategically prevent key enemies from using their ultimates by draining their energy at the right time

Serenity

This skill heals two nearby allies and removes negative effects (debuffs). If only one ally is nearby, the healing goes to that hero. Very useful for keeping the team alive, especially in close formations.

Evolution Priority: High - Great for team survival, and it activates more often than the ultimate.

Ascension skill: Absolute Serenity ‚Äì If an ally receives a debuff within 4 seconds after Serenity, Nebula cancels the effect and heals again. This makes her healing even more powerful and reactive.

Disharmony

Automatically deals damage to the enemy with the lowest health. It‚Äôs a simple attack that helps finish off enemies, but it‚Äôs not as impactful as her support skills.

Evolution Priority: Medium - Decent damage, especially in long fights. It happens automatically and helps secure kills.

Equilibrium

This is Nebula‚Äôs core skill. It turns her basic attacks into support boosts, increasing the physical and magical attack of nearby allies. This happens constantly and makes a huge difference for damage dealers.

Evolution Priority: Very High - Her most important skill. It defines her role in the team and improves the whole team's performance.

Pros and Cons of Using Nebula

Nebula is a powerful support hero that enhances nearby allies' damage while also providing healing and debuff removal. Her performance depends heavily on positioning and timing, especially in high-level PvP and boss battles.

  • Pros:
    • Boosts both Physical and Magic Attack of nearby allies with Equilibrium, making her extremely versatile in hybrid teams.
    • Serenity heals two nearby allies and removes debuffs, greatly increasing team survivability.
    • Disharmony helps secure kills by targeting the enemy with the lowest health.
    • Astral Projection can burn enemy energy, preventing key ultimates if activated strategically.
    • Strong synergy with main damage dealers (Dante, K'arkh, Yasmine, Ishmael, Keira, Jhu).
    • Useful in multiple game modes: Campaign, Tower, Pet Adventures, Guild Wars, Arena, and Asgard (Osh fights).
  • Cons:
    • Requires proper positioning to maximize her Equilibrium buffs.
    • Astral Projection takes time to charge and may be wasted if not activated manually.
    • Limited impact without strong damage dealers to support.
    • Healing power depends on her Physical Attack, requiring investment in stats to be fully effective.
    • Manual activation of skills may be challenging for beginners or in auto-battle modes.

His bonus energy on successful dodges allows Nebula to use her ultimate Astral Projection faster, making her even more effective at energy control in PvP, Guild Wars, and Asgard boss battles.

How to Counter Nebula in Hero Wars: Dominion Era

1. Disrupt Her Positioning

Why it works:
Nebula's Equilibrium only buffs nearby allies. Forced repositioning breaks her synergies.

Maya (3rd Skill - Living Sprouts)

Pulls far-left/right enemies together, potentially isolating Nebula from her core team.
Bonus: Poison deals pure damage every 6 seconds.

Lars (1st Skill - Cyclone)

Displaces enemies with AoE knockbacks, often moving Nebula away from her damage dealers.

2. Burst Damage

Why it works:
Nebula has low survivability when focused.

  • Dante/Yasmine/Keira: High physical burst to eliminate her quickly
  • K'arkh/Ishmael: Cleave damage bypasses frontline protection
  • Jhu: Executes low-health targets after initial damage

3. Debuff Her Buffs

Amira (2nd Skill - Desperate Fury)

Causes Agility-based heroes (like Nebula's allies) to miss critical hits for 7 seconds.
Note: Less effective against non-crit teams.
