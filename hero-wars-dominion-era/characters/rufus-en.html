Who Is Rufus?

Rufus is a legendary tank hero known for his resilience against magic damage. Originally a cheerful drunk, he became a Guardian after trapping a demon inside his shield. That demon now enhances his abilities, making Rufus nearly immune to magic-based threats.

  • Class: Tank
  • Position: Front Line
  • Main Stat: Strength

Rufus stands as one of the most formidable defensive tanks in Hero Wars: Dominion Era, particularly effective against magic-based teams. His unique ability to resurrect after suffering fatal magic damage makes him invaluable in specific matchups.

Beyond his resurrection capability, Rufus provides crucial team support by generating magic damage shields for allies and converting absorbed damage into health restoration. These traits make him especially potent against popular magic dealers like Augustus and Orion, as well as against the Asgard boss Osh.
