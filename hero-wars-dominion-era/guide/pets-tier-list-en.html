The arrival of pets in Hero Wars: Dominion Era, brought a major transformation to the game's meta. Pets offer crucial advantages in battles, whether through direct support skills or stat enhancements via their patronage system.

This comprehensive hero wars pets tier list will help you understand the best companions to focus on, how to evolve them, and how to maximize their potential in every battle scenario.

How to Summon Pets in Hero Wars

If you're wondering how to summon pets in Hero Wars, you'll need to progress in the game until you unlock the Pet Summoning system via the Sanctuary. You'll require Pet Summoning Eggs, which can be acquired through various in-game activities, events, and guild wars. After collecting the eggs, visit the Sanctuary to hatch them and start nurturing your pets.

Hero Wars How to Get Pets and Evolve Them

To increase a pet’s strength, you’ll need to evolve them using Gold and Pet Soul Stones. As the pet’s star rating increases, so do its core attributes. Below are the required Pet Soul Stones for each star level of evolution:

  • 1-Star: 10 Pet Soul Stones
  • 2-Star: 50 Pet Soul Stones
  • 3-Star: 150 Pet Soul Stones
  • 4-Star: 500 Pet Soul Stones
  • 5-Star: 1,000 Pet Soul Stones
  • 6-Star: 2,000 Pet Soul Stones

Source: HW Library - General Information About Pets

Maximizing your pet's evolution is crucial to unlock their full potential in battles and through sponsorship boosts to your heroes.

Hero Wars Pets: Abilities and Patronage

Every pet has two types of abilities:

  • Active Abilities: Pets that participate in battle can unleash active skills. The first active skill is available immediately and can be manually triggered or set to auto-use when the energy bar is full. When a pet evolves to the green stage, it unlocks a second active ability that triggers automatically after a cooldown period.
  • Patronage Abilities: At the blue evolution stage, pets gain the ability to become patrons for certain heroes, enhancing the chosen hero’s stats during battles. Upon reaching the violet stage, pets also gain a special patronage ability, further enhancing their master's strength. Importantly, the pet doesn’t need to be physically present in battle for the sponsorship bonuses to apply.

Each pet can only sponsor specific heroes. To find out which heroes are supported, go to "Pets in the Sanctuary," choose a pet, and check the "Patronage" section.

Hero Wars Pets Tier List: Best Pets Ranked

Choosing the right pets is essential. Here’s the definitive hero wars pets guide you need for building an unstoppable team.

1st Place:

Axel

Axel shines as the most versatile pet. He applies a powerful shield that disperses incoming physical and magical damage among all allies based on their remaining health. The shield’s incredible strength scaling with skill power makes Axel invaluable against dangerous heroes like Dante and K’arkh. He prevents sudden eliminations of your backline and stabilizes the team quickly, making him the top pick for beginners and veterans alike. Many players prioritize evolving Axel over Oliver after early game stages for better performance.

2nd Place:

Khorus

Khorus excels in countering control effects. Every three seconds, or when control effects are inflicted by enemies, Khorus charges Runes that later inflict damage over time to enemies responsible for the most debuffs. His ability to disrupt teams heavy on crowd control makes him a must-have for both offense and defense in the Clash of Worlds. Note: Khorus doesn’t protect against Jorgen's energy manipulations, but still significantly improves debuff resistance overall.

3rd Place:

Albus

Albus targets enemies with the highest health, delivering pure damage that bypasses defense. Albus sees heavy use in Guild Raids and synergizes particularly well with Corvus and Aidan setups. His value against specific tanks like Fafnir and against solo opponents like Lian further cements his place in the meta.

4th Place:

Biscuit

Biscuit offers a strategic advantage by summoning an Icy Storm that blocks enemy healing for five seconds a crucial window during tough battles. Biscuit shines in Grand Arena and Clash of Worlds battles, where blocking healing can dramatically influence the result. Despite requiring minimal power to be effective, tactical players can achieve surprising victories by integrating a low-level Biscuit in their lineups.

5th Place:

Cain

Cain gains charges from successful Dodges and unleashes powerful physical attacks. While Cain primarily supports Dante-centered dodge teams, choosing between Cain and Axel often depends on the enemy composition. His focused physical burst can swing tight matches when well timed.

6th Place:

Oliver

Oliver is often the first pet that new players heavily upgrade due to his healing capabilities. He shrouds allies in magic to heal them whenever they suffer physical attacks. Oliver helps a lot in the campaign, tower, and various stages for beginners, making him a valuable early-game companion. However, many players transition from Oliver to Axel for greater effectiveness. In niche cases like Arachne tank setups or control-heavy teams, Oliver can sometimes outperform Axel if used strategically.

7th Place:

Fenris

Fenris blinds enemies and deals physical damage. He's part of the rare "Fenris Attack" formations focusing on fast, overwhelming offense. Though less commonly used today, he can still be a surprise pick for aggressive compositions aiming to disable enemy damage dealers quickly.

8th Place: Merlin

Merlin specializes in enhancing magic attacks. By creating a distortion zone that amplifies magic damage, Merlin supports magic-heavy teams, particularly in guild raid scenarios. However, outside of specific compositions, he remains a niche pick.

9th Place: Mara

Mara capitalizes on crowd control by dealing triple damage to enemies affected by debuffs like blindness, stun, charm, or silence. Although potent in theory, Mara’s utility is limited because many teams function efficiently without her, and her setup requirements make her a less consistent choice.

Best Pets Hero Wars: Final Thoughts

When building your ultimate team, understanding each pet’s strengths, synergy with heroes, and how they fit your overall strategy is critical. Axel remains the all-around best pet for most players, providing unmatched team durability. Khorus and Albus fill vital roles in handling debuff-heavy and high-health opponents, respectively. Biscuit can offer an unexpected edge, especially for disrupting healers, while Cain and Oliver shine in more specific situations.

Always consider the enemy team composition, your heroes' build, and battle objectives before selecting your pets. With this hero wars pets best guide, you're ready to dominate the Dominion Era!
