What Are Hero Wars Promo Codes?

Promo codes in Hero Wars: Dominion Era are special alphanumeric codes shared by the developers through events, social media campaigns, partnerships, or influencers. These codes usually reward you with items such as:

  • Energy
  • Gold
  • Emeralds
  • Hero soul stones
  • Skins or other upgrade materials

They're an easy and free way to boost your progress as long as you redeem them before they expire.

How to Redeem a Hero Wars Code on PC

If you're playing Hero Wars: Dominion Era on your PC Web browser, follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your Hero Wars: Dominion Era account.
  2. Open the game, and in the top-right corner, click the three horizontal bars menu (≡) to open the Settings panel.
  3. Click the "Promo Code" tab on the drop-down menu.
  4. Enter the Hero Wars promo code into the field exactly as shown.
  5. Click "Activate" and your rewards will be sent directly to your in-game mail.

Note: Promo codes are only available in the web version of Hero Wars: Dominion Era. They cannot be redeemed in the Facebook version.
