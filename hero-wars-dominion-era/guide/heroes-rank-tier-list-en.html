In the current meta of Hero Wars: Dominion Era, Julius stands out as the strongest tank. His powerful shields can be further enhanced when paired with other heroes who also provide shields. Heidi is considered the best counter to Julius, making her a great addition to your meta team.

There are several strategic options to consider: Thea and Rufus are very effective against magic damage, Heidi is excellent for dealing with evasion-based enemies, and Nebula is essential for cleansing debuffs and boosting allies. Besides hero selection, choosing the correct pets for each ally and team composition is crucial. For a detailed pet selection guide, check out our Pets Tier List.
