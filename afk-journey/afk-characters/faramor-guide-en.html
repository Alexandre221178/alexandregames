Faramor is the latest addition to AFK Journey, bringing a unique playstyle to the battlefield. As a Wilder Rogue, he specializes in physical damage and excels at controlling enemy healing while boosting his allies. His ability to disrupt enemy sustain and deal true damage makes him an invaluable asset in many team compositions.

In this guide, we will dive deep into his abilities, stats, and strategies to maximize his potential, helping you utilize him effectively in different situations.

Hero Overview

Character Level:A

Faction: Graveborn

Class Role: Rogue

Damage Type: Physical

Range: 1 Tile

Max Ascension: Supreme+

Stats Breakdown

  • Crit Resist: 48.4% - Reduces enemy critical hits, improving survivability.

Faramor Gameplay Overview

Faramor’s gameplay revolves around precise positioning, timing, and synergy with buff-supporting allies. As a frontline rogue who thrives in close combat, he uses his abilities to control the battlefield, deny enemy healing, and empower his team.

His skillset rewards strategic play, making him especially effective in compositions that amplify his burst and crowd control potential. Mastering Faramor means understanding when to engage, when to retreat, and how to maximize the devastating effects of his Sanctified Circle.

Abilities and Tips

1- Sanctified Circle (Ultimate)

Description: Faramor creates a magic circle, dealing true damage over time while preventing enemies from healing. The longer enemies stay in the circle, the more damage they take based on their missing HP. This ability is especially useful against heroes who rely on regeneration or lifesteal mechanics.

Tips:

  • Best used against tanky enemies that rely on healing.
  • Try to position him so that multiple enemies get caught in the circle.
  • If no enemies remain in the circle, the effect ends early, so timing is crucial.
  • Use alongside heroes that can immobilize or trap enemies within the circle.

2- Glory's Embrace

Description: Faramor gains a shield and deals massive damage. If he has an active buff from an ally, the damage is converted into true damage, making this ability extremely potent against high-defense opponents.

Tips:

  • Pair him with allies that provide buffs to maximize the true damage effect.
  • The shield adds survivability, making him more durable in fights.
  • Time this ability to maximize burst damage when the enemy is at low HP.

3- Sacred Pledge

Description: Before battle, Faramor blesses an ally, increasing both their ATK stats. While the buff is active, nearby enemies take periodic damage and are stunned, adding extra crowd control to his kit.

Tips:

  • Position Faramor next to a high-damage ally to maximize the buff.
  • The stun effect is useful for disrupting enemy formations.
  • Great synergy with heroes that benefit from ATK boosts, such as burst damage dealers.

4- Hero Focus

Description: Increases Haste during battle, allowing Faramor to attack more frequently and cycle through his abilities faster.

Tips:

  • Higher haste means more frequent attacks, boosting damage output.
  • Works well with attack-speed-focused builds and support heroes.

5- Bitter Requiem

Description: When the magic circle is active, Faramor’s basic attacks are replaced by Glory's Embrace, and enemies inside the circle take additional true damage, making it a devastating combo.

Tips:

  • Combine with heavy AoE damage heroes to maximize its effects.
  • The extra damage helps take down priority targets faster.
  • Focus on trapping enemies inside the magic circle for sustained damage.

6- Enhance Force

Description: If an enemy revives inside the magic circle, their vitality is reduced significantly, making it a strong counter against revival-based teams.

Tips:

  • Strong counter against teams with revive mechanics.
  • Prevents enemies from regaining full strength after resurrection.

Pros and Cons of Faramor

Pros

  • Excellent healing suppression through his Sanctified Circle ability.
  • High critical chance and solid physical damage output.
  • Versatile utility thanks to shields, stuns, and ally buffs.
  • Great synergy with buff-heavy allies to trigger true damage.
  • Strong counter to teams that rely on reviving heroes.

Cons

  • Relies heavily on proper positioning to maximize his circle’s effect.
  • Effectiveness drops against teams that don’t rely on healing.
  • Squishy if not properly protected or shielded.
  • Requires specific team compositions to unlock full potential.

Use his skills wisely, and he will be a key asset in both PvP and PvE battles!
