How to Claim Daily Rewards in Hero Wars: Alliance

To receive your daily rewards in Hero Wars: Alliance, you must complete the specified campaign mission. Each day, you'll see a mission such as "2-4" this refers to Chapter 2, Stage 4 of the campaign map.

To complete it, go to the campaign map, navigate to Chapter 2, then select Stage 4. You can either play the mission manually or use the "Raid" option if it's already completed. Once the mission is done, the reward will be sent to your in-game mailbox.

Open the mailbox and claim your reward. If the reward doesn't appear immediately, try restarting the game. Keep in mind that daily rewards are time-limited make sure to claim them each day before they expire!
