About the Author


Hey there, fellow gamer! I'm Alexandre Domingos, a proud Brazilian, happily married, and a postgraduate in Production Engineering. My love affair with games started in childhood and has only grown stronger over the years. Three years ago, I embarked on a new adventure by creating gaming content on YouTube. Now, alongside my incredible wife, Kilvia Ferreira Domingos, I'm dedicated to crafting captivating content for Alexandre Games Blog. Kilvia's unwavering support and encouragement fuel my passion for gaming, ensuring that I never stop striving for excellence. Together, we're committed to delivering the latest news, tips, and insights to enrich your gaming experience. Join us on this epic journey, and let's explore the world of mobile gaming together!
