How to Master Dyrroth in Mobile Legends

Dyrroth is a powerful fighter in Mobile Legends, known for his assassin-like playstyle and strong durability. Mastering Dyrroth requires understanding his abilities, positioning in team fights, and executing combos effectively. In this guide, we'll explore Dyrroth's skills and provide strategies on how to dominate the battlefield with this hero.

Understanding Dyrroth's Abilities

Passive - Wrath of the Abyss: When Dyrroth's Fury reaches 50%, he enhances Burst Strike and Blast Step. Every 2 basic attacks, Dyrroth unleashes Circle Attack, dealing physical damage to enemies in the circle and regenerating HP based on the damage dealt.

Skill 1 - Burst Strike: Dyrroth unleashes a burst blow in a designated direction, dealing physical damage and slowing enemies. When enhanced, Burst Strike has a greater range and double the slowing effect.

Skill 2 - Specter Step: Dyrroth dashes in a designated direction, stopping when he hits a target. He then deals explosive damage to the target. When enhanced, Killing Blow deals increased damage, slows the target, and reduces their physical defense.

Ultimate - Abyss Strike: Dyrroth charges and unleashes Abyss Strike, dealing massive physical damage plus additional damage based on the target's lost HP. Abyss Strike also slows the targets' movement speed.

Strategies for Playing Dyrroth

1. Early Game Farming: Focus on farming minions to build up gold and experience. Use Burst Strike to clear minion waves quickly and efficiently.

2. Engage Wisely: Dyrroth is durable but not invincible. Engage in fights where you have the advantage, such as outnumbering the enemy or when your team is ready to follow up.

3. Target Selection: Prioritize squishy targets like mages and marksmen. Use Specter Step to lock onto them and eliminate them quickly before they can deal significant damage.

4. Combo Execution: Use Dyrroth's abilities in quick succession to maximize damage. Start with Burst Strike to slow enemies, then use Specter Step to engage and deal damage, and finish with Abyss Strike to secure kills.

5. Positioning in Team Fights: Stay on the frontline but be mindful of your positioning. Avoid being caught out alone, and always aim to disrupt the enemy backline while protecting your own.

6. Build for Durability and Damage: Equip items that enhance Dyrroth's durability and damage output. Items like Blade of the Heptaseas, Queen's Wings, and Immortality can greatly increase his survivability in fights.

7. Map Awareness and Rotation: Keep an eye on the map and rotate to help your teammates when needed. Dyrroth's mobility allows him to quickly move between lanes and support his allies.

8. Split-Pushing: Dyrroth excels at split-pushing due to his ability to clear waves quickly and escape from sticky situations. When split-pushing, always keep an eye on the map and be ready to join team fights if needed.

9. Counter-Building: Adapt your build to counter the enemy team. If they have a lot of crowd control, consider items like Athena's Shield or Immortality. If they are tanky, items like Blade of Despair or Malefic Roar can help you deal more damage.

10. Use Flicker Wisely: Flicker can be a valuable tool for engaging or escaping. Use it wisely to surprise enemies or to get out of dangerous situations. Save it for crucial moments in team fights or when you need to secure a kill.

11. Cooldown Management: Dyrroth's abilities have relatively low cooldowns, especially when enhanced. Manage your cooldowns effectively to constantly apply pressure in fights and skirmishes.

12. Practice Makes Perfect: Like any hero, mastering Dyrroth takes practice. Spend time in matches to understand his mechanics, combos, and matchups against different heroes.

By following these strategies and tips, you can become a formidable Dyrroth player in Mobile Legends. With his strong damage output, durability, and mobility, Dyrroth is a hero capable of turning the tide of battles and leading your team to victory.

Dyrroth: Strengths and Weaknesses

Positive Points

  • Escape Skill: Dyrroth's second skill, Specter Step, allows him to quickly dash away from danger, making him difficult to catch.
  • Passive Skill: His passive, Wrath of the Abyss, grants him enhanced abilities and bonus effects when his Fury reaches 50%, enhancing his combat capabilities.
  • High Attack Damage: Dyrroth boasts high physical attack damage, allowing him to deal significant damage to enemies.
  • Movement Speed: With his skills, Dyrroth can quickly move around the battlefield, enabling him to engage or disengage from fights effectively.
  • Durability: Despite his assassin-like playstyle, Dyrroth has good durability, making him resilient in prolonged engagements.

Negative Points

  • Dependent on Skills to Deal Damage: Dyrroth's damage output is heavily reliant on landing his skills, making him less effective if his skills are on cooldown or miss their targets.
  • Too Many Counters: He has several heroes who can counter him effectively, such as those with high mobility or burst damage.
  • Weak Against Control Heroes: Heroes with crowd control abilities can disrupt Dyrroth's combos and prevent him from dealing damage effectively.
  • Weak against Healing Reduction: Dyrroth's sustainability in fights is diminished when facing enemies with healing reduction abilities or items.

By understanding these strengths and weaknesses, players can effectively utilize Dyrroth's strengths while mitigating his weaknesses to dominate the battlefield.

Dyrroth Best Builds

To counter teams with a plethora of magic heroes boasting control skills, consider equipping Vigor Boots. These boots mitigate the impact of such negative effects, bolstering your resilience in the face of magical onslaughts. On the other hand, when faced with enemy soldiers, opting for Warrior Boots is prudent. These boots augment your physical defense, offering a crucial edge in combat.

The Bloodlust Axe stands out as a crucial item for Dyrroth, enhancing his vampirism while also boosting physical attack and reducing cooldowns. Consider complementing this with Blades of Despair to significantly amplify your damage output. As the game progresses, investing in Immortality can be wise, granting you the ability to revive upon death, or Queen's Wings to bolster your defensive capabilities.


Dyrroth Guide Conclusion

In conclusion, Dyrroth is a versatile hero in Mobile Legends, capable of making a significant impact on the battlefield. With his ability to quickly escape with Specter Step, he can effectively engage and disengage from fights. His passive ability, Wrath of the Abyss, enhances his abilities in critical moments, increasing his ability to deal damage and sustain himself during battles.

To maximize Dyrroth's potential, it is essential to master his abilities and combos. Proper use of items such as Bloodlust Axe and Blades of Despair can significantly increase his fighting power and his ability to deal team damage.

However, Dyrroth also has his weaknesses, being especially vulnerable to heroes with control and healing reduction. It is important to be aware of these vulnerabilities and adapt your gameplay strategy accordingly.

With practice and understanding of his abilities, Dyrroth can become a key player on your team, capable of turning the tide of the game and leading your team to victory.
