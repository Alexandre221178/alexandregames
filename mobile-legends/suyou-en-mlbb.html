Suyou Guide Introduction

Suyou is a versatile assassin/fighter hero in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang, perfectly suited for the jungle or XP lane. His abilities allow him to deal significant physical damage, making him a powerful asset in any battle. Below, we'll break down his skills, combos, and strategies to help you master this new hero.

Suyou's Skills Breakdown

Passive: Transient Immortal

  • Tap Cast: Increases movement speed after casting any skill.
  • Hold Cast: Increases damage reduction, which scales with Suyou's physical attack stats.

Suyou's passive is crucial for mobility and survivability. Using it effectively allows you to either chase down enemies or withstand attacks during engagements.

Skill 1: Blade Surge

  • Tap Cast: Suyou tosses his weapons and blinks to their location, performing a damaging slash. This enhances his agility.
  • Hold Cast: Charges forward, stunning enemies on impact. The distance scales with the time charged.

Tip: Use the tap cast for mobility and quick engagements, while the hold cast provides crowd control (stun) and longer-range engagement.

Skill 2: Soul Sever

  • Tap Cast: Executes a backward-moving slash that deals heavy damage in a fan-shaped area.
  • Hold Cast: Delivers three powerful cleaves, with the final blow dealing massive damage and healing Suyou.

Tip: The hold cast is your main burst damage skill. Combine it with Skill 1 to chase or retreat while dealing high damage.

Skill 3: Evil Quelling

  • Tap Cast: Deals damage in a rectangular area and slows enemies while Suyou glides backward.
  • Hold Cast: Fires a deadly arrow in a target direction, dealing significant damage.

Tip: This skill is effective both for controlling enemy movement and finishing off low-health targets from a distance.

Combos & Strategies

1. Ganking and Camping Combo

  1. Hold cast Skill 1 (Blade Surge) to engage and stun the target.
  2. Hold cast Skill 2 (Soul Sever) for burst damage.

Result: This combo is perfect for jungle ganks or ambushing enemies.

2. Slowing Down Target Combo

  1. Hold cast Skill 1 (Blade Surge) to engage.
  2. Tap cast Skill 3 (Evil Quelling) to slow the enemy.
  3. Finish with hold cast Skill 2 (Soul Sever).

Result: This combo is useful for controlling enemy movement and landing critical hits.

3. Long Range Combo

  1. Hold cast Skill 3 (Evil Quelling) to hit enemies from a distance.
  2. Follow up with hold cast Skill 1 (Blade Surge) to close the gap.
  3. Finish with hold cast Skill 2 (Soul Sever) for maximum damage.

Result: Ideal for picking off distant enemies before engaging them up close.

4. Surprise Attack Combo

  1. Start with hold cast Skill 3 (Evil Quelling).
  2. Quickly tap cast Skill 1 (Blade Surge) to engage.
  3. Finish with hold cast Skill 2 (Soul Sever).

Result: This burst combo is effective in taking down unsuspecting enemies in a fast-paced attack.

5. Kill Secure Combo

  1. Hold cast Skill 1 (Blade Surge) to engage the fleeing target.
  2. Hold cast Skill 2 (Soul Sever) to deal burst damage.
  3. Hold cast Skill 3 (Evil Quelling) to finish off the target.

Result: Great for chasing down low-health enemies trying to escape.

Gameplay Tips

  • Upgrade Priorities:
    • Skill 2 (Soul Sever) should be maxed first for its high burst damage and healing.
    • Skill 3 (Evil Quelling) should be your second priority, focusing on its slow effect and long-range damage.
  • Positioning: Always use your passive and mobility skills to position yourself for the next attack or retreat. Suyou excels in hit-and-run tactics, so never stay in one place for too long.
  • Skill Management:
    • Tap cast skills are best for repositioning or escaping.
    • Hold cast skills are your main source of damage and crowd control.
  • Itemization: Build items that enhance Suyou's physical attack and survivability. Items like Blade of Despair, Endless Battle, and Queen's Wings are excellent choices.

Conclusion

Suyou is a highly strategic hero that requires quick thinking and precise skill usage. With his mobility, crowd control, and burst damage potential, he can be a game-changing assassin or fighter. Focus on mastering his combos and understanding when to engage or retreat to maximize your effectiveness in the game.

Now go and dominate the battlefield with Suyou, The Immortal’s chosen warrior!
