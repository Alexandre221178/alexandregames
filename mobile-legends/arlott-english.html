Understanding Arlott Skills

Skill Breakdown:

  • Passive - Demon Gaze: Arlott's demonic eye marks enemies near her who are affected by control effects for 8 seconds. It also automatically marks a nearby enemy hero every 8 seconds. This passive is crucial for setting up her other skills and maximizing her damage output.
  • 1st Skill - Dauntless Strike: Arlott throws her spear forward, dealing 200 (+60% Total Physical Attack) physical damage and briefly stunning targets within the area of effect. Targets hit at the edge of the area of effect will be stunned for 1 second. This skill is great for initiating fights and catching enemies off guard.
  • 2nd Skill - Vengeance: Arlott dashes at an enemy, dealing 110 (+60% Total Physical Attack) physical damage. If Arlott uses this skill on a target with the mark, it deals double damage and immediately resets the skill's cooldown, in addition to restoring (5% of Total HP) HP (only restores half the amount when the target is not a hero). This skill is Arlott's primary source of damage and sustain.
  • 3rd Skill - Final Slash: Arlott sweeps the path in front of her in a large fan-shaped area, dealing 400 (+80% Total Physical Attack) physical damage. This skill also quickly pushes enemies hit to the end of the area of effect, as well as briefly revealing their location. Final Slash is an incredibly powerful skill that can turn the tide of battles when used correctly.

Game-Play Strategy:

Early Game: In the early game, focus on farming and harassing enemies with your skills. Use Vengeance to engage and disengage from fights, and use Dauntless Strike to stun enemies and set up kills for your team.

Mid Game: By this point, you should have your core items and be ready to team fight. Use Final Slash to initiate fights and catch enemies off guard. Coordinate with your team to maximize the impact of your skills.

Late Game: In the late game, Arlott becomes a formidable force. Use your skills to disrupt enemy formations and pick off priority targets. Remember to use Vengeance wisely to sustain yourself in fights and turn the tide in your favor.

Advanced Tips:

  • Positioning: Positioning is key for Arlott. Stay on the outskirts of team fights, using your skills to poke at enemies and engage when the time is right. Be mindful of your surroundings and avoid getting caught out of position.
  • Combo Potential: Arlott has great combo potential with her skills. Try using Dauntless Strike to stun enemies, followed by Vengeance for a burst of damage. Then, use Final Slash to finish off low health enemies or reposition them for your team.
  • Map Awareness: Keep an eye on the map at all times. Arlott is great for split-pushing lanes and catching enemies off guard. Use this to your advantage by constantly applying pressure on the map.
  • Itemization: Choose your items wisely based on the enemy team composition. Items like Blade of Despair, Endless Battle, and Immortality can greatly enhance Arlott's damage and survivability.
  • Communication: Communication is key when playing Arlott. Coordinate with your team to maximize the impact of your skills and secure objectives.

Arlott's Strengths and Weaknesses

Strengths:

  • Stun Enemy Skill: Arlott's Dauntless Strike skill can stun enemies, making her a formidable presence in team fights and skirmishes.
  • Area Damage Skill: Her Final Slash skill deals area damage, allowing her to hit multiple enemies at once and control the battlefield.
  • HP Regeneration: With her Vengeance skill, Arlott can regenerate health, increasing her sustainability in fights and allowing her to stay longer in battle.
  • Movement Speed: Arlott has good mobility with her skills, allowing her to engage and disengage from fights quickly.
  • Automatically Mark Enemy Skill: Her Demon Gaze passive automatically marks enemies affected by control effects, helping her to target vulnerable enemies.

Weaknesses:

  • Weak Against Control: Arlott is weak against crowd control effects, as they can disrupt her skills and limit her mobility.
  • Depends on Demon Eye Mark to Deal More Damage: To maximize her damage output, Arlott relies on her Demon Gaze passive to mark enemies. Without this mark, her damage potential is reduced.
  • Weak Against Healing Reduction: Heroes or items that reduce healing can be problematic for Arlott, as it limits her ability to sustain herself in prolonged fights.

Understanding Arlott's strengths and weaknesses is crucial for mastering her gameplay and effectively countering her opponents.

Combo Tips for Arlott MLBB

Mastering combos is crucial for maximizing Arlott's damage output and effectiveness in battles. Here are some combo sequences along with the skills you should execute:

Sequence Combo 1

  • Skill 3 (Final Slash) - Use to initiate and deal area damage.
  • Skill 1 (Dauntless Strike) - Stun targets hit by Final Slash.
  • Skill 2 (Vengeance) - Dash towards a marked enemy for extra damage and HP regeneration.
  • Basic Attack - Follow up with basic attacks for additional damage.

Sequence Combo 2:

  • Skill 1 (Dauntless Strike) - Use to stun and initiate the combo.
  • Skill 2 (Vengeance) - Dash towards the stunned target for extra damage.
  • Basic Attack - Follow up with basic attacks for continuous damage.
  • Skill 2 (Vengeance) - If the target is still alive, use Vengeance again for additional damage.
  • Basic Attack - Continue with basic attacks to finish off the target.
  • Skill 3 (Final Slash) - Use to finish off enemies or reposition them for your team.

Sequence Combo 3:

  • Skill 3 (Final Slash) - Initiate the combo and deal area damage.
  • Skill 1 (Dauntless Strike) - Stun targets hit by Final Slash.
  • Skill 2 (Vengeance) - Dash towards a marked enemy for extra damage and HP regeneration.
  • Basic Attack - Follow up with basic attacks for continuous damage.
  • Skill 2 (Vengeance) - If the target is still alive, use Vengeance again for additional damage.
  • Basic Attack - Continue with basic attacks to finish off the target.

These combos require practice to execute perfectly, but mastering them will greatly increase your effectiveness as an Arlott player in Mobile Legends. Experiment with different timings and orders to find the combos that work best for your playstyle and the situation at hand.

Arlott Best Spells

The most commonly used and recommended spells for Arlott are Petrify and Retribution for their versatility and effectiveness in different situations.

1. Petrify

Petrify is a great spell for Arlott as it can be used both offensively and defensively. It can be used to stun enemies, allowing Arlott to deal damage safely or escape from dangerous situations. It's particularly effective when used in combination with her combo skills to burst down enemies quickly.


Petrify

2. Retribution

Retribution is another strong spell choice for Arlott, especially when focusing on jungle objectives. It provides additional damage against jungle enemies, making it easier for Arlott to become strong quickly and take down important targets like enemy carries or high-priority enemies.

Both spells offer Arlott additional crowd control and burst damage potential, enhancing her ability to control team fights and secure kills. Experiment with these spells to see which ones best suit your playstyle and the current game situation.

Arlott Best Builds in Mobile Legends

When building Arlott, focus on items that enhance her damage, sustain, and survivability. Here are some recommended build options:

1. Core Items

  • Bloodlust Axe: Provides significant spell vamp, allowing Arlott to recover health while using her abilities.
  • Endless Battle: Offers a mix of damage, lifesteal, and true damage, synergizing well with Arlott's skills.

2. Damage and Penetration

  • Malefic Roar: Increases physical penetration, making Arlott's attacks more effective against armored targets.
  • War Axe: Provides additional physical attack and a passive that increases damage after using a skill.

3. Damage Boost

  • Blades of Despair: Increases physical damage, making Arlott's attacks hit harder.

4. Late Game Defense

  • Queen's Wings: Offers additional defense and a shield that activates when health is low, providing survivability in team fights.
  • Brute Force Breastplate: Provides armor and a passive that increases physical attack and movement speed, enhancing Arlott's survivability and damage potential.

Experiment with these items and adjust your build based on the enemy team composition and game situation. Prioritize items that complement Arlott's abilities and playstyle to maximize her effectiveness on the battlefield.
