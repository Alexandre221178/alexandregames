Strategy on how to play with Arlott

The skill 2 Revenge is Arlott's primary source of damage and sustain, If an enemy is marked use it to charge at the enemy dealing damage, and then it will have the recharge time zeroed and can be used again, thus, Arlott regenerates her life.


  • Advertisement
    •      

If there are no enemies in Arlott's range, stun the Minions and use Skill 2 Vengeance to jump in and quickly clear the wave of Minions. When low on health, Arlott can mark a group of Minions with the Mark (passive ability) and cast Skill 2 Revenge and recover your health quickly.

The 3 Final Slash skill is an insane skill with area damage and displacement effect that can push enemies clockwise and gather them all in one position, Immediately stun them all using the 1st skill Dauntless Strike at the perfect moment to catch the entire enemy team and make a legendary or a maniac.

The Final Slash ability can also pull enemies through walls and even move them under the tower, the ideal time to use the 1st skill Dauntless Strike again and stun them under the tower.

Early game and mid-game Arlott is an excellent hero, using the passive skill repeatedly, marking enemies automatically, and is able to use the 2nd Skill Vengeance many times while regenerating his life.

At the end of the game (end game) Arlott becomes even deadlier with more defense and damage and with its ability to constantly regenerate health he makes an insane opponent and sure is a Catch or BANN type hero...
