Mobile Legends Best Marksman - Tier List

Marksmen are high-damage, ranged heroes who excel at dealing massive amounts of damage over time, especially in the later stages of the game. Their range allows them to position safely behind the front lines, taking down enemies from a distance. Key marksmen like Roger can also double as fighters, excelling in jungle rotations and becoming incredibly dangerous in mid-game fights.

Some Marksmen, such as Popol and Kupa, provide more than just high DPS, offering crowd control and zone control through pets or turrets, while Edith shines in rotations, making her a flexible choice across various roles. Success with a marksman often depends on good positioning and support from allies to cover their vulnerabilities.

Mobile Legends Best Assassin - Tier List

Assassins are agile heroes skilled at delivering high burst damage, often eliminating key enemies in seconds.

Their primary role is to eliminate backline threats, especially marksmen and mages, before they can impact team fights. Arlott, for example, doubles as a fighter and assassin, thriving in the EXP lane with a powerful skillset. In contrast, Mathilda combines her role with support capabilities, excelling in high-level rotations and aiding her team significantly.

This flexibility makes assassins a valuable asset in jungle roles, where they can roam and secure kills early on. Many assassins require fast reflexes and excellent map awareness to maximize their potential, but when mastered, they can single-handedly turn the tide of a match.

Mobile Legends Best Fighter - Tier List

Fighters are durable front-liners, capable of withstanding considerable damage while dealing substantial damage in return.

Many fighters, like Arlott and Julian, perform exceptionally well in the EXP lane, allowing them to dominate 1v1 skirmishes. Masha is another example of a hybrid tank-fighter, who thrives with her unique health regeneration skills, while Yin excels only in specific jungle settings.

Fighters generally offer strong crowd control or tank-like durability, making them valuable in team fights where they can absorb damage and disrupt the enemy formation. Their versatility and resilience make them a cornerstone of any team, providing both offense and defense in equal measure.

Mobile Legends Best Mages - Tier List

Mages are high-burst, magic-damage dealers who specialize in disrupting enemies with powerful spells and area control. Julian is a unique hybrid between mage and fighter, excelling in jungle roles and capable of playing as an assassin. Mages like Harley and Cyclops also perform well in the jungle, adding more versatility to this class.

With a mix of crowd control and damage-over-time abilities, mages can control the battlefield effectively. While fragile, their high burst potential and game-changing abilities make them a vital part of any team composition, especially in mid and late-game team fights.

Mobile Legends Best Tanks - Tier List

Tanks are the backbone of any team, absorbing damage and initiating team fights with their crowd control abilities. Some tanks, like Minotaur and Carmilla, double as support characters, offering healing and defensive skills. Others, such as Fredrinn and Esmeralda, are more effective in the EXP lane or jungle, bringing additional flexibility to the class.

A good tank can create opportunities for the team by controlling enemy movement and soaking up damage. Tanks are especially critical in team fights, where they protect allies, initiate attacks, and provide cover for squishy backliners.

Mobile Legends Best Support - Tier List

Support heroes are essential for empowering allies, offering healing, shields, or crowd control to enable better positioning and survival. Unlike tanks, supports focus on empowering their teammates rather than leading the front lines. Minotaur and Carmilla offer a unique mix of support and tank roles, capable of withstanding moderate damage while aiding teammates.

Supports like Mathilda and Angela also offer excellent mobility and control, making them invaluable for team rotations and ambushes. They are best paired with allies who can utilize their skills effectively, as their low durability makes them vulnerable when isolated.
