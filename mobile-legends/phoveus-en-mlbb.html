Mastering the New Phoveus in Mobile Legends Bang Bang (2024)

Phoveus has undergone a significant rework, bringing new dynamics to his gameplay in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang. This guide will provide a detailed analysis of Phoveus's reworked skills, offer strategic tips for utilizing them in battles, discuss his strengths and weaknesses against other heroes, and suggest optimal builds and emblems to maximize his potential.

I. Understanding Phoveus's Reworked Skills

Phoveus's rework has redefined his role in the game, focusing more on punishing enemies who rely on mobility. His abilities now have better synergy and offer greater tactical options. Here’s a breakdown of each skill and how to use them effectively:

1. Passive Skill: Demonic Force

Effect: Phoveus's next Basic Attack allows him to charge at the enemy, knocking them back, dealing extra Magic Damage equal to 4% of the enemy's Max HP, and gaining a Shield equal to 8% of his Max HP. The cooldown for this effect ranges from 26 to 14 seconds based on the level of his ultimate, Infernal Pursuit.

Buff: Whenever an enemy hero blinks, dashes, or is displaced within 8 units of Phoveus, the cooldown of Demonic Force is reduced by 20%, and the cooldown of his other skills is reduced by 0.5 seconds.

Strategic Tips:

  • Engage Tactically: Use Demonic Force to initiate fights or follow up on your team’s crowd control (CC). The knockback can disrupt enemy formation and give your team a positional advantage.
  • Capitalize on Mobility: Since Demonic Force benefits from enemy mobility, try to stay near heroes with dashes or blinks. This will allow you to spam your abilities more frequently, giving you an edge in prolonged engagements.
  • Shield Timing: Time your Basic Attack after activating Demonic Force to maximize the shield’s effectiveness, especially when you anticipate heavy damage.

2. First Skill: Demonic Impact

Effect: Phoveus slams his monolith into the ground, dealing 200 (+120% Total Physical Attack) Physical Damage to enemies in the area and slowing them by 70% for 0.5 seconds. If this skill hits an enemy, the next Demonic Impact will leave behind an Astaros Eye, which implodes after a short delay, dealing 100 (+60% Total Physical Attack) Magic Damage to enemies.

Strategic Tips:

  • Crowd Control: The slow effect from Demonic Impact is crucial for controlling enemy movements. Use it to secure kills or prevent escapes.
  • Astaros Eye Placement: If you manage to hit an enemy, the subsequent Astaros Eye provides additional damage and control. Position the eye strategically in high-traffic areas or where you anticipate the enemy will move.
  • Skill Combo: Follow up with Demonic Impact after other CC skills from your team to maximize its effectiveness. The slow will make it harder for enemies to avoid the delayed explosion of the Astaros Eye.

3. Second Skill: Dark Wave

Effect: Phoveus releases his demonic power in a target direction, knocking enemies airborne, dragging them along, and dealing 250 (+100% Extra Physical Attack) Magic Damage.

Strategic Tips:

  • Engage and Disrupt: Dark Wave is a powerful initiation tool. Use it to disrupt enemy formations by knocking them airborne, making them vulnerable to follow-up attacks from your team.
  • Combo Potential: This skill is perfect for setting up your ultimate or other skills. Combine it with Demonic Impact for a devastating chain of crowd control and damage.
  • Defensive Utility: Don’t hesitate to use Dark Wave defensively. It can create space between you and diving enemies or peel off attackers from your backline.

4. Ultimate: Infernal Pursuit

Effect: Phoveus's abilities Demonic Force, Astaros Eye, and Dark Wave can apply Sinner's Mark to enemy heroes. When an enemy hero with Sinner's Mark is within range, Phoveus can leap into the air and smash down with his monolith, dealing 250 (+100% Total Physical Attack) plus extra Physical Damage equal to 5% of the target's Max HP. Phoveus also recovers 50 plus 6% of his Lost HP. The HP recovery is reduced by 80% when hitting multiple enemies.

Strategic Tips:

  • Finisher Move: Use Infernal Pursuit to finish off low-health enemies or dive into the backline during team fights. The damage and HP recovery make Phoveus a formidable threat in skirmishes.
  • Timing Is Key: Look for enemies marked with Sinner’s Mark and time your leap for maximum impact. Remember that hitting multiple enemies reduces your healing, so try to target isolated heroes for the best results.
  • Sustain in Fights: The healing effect from Infernal Pursuit can keep you alive in prolonged engagements, especially in one-on-one duels. Use this to your advantage by continuously applying pressure on your opponents.

II. Phoveus Reworked Counters and Synergies

1. Counters to Phoveus


 Zhask: Zhask’s spawnables are unaffected by Phoveus's abilities, rendering many of his skills ineffective. Zhask can overwhelm Phoveus with constant pressure.

Esmeralda: Esmeralda’s ability to absorb shields directly counters Phoveus's shield from Demonic Force, making her a tough opponent.

Alucard: Alucard's high sustain and mobility make it difficult for Phoveus to outlast him in fights.

Baxia: Baxia’s anti-heal capabilities reduce the effectiveness of Phoveus's Infernal Pursuit, cutting down his sustain in battles.

Gord: Gord’s long-range and burst damage can keep Phoveus at bay, exploiting his lack of gap-closing abilities outside his ultimate.

Hylos: Hylos’s ultimate can negate Phoveus’s mobility advantage, making it hard for him to engage or disengage effectively.
Aulus: Aulus can outtrade Phoveus in sustained fights, especially with his continuous damage and self-heal.

Minsitthar: Minsitthar’s King’s Calling can prevent Phoveus from using his mobility-based skills effectively.

2. Phoveus's Strengths Against Specific Heroes


 Wanwan: Wanwan relies heavily on her dashes to reposition in fights, making her an easy target for Phoveus's abilities.

Benedetta: Benedetta's constant dashing plays right into Phoveus’s hands, allowing him to reduce his cooldowns and punish her mobility.

Harith: Harith's reliance on his blink skills makes him vulnerable to Phoveus's skill set, especially in prolonged engagements.

Ling: Ling’s frequent use of dashes and blinks gives Phoveus plenty of opportunities to capitalize on his passive and ultimate.

Valentina: Valentina’s skillset, which includes dashing abilities, makes her susceptible to Phoveus’s abilities, especially if she tries to engage him head-on.

III. Phoveus Top Global Build 2024

Warrior Boots

Attributes: +40 Movement Speed, +18 Physical Defense

Unique Passive - Valor: Gain 4 extra Physical Defense for 3 seconds when taking Physical Damage, up to 20.

Why?: Warrior Boots provide essential movement speed and physical defense, making Phoveus more resilient in the early game while allowing him to stick to his targets.

Thunder Belt

Attributes: +800 HP, +15 Physical Defense, +15 Magic Defense, +20 Movement Speed

Unique Passive - Thunderbolt: Every 4 seconds, the next Basic Attack deals extra True Damage and briefly slows enemies.

Why?: Thunder Belt enhances Phoveus’s Basic Attack, adding true damage and a significant slow effect that synergizes well with his Demonic Force.

War Axe

Attributes: +35 Physical Attack, +400 HP, +10% Cooldown Reduction, +12% Spell Vamp

Unique Passive - Fighting Spirit: Attacks grant extra Physical Attack and True Damage based on the number of stacks.

Why?: War Axe is essential for Phoveus's damage output and sustainability, especially in prolonged fights where he can maximize the passive effect.

Brute Force Breastplate

Attributes: +600 HP, +23 Physical Defense, +10% Cooldown Reduction

Unique Passive - Brute Force: Each second after dealing damage, gain extra Adaptive Attack and Movement Speed.

Why?: This item provides a balance of defense, cooldown reduction, and damage, making Phoveus even more durable while enhancing his mobility and attack power.

Queen's Wings

Attributes: +40 Adaptive Attack, +600 HP, +10% Cooldown Reduction, +10% Spell Vamp

Unique Passive - Demonize: When HP drops below 40%, gains 30% Damage Reduction for 3 seconds and reduces skill cooldowns by 2 seconds.

Why?: Queen's Wings offer excellent survivability and sustain in fights, giving Phoveus the ability to take more damage while dishing out powerful hits.

Oracle

Attributes: +20 Physical Defense, +20 Magic Defense, +10% Cooldown Reduction

Unique Passive - Bless: Received Shield and HP Regen effects are increased by 30%.

Why?: Oracle synergizes well with Phoveus's shield and healing abilities, enhancing his sustain and survivability in team fights.

IV. Phoveus Top Global Emblems 2024

Custom Fighter Emblem

The Custom Fighter Emblem is the ideal choice for Phoveus, enhancing his durability, damage, and sustain in battles. Below are the recommended talents to optimize his performance:

Agility

Effect: Gain 4% extra Movement Speed.

Why? Agility helps Phoveus to engage or disengage more effectively, making it easier to chase down enemies or reposition in fights.

Festival of Blood

Effect: Gain 6% Spell Vamp. Each hero kill or assist grants an additional 0.5% Spell Vamp, up to 8 stacks.

Why? Festival of Blood significantly enhances Phoveus's sustain, allowing him to heal more from his abilities, especially during extended fights.

Brave Smite

Effect: Dealing skill damage to an enemy hero recovers 5% of Max HP. Cooldown: 6 seconds.

Why? Brave Smite provides additional healing, ensuring that Phoveus can maintain his presence in the fight and absorb more damage.

Phoveus Reworked Guide Conclusion

The rework of Phoveus in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang has turned him into an even more formidable hero against opponents who rely on high mobility. With abilities that now offer greater synergy and flexibility, Phoveus can effectively punish enemies that use dashes and blinks, controlling the battlefield with precision. Mastering his passive, Demonic Force, and strategically using his crowd control and damage-dealing skills will make you a dominant presence in team fights and skirmishes.

However, like any hero, Phoveus has his weaknesses. Identifying the heroes who can counter his abilities and adapting your strategy accordingly is crucial to ensuring your success on the battlefield. With the right build of items and emblems, and a clear understanding of his synergies and counters, you'll be well-equipped to take Phoveus to the top of the rankings. Whether you're facing agile enemies or defending your team, the reworked Phoveus offers a new range of opportunities for players looking to master the game. Good luck, and may the demonic force be with you!
