Odette Guide Introduction

Odette is one of the most powerful mages in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang (MLBB), revered for her exceptional ability to deal significant area-of-effect (AoE) damage and crowd control. Despite her strengths, mastering Odette can be a challenge, especially for newer players. This guide will explore her skills, passive abilities, builds, emblems, and gameplay tips to help you dominate the battlefield with her.

1. Overview of Odette's Strengths and Role

Odette is primarily a Mage hero known for her ability to burst down multiple enemies at once. Her damage potential, especially in team fights, is immense due to her AoE skills. If positioned correctly, Odette can single-handedly turn the tide of a game. However, she is highly vulnerable to crowd control (CC) and can be easily interrupted if not played carefully.

Odette excels in the following areas:

  • High AoE burst damage
  • Strong crowd control with her second skill
  • Good utility in team fights
  • Can quickly farm and deal with enemy waves

Her ultimate, Swan Song, is her most iconic skill, dealing massive damage in a large area. However, timing and positioning are crucial to avoid getting interrupted by enemies with CC abilities.

2. Odette’s Skills and Passive Explained

To play Odette effectively, you need a clear understanding of how her skills work and how to maximize her damage output. Let’s break down each of her abilities:

2.1. First Skill: Avian Authority

Odette’s first skill allows her to concentrate magical energy, summoning a swan that flies toward enemies. This skill deals 300 magic damage to enemies and slows them by 30% for 2 seconds.

Usage Tips:

  • Great for poking enemies in the laning phase.
  • Useful for clearing minion waves quickly.
  • Max this skill first, as it serves as your primary damage-dealing ability in the early game.

2.2. Second Skill: Blue Nova

Odette’s second skill fires out two energy balls in a straight line. Upon hitting an enemy, the target and nearby enemies are immobilized for 1.5 seconds, taking 250 magic damage. If enemies are clustered, multiple targets can be immobilized.

Usage Tips:

  • Essential for crowd control—use it to immobilize key targets during ganks or team fights.
  • Can also be used defensively to slow down pursuing enemies while you escape.

2.3. Passive: Lakeshore Ambience

Odette’s passive ability makes her basic attack or next skill bounce between enemies whenever she casts a skill. It deals 200 magic damage per bounce. If there’s only one enemy nearby, the attack will bounce between Odette and that enemy, greatly enhancing her burst potential.

Usage Tips:

  • Enhances your poke damage during the laning phase.
  • Provides additional AoE damage in team fights when multiple enemies are grouped together.

2.4. Ultimate: Swan Song

Odette’s ultimate skill is what makes her truly devastating in team fights. She channels for 5 seconds, dealing 2,400 magic damage over time to enemies within a large radius. While channeling, she gains a 1,400 shield, offering some protection from incoming damage. You can reposition slightly by tapping the skill again to perform a small dash.

Usage Tips:

  • Best used in the center of a team fight to maximize the damage.
  • Beware of heroes with CC, as they can interrupt and cancel your ultimate.
  • Refrain from using other skills or basic attacks while channeling, as this will also cancel the ultimate.

3. Combos for Optimal Damage

Odette's skill combo is essential for maximizing her damage potential in both duels and team fights. Here’s a typical combo sequence:

  • Start with Blue Nova (Second Skill): Immobilize enemies and set up your next attacks.
  • Follow up with Avian Authority (First Skill): Deal damage and slow down enemies to make them more vulnerable to your ultimate.
  • Activate Swan Song (Ultimate): Use this as your finisher to deal massive damage to immobilized and slowed enemies.

5. Best Emblems and Talents for Odette

The Mage Emblem is the most suitable for Odette, offering the right balance of magic power, cooldown reduction, and magic penetration.

Recommended Talents:

  • First Talent: Rapture or Thrill – Provides adaptive damage, allowing Odette to deal more damage.
  • Second Talent: Weapon Master – Increases the magic power that Odette gains from your items and emblems by 5%.
  • Core Talent: Lethal Ignition – Helps Odette finish off low-health enemies more efficiently.

6. Odette Gameplay Strategy by Phases

Odette’s gameplay strategy evolves as the game progresses. Here’s how to approach each phase:

6.1. Early Game

  • Skill Priorities: Max your first skill (Avian Authority) first to poke enemies and clear waves.
  • Focus on Farming: Stay safe and avoid unnecessary deaths. Dying early can significantly reduce your farm.
  • Rotate and Gank: Once you hit level 2, start rotating to help side lanes, but be cautious if your tank isn’t nearby.
  • Secure Objectives: Help your team secure the turtle for extra gold and shields.

6.2. Mid Game

  • Dealing More Damage: By mid-game, Odette’s damage spikes significantly. This is where you can start to shine in team fights.
  • Positioning: Always stay behind your team, as enemies will target you first.
  • Team Fights: If the enemy team has heavy CC, wait for them to use their abilities before flickering into the center to use Swan Song.

6.3. Late Game

  • Patience is Key: In late-game fights, your team’s success often depends on your ability to survive and deal damage.
  • Time Your Entry: Don’t rush into team fights. Wait for enemies to use their CC before committing with your ultimate.
  • Avoid Dying Early: Your survival is critical. If you die early, your team will likely lose the fight.

7. Odette Counters and Weaknesses

Odette is vulnerable to crowd control and burst heroes. Here are some of her main counters:

  • Saber: Saber can quickly attack and eliminate Odette with his burst damage.
  • Franco: Can hook and suppress her, preventing her from using her ultimate.
  • Chou: Can easily interrupt and knock her up during her ultimate.
  • Guinevere: Her knock-up can cancel Odette’s ultimate.
  • Nana: Nana’s second skill can silence Odette, interrupting her channel.

When playing against these heroes, positioning and timing are critical.

8. Conclusion and Final Thoughts

Odette is a high-damage mage with excellent team fight capabilities. However, she is also one of the more challenging mages to master due to her vulnerability to CC and reliance on proper timing. By mastering her combos, building the right items, and positioning yourself well during fights, you can maximize her potential and dominate the battlefield.
