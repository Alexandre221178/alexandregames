Strategy on How to Play with Tigreal in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang

Tigreal Gameplay Tips

As a tank, Tigreal excels at providing support and creating opportunities for your team. Here are some essential tips to dominate the battlefield:

  • Normal Tank Duties: Check bushes, support allies, and ensure you maintain map pressure.
  • Map Pressure: Let the enemy know you control the map and them. Always be a threat, giving them no space and constantly looking for opportunities to set up engagements.

Tigreal Tank Tips: How to Maintain Map Pressure

  • Assert Dominance: Show the enemy you are the alpha. Your presence should always be felt on the map.
  • Be a Constant Threat: Don't give the enemy team any breathing room. Always look for chances to engage and disrupt.
  • Prioritize Allies' Safety and Gold Income: Ensure your allies get the kills and gold they need. Don't steal kills or clear waves unnecessarily. If an ally is about to spawn, freeze the lane. If they are 15 seconds from respawning, clear the lane to keep the tower healthy.
  • Selflessness: Be willing to sacrifice yourself for your carry or damage dealers. This helps secure a win, especially if your team is behind early or ensures your team can finish the game strong.
  • Aggressive Play: Don‚Äôt hesitate to dive towers if you think you can take out multiple enemies. Make sure your allies are ready to follow up.

Tigreal Specific Tips

  • 1st Skill/Dash: This is a crucial ability that can act as a secondary flash. Use it wisely to engage or escape.
  • Cooldown Management: Always have either your 1st skill or Flicker ready for setting up your ultimate.
  • Combo Play: Using Flash followed by your 1st skill can be very effective early on. It pushes the enemy away from their tower and provides a long stun (dash duration + airborne).

Tigreal Laning Phase

  • Clearing Mid: Use your 2nd skill to help clear the mid lane, allowing your mid laner to rotate more effectively.
  • Rotation: Rotate with your jungle or mid laner, ideally both. Don't wander aimlessly in lanes. Support a lane solo if needed, especially if the other lane is in dire need of assistance.
  • Map Control: Don‚Äôt get stuck in the gold lane. Remember, you control the entire map, not just one lane.

General Tips for Playing Tigreal

  • Tank Role: As a tank, your primary role is to absorb damage for your team and provide crowd control. Position yourself between your team and the enemy to protect your allies.
  • Build: Focus on building items that enhance your tankiness, such as armor, magic resistance, and health items. Consider items that provide additional crowd control or utility to further disrupt enemies.
  • Communication: Coordinate with your team to ensure they can follow up on your crowd control abilities. Effective communication can turn the tide of battle in your favor.

By following these tips, you can become a formidable Tigreal player and lead your team to victory in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang.

Guide to Using Tigreal's Skills in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang

Tigreal is a powerful tank hero in Mobile Legends Bang Bang, renowned for his crowd control abilities and resilience. This guide provides an analytical look at his skills and offers tips on how to maximize their effectiveness in battle.

Fearless (Passive)

Description: Tigreal gains a stack of Fearless each time he uses a skill or is hit by a Basic Attack. After accumulating 4 stacks, he consumes all Fearless stacks to block the next incoming Basic Attack, including turret attacks. Note that attacks from minions do not contribute to Fearless stacks nor trigger its effect.

  • Skill Timing: Utilize your skills strategically to build up Fearless stacks before engaging in combat. This will ensure you have an extra layer of protection when facing enemy heroes.
  • Engagement Strategy: Initiate fights with a full stack of Fearless to mitigate initial burst damage from enemies.
  • Turret Diving: Fearless can be particularly useful when diving under enemy turrets, as it allows you to block turret shots that can be decisive in securing kills.

Attack Wave (First Skill)

Description: Tigreal smashes the ground with his hammer, sending a shockwave that erupts 3 times in the target direction. Each eruption deals Physical Damage and slows enemies by 20%/40%/60% for 1.5 seconds.

  • Crowd Control: Use Attack Wave to slow multiple enemies, making it easier for your team to engage or disengage from fights.
  • Damage Output: This skill can deal significant damage, especially in the early game, making it a good tool for both harassing enemies and clearing minion waves.
  • Combo Potential: Combine Attack Wave with Sacred Hammer or Implosion to maximize crowd control and damage output.

Sacred Hammer (Second Skill)

Description: Tigreal charges in the target direction, dealing Physical Damage to enemies along the way and pushing them to the end of the charge. He can use this skill again within 4 seconds to deal additional damage and knock enemies airborne.

  • Initiation: Use Sacred Hammer to initiate fights by charging into enemy formations, disrupting their positioning and setting them up for follow-up attacks.
  • Combo Execution: Follow up with the second activation of Sacred Hammer to knock enemies airborne, creating opportunities for your team to capitalize on the crowd control.
  • Escape Tool: Sacred Hammer can also be used defensively to escape from dangerous situations by charging away from enemies.

Implosion (Ultimate)

Description: Tigreal unleashes the power of his hammer, pulling nearby enemies to him and stunning them for 1.8 seconds while dealing Physical Damage. The first half of the channeling can be interrupted by control effects, while the second half can only be interrupted by Suppression.

  • Team Fights: Implosion is best used in team fights to disrupt multiple enemies and set them up for follow-up attacks from your team.
  • Positioning: Ensure you are in the midst of the enemy team before using Implosion to maximize the number of enemies affected.
  • Timing: Be mindful of enemy control abilities that can interrupt the channeling of Implosion. Timing this skill correctly can be the difference between a successful initiation and a failed attempt.

By mastering Tigreal's skills and understanding when and how to use them, you can become a formidable force on the battlefield, leading your team to victory with your powerful crowd control and resilience.

Combo 1: Basic Engagement Combo

Skills Used: Sacred Hammer (First Charge) + Sacred Hammer (Second Charge) + Attack Wave

Execution:

Tips:

  • Time the second charge of Sacred Hammer precisely to maximize the airborne duration and follow up with Attack Wave for additional damage and control.

Tips:

  • Communicate with your team before initiating to ensure they are ready to follow up on your crowd control.

Combo 3: Flicker Surprise Combo

Skills Used: Attack Wave + Flicker + Implosion
