Strategy on how to play with Angela

Angela shines when paired with a marksman in the gold lane, giving them good durability while increasing damage against enemies to help eliminate them. Her Wave of Love ability heals allies and her puppet line slows the movement of enemy targets.

Angela's ultimate ability could be a life-saving ability with her powerful shield. Stay aware of what's happening on the map at all times, ready to save allies if necessary.

Know who is performing well. If you use your shield and join an ally in battle, and they die soon because they are in a bad position or underperforming, you will probably die with them!

There is no point in trying to save someone who is already dying, as Angela will die with them.

Before using Angela's ultimate, position yourself in the bushes or under the towers!

Angela Best Spells

The first battle spell recommended for Angela is Sprint. Use it to escape confrontations after the Ultimate use time ends.

The Flameshot is ideal for Angela to fend off enemies in battle or to finish off an enemy that tries to escape with little health.

The Flicker is ideal for Angela to escape from enemies with control abilities, as well as help to escape through walls or escape when the Ultimate timer runs out.

Angela Best Builds 2023

Choose the boot depending on the enemy team; Tough Boots, Magic Shoes, and Demon Shoes are the most recommended for Angela.

Angela Skills

Smart Heart - Passive

Angela gains 15% extra Movement Speed for 4s each time she casts a skill (up to 30%). When she's attached to an allied hero, the Movement Speed boost is transferred to the allied hero.

1st Love Waves

Angela sends Love Waves in the target direction, dealing 170 (+80% Total Magic Power) Magic Damage to enemies hit, revealing their positions briefly, and applying a stack of Lover's Mark (up to 5 stacks). Each stack of Lover's Mark increases the damage by 20% and slows enemies hit by 8% for 3s.
Love Waves also restore 150 (+60%Total Magic Power) HP to allied heroes hit. Angela can store up to 5 charges of this skill and the recharge time is affected by Cooldown Reduction.

2nd Puppet-on-a-String

Angela launches a Puppet String at the target enemy, dealing 300 (+40% Total Magic Power) Magic Damage and gradually slowing them by up to 80%. If the enemy is still connected to the string after 3s, they'll be immobilized for 1.5s and dealt 450 (+60% Total Magic Power) Magic Damage.
Each stack of Lover's Mark increases the final damage by 20%.

3rd Heartguard

Angela begins channeling, applying a 1200 (+200% Total Magic Power) shield that lasts 6s to the target allied hero regardless of distance. After the channeling is complete, she attaches herself to the allied hero for 12s, during which she can cast skills at zero Mana cost.
Angela can cast this skil again to cancel the channeling or detach herself from the allied hero. The attaching state will end early if the allied hero is killed.
