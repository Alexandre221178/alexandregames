Strategy on how to play with Miya

Miya is a weak hero in the early game, and it is more important to build your items to strengthen yourself in battle. First, focus on building your builds in the early stages of matches, earning gold and leveling, and avoid team fights in the beginning.

Before going into battle, try to get a full pack of Moon's Blessing stacks for attack speed and physical damage. Skill 1 Moon Arrow is very useful for quickly clearing lanes or even taking down towers and helping allies with turtles and lords.

Arrow Eclipse skill 2 is good for slowing down enemies and dealing damage, and great for starting fights, so be sure to use it wisely.

Her ability 3 Hidden Moonlight is great for both starting a fight and escaping, as it increases movement speed by 35% and removes all of Miya's negative effects, as she is able to camouflage herself and become invisible.

At the end of the match, if your Miya is already strong, try to stay behind your team or allied tank, keep a safe distance and deal a lot of damage to enemies.

Miya Best Spells

The best battle spell for Miya is Inspire, to increase attack speed in combos and deal more damage to enemies. Additionally, it is possible to use the Flicker spell to evade enemies or control damage, or Reflect when fighting on the same route with snipers like Melissa, who deal a lot of damage with basic attacks.

Miya Skills in Mobile Legends

Moon Blessing - Passive

Each time Miya hits a target with her Basic Attack, she gains 5% Attack Speed for 4s. Stacks up to 5 times.
After reaching full stacks, Miya summons a Moonlight Shadow with each Basic Attack that deals 30 (+30% Total Physical Attack) Physical Damage and inherits a portion of Attack Effects.

1st Moon Arrow

Miya shoots two extra arrows with each Basic Attack, dealing 10 (+ 100% Total Physical Attack).
Physical Damage to the target enemy and 30% damage to nearby targets. This effect lasts 4s. Each extra arrow inherits a portion of Attack Effects.

2nd Arrow of Eclipse

Miya launches an empowered arrow on the target area, dealing 90 (+45% Total Physical Attack).
Physical Damage to enemies within and immobilizing them for 1.2s. The arrow then splits into 8 scattering minor arrows, each dealing 30 (+15% Total Physical Attack) Physical Damage to the first enemy hit and slowing them by 30% for 2s.

3rd Hidden Moonlight

Miya removes all debuffs on her and conceals herself, gaining 35% extra Movement Speed. This state lasts 2s or until she launches an attack.
Miya gains full stacks of Moon Blessing upon leaving the state.
