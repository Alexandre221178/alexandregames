How to Play Cici in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang

Cici is a highly mobile hero with strong kiting abilities, allowing her to outmaneuver and whittle down enemies while maintaining her stacks of Delight for enhanced movement speed and spell vamp. Here's a comprehensive guide to mastering Cici in MLBB:

General Playstyle

  • Kiting with Yo-Yo Blitz: Cici's first skill, Yo-Yo Blitz, is the core of her playstyle. Since she can move and use other abilities during this skill, it enables her to continuously kite enemies‚Äîhitting them while staying just out of their reach.   • Maintaining Delight Stacks: Cici‚Äôs passive, Performer‚Äôs Delight, grants her movement speed and spell vamp as she builds up stacks. This allows you to keep the movement speed boost and spell vamp active, making you harder to kill and more effective in sustaining damage.

Tactics

Early Game (Level 1-7)

  • Focus on Kiting and Poking: In the early game, your goal is to harass and kite your opponents using Yo-Yo Blitz. Pair this with Buoyant Bounce (Cici's second skill) to reposition yourself and dodge enemy abilities. Use your passive's movement speed boost to stay just outside your enemy‚Äôs reach, making it difficult for them to engage you effectively.
  • Lane Management: During the laning phase, you should focus on farming and poking your opponent. Use Yo-Yo Blitz to chip away at their health while staying safe. If they try to retaliate, use Buoyant Bounce to quickly reposition and escape. This will keep you in control of your lane, allowing you to farm effectively while keeping your opponent pressured.

Mid to Late Game (Level 8+)

  • Enhanced Kiting and Split Pushing: By mid to late game, after you‚Äôve completed most of Cici‚Äôs build, the cooldown on Yo-Yo Blitz will be significantly reduced, allowing you to use it frequently. This makes Cici a powerful split pusher, as she can clear waves quickly and move between lanes efficiently.
  • Joining Team Fights: As a split pusher, Cici excels at quickly joining team fights after pushing a lane. Her high mobility allows her to engage weakened enemies, picking them off with her abilities. When joining a fight, use Curtain Call (Cici‚Äôs ultimate) to target high-priority enemies, amplifying the damage they take and slowing them down, making it easier for your team to finish them off.
  • Finishing Off Enemies: In team fights, Cici‚Äôs role is to target squishy or low-health opponents. Use Yo-Yo Blitz and Curtain Call in combination to maximize your damage output, and use Buoyant Bounce to either chase down fleeing enemies or escape if the fight turns against you.

Tips and Tricks

  • Positioning is Key: Always keep your distance from the enemy. Use Buoyant Bounce to adjust your positioning constantly, making it difficult for enemies to land skill shots or close in on you.
  • Timing Your Ultimate: Curtain Call should be used when you can maximize its damage amplification, either to burst down a high-value target or to help your team secure a kill. Don't waste it on tanks unless necessary to secure a kill.
  • Farm Efficiently: Use Cici‚Äôs mobility to move between lanes and jungle camps quickly. This will allow you to keep up in gold and experience, ensuring you reach your late-game power spikes faster.
  • Map Awareness: Keep an eye on the minimap and be ready to join fights quickly. Cici's ability to move rapidly across the map means you can often turn the tide of a battle by arriving just in time to pick off weakened enemies.

Cici Combo Strategy in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang

Cici‚Äôs combos are essential to maximizing her damage output and controlling the battlefield. Below are two basic combos for different phases of the game, designed to help you master her playstyle.

Combo 1: Laning Phase

Combo Sequence: BA (Basic Attack) -> Skill 1 (Yo-Yo Blitz) -> Skill 2 (Buoyant Bounce)

How to Use It:

  • BA (Basic Attack): Start with a basic attack to build up a stack of Delight from Cici's passive. This will increase your movement speed slightly and set up the following abilities.
  • Skill 1 (Yo-Yo Blitz): Immediately follow up with Yo-Yo Blitz to lock onto the closest enemy within range. This ability will deal consistent damage over time while you remain mobile.
  • Skill 2 (Buoyant Bounce): Use Buoyant Bounce to either close the gap if your target is retreating or to reposition yourself defensively if they attempt to counter-attack. This leap will allow you to dodge incoming damage while applying more pressure on your opponent.

When to Use It:

This combo is ideal during the laning phase when you're poking and harassing your lane opponent. It allows you to deal damage while staying out of reach, making it difficult for the enemy to retaliate effectively.

Combo 2: Team Fights or Ganks

Combo Sequence: Skill 3 (Curtain Call) -> Skill 2 (Buoyant Bounce) -> Skill 1 (Yo-Yo Blitz) -> BA (Basic Attack)

How to Use It:

  • Skill 3 (Curtain Call): Start the combo by targeting a high-priority enemy with Curtain Call. This will slow them down and increase the damage they take by 50%, setting them up for a powerful follow-up.
  • Skill 2 (Buoyant Bounce): Quickly leap toward your target using Buoyant Bounce. This serves to close the distance and apply additional damage, making it harder for them to escape.
  • Skill 1 (Yo-Yo Blitz): Once in range, activate Yo-Yo Blitz to continuously deal damage to the target. The combination of Curtain Call‚Äôs damage amplification and Yo-Yo Blitz‚Äôs persistent hits will quickly wear down even the toughest opponents.
  • BA (Basic Attack): Finish the combo with a basic attack to maintain your Delight stacks and keep the spell vamp and movement speed bonuses active. If the target is still alive, continue to kite them using Yo-Yo Blitz while staying mobile.

When to Use It:

This combo is best used in team fights or when ganking a vulnerable enemy. The initial burst from Curtain Call, followed by the sustained damage from Yo-Yo Blitz, makes it extremely difficult for your target to survive. Use Buoyant Bounce to maintain positioning, either to chase down the target or escape if the fight turns against you.
