Introduction to Ling

Ling is an exceptional assassin known for his unique mobility and ability to traverse walls, making him a formidable force in the game. However, mastering Ling requires a thorough understanding of his skills and mechanics.

Why Ling?

  • High Mobility: Ling can jump between walls, allowing for quick escapes and surprise attacks.
  • Strong Burst Damage: Ling can quickly take down squishy targets, making him a threat to enemy backlines.
  • Invulnerability: His ultimate skill provides invincibility and crowd control, turning the tide in team fights.

Ling's Skills and Passive

Passive: Cloud Walker

Description:

  • Ling's Lightness Skill allows him to leap among walls, gaining 4 Lightness Points per second on a wall and 5 points when dealing damage.
  • Ling gains 1.6 times Crit Chance from all sources but has a 150% Crit Damage cap.

Analysis:

Ling's passive is vital for energy management and enhancing his critical strike capability. His Lightness Points (energy) regenerate faster on walls and through attacks. The increased Crit Chance makes crit-based items highly effective on him.

Skill 1: Finch Poise

Type: Mobility, Buff
Cooldown: 20s
Energy Cost: 30

Passive Effect: Ling's Crit Chance is increased by 2.5%.

Active Effect: Ling leaps onto a designated wall, entering a half-stealth state, restoring Lightness Points faster, and gaining 30% Movement Speed. If damaged or controlled, he exits stealth and falls, slowed by 30% for 2 seconds.

Analysis:

Ling's first skill is his primary mobility tool, allowing him to jump between walls and remain elusive. The half-stealth state provides a tactical advantage, making it harder for enemies to target him. Always be aware of enemy crowd control abilities that can force you off walls.

Skill 2: Defiant Sword

Type: Damage, Mobility
Cooldown: 2.5s
Energy Cost: 35

Effect: Ling dashes in the target direction, stabbing nearby enemies for 300 (+50% Total Physical Attack) Physical Damage. When used from a wall, it costs no energy, slows enemies by 30% for 1.5 seconds, and deals the same damage.

Analysis:

Defiant Sword is Ling's main source of damage and mobility. It can be used frequently due to its low cooldown, making it essential for both engaging and escaping. Its dual functionality (ground and wall) offers flexibility in combat scenarios.

Ultimate: Tempest of Blades

Type: Burst, CC
Cooldown: 52s

Effect: Ling leaps into the air, becoming invincible and gaining 10% extra Movement Speed for 1.5 seconds. Upon landing, he deals 250 (+100% Total Physical Attack) Physical Damage, knocks up enemies in the center for 1 second, and creates a Sword Field for 8 seconds. Touching the swords reduces Finch Poise's cooldown by 4s, resets Defiant Sword's cooldown, and grants 25 Lightness Points.

Analysis:

Ling's ultimate is a game-changer in team fights. It provides invulnerability, crowd control, and resets his mobility skills. Mastering the placement and timing of his ultimate can turn the tide of battles, allowing for devastating combos and swift eliminations.

Ling's Combos and Techniques

Basic Combos

First Combo/Turret Dive:
Skill 2 (Defiant Sword) + Basic Attack + Ultimate (Tempest of Blades) + Skill 1 (Finch Poise) to escape.
Use this combo to quickly dive under turrets and secure kills.

Second Combo:
Skill 2 (Defiant Sword) + Basic Attack + Skill 1 (Finch Poise) + Basic Attack + Skill 2 (Defiant Sword) + Ultimate (Tempest of Blades).
This combo maximizes damage output and mobility, ideal for engaging and disengaging from fights.

Tips for Using Ling Combos

  • Wall Usage: Always position yourself on walls to maximize energy regeneration and mobility.
  • Energy Management: Keep an eye on your Lightness Points. Efficient use of Finch Poise and Defiant Sword ensures you always have energy to escape or chase.
  • Ultimate Timing: Use Tempest of Blades to initiate team fights or escape when in danger. The invincibility frame is crucial for dodging deadly abilities.
  • Vision Control: Use Finch Poise to scout bushes and avoid ambushes. Ling's stealth on walls can provide valuable information on enemy movements.

Best Builds for Ling in MLBB

Recommended Build

  1. Tough Boots

    Stats: +40 Movement Speed, +18 Magic Defense.
    Passive: Reduces CC duration by 30%.

    Analysis: These boots provide essential survivability for Ling, reducing the duration of crowd control effects. This is crucial for maintaining mobility and escaping tricky situations. The added magic defense helps against mage enemies, and the movement speed boost enhances his already high mobility.

  2. Berserker's Fury

    Stats: +65 Physical Attack, +25% Crit Chance.
    Passive: Critical strikes grant 5% extra Physical Attack for 2 seconds.

    Analysis: This item significantly boosts Ling's damage output by increasing his critical chance and providing additional physical attack. The passive effect further amplifies his damage, making his critical strikes even more devastating. This item is a core part of Ling's burst damage build.

  3. Haas' Claws

    Stats: +30 Physical Attack, +15% Attack Speed, +20% Crit Chance.
    Passive: Critical strikes grant 20% Attack Speed for 2 seconds.

    Analysis: Haas' Claws provide lifesteal and enhance Ling's survivability in prolonged fights. The additional attack speed from critical strikes makes Ling's attacks faster and more frequent, allowing for sustained damage output and better lifesteal efficiency.

  4. Endless Battle

    Stats: +60 Physical Attack, +250 HP, +10% Cooldown Reduction, +8% Hybrid Lifesteal, +5% Movement Speed, +5 Mana Regen.
    Passive: After casting a skill, the next Basic Attack deals extra True Damage.

    Analysis: Endless Battle enhances Ling's overall performance by providing a mix of attack, survivability, and utility stats. The cooldown reduction allows Ling to use his skills more frequently, while the passive adds significant burst damage to his attacks. The hybrid lifesteal also ensures he can sustain himself during fights.

  5. Blade of Despair

    Stats: +160 Physical Attack, +5% Movement Speed.
    Passive: Increases Physical Attack by 25% for 2 seconds when damaging enemies below 50% HP.

    Analysis: Blade of Despair is a key item for maximizing Ling's burst damage. The high physical attack boost significantly increases his damage output, and the passive effect allows Ling to finish off low-health enemies quickly. The movement speed bonus also complements his high mobility playstyle.

  6. Malefic Roar

    Stats: +60 Physical Attack.
    Passive: Gains extra Physical Penetration based on enemy's Physical Defense.

    Analysis: Malefic Roar provides Ling with the physical penetration needed to deal with tankier enemies. This item ensures that his attacks penetrate through enemy defenses, making his damage more effective against high-defense targets. It's especially useful in the late game when enemies build more defensive items.

Playing Ling in Different Phases

Early Game

  • Focus on Farming: Secure jungle camps and reach level four as quickly as possible.
  • Blue Buff Control: Ensure you get the blue buff for energy regeneration.
  • First Gank: Target the gold lane for early kills and pressure.

Mid Game

  • Target Priority: Aim for enemy Mages and Marksmen to weaken their damage output.
  • Objective Control: Secure the turtle and maintain control of the blue buff.
  • Roaming: Use your mobility to assist lanes and secure kills.

Late Game

  • Team Fights: Position yourself well and wait for enemy stuns to be used before engaging.
  • Lord Control: Help your team secure the Lord for a game-winning push.
  • Split Pushing: Use your mobility to apply pressure on multiple lanes.

Countering Ling

Hero Counters

  • Kufra: His crowd control can knock Ling off walls and disrupt his mobility.
  • Ruby: Her control abilities make it hard for Ling to stay on walls and engage effectively.
  • Eudora: Burst damage and stuns from Eudora can eliminate Ling quickly.

Item Counters

  • Wind of Nature: Provides immunity to Physical Damage for a short duration.
  • Immortality: Grants a second chance at life, giving you time to counter-attack or escape.

Ling Guide Conclusion

Ling is a powerful assassin with unique mobility and burst damage potential. Mastering Ling requires understanding his skills, managing his energy, and timing his abilities perfectly. By following this guide, you'll be well on your way to becoming a top-tier Ling player in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang. Remember, practice makes perfect, so keep honing your skills and you'll dominate the battlefield in no time.
