Detailed Analysis of Nana's Abilities in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang

Molina's Gift (Passive)

Type: Passive

Effect:

  • Upon taking fatal damage, Nana removes all debuffs and transforms, becoming untargetable and invincible for 2 seconds.
  • Gains 30% movement speed for 2 seconds.
  • Recovers 10% of her max HP during the transformation.
  • Cooldown: 150 seconds.
  • Limitations: Nana cannot cast skills in the transformed form.

Analysis: This passive significantly enhances Nana‚Äôs survivability. By removing debuffs and becoming invincible, she can escape from dangerous situations and avoid death, making her an excellent choice for prolonged engagements. The movement speed boost and HP recovery further aid in her survival and repositioning.

Tip: When Molina's Gift is active, you will see a circle around Nana's feet. Keep an eye on this indicator to know when her passive is ready, allowing you to play more aggressively or take calculated risks.

Magic Boomerang (First Skill)

Type: AOE

Cooldown: 5.5 seconds

Mana Cost: 50

Spell Vamp Ratio: 50%

Effect:

  • Nana hurls her Magic Boomerang in the target direction, dealing damage to enemies on its way out and back.
  • Deals 300 (+90% Total Magic Power) magic damage to the first enemy hit.
  • Damage is reduced by 10% for each additional enemy hit, up to a maximum reduction of 30%.

Analysis: This skill is excellent for poking and dealing sustained AOE damage. The high magic power scaling makes it a strong source of damage, especially against the first enemy hit. The spell vamp ratio helps in sustaining Nana during fights.

Molina Smooch (Second Skill)

Type: CC, Debuff

Cooldown: 14.5 seconds

Mana Cost: 80

Effect:

  • Nana throws Molina at the target location, dealing 250 (+50% Total Magic Power) magic damage to enemies within range and transforming enemy heroes hit into small animals.
  • If Molina does not hit an enemy hero, she remains on the ground and chases the nearest enemy hero, transforming the first hero she touches.
  • Transform Effect: Reduces the target‚Äôs movement speed by 50% and magic defense by 30% for 1.5 seconds. Transformed heroes cannot attack or use skills.

Analysis: This skill provides strong crowd control and disruption. The transformation effect is particularly useful for neutralizing high-priority targets and disrupting enemy formations. The reduction in movement speed and magic defense also makes enemies more vulnerable to follow-up attacks.

Molina Blitz (Ultimate)

Type: Burst

Cooldown: 36 seconds

Mana Cost: 135

Spell Vamp Ratio: 50%

Effect:

  • Nana summons Molina to strike the target area three times.
  • Each strike deals 440 (+160% Total Magic Power) magic damage to enemies within and slows them by 30% for 1 second.
  • Enemies hit consecutively will be stunned for 1 second.

Analysis: Molina Blitz is Nana's most potent damage-dealing ability. The high magic power scaling ensures substantial burst damage, while the slow and stun effects provide excellent crowd control. This ultimate can be used to initiate fights, disrupt enemy backlines, or secure kills on fleeing enemies.

Strategic Usage Tips

  • Positioning: Nana's abilities have relatively short ranges, so good positioning is crucial to maximize her effectiveness while staying safe.
  • Combos: Use Molina Smooch to transform and debuff high-priority targets before unleashing Magic Boomerang and Molina Blitz for maximum damage.
  • Cooldown Management: Pay attention to the cooldown of Molina's Gift, as it significantly impacts Nana's survivability in extended fights.
  • Team Play: Nana excels in team fights with her crowd control and burst damage. Coordinate with your team to make the most out of her abilities.

By understanding and utilizing Nana's abilities effectively, players can maximize her potential as a powerful support/mage in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang.

Pros and Cons of Nana in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang

Pros:

  • High Survivability:
    • Molina's Gift (Passive): Provides invincibility, untargetability, and HP recovery, making Nana very hard to kill.
  • Strong Crowd Control:
    • Molina Smooch (Second Skill): Transforms enemies into small animals, reducing their movement speed and magic defense, and preventing them from attacking or using skills.
    • Molina Blitz (Ultimate): Slows and stuns enemies, providing significant crowd control in team fights.
  • Effective Poking and AOE Damage:
    • Magic Boomerang (First Skill): Deals high AOE magic damage with good scaling, useful for poking and wave clearing.
  • Utility and Support:
    • Debuff Removal: Molina's Gift removes all debuffs when triggered, which can save Nana from dangerous situations.
    • Transformation Mechanic: Transforms key enemy heroes, disrupting their abilities and reducing their effectiveness in fights.

Cons:

  • Skill Reliance: Nana heavily relies on her skills for damage and utility, making her vulnerable when abilities are on cooldown.
  • Squishy Nature: Despite her passive, Nana remains a relatively squishy hero with low defense and HP, making her susceptible to burst damage.
  • High Cooldowns: Molina Blitz (Ultimate) has a long cooldown, limiting the frequency of its use in fights.
  • Limited Mobility: Lacks natural escape abilities apart from the passive‚Äôs temporary invincibility and movement speed boost.
  • Skill Shots: Abilities like Magic Boomerang and Molina Smooch require precise aiming, which can be difficult to land consistently in high-pressure situations.

Strategy on How to Play with Nana in Mobile Legends

Early Game

  • Poking with Second Skill: Use Nana's second skill, Molina Smooch, to constantly poke enemies. This skill can be used to harass enemy heroes and disrupt their farming.
  • Bush Checking: Use Molina Smooch to check bushes for enemies. This helps avoid ambushes and provides vision for your team.
  • Jungler Support: Assist your jungler in contesting the turtle. Nana can easily stun the enemy jungler during a retribution battle, providing a significant advantage.
  • Crowd Control: If enemies group up, Nana's second skill can stun multiple enemies, making it easier to control team fights.

Mid Game

  • Stay with Your Team: Always stay with your team during the mid game. Nana excels in team fights due to her crowd control and damage abilities.
  • Team Fights: Use your ultimate, Molina Blitz, strategically in team fights to stun multiple enemies. This can turn the tide of battle in your favor.
  • Passive Management: Check your passive, Molina's Gift, before turret dives. If your passive is not available, avoid playing too aggressively.

Late Game

  • High Impact: In the late game, Nana's abilities can impact almost every enemy hero. This is where she shines the most.
  • Practice Makes Perfect: If you're new to Nana, practice using her skills and combos before jumping into ranked games. Unlike other heroes, Nana is relatively easy to use, making her a great choice for beginners.

Tips

  • Poking: Consistently poke enemies with Molina Smooch to keep them on their toes.
  • Vision: Use Molina Smooch to maintain vision in key areas, preventing enemy ambushes.
  • Ultimate Timing: Place your ultimate in the center of team fights to maximize its stun potential.
  • Positioning: Stay behind your team's frontline to avoid being targeted by enemies.
  • Cooldown Awareness: Keep track of your skill cooldowns to ensure you can execute your combos effectively.

Best Allies for Nana in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang

  • Atlas

    Synergy: Atlas's ultimate, Fatal Links, pulls enemies together, creating the perfect opportunity for Nana to use her Molina Blitz and maximize her area damage.

  • Minotaur

    Synergy: Minotaur's ultimate, Minoan Fury, gathers and stuns enemies in an area, allowing Nana to capitalize on the grouped enemies with her Molina Blitz for maximum impact.

  • Tigreal

    Synergy: Tigreal's ultimate, Implosion, pulls enemies into a central location, setting them up perfectly for Nana's Molina Blitz to deal massive area damage.

These allies enhance Nana's effectiveness by grouping enemies together, allowing her to unleash her ultimate for devastating results.

Nana Counters in Mobile Legends

In Mobile Legends: Bang Bang, Nana can be countered effectively by heroes who can either bypass her crowd control abilities, burst her down quickly, or disrupt her gameplay. Here are the five best counters for Nana:

  1. Chou

    Abilities: Chou's skill set allows him to easily catch and burst down squishy heroes like Nana. His second skill, Shunpo, grants him crowd control immunity, allowing him to avoid Nana's Molina Smooch transformation. Additionally, his ultimate, The Way of Dragon, can isolate and eliminate Nana swiftly.

  2. Helcurt

    Abilities: Helcurt can silence Nana with his passive, Race Advantage, and prevent her from using her skills. His ultimate, Dark Night Falls, blinds the entire enemy team, making it difficult for Nana to position herself safely. Helcurt's burst damage can quickly take down Nana before she can activate her passive.

  3. Hayabusa

    Abilities: Hayabusa's high mobility and burst damage make him an excellent counter to Nana. His skill, Ougi: Shadow Kill, allows him to quickly jump on Nana and burst her down, preventing her from utilizing her crowd control abilities effectively.

  4. Franco

    Abilities: Franco's hook (Iron Hook) can pull Nana out of position, making her vulnerable to follow-up attacks. His ultimate, Bloody Hunt, suppresses the target, rendering Nana unable to use her skills or escape with her passive.

  5. Ling

    Abilities: Ling's high mobility and ability to traverse walls with his Finch Poise make it difficult for Nana to land her crowd control skills on him. Ling can quickly dive onto Nana and eliminate her with his high burst damage before she can react.

Additional Tips:

  • Timing: Pay attention to the activation of Nana's passive, Molina's Gift. Avoid wasting your burst abilities when her passive is active, as it grants her invincibility and untargetability.
  • Positioning: Heroes with long-range abilities or high mobility can take advantage of Nana's relatively squishy nature and low mobility.
  • Coordination: Coordinate with your team to focus Nana down in team fights, ensuring she cannot use her crowd control abilities effectively.
