Strategy on how to play with Change

Change the Trouble Maker skill, a passive ability marks enemies by increasing the damage to the next ability from 2% to 40% damage.

The first Star Moon Shockwave skill launches a ball in the selected direction, dealing magic damage and slowing enemies by 20% for 1.5 seconds. The idea is to use the first skill after the second Crescent Moon skill, as it gives a buff to the skill, increasing it to 4 balls, and the slowness can be accumulated and increased by up to 40%.


Crescent Moon Ability and Marks of Change

Change's Ultimate Meteor Shower increases movement speed by 10% and fires 30 meteors in a straight direction for 4 seconds, dealing a lot of magic damage to the first enemy hit. If you use this skill with the 2nd Crescent Moon skill, each meteor deals 33% magic damage.

Change's Ultimate deals 100% additional damage to minions and monsters and is very useful to help your team on Turtle and Lord. The Crescent Moon ability should always be used before activating the Ultimate to cause more damage to enemies.


Changes' Ultimate Meteor Shower

The 2nd ability of Change Crescent Moon is great, as it gives a shield of 300% of magical power for defense and increases movement speed by 10%, and an additional 50% speed if you use a skill while Crescent Moon is active. This ability also increases Change's base damage, while also reducing the cooldown of this ability.
