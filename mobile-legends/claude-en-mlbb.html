Gameplay Strategy

Early Game

Claude is relatively weak in the early game and highly reliant on Demon Hunter Sword to deal substantial damage. Focus on farming safely and avoid unnecessary fights until you secure your first core item. If possible, coordinate with your support or tank to avoid getting bullied by stronger early-game heroes.

Objective: Prioritize farming and avoid overcommitting to fights. Once you reach level 4 and unlock your ultimate, you can start engaging more confidently.

Mid Game

By the mid-game, Claude becomes significantly stronger, especially with two or three core items. Rotate with your team, focusing on securing kills, pushing turrets, and joining team fights. Always position yourself behind your teammates and use Blazing Duet to deal massive AoE damage during fights.

Objective: Farm and rotate to join team fights, but donâ€™t overstay. Claudeâ€™s survivability depends on positioning, so be mindful of enemy assassins and fighters.

Late Game

In the late game, Claude is a high-priority target for enemies due to his ability to melt entire teams with his ultimate. Always stay near your team, and be patient with your engagements. Position yourself carefully, and wait for the right moment to unleash Blazing Duet. Keep an eye on the mini-map to avoid split pushes or unexpected ganks.

Objective: Be present in all team fights, stay at a safe distance, and maximize your damage output with perfect timing and positioning.
