Strategy on how to play with Layla

Layla is a very slow hero in terms of mobility, but with a lot of damage from the beginning of the game due to her skills. She also adapts extremely well towards the end of the game, being able to carry the team. Don't be afraid to participate in team fights or help your teammates. Layla can be a great hero when played correctly!

Layla's Ultimate damage range can hit other heroes from a distance, make use of it! Position yourself behind your team, stay in a safe position, use the map to aim with your Ultimate, and attack enemies with low health before they escape. Don't get too far ahead, and don't advance alone across the map. Layla moves slowly!

At the beginning of the game, Layla should stay as close to the tower as possible, farm without rushing, and only move forward if there is support from allies. Use the second skill to slow enemies, and if they try to invade your tower, use Combo 3 to protect yourself.

Adjust your build items according to your play style and match! Pay attention to the enemy's buildings.

Layla Skills in Mobile Legends

Malefic - Passive

Layla deals increased damage to enemies farther away from her (starting at 100% and increasing to 130% at 6 units away). This does not include Turrets.

1st Malefic Bomb

Layla fires a Malefic Bomb in the target direction, dealing 200 (+80% Total Physical Attack) Physical Damage to the first enemy hit (can critically strike).
Upon hitting an enemy, Layla's Basic Attacks and Void Projectile gain extra range for 3s, and she gains 60% extra Movement Speed that decays over 1.2s. The duration of the Movement Speed boost is doubled if an enemy hero is hit.

2nd Void Projectile

Layla fires an orb of Malefic Energy at the target enemy that explodes on hit, dealing 170 (+65% Total Physical Attack) Physical Damage to targets in the area and applying a Magic Mark on them for 3s.
When Layla hits an enemy with a Magic Mark, she deals 100 (+35% Total Physical Attack) Physical Damage to nearby enemies and stuns them for 0.25s.

3rd Destruction Rush

Layla fires a blast of Malefic Energy in the target direction, dealing 500 (+ 150% Total Physical Attack) Physical Damage to enemies in a line.
Passive: The range of Layla's Void Projectile and Basic Attacks is increased by 0.6 units. Her sight range is also slightly increased each time this skill is upgraded.
