7 Best Control Heroes in Hero Wars Alliance (2025)

In the magical and chaotic battlefield of Hero Wars Alliance, the Control class holds a special place. These heroes don’t just strike they manipulate, disable, and frustrate their enemies. Their presence can tilt the balance of power in any match.

Out of the 9 control heroes Andvari, Arachne, Faceless, Jorgen, Judge, Lian, Peppy, Phobos, and Polaris - we’ll uncover the 7 most impactful ones in 2025.

This guide focuses on the 7 best control heroes in 2025, based on their battlefield utility, synergy with meta teams 2025, and current relevance in the evolving Arena landscape.

Fallen from the Top

Peppy and Arachne are two characters who once stood tall but have now faded from the spotlight. Despite their potential, both have become too weak in the current meta. Peppy’s chaotic energy and Arachne’s stuns aren’t enough to compete with today’s dynamic lineup. We hope to see them return to glory someday, but for now, they remain outside the top seven.

7th Place:

Jorgen

Jorgen is a silent saboteur. He doesn’t scream power he whispers control. He steals energy from enemies and places a curse that prevents them from gaining more. In a world where ultimate abilities can end battles in seconds, Jorgen is the anti-ultimate guardian. While he sits at 7th due to the fierce competition, his skill set remains indispensable in many control-focused strategies.

6th Place:

Lian

Lian is elegance and power blended into one. With a flick of her wrist, she puts enemies into a magical sleep for 7 seconds, rendering them useless. Once a hard counter to Keira, Lian has become more of a niche pick due to Keira’s decline. Yet, even in today’s meta, she holds power. She doesn’t need to be over-leveled her charm works wonders even with modest investment. Perhaps that’s why she’s overlooked a sleeping giant among giants.

5th Place:

Judge

Judge is a guardian of Progress, both literally and figuratively. Outside Progress teams, his shine dulls. But build a squad around him, and he’ll reward your loyalty with stunning AOE power and protective shields. He’s a tactician’s dream conditional but deadly. His weakness lies in his short stun duration, but his synergy with heroes like Isaac and Jet make him a threat to underestimate.

4th Place:

Phobos

Phobos is terror in mage form. He locks onto the enemy with the highest magic power, stunning them for 6.6 seconds an eternity in Hero Wars combat. Then, he drains their energy and lowers their damage for 12 seconds. His haunting presence isn’t just psychological; it’s battle-shifting. Facing a magic-heavy enemy team without Phobos feels like going into battle without armor.

3rd Place:

Faceless

Faceless is the wildcard. His ability to copy ally or enemy ultimates gives him insane versatility. Need a second Astaroth shield? Copy it. Want to turn enemy power against them? Do it. But his greatest trick is lifting the frontline enemy and slamming them into their allies. Tanks lose positioning, squishies panic, and Faceless smiles from the backline. He is chaos in form a strategic weapon in skilled hands.

2nd Place:

Andvari

Andvari is a paradox: a warrior with the heart of a protector. He guards allies against displacement a key counter to K’arkh and other forced-movement threats. His ultimate slams the front enemies with a stone fist, stunning them for 3 seconds. He then applies a protective shield to the ally with the lowest armor, soaking physical and pure damage. Andvari is both sword and shield a defensive genius who defends with brutal offense.

1st Place:

Polaris

Polaris is the uncontested queen of control. Every single one of her abilities applies some form of crowd control, most notably stun. But it’s her passive, Aurora Borealis, that makes her shine like no other:

  • Increased Damage: +50
  • Increased Damage from Progress Heroes: +100
  • Radiance Effect: If Polaris or an ally stuns an already stunned enemy, that enemy takes increased magic damage for 8 seconds.

In battle, Polaris doesn’t just control. She inspires. Her mere presence amplifies your team’s potential, especially those from the Progress faction. She’s more than meta she’s mythical.

Final Thoughts

The Control class is the heartbeat of strategic play in Hero Wars Alliance. Each hero in this list offers something unique from energy denial to disruptive positioning, from protective shields to magical domination. Whether you’re building your Arena defense or prepping for guild war, these seven stand as the pillars of 2025’s most powerful crowd controllers.

Choose wisely, build creatively, and may your enemies never cast their ultimates again.
