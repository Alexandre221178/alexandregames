Exclusive Skills

Perhaps the most thrilling aspect of Relics is the introduction of Exclusive Skills. These skills dramatically enhance your gameplay and can be the deciding factor between victory and defeat. There are four milestones in Relic development:

  • Level 1: Unlocks a brand-new active skill unique to that hero.
  • Level 10: Enhances the ultimate ability, adding new effects and power. In Hero Wars, the ultimate is the skill a hero uses once their energy bar is fully charged.
  • Level 20: Upgrades another core skill (different depending on the hero it may enhance either the green or blue skill).
  • Level 30: Unlocks a powerful new passive skill.

Enhanced skills retain their original function but gain additional effects, making them stronger and more versatile. To highlight this, their icons also transform into golden versions. Keep in mind that new skills unlocked at levels 1 and 30 don‚Äôt automatically generate energy unless their description specifically states otherwise.

When a hero has all Relic skills unlocked, they receive one enhancement to their ultimate plus two entirely new abilities. This means a fully upgraded Relic hero can bring a total of six active/passive skills into battle, greatly expanding their tactical impact.

Upgrading Relics

Relics can be upgraded up to level 30. Each level boosts stats, improves abilities, and unlocks milestones. To level them up, you‚Äôll need Relic Shards, a valuable resource tied directly to hero progression.

Unlike ordinary upgrades, Relic progression demands careful resource management. You‚Äôll want to decide whether to invest in one hero heavily or spread your shards across multiple heroes. This choice can significantly impact your team synergy.

Relic Shards Explained

Relic Shards come in two forms: individual shards (specific to each hero) and universal shards (usable for any hero). You can mix and match them, but only individual shards can be used to initially unlock a Relic.

With the introduction of Relics, a new seasonal feature was added: the Relic Pass. Each pass focuses on a particular hero, offering players a chance to farm individual shards more efficiently.

To earn rewards, players must complete quests from ongoing special events. By participating, you‚Äôll not only gather shards but also progress faster toward maxing out your favorite heroes.
