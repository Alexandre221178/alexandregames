Eternal Frontier is one of the most challenging and rewarding PvE game modes in Hero Wars Alliance. Designed to test your strategic knowledge and the strength of your heroes, this mode presents 100 frontiers divided into themed worlds.

As players progress, enemies become stronger, and tougher decisions must be made. This comprehensive guide will walk you through everything you need to know to dominate this feature – including rewards, hero selection, energy management, team composition, tips for maximizing performance, and strategies for long-term progression.er.

What is Eternal Frontier?

In Eternal Frontier, your journey is divided into Worlds, each composed of 100 Frontiers. Completing frontiers earns you rewards and gradually increases the strength requirement of your hero squads. As you progress, you’ll need more squads to complete battles.

After winning battles, you receive Eternal Coins, Rune Stones, and eventually Rune Spheres depending on your progress. You also unlock the AFK Reward Chest, which accumulates rewards over time and gives the best payout every 24 hours.

How to Access Eternal Frontier

Eternal Frontier can be accessed from the main game screen once you've reached a certain campaign progress milestone. This mode uses a unique energy system that regenerates daily, separate from regular campaign energy. You don’t need to spend emeralds or outland coins, making it an excellent free-to-play feature for long-term progression.

Reward Types

Skip Battle System

Eternal Frontier allows players to skip battles if they meet certain power requirements. Skipping saves time and still grants full rewards, except for the story (lore).

  • You need to manually complete some battles before skip becomes available again.
  • The power of your first squad determines skip eligibility in earlier tiers.
  • No heroes die permanently; you can retry battles as much as needed.
  • The reward chest accumulates even while you’re offline (AFK).

Skip Requirements

Power thresholds by level for skip (first team only):

  • Level 601: 550k
  • Level 701: 660k
  • Level 801: 715k
  • Level 901: 770k
  • Level 1001: 825k

Once your first team reaches 825k power, you are eligible for skip on all content that only checks one squad. Higher tiers (Tier 8 and above) check multiple squads.

Tier 8 and Higher: Multi-Team Skip Strategy

Tier 8 starts at level 3501 and checks all 8 teams. To skip to level 3801, you need:

  • 6 teams with at least 825k power each
  • 1 team with 715k
  • 1 team with 275k

Total combined power needed: 5.94 million across 8 teams.

Eternal Frontier Shop Tips

Use Eternal Coins wisely! The Frontier Shop offers valuable items including first talismans for heroes. However, some heroes’ first talismans don’t appear in the shop: Lara Croft, Polaris, Guus, Ninja Turtles, Peech.

What to prioritize in the shop:

  1. First Talismans for meta heroes
  2. Exclusive orange items (not obtainable elsewhere)
  3. Mastery Crystals (for talisman evolution)
  4. Soleil’s hero stones (only to unlock him)

What NOT to buy

  • Orange items available from campaign or other shops
  • Metacubes (available in the Port)
  • Basic hero soul stones (unless unlocking)
  • Mastery Crystals when they’re available via events

Advanced Tips

  • Your strongest team should always be in the first squad slot.
  • Be patient with rewards – the AFK chest gives better returns over time.
  • Monitor daily shop changes – talismans and items rotate.
  • Only upgrade talismans of heroes you actively use.
  • Free-to-play players should avoid wasting coins on non-essential purchases.

Final Thoughts – Why Eternal Frontier is Worth It

Eternal Frontier is more than just another PvE mode – it’s a consistent source of hero resources, tactical challenge, and long-term engagement. For players looking to grow their account steadily, it’s essential to play this mode daily. The rewards are generous, the difficulty curve is balanced, and it encourages creativity in team building.

Make it part of your daily routine, and you’ll see progress in your hero upgrades, gold reserves, and game knowledge. Whether you're free-to-play or a spender, Eternal Frontier offers something for everyone – and those who master it will gain a serious edge in both PvE and PvP.
