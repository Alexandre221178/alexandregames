Once known as the fearless berserker of the Way of Nature, Jhu has returned with renewed fury! His long-awaited rework transforms him into a powerhouse capable of dominating both PvE and competitive battles. This isn’t just a minor upgrade — it’s a rebirth of a legend.

With his new Relic amplifying his already brutal damage output and a few smart tweaks to his abilities, Jhu now stands among the strongest physical attackers in the Alliance version. Whether you’re a veteran player or just unlocking him, this guide will help you unleash his full potential.

Who Is Jhu?

Jhu is a mid-line marksman and one of the most iconic members of the Way of Nature faction. Known for his relentless rage and self-sacrificing battle style, Jhu focuses on dealing massive damage as his health drops, making him a thrilling and risky pick for skilled players.

  • Glyph Class: Marksman
  • Position: Middle Line
  • Main Stat: Strength
  • Faction: Way of Nature
  • How to get Soul Stones: Heroic Chest, Events, Guild War Shop
  • Synergy: Dark Star, Elmir, Mojo
  • Hydra Tier List: S+

Jhu’s rework enhances his survivability while maintaining his raw offensive power. His attacks now scale better with Strength, allowing him to deal consistent damage even when heavily targeted. Combined with his Relic’s bonus to critical damage, Jhu has become a fearsome weapon in any physical-based team.

Whether you’re fighting the Hydra or climbing the Arena ranks, Jhu’s new build makes him a valuable investment for every Hero Wars Alliance player.

Jhu Pros and Cons - Hero Wars Mobile

✅ Pros

  • Becomes nearly immortal when using his ultimate skill, allowing him to deal continuous damage even at low HP.
  • High Armor Penetration makes him effective against tanky heroes with strong defenses.
  • Excellent synergy with heroes like Elmir and Dark Star, who boost his damage and armor penetration further.
  • Very strong in Hydra battles due to his self-sustain and consistent damage output.
  • Scales well with critical hit and physical attack stats, increasing his late-game potential.

❌ Cons

  • Can be hard-countered by heroes who block or delay energy gain, such as Jorgen and Polaris.
  • Relies heavily on proper artifact and glyph evolution to reach full potential.
  • His immortality can be wasted if enemies control him before his ultimate activates.
  • Weak against magic-heavy teams since he lacks strong magic defense or evasion.
  • Needs proper support heroes to stay alive long enough to use his ultimate effectively.

Jhu Legendary Relic Skills Upgrade Priority - Hero Wars Alliance

Discover the best order to upgrade Jhu’s skills after his rework! Learn how each ability works and why some upgrades matter more than others.

1st – You Will Not Stop Me

This is Jhu’s ultimate skill and his signature move. When activated, he becomes enraged for 7 seconds, gaining an incredible +180% attack speed and turning into an unstoppable force that cannot die during that time. This skill lets Jhu fight until the very last moment, even against stronger enemies. Formula: (Physical attack × attack speed bonus).

Evolution Priority: Very High – This skill defines Jhu’s identity. It’s what makes him survive and deal immense damage even under pressure. Always upgrade this first to maximize his performance in all battle types.

1st – Enhanced Legendary Relic: You Will Not Stop Me

When enraged, Jhu’s attacks now reduce enemy Armor for 4 seconds. Critical Hits reduce it even more effectively. Formula: (10% Physical attack).

Skill Info

  • Relic Unlocks at Level: 10
  • Relic Shard Necessary (Level 2–10): 300
  • Total Relic Shard Necessary (Level 0–10): 350

Evolution Priority: High – This relic upgrade adds team utility by lowering enemy armor. It’s great for making Jhu stronger and also helping his entire team hit harder.

2nd – I Will Take Your Life

This ability shows Jhu’s risky but powerful nature. Each of his attacks deals Pure Damage to enemies, equal to 7% of their current Health, while Jhu sacrifices 2% of his own. This makes him deadly to bosses and tanky enemies, but also fragile if not supported by healers or shields. Formula: (2.2 + 0.04 × Level % Pure Damage).

Evolution Priority: High – It’s Jhu’s main source of extra damage. Because it scales with enemy health, it’s extremely strong in Hydra battles and boss fights.

2nd – Enhanced Legendary Relic: I Will Take Your Life

Jhu’s attacks deal bonus Pure Damage to shielded enemies, based on their Max Health. This makes him the perfect counter to heroes like Aidan or Julius that rely on shields. Formula: (Pure Damage + % Max Health).

Skill Info

  • Relic Unlocks at Level: 20
  • Relic Shard Necessary (Level 11–20): 800
  • Total Relic Shard Necessary (Level 0–20): 1150

Evolution Priority: Medium-High – It’s situational but extremely valuable in PvP battles against shield-heavy teams.

3rd – I See You

For 10 seconds, Jhu increases his attack power and targets a ranged enemy. This helps him eliminate damage dealers faster and boost his offensive output. Formula: (70% Physical attack + 150 × Level + 3000).

Evolution Priority: Medium – It’s good for increasing damage, but not as vital as his first two skills. Upgrade it once the main ones are strong enough.

4th – The Spirits Will Heal Me

After using his first skill, if Jhu isn’t stunned or controlled, he regenerates a large amount of health. Formula: (200% Physical attack + 57000 Health). It’s a small window of survival that helps him stay alive longer during fights.

Evolution Priority: Medium – Helpful for endurance, but Jhu’s main purpose is damage, not healing. Keep it balanced but not your first focus.

5th – Legendary Relic: Mark of Pain

The Mark of Pain relic automatically applies a mark to the farthest enemy every 20 seconds, causing all of Jhu’s basic attacks to also strike the marked target. It’s like giving Jhu a double hit — one on his main target and another on the marked foe!
Formula: (Physical attack).

Important Note: Hydra heads are immune to marks and other debuffs, meaning this relic’s effect won’t apply in Hydra battles. However, it remains a strong choice for PvP, Outland bosses, and campaign fights where the mark can dramatically boost Jhu’s sustained DPS.

Skill Info

  • Relic Unlocks at Level: 1
  • Relic Shard Necessary (Level 0–1): 50
  • Total Relic Shard Necessary (Level 0–1): 50

Evolution Priority: High – Excellent in long PvE or PvP battles, but has no effect against Hydra due to mark immunity.

6th – Legendary Relic: Triple Fury

This relic makes all of Jhu’s Critical Hits deal triple damage instead of double! It turns his already powerful attacks into devastating strikes that can wipe out enemies in seconds. Formula: (3× Physical attack critical damage).

Skill Info

  • Relic Unlocks at Level: 30
  • Relic Shard Necessary (Level 21–30): 1750
  • Total Relic Shard Necessary (Level 0–30): 2900

Evolution Priority: Very High – This is one of the best relic upgrades in the game for damage dealers. Every critical hit becomes a deadly explosion of damage, making Jhu a monster in both PvE and PvP.

How to Counter Jhu - Hero Wars Alliance

Jhu is a strong physical damage dealer known for his immortality skill that allows him to keep fighting for seven seconds even after taking fatal damage. To effectively counter Jhu, heroes who can block his energy gain, stun or control him before his ultimate, or force him to target tough backline heroes are highly effective.

Jorgen

Why he counters Jhu: Jorgen’s Leper and Torment of Power skills block enemies from gaining energy, preventing Jhu from activating his immortality ultimate. Without his ultimate, Jhu becomes much easier to defeat before he can deal massive damage.

Martha

Why she counters Jhu: Martha is a backline tank with high armor and a healing totem. Since Jhu targets backline heroes, he often focuses on Martha — which only helps her charge her ultimate faster. Once she activates her Tea Party, her healing and speed boost keep the entire team alive while Jhu wastes his attacks.

Polaris

Why she counters Jhu: Polaris can stun multiple enemies in an area with her ultimate. By stunning Jhu before he activates his immortality, she completely neutralizes his biggest strength. Her control abilities delay his energy gain and prevent him from unleashing his full power in battle.

Jhu Synergy in Hero Wars Alliance

Jhu synergizes best with heroes that can amplify his physical damage, extend his survivability, or increase his attack uptime during his immortality phase. The following heroes offer powerful combinations that make Jhu one of the deadliest physical attackers in the game.

Dark Star

Why she synergizes with Jhu: Dark Star’s Black Arrows marks enemies for seven seconds, causing them to take +20% more damage from all allies and +40% more from Nature heroes. Formula: Physical Attack + (20% × Physical Attack) + 6000 This synergy greatly enhances Jhu’s total physical output, especially when he activates his ultimate and becomes immortal, letting him exploit the full duration of Dark Star’s mark.

Elmir

Why he synergizes with Jhu: Elmir jumps behind Jhu when using his Mirage ability, activating his artifact buff at the same time. His constant Armor Penetration increase allow Jhu’s strikes to deal full damage even against armored tanks.

Mojo

Why he synergizes with Jhu: Mojo and Jhu form a devastating combo, especially against Hydras. Mojo’s Hex and Cursed Bones continue to deal damage even after he dies, while Jhu stays alive through his immortality skill. The combination of continuous magic and physical damage overwhelms most enemies and boss fights, making this pair extremely effective in multi-phase battles.

Conclusion – Jhu Hero Wars Alliance

Jhu is one of the most aggressive and resilient physical damage dealers in Hero Wars Alliance. His unique ability to keep fighting even at 1 HP makes him a powerful finisher capable of turning battles around when used correctly. However, his true potential shines only when paired with heroes that amplify his strengths — such as Elmir for mobility and armor penetration boosts, and Dark Star for enhanced team damage through her marks.

To make the most out of Jhu, focus on maximizing his Armor Penetration, Critical Hit, and Physical Attack stats through glyphs and artifacts. Be mindful of enemy heroes who can disrupt his energy gain, like Jorgen or Polaris, as they can completely negate his ultimate’s impact.

In the right setup, Jhu becomes an unstoppable force capable of crushing tanks and backline enemies alike. Whether you use him for Hydras or in PvP battles, mastering his timing and synergies will make him one of your most reliable damage dealers in Hero Wars Alliance.
