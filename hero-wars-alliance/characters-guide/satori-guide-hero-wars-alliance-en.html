Satori's Skill Overview and Revamp Details

  • 1. Ravenous Pack (Main Skill)
    Basic attacks apply Fox Fire marks. When activated, deals 70% Magic Attack + 18000 per mark. Satori is immune to control effects while channeling this skill.
    Revamp: Increased from 50% to 70% Magic Attack per mark.
  • 2. Spirit Banishment
    Deals damage to all enemies: 60% Magic Attack + 3600. Applies one Fox Fire mark (plus an extra for each allied Mystery Hero). Lower chance if target's level > 120.
    Revamp: Damage increased from 40% to 60%, mark application more frequent, cooldown reduced from 20s to 15s.
  • 3. Centurial Wisdom
    Steals 600 Intelligence per hit. Each successful attack heals Satori for 300% of the damage dealt.
    Revamp: Steals Intelligence from all damage sources, not just basic attacks. Healing increased from 200% to 300%.
  • 4. Looming Justice
    Applies 9 Fox Fire marks when enemies gain bonus energy (excluding energy from damage, base attacks, or enemy kills).
    Revamp: No changes.

Satori thrives in Mystery teams and pairs well with heroes who provide durability and sustain:

  • Best Tank: Astaroth – Resurrection and shielding synergy

Countering Enemies with Bonus Energy

Satori’s Looming Justice skill punishes heroes that gain bonus energy. He’s especially effective against:

  • Jorgen
  • Orion
  • Octavia & her team

Before the revamp, Satori struggled against Octavia teams. With his new scaling power, he might now shift the meta, time will tell.

Strategy Tips

Team Placement

Position Satori behind durable tanks and near energy-boosting support heroes. He’s vulnerable on defense, so keeping him alive early is key to unlocking his late-game power scaling.

Ability Timing

Time the activation of Ravenous Pack when enemies have many Fox Fire marks for maximum burst damage. Coordinate with your team’s stuns or energy denial for best results.

Late Game Monster

Unlike before, Satori now scales through the whole fight. His stolen Intelligence and massive heals allow him to remain a consistent threat the longer the fight, the deadlier he becomes.

Satori's revamp breathes new life into this mysterious fox mage. While he still requires proper protection, his potential to dominate the battlefield has reached new heights.

Analysis of Satori's Talismans in Hero Wars Alliance

Satori’s first talisman, the Talisman of Cunning, focuses on intelligence and magic penetration, making it excellent for enhancing his offensive capabilities. Magic penetration allows Satori to deal more damage by bypassing enemy defenses, making this talisman ideal for attack-oriented strategies.

The second talisman, the Talisman of Prudence, provides armor and health, improving Satori's survivability. This talisman is best suited for defensive setups, helping him endure longer in battles against high-damage teams. While the added durability is beneficial in certain situations, it doesn’t boost his damage output significantly.

Satori's Talismans Overview

The Talisman of Cunning is superior for offense and maximizing Satori's potential as a damage dealer, while the Talisman of Prudence is more suitable for defensive compositions or when facing teams that can quickly eliminate squishy heroes. For most players, prioritizing the first talisman will yield better results unless specific survivability needs arise.

Harnessing the Power of Satori's Magic Defense Skin

With his new magic defense and magic penetration skin, Satori becomes an even more formidable force on the battlefield. The increased magic defense makes him more resilient against enemy mages, while the magic penetration allows him to bypass enemy defenses more effectively. This makes Satori teams a formidable force to be reckoned with in Hero Wars Alliance battles.

Satori's Confrontation with the Hydra

While Satori may not stand out as a primary counter against hydras, his vampirism ability grants him a lifeline in prolonged engagements, enabling him to withstand their onslaught and inflict considerable damage in return. A well-rounded team composition, such as Helio, Dorian, Orion, Satori, and Rufus, showcases his resilience and offensive capabilities, culminating in formidable damage output against the wind Hydra.

  • Satori's team suggestion for Wind Hydra: Helio, Dorian, Orion, Satori, Rufus (Damage: 4M to 6M)

Best Teams for Satori: Building the Ideal Team Composition

To fully utilize Satori's abilities, it is crucial to build a team that complements his strengths and covers his weaknesses. Pairing Satori with tanks such as Aurora, Astaroth, Andvari, or Rufus can provide the necessary protection, allowing him to focus on dealing damage. Additionally, support heroes like Martha or Thea can provide healing and buffs, further enhancing Satori's effectiveness on the battlefield.

Satori's Revamp Guide Conclusion

Satori's revamp has transformed him from a hero who excelled only in the early stages of battle into a true late-game powerhouse. With significantly enhanced scaling through Intelligence steal and increased damage output across his skills, he now poses a growing threat as fights progress. This shift allows players to build strategies around Satori’s ability to deal sustained and overwhelming magical damage, especially when paired with the right team composition and Mystery faction allies.

While his offensive potential has greatly improved, Satori still requires thoughtful positioning and support to survive and thrive in combat. His low defense makes him vulnerable, so pairing him with reliable tanks and healers is essential. By mastering his timing, skill usage, and synergy with other heroes, players can unlock the full potential of Satori and turn him into a decisive force on the battlefield in Hero Wars Alliance.
