Meet Peech: The Grand Spectacle of Battle

Hey there, Hero Wars fans! Alexandre here, and today we’re diving into the dazzling world of Peech, the newest addition to the Hero Wars Alliance roster. If you’re looking for a hero who can turn every battle into a grand spectacle, Peech is your girl. She’s a star who shines brighter the longer the fight goes on, and trust me, you’re going to want her on your team.

At first glance, Peech might seem a bit fragile, but don’t let that fool you. She is a Warrior whose main stat is Agility, making her a swift and precise damage dealer. As a member of the Way of Chaos faction, her abilities grow stronger as the battle progresses. Let’s break down everything you need to know about Peech, from her skills to her synergies with other heroes.

Peech’s Skills: A Showstopper in Every Sense

Peech’s abilities are as flashy as they are effective. Here’s a rundown of what she brings to the battlefield:

Theater of Pain

Knockback, Control
Damage per strike: (60% Phys. atk. + 4800)
Peech strikes the nearest enemy 3 times. Each strike deals physical area damage and slightly pushes back the affected enemies. This is great for controlling the battlefield and keeping enemies at a distance.
Passive Effect: Peech increases the range of her basic attacks and the area of effect of her basic attacks and first skill by 25% for each Chaos ally she starts the battle with. This means the more Chaos allies you have, the stronger and wider her attacks become!

Harlequin's Punishment

Buff, Debuff
Damage: (50% Phys. atk. + 8400)
Armor reduction: 50%
Physical or Magic attack increase: 50% of the target's Armor
Peech deals physical damage to the enemy with the highest Armor. Then, for 9 seconds, she reduces the target's Armor and increases the Physical or Magic attack of all allies whose Intelligence is higher than the target's. The boost depends on the target's current Armor.
Bonus: Allies gain a boost to the stat (Physical or Magic attack) that was higher at the start of the battle. This makes your team hit even harder!

The Final Act

Debuff
Physical attack steal: (15% Phys. atk. + 1800) every 0.5 seconds
Peech links herself to the enemy with the highest Physical attack for 6 seconds. During this time, the target cannot move, and Peech steals a portion of their Physical attack every 0.5 seconds. Since the effect lasts 6 seconds and she steals every 0.5 seconds, she will steal a total of 12 times during this period!
She keeps the stolen Physical attack for 6 seconds after the effect ends or is interrupted.
Note: This ability doesn’t stop the enemy from fighting, but it’s interrupted if Peech is hit by a control effect (like stun or freeze).

Stage Presence

Passive Skill
Damage bonus per ally: (15% Phys. atk. + 1200)
Damage bonus per Chaos ally: 11636 (15% Phys. atk. + 2400)
This is a passive skill, meaning it’s always active. Each ally positioned in front of Peech at the moment of her attack increases all damage she deals. If the ally belongs to the Chaos faction, the bonus is even bigger!
Important: Only the allies Peech starts the battle with are counted for this bonus. Heroes like Ninja Turtles, who join the fight after the battle has started, do not count. So, choose your team wisely!

Artifacts: Prioritizing Peech’s Power

To get the most out of Peech, you’ll want to focus on upgrading her artifacts in the following order:

1. Heartbreaker (Armor)

This should be your first priority. Increasing Peech’s Armor will make her more durable, allowing her to stay in the fight longer and continue building her strength.

2. Alchemist's Folio (Armor Penetration + Physical Attack)

Next up is the Alchemist's Folio. Boosting Peech’s Armor Penetration and Physical Attack will make her hits even more devastating, especially when paired with her Harlequin's Punishment skill.

3. Ring of Agility (Agility)

Finally, the Ring of Agility will enhance Peech’s Agility, improving her Physical Attack and Armor. This is a great way to round out her stats and make her a true force to be reckoned with.

Peech Skins Priority Explanations

1st - Mythical Super Skin: Crushing greatly increases all damage dealt to enemies with low health. This works with all types of damage and synergizes perfectly with Peech’s area attacks and her continuous damage from “The Final Act.” Essential for finishing off enemies effectively.

2nd - Default Skin (Agility): Boosts physical attack, and armor. This directly enhances Peech's offensive capabilities and survivability, making it the second most important skin to upgrade.

3rd - Romantic Skin (Health): While the health bonus improves survivability, it is less of a priority since Peech usually stays at a distance and controls the battlefield using knockback and debuffs.

Guide to Peech Talisman of Actress

The Talisman of Actress for Peech is an essential item to maximize her potential as a dominant warrior in Hero Wars Alliance. This talisman focuses on increasing Peech's physical attack, allowing her to deal massive damage and maintain a strong presence on the battlefield.

Main Attributes:

  • +2,000 Agility
  • +3,800 Physical Attack

Additionally, the slots of the talisman offer extra bonuses that further enhance her offensive power:

Slot Bonuses:

  • Slot 1: +3,000 Physical Attack
  • Slot 2: +2,000 Physical Attack
  • Slot 3: +2,000 Physical Attack

Thanks to these attributes, the Talisman of Actress makes Peech one of the deadliest warriors in her class. Her high physical attack, combined with area damage and control abilities, makes her a key asset in teams focusing on fast and devastating offensives. If you want to maximize Peech's performance, this talisman is a must-have choice!

Synergies: Peech and the Chaos Crew

Peech works best with other Chaos heroes, and her abilities synergize particularly well with Krista, Lars, Xe'sha, Kayla, Aidan, Astaroth, and Cleaver. Let’s take a closer look at how these heroes can complement Peech’s skills:

Krista

Krista’s Icy Vengeance and Chains of Frost skills are perfect for setting up Peech’s attacks. Chains of Frost reduces the enemy’s Magic Defense, making them more vulnerable to Peech’s physical attacks. Plus, the Mark of Water applied by Krista’s skills can be exploited by other heroes like Lars.

Lars

Lars’ Lord of the Storm and Lightning Bolt skills are great for crowd control. His ability to drag enemies to the center of the battlefield and stun them creates the perfect opportunity for Peech to unleash her Theater of Pain and Harlequin's Punishment.

Xe'sha

Xe'sha’s Deadly Ray and Soaring Might skills make her a powerful ally for Peech. Deadly Ray drains health from allies to deal massive damage to enemies, while Soaring Might gives Xe'sha a permanent Magic Attack bonus based on the health lost by allies. This can create a deadly combo when paired with Peech’s damage-boosting abilities.

Kayla and Aidan

Kayla’s Phoenix's Fury and Aidan’s Phoenix Ritual are a match made in heaven for Peech. Kayla’s ability to deal damage and increase her health pairs well with Peech’s Stage Presence, while Aidan’s sacrifice-based skills can boost the team’s overall survivability.

Astaroth and Cleaver

Astaroth’s Flame Veil provides a shield that blocks physical damage, giving Peech more time to build up her strength. Cleaver’s Rusty Hook and Putrefaction skills are great for controlling the battlefield and dealing damage to enemies, making him a valuable ally for Peech.

Final Thoughts: Peech is a Must-Have

Peech is a hero who truly shines in the heat of battle. Her abilities are designed to make her stronger as the fight goes on, and her synergies with other Chaos heroes make her a versatile and powerful addition to any team. Whether you’re looking to dominate in the Arena or take on the toughest PvE challenges, Peech is a hero you’ll want to have in your lineup.
