Krista Revamp – New Skills Breakdown

Krista’s revamp in Hero Wars Alliance transformed her into a much more aggressive and tactical mage. Her new abilities emphasize control, burst damage, and survivability, making her a real threat in both PvE and PvP. Here's a complete breakdown of her updated skills:

Skills

Icy Vengeance

Each crystal now deals 35% of Magic Attack as damage and slows enemies by 30% for 5 seconds.

Evolution Priority: High

Chains of Frost

Reduces enemy Magic Defense by 35% of Krista’s Magic Attack, making them vulnerable to magic attacks.

Evolution Priority: Medium

Frozen Needles

Deals 40% of Magic Attack + 50xLevel, with faster cooldown and earlier activation.

Evolution Priority: Very High

Crystallization

Protects Krista with an ice block and grants 100 energy per hit taken while shielded.

Evolution Priority: Low

General Tips

  • When using Krista in your team, it's important to maximize her crowd control and area damage potential.
  • Combine her abilities with other heroes who can capitalize on Watermark, thus increasing the damage dealt to enemies.
  • Manage the cooldown of her abilities and carefully plan their sequence of use to maximize Krista's impact on the battlefield.

Synergy with Lars

As mentioned, Krista has exceptional synergy with her brother, Lars. Here's how their abilities combine to create a powerful team:

Lars summons a storm in the enemy backline:

This ability of Lars not only deals damage to enemies but also drags them to the center of the battle, where Krista can apply her Watermark and ice spikes.

Krista applies Watermark to each enemy on the ice spikes:

When enemies are dragged to the center of the battle, they inevitably face Krista's ice spikes, suffering additional damage due to Watermark.

Team Strategies

Now that we understand Krista's individual abilities and how they combine with Lars', here are some strategies to maximize her effectiveness in a team:

  1. Proper positioning:

    2. Place Krista at the front of the team so she can absorb as much damage as possible with her Ice Block ability. This will protect her and allow her to keep enemies under control with her ice spikes.

  2. Timing of abilities:

    3. Coordinate Krista and Lars' abilities to maximize damage. Use Lars' storm to group enemies, and then use Krista's ice spikes to deal area damage while applying Watermark.

  3. Synergy with other heroes:

    4. Consider adding heroes that complement Krista and Lars' abilities. For example, heroes that deal area damage can benefit from the crowd control provided by Krista's ice spikes.

  4. Ability enhancement:

    5. Prioritize enhancing Krista's abilities that increase her crowd control and damage dealt. This includes improvements in the duration and damage of her ice spikes, as well as the application of Watermark.

Krista's First Talisman Guide

Krista's Talisman of Hurricanes

Talisman of Hurricanes: The Talisman of Hurricanes significantly boosts Krista's intelligence and magic attack, turning her into a formidable damage dealer. This talisman enhances her ability to inflict substantial damage on enemies, particularly when combined with her crowd control skills. By focusing on magic attack, Krista can maximize her area damage, making her a crucial asset in battles. This talisman is ideal for players who want to leverage Krista's offensive capabilities and dominate the battlefield with her powerful spells.

Krista's Second Talisman Guide

Krista's Talisman of Ice

Attributes:

  • Health
  • Armor Penetration - Reroll

Talisman of Ice Analysis: Krista’s second talisman boosts her health and provides armor penetration, enhancing her role as a tank. The first talisman, focusing on intelligence and magic attack, makes her a potent damage dealer. The second talisman, however, makes her more durable and effective on the front lines. The increased health improves her survivability, while the armor-penetration attribute allows her to deal more damage to enemy tanks. This makes her an excellent choice for players who want to use Krista as a tank, providing a good balance between offense and defense.

Guide to the Best Krista Teams

Krista is a versatile hero who can fit into various teams in Hero Wars Alliance. Here are some of the best team compositions that synergize well with Krista:

1. Martha, Lars, Jorgen, Krista, Astaroth:

This team is centered around Lars and Krista, leveraging Jorgen's crowd control and Martha's healing support. Astaroth provides tanking and additional resurrection.

2. Lars, Nebula, Judge, Krista, Astaroth:

Nebula boosts Lars and Krista's damage, while Judge helps disrupt enemy attacks. Astaroth offers tanking and additional protection.

3. Dorian, Lars, Iris, Krista, Astaroth:

Dorian can provide team-wide life sustain, while Iris boosts magical damage. Lars and Krista continue to deal damage, with Astaroth as the primary tank.

4. Dorian, Lars, Xe'sha, Krista, Astaroth:

Xe'sha provides additional crowd control and damage, complementing Krista's abilities. Dorian and Astaroth offer sustain and protection.

5. Lars, Iris, Krista, Dante, Astaroth:

Dante offers significant physical damage and pushes enemies over ice, while Krista provides crowd control and magical damage. Iris and Astaroth complete the team with support and tanking.

6. Lars, Jorgen, Celeste, Krista, Astaroth:

Celeste offers additional healing and enemy healing block, while Jorgen disrupts enemies. Lars and Krista remain the main damage dealers, with Astaroth as the tank.

7. Dorian, Lars, Jorgen, Krista, Astaroth:

This team combines Dorian's life sustain with Jorgen's crowd control. Lars and Krista continue to be the main damage dealers, while Astaroth protects the team.

8. Lars, Nebula, Celeste, Krista, Astaroth:

Nebula boosts Lars and Krista's damage, while Celeste provides additional healing. Astaroth offers tanking and protection.

9. Faceless, Lars, Jorgen, Krista, Astaroth:

Faceless provides additional damage and crowd control, while Jorgen disrupts enemies. Lars and Krista remain the main damage dealers, with Astaroth as the tank.

These are just some of the many possible combinations that can be effective with Krista on your team. Experiment with different compositions to find the one that best suits your playstyle and the specific needs of each battle.

Krista is an exceptional hero who can play various roles, from tank to area damage dealer and crowd controller.

Her unique ability to summon an ice block to temporarily protect herself makes her a valuable addition to any team. Additionally, her synergy with Lars creates a powerful combination capable of controlling the battlefield and inflicting significant damage to enemies.

By understanding her abilities and team strategies, players can maximize Krista's potential and build effective teams to tackle challenges in Hero Wars Alliance. With the knowledge gained from this guide, players can explore Krista's full potential and make the most of her abilities in their game adventures.
