The last leader and legendary hunter of a forgotten tribe, Oya is a fearsome Front Line warrior. Her brutal strikes can break through any shield, making her one of the best counters to tanks and heroes with protective skills in Hero Wars Alliance.

In this guide, you'll discover Oya’s role, her best team synergies, glyph class, how to obtain her Soul Stones, and where she ranks in the tier list for both overall combat and Hydra battles.

Who Is Oya?

Oya is a powerful Front Line warrior from the Way of Nature faction. As a strength-based hero, her skillset allows her to tear through enemy defenses, particularly effective against shielded enemies.

  • Class: Warrior
  • Position: Front Line
  • Main Stat: Strength
  • Faction: Nature
  • How to get Soul Stones: Events, Heroic Chest
  • Hero Overall Tier List: A
  • Hydra Tier List: B+
  • Synergy: Yasmine, Chabba, Jhu, Ishmael, Guus

Oya works exceptionally well with critical damage dealers like Yasmine, Jhu, and Ishmael, forming high-pressure offensive teams. Her synergy with Guus enhances survivability and control, while Chabba adds frontline support and distraction, creating space for backline assassins to operate freely.

Thanks to her ability to ignore shields, Oya is particularly effective against tanky lineups and protector heroes. Although she’s less efficient against magic-heavy teams, she shines in physical compositions, especially in PvP battles where shield-breaking and strong frontline presence make a significant impact.

Oya Pros and Cons – Hero Wars Alliance

✅ Pros

  • ✔ Excellent at breaking shields and bypassing defensive barriers, making her strong against tank-heavy teams.
  • ✔ High synergy with crit-based allies like Guus, Yasmine, Ishmael, and Jhu, enabling strong healing loops and burst damage.
  • ✔ Versatile talismans allow you to adapt her to crit-focused or burst-focused strategies.
  • ✔ Can drain energy from marked enemies, weakening support heroes and preventing ultimates.
  • ✔ Benefits greatly from allies that increase critical hit chance or boost survivability.

❌ Cons

  • ✖ Less effective against magic-heavy teams due to limited magical defense and lack of resistance tools.
  • ✖ Relies heavily on positioning and synergy — without the right team, her full potential is harder to unlock.
  • ✖ Needs investment in critical chance and physical attack to become impactful, making her gear-dependent.
  • ✖ Shieldbreaker’s passive bonus is only impactful if enemy teams use shield-based protectors.

Oya Skills Upgrade Priority - Hero Wars Alliance

Find out which Oya skills to upgrade first and why. Discover the secrets behind her shield-breaking power and healing synergy with Guus!

Shieldbreaker

Oya’s ultimate skill is her most powerful weapon. It hits the closest enemy hard and spreads 30% of that damage to all marked enemies. The passive part is even better: any enemy with a shield takes double damage from all her skills. She also regains energy when killing enemies, allowing her to use this ability again faster.

Evolution Priority: Very High – This is the core of Oya’s identity. It maximizes her damage and ability to break through enemy shields.

Hunting

This skill marks the weakest enemy and forces them to walk toward Oya, interrupting their strategy. All attacks on the marked enemy burn their energy. Even better, allies that are healing or receiving healing from Oya gain a critical hit bonus. Nature heroes get an even higher bonus!

Evolution Priority: High – It enhances control and boosts crits for Nature allies, especially powerful when combined with Guus and other Nature-based teams.

Overwhelming

This is Oya’s quick triple attack. With each hit, the damage increases, making it great for finishing enemies. If enemies behind the target have shields, they take part of the damage too. It’s simple, strong, and helps apply pressure quickly.

Evolution Priority: Medium-High – It deals decent damage and adds value against shielded backlines. Upgrade it after her core abilities are done.

Brutal Onslaught

This ability heals allies when they land critical hits or break shields. It gives extra healing to Nature Heroes. When combined with Guus, it becomes more effective thanks to his Golden Feather buffs, which increase crit chance and healing triggers.

Evolution Priority: Medium – Useful in longer fights and when paired with Guus, but not as impactful in fast PvP battles. Upgrade it later if you rely on healing synergy.

Best Skin for Oya Hero Wars Alliance

Find out the best skins to upgrade for Oya in Hero Wars Alliance based on how they improve her performance in battle and skill impact.

Default Skin

Strength +1,365. Each point of Strength provides 40 Health and 1 bonus to Physical Attack if Strength is the main stat. Since Oya’s first three skills scale with Physical Attack and her fourth scales with Health, this skin boosts all her abilities, making it her most important skin.

Evolution Priority: Very High – Core stat that maximizes both damage and survivability. Always upgrade this one first.

Mythical Skin

+7,095 Physical Attack. Oya’s main offensive skin: boosts the damage of Shieldbreaker, Hunting, and Overwhelming. Since these three skills scale directly with Physical Attack, this skin has excellent synergy with her kit.

Evolution Priority: High – The best offensive choice after Strength, especially for maximizing burst and sustained DPS.

Solar Super Skin

Health +213,290. Increases Oya’s survivability and directly improves her fourth skill, Brutal Onslaught, which restores Health based on her max HP. Also enhances synergy with Guus, boosting sustain in critical-heavy teams.

Evolution Priority: High – Essential for balance between durability and healing support. Great in long fights or defensive PvP setups.

Winter Skin

Armor +10,650. Provides resistance against physical damage, useful against marksmen and warriors. However, it does not improve Oya’s skill scaling directly.

Evolution Priority: Low – Defensive but situational. Consider only if facing heavy physical damage compositions.

Sport Skin

Crushing +5,920. Crushing increases all outgoing damage against low-health enemies, applying to Physical, Magical, and Pure damage (but not allied health-loss effects). Formula: (Crushing / (Crushing + target’s main stat)) × 100%. This skin makes Oya much deadlier as a finisher, securing kills on weakened enemies.

Evolution Priority: Medium – Great for late-fight damage pressure, but less impactful on base skill scaling compared to Strength, Physical Attack, or Health.

Oya Artifact Evolution Priority Hero Wars Alliance

Discover which artifacts to prioritize for Oya in Hero Wars Alliance to enhance her Shieldbreaker and team synergy in battles.

1st – Weapon Artifact: Ancestor's Sword

Critical Hit Chance +8,898. This weapon activates every time Oya uses her ultimate skill, Shieldbreaker, which is her most important ability. The artifact grants a critical hit bonus to the entire team for 9 seconds, making it incredibly valuable in both offense and team synergy.

Evolution Priority: Very High – Activates with her main skill and benefits the entire team. Core to her performance in battle.

2nd – Book Artifact: Warrior's Code

Critical Hit Chance +2,967, Physical Attack +3,561. This artifact increases both her damage potential and the chance to trigger healing and bonus effects when landing critical hits. It's a solid boost to her offensive capabilities, especially useful when combined with Guus or other Nature allies.

Evolution Priority: High – Supports critical hit builds and overall damage output, complementing her skill synergy well.

3rd – Ring Artifact: Strength Ring

Strength +3,990. Each point of Strength gives Oya +40 Health and +1 Physical Attack. While this artifact boosts both survivability and power, its impact is slower and less direct than her weapon or book, especially in shorter PvP fights where fast bursts matter more than long-term stats.

Evolution Priority: Medium-High – Important for overall scaling, but lower priority compared to her weapon and book, which affect active combat more directly.

Oya Glyph Evolution Priority

Find out which glyphs you should level first for Oya in Hero Wars Alliance to maximize her shield-breaking power and synergy with Nature heroes.

1st – Physical Attack Glyph

+8,340 Physical Attack. All of Oya’s offensive skills scale with Physical Attack — from her triple strike to her ultimate. Boosting this stat increases her overall damage and helps her eliminate shielded enemies faster.

Evolution Priority: Very High – Directly powers up every damaging skill. Core to Oya’s role as a damage-dealing frontline hero.

2nd – Health Glyph

+122,800 Health. Oya fights on the front line, which means she takes a lot of incoming damage. More health means greater survivability and better synergy with healing effects from skills like Brutal Onslaught and allies like Guus.

Evolution Priority: High – Helps her stay alive long enough to keep applying damage and shield-break effects.

3rd – Critical Hit Chance Glyph

+4,170 Crit Hit Chance. Critical hits are important for triggering healing from Brutal Onslaught and maximizing damage. This becomes even more powerful when Oya is in a team with Guus or other crit-focused allies.

Evolution Priority: Medium-High – Great for synergy and healing triggers, but less important than pure Physical Attack.

4th – Magic Defense Glyph

+12,850 Magic Defense. While this glyph doesn’t boost Oya’s offensive power, it helps her survive longer against magic-heavy teams. Useful in PvP when facing mages, but not a main focus for most builds.

Evolution Priority: Medium – Good defensive boost, but not urgent unless you're constantly facing mages.

5th – Strength Glyph

+2,110 Strength. Each point of Strength grants +40 Health and +1 Physical Attack. While it helps with both survivability and damage, it gives less benefit per level compared to the direct glyphs for Health and Physical Attack.

Evolution Priority: Low – Offers mixed stats, but should be upgraded last for maximum efficiency.

Oya Talisman Guide - Hero Wars Alliance

Oya has access to two powerful talismans, but only one can be equipped at a time. Unlike skins, where all bonuses apply regardless of which one you wear, only the equipped talisman grants its bonus effects. Choosing the right one can drastically influence your battle outcome. Let’s break down the options.

Talisman of Prophecy

  • Main Stat: Strength +2000 – As Oya’s main stat, Strength increases both her Health and Physical Attack, making it a valuable base bonus.
  • Bonus Reroll (Combined): Critical Hit Chance +6,600 – This significantly increases Oya’s ability to land critical hits, which is essential for activating healing effects through her Brutal Onslaught skill. This synergy becomes even stronger when paired with Guus, who boosts crit effectiveness.
  • Best Use: Ideal for crit-focused team comps with Nature synergy, especially when you want Oya to self-sustain while dishing out consistent crit-based damage.

Talisman of Warrioress

  • Main Stat: Physical Attack +8000 – A direct and massive boost to Oya’s offensive power, amplifying her damage in every skill, especially Shieldbreaker and Overwhelming.
  • Bonus Reroll (Combined): Crushing +13,200 – Crushing increases all damage dealt to low-health enemies, making Oya far more lethal when finishing off opponents. The bonus affects Physical, Magical, and Pure damage, making it versatile across all team setups.
  • Best Use: Perfect for aggressive and bursty strategies where you want Oya to secure kills fast and dominate late-stage PvP or boss fights. Works best in offensive lineups where cleanup is key.

Which One Should You Choose? If you’re building Oya for long-term crit reliability and synergy with Guus or other Nature heroes, go with the Talisman of Prophecy. If you want Oya to deal massive burst damage and finish enemies swiftly, especially in PvP, the Talisman of Warrioress will give you the edge. Equip wisely — your choice can shift the tide of battle.

Which Allies have Best Synergy with Oya?

Ally Skill Synergy

Yasmine
Yasmine is a character with a high chance of critical damage. Whenever an ally of Oya deals critical damage, they receive healing and a temporary critical hit bonus from Oya’s passive effects.

Chabba
Chabba is a durable tank who can devour and disable enemies in the front line. This creates space for Oya to strike unchallenged while reducing incoming pressure. His crowd control and survivability allow Oya to stay active longer and consistently break enemy shields.

Elmir
Elmir shifts to the back line after cloning himself, dealing ranged damage from a safe position. His weapon artifact increases Oya’s armor penetration. In teams with Dorian, he gains bonus critical damage, synergizing perfectly with Oya’s crit-based healing.

Guus
Guus boosts critical hit chance and heals allies with Golden Feathers. Oya benefits immensely from these buffs, triggering more critical hits and healing effects with Brutal Onslaught. A top synergy for sustained crit builds.

Jhu
Jhu deals massive damage the lower his health gets. Oya’s shield-breaking and energy-drain effects help soften targets, allowing Jhu to secure kills faster. They both benefit from critical-hit-centered compositions.

Ishmael
Ishmael thrives in crit-heavy teams. Oya’s passive bonus to critical hit chance supports his burst playstyle. In longer battles, their combined crit scaling can overwhelm enemy frontlines.

How to Counter Oya

Counters
 How enemies defeat Oya

Astaroth
Once per battle, Astaroth can resurrect an ally and when Oya uses her Hunt skill, she eliminates enemies quickly, and Astaroth revives them with full energy.

Iris
Iris is immune to debuffs, but not the Mark, and if an ally receives a debuff or mark Iris reduces the physical and magical attacks of the enemy that applies, in addition to burning them and causing damage. However, to use Iris against Oya you need to have another ally with less health in the team or Astaroth to resurrect her.

Satori
Oya receives 700 extra energy when eliminating an enemy, so Satori applies his marks. But Satori has little health and can be the target with less health, so it's good to have another ally with less health or Astaroth to resurrect him with full energy and counterattack.

Conclusion – Oya Guide Hero Wars Alliance

Oya is a powerful and versatile frontline hero who can make a huge difference in battle thanks to her ability to ignore shields and boost allies’ critical hit potential. She excels in physical and crit-based team compositions, especially when paired with heroes like Guus, Yasmine, Ishmael, and Jhu.

Her synergy with support heroes that enhance critical damage and healing, such as Guus and Aidan, makes her a strong choice for both offensive and defensive strategies. Choosing the right Talismans, Glyphs, Skins, and Artifacts can greatly impact her effectiveness, so careful investment is key.

Whether on attack or defense, Oya brings valuable pressure against shield-heavy tanks and energy-dependent enemies. When used with the right allies, she becomes a central piece in winning team setups.
