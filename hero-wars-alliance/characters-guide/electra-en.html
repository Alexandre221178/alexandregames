Who Is Electra?

Electra is a frontline tank belonging to the Way of Eternity faction. Her design focuses on absorbing enemy damage while boosting her Health, making her an unusual yet resilient protector. She sacrifices part of her defensive stats for greater durability, which gives her a unique playstyle among tanks.

  • Glyph Class: Tank
  • Position: Front Line
  • Main Stat: Strength
  • Faction: Way of Eternity
  • How to get Soul Stones: Only through special events
  • Synergy: Mages and Eternity Heroes
  • Hydra Tier List: To be determined as players test her

Electra brings an exciting balance of sacrifice and survival that makes her stand out. She is not the traditional tank that stacks defenses endlessly but instead leverages her immense Health pool to stay in the fight longer.

Players looking for creative team setups will find her particularly valuable alongside Eternity heroes, where her synergy helps unlock hidden potential. Her limited availability through events makes her a rare and desirable addition to any roster.
