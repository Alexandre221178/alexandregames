What is the Tournament of Titan Power?

The Tournament of Titan Power is a three-day event that pits players against each other in a race to strengthen their Titans. Points are earned based on the power increases of your Titan teams through upgrades such as leveling up artifacts, star ranks, and skins. The player with the most points by the end of the event claims the top spot and receives valuable rewards.

The ultimate prize for winning includes an exclusive Avatar, 10.000 Fairy Dust, and 50.000 Titan Potion, making this event highly coveted. Whether you're competing for glory or simply looking to improve your Titan team, understanding the mechanics and developing a winning strategy is critical.
