Way of Mystery Event Introduction

The "Way of Mystery" is an exciting faction-themed event in Hero Wars Alliance. It highlights a specific faction and features a star hero, offering players three days of intense gameplay opportunities. During this period, players can earn event coins to spend in a special event shop. This shop offers the featured hero, their soul stones, and various resources for upgrading other heroes of the Mystery Faction.

To help you maximize your gains during this event, this guide will walk you through every aspect of the "Way of Mystery" event, including strategies, hero recommendations, and tips for success.

Overview of the Mystery Faction

The Mystery Faction is one of the most versatile and powerful factions in Hero Wars Alliance, featuring heroes with unique skills suited for a wide variety of battle situations. The star hero of this faction is Folio, a new addition who specializes in powerful magic damage and disrupting the enemy team. Alongside Folio, the Mystery Faction is home to other standout heroes like Satori, a top-tier assassin capable of eliminating key enemies, and Amira, who excels in countering critical damage teams, particularly those from the Honor Faction, such as Artemis' FART team.

Notable Heroes in the Mystery Faction:

  • Folio: Magic damage dealer with powerful area-of-effect attacks and debuffs.
  • Satori: Assassin and key damage dealer.
  • Amira: Counter to critical damage teams and a defensive powerhouse against Artemis.
  • Heidi: Known for countering dodge teams.
  • Ziri: The faction's main tank, boasting high armor and durability.
  • Andvari: Effective against the popular Karkh teams.
  • Celeste and Martha: Widely regarded as some of the best healers in the game.
  • Lian: Capable of charming and controlling enemies with her abilities.
  • Rufus: An invincible tank against magic damage teams.
  • Maya: Healer with strong pure damage, effective against evasion-based teams.

Tips and Strategies for Event Quests

1. Day After Day

Objective: Log in daily to earn Event Coins and Hero's Way Points.

Strategy:

  • Consistency is Key: Ensure that you log in every day during the event. Even if you don't have time to play, a quick login can net you some valuable rewards.
  • Plan Ahead: If you anticipate being busy, log in early in the day to secure your daily rewards without stress.

2. Value Exchange

Objective: Spend emeralds to earn Event Coins, Artifact Coins, and Hero's Way Points.

Strategy:

  • Smart Spending: Avoid frivolous spending. Plan your emerald expenditure ahead of time, using them for upgrades that contribute to your long-term goals.
  • Look for Discounts: Pay attention to any event offers or discounts that can help you stretch your emeralds further.

3. Energy of Evolution

Objective: Spend energy to gain Event Coins.

Strategy:

  • Energy Management: Focus on campaign missions that drop useful items or soul stones to maximize the efficiency of your energy expenditure.
  • Energy Boosts: Utilize energy potions and energy-boosting items at key moments to earn more Event Coins without over-spending your energy.

4. Generous Soul

Objective: Earn Event Coins for scoring VIP points.

Strategy:

  • VIP Point Accumulation: Complete activities that reward VIP points, such as purchasing small, necessary in-game packages.
  • Daily Quests: Complete quests that give VIP points to maximize the number of Event Coins you can collect.

5. Secrets of Outland

Objective: Open Outland chests to earn Event Coins.

Strategy:

  • Save Chests: Stockpile your Outland chests before the event starts and open them during the event for maximum rewards.
  • Target Outland Bosses: Focus on defeating Outland bosses that drop the most valuable rewards based on your current team needs.

6. Journey

Objective: Collect Event Coins and Heroâ€™s Way Points by starting Expeditions.

Strategy:

  • Expedition Planning: Start longer expeditions as soon as the event begins. Expeditions with longer durations but better rewards should be prioritized.
  • Completion Timing: Plan your expeditions so that they complete while youâ€™re online, allowing you to begin new ones right away.

7. The Way Up

Objective: Open Tower chests to earn Event Coins and Hero's Way Points.

Strategy:

  • Tower Strategy: If you're a beginner, focus on reaching higher floors of the Tower to unlock better rewards.
  • Chest Selection: Prioritize opening chests with orange items that are more difficult to obtain.

8. Promote Hero

Objective: Promote the featured event hero to earn Event Coins and Hero's Way Points.

Strategy:

  • Focus on the Event Hero: Save resources to promote the event's featured hero during the event. This can earn you significant rewards and improve your team.
  • Resource Allocation: Plan your resource spending to ensure you have enough to promote the hero effectively.

Event Shop Guide

The Way of Mystery Shop offers an array of items for purchase, including equipment, artifacts, and Demon Dolls. These dolls contain a variety of rewards, such as hero soul stones, gold, and artifact chests.

Recommended Purchases

  • Artifact Boxes: These are a must-buy. Investing in artifact fragments, especially the orange ones, will pay off in the long run. Orange artifacts are harder to obtain but are necessary for upgrading your heroes' equipment.
  • Event-Specific Artifacts: Focus on acquiring the featured heroâ€™s weapon, book, and ring artifacts. These items are usually rarer and harder to get during regular gameplay.

Items to Avoid

  • Blue, Purple, and Orange Hero Equipment: These items are better obtained through regular energy spending in campaigns. Only purchase them from the event shop if absolutely necessary.
