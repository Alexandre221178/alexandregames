Tempus: Master of Time in Hero Wars Alliance

In the ever-expanding universe of Hero Wars Alliance, a new Guardian has emerged Tempus, a master of time manipulation. Born from the boredom of eternal life, Tempus has chosen to join the ranks of the Way of Eternity faction, bringing his unique time-altering abilities to aid his fellow Guardians in combat. As a Support Hero who operates from the Middle Line, Tempus specializes in controlling the flow of battle, weakening enemies, and enhancing the power of his team, particularly magic users.

Event Overview: Dark Carnival

The Dark Carnival event features several daily and cumulative missions that players need to complete to earn valuable rewards, including Tempus’s Soul Stones, Rune Stones, Avatars, and powerful artifacts. Given the 28-day duration and the division into two 14-day segments, the event offers a second chance to accomplish the same objectives and claim rewards.

Strategic Breakdown by Event

1. Day after Day

Objective: Log in daily for 14 days.

Rewards: Rune Stones, 10 fragments of Tempus’s weapon artifact, and one Avatar.

Strategy: Ensure consistent daily logins throughout both parts of the event. Missing a day can result in lost rewards, which are essential for advancing Tempus’s power. Set reminders or alarms to log in every day if necessary.

2. Value Exchange

Objective: Spend emeralds to earn Tempus’s Soul Stones and other valuable items.

Reward Tiers:

  • 200 emeralds: 1 Soul Stone
  • 500 emeralds: 2 Soul Stones
  • 1000 emeralds: 3 Soul Stones
  • 1500 emeralds: 4 Soul Stones
  • 2100 emeralds: 5 Soul Stones
  • 3000 emeralds: 7 Soul Stones
  • 5000 emeralds: 9 Soul Stones
  • 7500 emeralds: 10 Soul Stones
  • 11,000 emeralds: 12 Soul Stones
  • 16,000 emeralds: 14 Soul Stones + Talisman
  • 22,000 emeralds: 16 Soul Stones
  • 30,000 emeralds: 20 Soul Stones
  • 40,000 emeralds: 25 Soul Stones
  • 50,000 emeralds: 40 Soul Stones

3. Generous Souls

Objective: Accumulate VIP points during the event.

Rewards: Large Skin Boxes and Rune Spheres.

Strategy: Focus on achieving VIP points daily, as this will allow you to unlock higher-tier rewards, which include essential items for leveling up your heroes. Since these rewards can only be claimed once per event part, make sure you reach the desired VIP level within the first 14 days and repeat the process in the second part.

4. Energy of Evolution

Objective: Spend up to 8000 energy daily to earn runes and Tempus’s Soul Stones.

Reward: 1 Soul Stone for every 400 energy spent.

Strategy: Prioritize spending at least 400 energy daily to secure 1 Soul Stone of Tempus each day. You can gain energy passively (per minute) and through daily logins (180 energy) and ads (40 energy). Additionally, consider buying energy (120 energy for 50 emeralds twice a day) to complete this task with minimal expenditure. If resources are limited, focus on reaching the 400 energy threshold daily.

5. Secrets of Outland

Objective: Open Outland chests to earn Large Skin Boxes.

Strategy: Open the 5 free Outland chests daily and consider purchasing 3 additional chests at a discounted rate (50 emeralds each). This should be done every day during the event to maximize rewards, but consider saving most of your emeralds for more significant spending opportunities later in the event.

6. Head Hunter

Objective: Win battles in Outland.

Rewards: Large Skin Boxes and Tempus’s Soul Stones (15 total).

Strategy: You have 14 days to win 70 Outland battles, which averages to 5 battles per day. Use your free daily battles and consider purchasing additional ones if needed to ensure you reach the target. The rewards include 15 Tempus Soul Stones, which are crucial for unlocking and upgrading Tempus.

7. Lord of the Arena

Objective: Participate in Arena battles.

Rewards: 1 Tempus Soul Stone and Hero Artifact Boxes per battle.

Strategy: Participate in your 5 daily Arena battles, focusing on your Arena reward times to maximize rewards. You can also spend emeralds to get extra battles, but remember that additional battles only offer Hero Artifact Boxes, not extra Soul Stones.

8. The Way Up

Objective: Open chests in the Tower.

Reward: 1 Tempus Soul Stone for every 10 chests opened.

Strategy: Open the 15 free Tower chests daily. While you can open more chests, the rewards beyond the first 10 chests are less impactful in this event. Focus on getting the 1 daily Soul Stone from Tempus without overextending your resources.

Additional Event: Great Archivist

Objective: Level up Tempus to earn additional rewards.

Tasks:

  • Leveling Tempus’s Skin: Earn Skin Stones.
  • Upgrading Hero Artifacts: Earn Hero Artifacts in return.
  • Star-Leveling Ring, Book, and Weapon Artifacts: Receive summoning materials.
  • Upgrading Equipment (Green to Orange+4): Earn gold and energy.
  • Leveling Tempus (0 to 120): Earn EXP.
  • Upgrading Glyphs: Earn runes.
  • Upgrading Gift of the Elements: Receive Spark of Power.

Strategy: This event is an excellent opportunity to level up Tempus quickly. The rewards, while not 100% of your investment, significantly help to boost Tempus’s stats and effectiveness. Focus on the tasks that align with your current resources, but prioritize tasks that give back essential items for future upgrades, such as Spark of Power and runes.

General Tips for Success

  • Resource Management: Efficiently manage your emeralds, energy, and other resources throughout the event. Prioritize spending on tasks that give you the highest returns, and save where possible for the second part of the event.
  • Daily Commitment: Ensure daily participation to maximize rewards. Even small daily gains can accumulate to significant rewards by the end of the event.
  • Focus on Soul Stones: As Tempus is a new hero, acquiring his Soul Stones should be your top priority to unlock and upgrade him during the event.
  • Strategic Spending: Save emeralds for essential tasks and energy purchases to ensure steady progress without overspending.

Tempus Event Conclusion

The Dark Carnival: Tempus Event in Hero Wars Alliance offers significant rewards for those who approach it strategically. By managing resources wisely and participating daily, you can unlock Tempus’s full potential and strengthen your team with his time-bending abilities. Good luck, Guardians!
