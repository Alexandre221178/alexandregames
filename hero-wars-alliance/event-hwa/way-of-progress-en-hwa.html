Way of Progress Event in Hero Wars Alliance: Comprehensive Guide

The "Way of Progress" is a thematic faction event in Hero Wars Alliance, specifically centered around the Progress faction. During this recurring event, which lasts for three days, players can earn event coins and exclusive rewards, including soul stones for various heroes in the Progress faction. These coins can be spent in a special event shop that offers unique resources and one featured hero from the Progress faction. The event is a great opportunity to strengthen your roster by enhancing the abilities of your Progress heroes.

This guide will provide a thorough overview of the event, the Progress faction, and tips on how to maximize your rewards.

Overview of the Progress Faction

The Progress faction is known for its exceptional synergy, particularly in team buffs that involve shields, protection, and critical damage enhancements. The famous "JJI" combo (Julius, Judge, and Isaac) is a hallmark of this faction, offering a powerful combination of defense, support, and offense that makes them a formidable force in battle.

Key Heroes in the Progress Faction

Julius:

  • Role: Tank
  • Abilities: Julius is a tank hero with a strong emphasis on protection. He provides healing, shields, and critical damage to his allies, making him a cornerstone in defensive strategies. His ability to absorb damage while simultaneously enhancing the critical hit rate of his team makes him invaluable in prolonged battles.

Judge:

  • Role: Support
  • Abilities: Judge is a support hero who specializes in shielding. He provides a powerful shield to the foremost and rearmost allies, ensuring that both your frontline and backline remain protected. His ability to sustain the survivability of key heroes in critical moments can turn the tide of battle.

Isaac:

  • Role: Support
  • Abilities: Isaac offers a balanced mix of support functions. He provides shields, healing, buffs to allies, and debuffs to enemies. Isaac's versatility makes him an excellent choice for any team composition, particularly when facing magic-heavy opponents.

Nebula:

  • Role: Support
  • Abilities: Nebula is a support hero who enhances the physical and magical attack of the two nearest allies, both in front and behind her. Her ability to amplify the damage output of your primary damage dealers makes her an essential part of many offensive strategies.

Sebastian:

  • Role: Support
  • Abilities: Sebastian is a support hero who specializes in critical damage buffs. His ability to increase the critical hit rate of his allies makes him a perfect fit for teams focused on high burst damage.

Astrid & Lucas:

  • Role: Marksman
  • Abilities: Astrid, alongside her pet Lucas, is a marksman hero who excels at dealing high damage to the enemy frontline. Her ability to sustain high damage output, combined with the additional support from Lucas, makes her a strong contender in both PvE and PvP scenarios.

Lara Croft:

  • Role: Marksman
  • Abilities: An excellent marksman with a unique set of artifacts, Lara Croft stands out due to her powerful single-target damage and versatile utility in battle.

Ginger:

  • Role: Marksman
  • Abilities: Ginger is a marksman who excels at dealing area-of-effect damage. Her ability to hit multiple enemies at once makes her particularly effective against teams with clustered formations.

Daredevil:

  • Role: Marksman
  • Abilities: Daredevil is another marksman who specializes in area-of-effect critical damage. Her ability to deliver devastating bursts of damage in a wide area makes her a key player in teams focused on rapid elimination of enemies.

Orion:

  • Role: Mage
  • Abilities: Orion is a mage marksman known for his speed and high damage output. His ability to quickly eliminate enemies with powerful magical attacks makes him a dangerous opponent in any battle.

Arachne:

  • Role: Control
  • Abilities: Arachne is a control hero who specializes in stunning and poisoning enemies. Her ability to disrupt the enemy team’s formation and incapacitate key heroes makes her a valuable asset in crowd-control strategies.

Jet:

  • Role: Support
  • Abilities: Jet is a support hero who boosts the critical damage of all allies. His ability to increase the overall damage potential of the team makes him a great addition to any critical hit-focused lineup.

Fox:

  • Role: Marksman
  • Abilities: Fox is a marksman who deals high single-target critical damage. Her ability to eliminate tanks or key enemies with a single, powerful attack makes her a feared opponent on the battlefield.

Rarity of Progress Faction Soul Stones

The rarity of soul stones for the Progress faction heroes varies, with some being easier to obtain than others.

  • Rare Soul Stones:
  • Julius: Souls Stones are only available through events or soul stone chests.
  • Lara Croft: Only available in special events and it is not possible to use the evolution book.
  • Jet: Only available in the soul stone shop, it is not possible to use the evolution book.
  • Medium Difficulty Soul Stones: These are available in various in-game shops. Heroes such as Astrid & Lucas, Nebula, Isaac, Judge, Sebastian, and Orion can be acquired this way.
  • Easy Soul Stones: These can be obtained through the campaign. Heroes like Ginger, Daredevil, Arachne, and Fox are more accessible to players.

Tips and Strategies for Way of Progress Event Quests

The Way of Progress event features several quest chains that players can complete to earn Event Coins, Hero's Way Points, and other valuable rewards. These quests are designed to be completed over the three days of the event, and each has its own specific requirements.

  1. Day After Day
    • Objective: Log in to the game daily.
    • Rewards: Event Coins, Hero's Way Points.
    • Strategy: Simply logging in every day during the event will grant you rewards. This quest is straightforward but essential for accumulating Event Coins.
  2. Value Exchange
    • Objective: Spend emeralds.
    • Rewards: Event Coins, Artifact Coins, Hero's Way Points.
    • Strategy: Plan your spending wisely. If you’ve been saving emeralds, this is the perfect time to use them. Focus on spending them on necessary upgrades or energy refills that will further aid in completing other event quests.
  3. Energy of Evolution
    • Objective: Spend energy.
    • Rewards: Event Coins.
    • Strategy: Use your energy to farm campaign missions that drop the soul stones of Progress faction heroes. This will not only help you complete this quest but also gather resources to upgrade your heroes.
  4. Generous Soul
    • Objective: Score VIP points.
    • Rewards: Event Coins.
    • Strategy: If you’re planning to make any purchases, do so during the event to maximize your VIP points and earn additional Event Coins.
  5. Secrets of Outland
    • Objective: Open chests in Outlands.
    • Rewards: Event Coins.
    • Strategy: Save your Outland keys for this event. Opening chests in the Outland will not only help you complete this quest but also give you a chance to earn valuable items and hero skins.
  6. Journey
    • Objective: Begin Expeditions.
    • Rewards: Event Coins, Hero's Way Points.
    • Strategy: Start expeditions as often as possible. Try to time your expeditions so that they finish during the event to collect the maximum number of Event Coins and Hero's Way Points.
  7. The Way Up
    • Objective: Open chests in the Tower.
    • Rewards: Event Coins, Hero's Way Points.
    • Strategy: Use your tower runs effectively. Make sure to open as many chests as possible each day to maximize your rewards.

Conclusion

The Way of Progress event is a fantastic opportunity for players to enhance their Progress faction heroes and gain valuable resources. By understanding the strengths of the Progress faction and strategically completing the event quests, you can maximize your rewards and strengthen your roster. Focus on the JJI combo (Julius, Judge, and Isaac) for a solid foundation, and use the event to gather the resources needed to upgrade your heroes to their full potential.

Good luck, and may your progress lead you to victory!
