Note: All information in this guide is based on the most recent occurrence of the Blessing of Worlds event.

The Blessing of Worlds event in Hero Wars Alliance is a limited-time opportunity that lasts three days, offering players a variety of daily missions and generous rewards. During the event, you can collect valuable resources such as hero artifact components, Titan Soul Stones from the Light and Darkness elements, Fairy Dust, Titan Potions, and Titan Skin Stones.

Whether you're aiming to evolve your Titans or improve your heroes' artifacts, this event is packed with opportunities to boost your progress. In this comprehensive guide, we'll break down every mission from the main event and the side missions Invoke Light and Touch of the Abyss, so you can maximize your rewards and make the most of all three days.

The Blessing of Worlds Event typically lasts three days and includes a variety of daily missions. These missions provide valuable rewards such as:

  • Artifact resources
  • Light and Darkness Titan Soul Stones
  • Fairy Dust
  • Titan Potions
  • Titan Skin Stones
  • Hero Soul Stones

Players can complete different mission paths across three separate sub-events within Blessing of Worlds:

  • Blessing of Worlds
  • Invoke Light
  • Touch of Abyss

Tips to Maximize the Event

  • Plan your energy usage: Use energy efficiently across all three days, especially for the Energy of Evolution missions.
  • Open chests in bulk: Save Outland and Artifact chests in advance to complete higher milestones quickly.
  • Coordinate with your guild: Guild activity and titanite contributions are crucial, so coordinate to earn maximum points.
  • Use Titan Summoning Spheres wisely: These are limited resources, so use them on days when they're needed for Power Unleashed.
  • Check daily tasks: Log in each day to secure free progress on "Day After Day" missions across all paths.

The Blessing of Worlds event is one of the best opportunities to focus on your Light and Darkness titans while also improving your hero collection. With careful planning and steady participation, you can make great progress in just three days!
