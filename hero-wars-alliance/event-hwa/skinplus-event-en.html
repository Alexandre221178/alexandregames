The Skin+ event in Hero Wars Alliance is a thrilling opportunity for players to enhance their heroes like never before. Over the course of 3 days, you'll gain access to exclusive skins and the highly coveted Skin+ which offers double the stat bonus of a regular skin!

To unlock a Skin+, you must first purchase the featured skin using real money. Only then will the Skin+ version be made available in the event shop, where you can use event coins to buy it. This limited-time event is your chance to make your strongest heroes even more powerful.

What Is the Skin+ System and Why Does It Matter?

Skin+ is an upgraded version of normal skins, offering 2x the stats of standard ones. These bonuses can dramatically improve your heroes' performance in battle, making them more competitive in both PvP and PvE modes.

But remember Skin+ isn’t free. It requires a two-step process: purchase the regular event skin with real money, and then spend your event coins to get the unlocked Skin+ version in the event shop.

Complete Event Missions to Earn Skin+ Coins

During the Skin+ event in Hero Wars Alliance, you’ll find a variety of missions that reward you with Skin+ Event Coins. These coins can be used in the event shop to purchase the powerful Skin+ or other valuable items like skin stones, season points, and more. Don’t miss the chance to maximize your rewards every task brings you closer to unlocking exclusive content!
