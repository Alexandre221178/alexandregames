Learn the best strategies to succeed in the Tournament of Power in Hero Wars Alliance. Discover tips on timing, resource management, and how to maximize your chances of winning valuable rewards. Perfect for beginners and advanced players alike.

Tournament of Power Introduction

The Tournament of Power in Hero Wars Alliance is a competitive event where players from various servers race to upgrade their heroes and claim valuable rewards. The ultimate prize for finishing in first place includes an exclusive avatar and 750 small skin stone chests, totaling 7,500 skin stones of your choice. This can be a game-changer for beginners but may hold less value for advanced players who require thousands of skin stones for higher-level upgrades.

Key Strategy for Success

1. Timing is Everything

The tournament lasts for three days. While it might be tempting to rush your upgrades early, the best strategy is to hold off until the last day, particularly in the final hours. This way, you can assess the competition and use your resources more efficiently when others might have already spent theirs.

By waiting until the final minutes to upgrade, you increase your chances of overtaking other players who may have already expended their resources.

2. Resource Management

Preparation is crucial. Throughout the month leading up to the tournament, save as many resources as possible, including gold, equipment, artifact components, glyphs, and skin stones. The more you have stocked up, the better your chances of making significant progress during the event.

Focus on efficiency. It’s vital to spend resources only when they contribute to tournament points. Hero level-ups, artifact enhancements, and skin upgrades are your primary sources of points. Remember, only hero-related upgrades count, Titan upgrades do not contribute.

3. Advantages for Newer Players

Beginner's Edge: Newer players are at a slight advantage in the Tournament of Power because they have more heroes to upgrade and can make substantial progress with fewer resources. Early-stage players can also unlock equipment, artifacts, and skins faster, giving them a natural advantage over veterans who need far more resources to achieve smaller gains.

Leverage your growth potential: As a newer player, focus on upgrading the most beneficial heroes and don’t spread your resources too thin. Aim for the upgrades that provide the highest point returns.

4. Outthinking Advanced Players (Whales)

More advanced players commonly referred to as “whales” have maxed out much of their content, meaning they have fewer upgrades left to gain points from. As a free-to-play or newer player, you can still outpace them by using your available resources smartly.

Resource stockpiling is key for both free-to-play and advanced players, but the timing strategy remains your most potent weapon in a competitive group.

Tournament Mechanics

Enrollment and Grouping: Players are grouped based on their team level and recent activity. Once the tournament starts, you are locked into that group for the entire event, and new participants will not be able to join the competition after the enrollment period ends (one day before the tournament concludes).

How Points are Calculated: Points are based on power increases from hero upgrades, including level-ups, artifacts, skins, glyphs, star ranks, and more. Each 1 point of power equals 1 tournament point. Focus your efforts on upgrades that give the highest power increase for maximum point efficiency.

Rewards System

Stage Rewards: These rewards are unlocked as you accumulate tournament points. Every day, a progress bar tracks your daily point gains and refreshes at 5:00 AM in your in-game time zone. Be sure to claim these rewards before the reset; otherwise, they will be lost.

First-Place Reward: The player with the most points at the end of the tournament will receive the exclusive first-place reward, including an avatar and the 750 skin stone chests. Results may take time to be processed, but once finalized, the reward can be claimed in the event tab.

Conclusion

The Tournament of Power is a challenging but rewarding event, especially for newer players. By conserving resources, upgrading strategically, and timing your efforts for the final hours of the event, you increase your chances of ranking highly and securing valuable rewards. Whether you win or not, it's a thrilling opportunity to boost your heroes' power and advance in the game. Give these tips a try, and you might find yourself standing at the top!
