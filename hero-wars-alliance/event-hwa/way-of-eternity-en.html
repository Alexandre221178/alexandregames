The "Way of Eternity" is a faction-themed event in Hero Wars Alliance, specifically focused on the Eternity faction. During this recurring event, which lasts three days, players can earn event coins and exclusive rewards, including soul stones for various heroes from the Eternity faction. These coins can be spent in a special event shop that offers unique resources and a featured hero from the Eternity faction.

The event is a golden opportunity for players to strengthen their Eternity faction heroes, as it provides access to rare soul stones and other valuable items that are otherwise difficult to obtain. Whether you're a new player looking to build your team or a veteran aiming to optimize your roster, the Way of Eternity event is a must-participate occasion to gain a competitive edge.

What makes the Way of Eternity event particularly exciting is its focus on the Eternity faction, a group of heroes known for their unique abilities and synergies. From powerful tanks like Corvus to versatile supports like Morrigan, the Eternity faction offers a wide range of heroes that can fit into various team compositions. During the event, players are encouraged to complete a series of quests that reward them with event coins, which can be used to purchase exclusive items in the event shop.

These quests range from simple tasks like logging in daily to more challenging objectives such as spending energy or opening chests in the Outland. By strategically planning your actions and prioritizing the most rewarding quests, you can maximize your gains and make significant progress in strengthening your Eternity faction heroes.

Rarity of Eternity Faction Soul Stones

  • Rare Soul Stones: Rare Soul Stones are only available through events or soul stone chests. Examples: Octavia, Tempus.
  • Medium Difficulty Soul Stones: These are available in various in-game shops, Soul Stone Chests, and Events. Examples: Dante, Faceless, Corvus, Iris, K'arkh, Morrigan.
  • Easy Soul Stones: These can be obtained through the campaign and events. Examples: Keira, Phobos.

Tips and Strategies for Way of Eternity Event Quests

The Way of Eternity event features several quest chains that players can complete to earn Event Coins, Hero's Way Points, and other valuable rewards. These quests are designed to be completed over the three days of the event, and each has its own specific requirements.

  1. Day After Day
    • Objective: Log in to the game daily.
    • Rewards: Event Coins, Hero's Way Points.
    • Strategy: Simply logging in every day during the event will grant you rewards. This quest is straightforward but essential for accumulating Event Coins.
  2. Value Exchange
    • Objective: Spend emeralds.
    • Rewards: Event Coins, Artifact Coins, Hero's Way Points.
    • Strategy: Plan your spending wisely. If youâ€™ve been saving emeralds, this is the perfect time to use them. Focus on spending them on necessary upgrades or energy refills that will further aid in completing other event quests.
  3. Energy of Evolution
    • Objective: Spend energy.
    • Rewards: Event Coins.
    • Strategy: Use your energy to farm campaign missions that drop the soul stones of Eternity faction heroes. This will not only help you complete this quest but also gather resources to upgrade your heroes.
  4. Generous Soul
    • Objective: Score VIP points.
    • Rewards: Event Coins.
    • Strategy: If youâ€™re planning to make any purchases, do so during the event to maximize your VIP points and earn additional Event Coins.
  5. Secrets of Outland
    • Objective: Open chests in Outlands.
    • Rewards: Event Coins.
    • Strategy: Save your Outland keys for this event. Opening chests in the Outland will not only help you complete this quest but also give you a chance to earn valuable items and hero skins.
  6. Journey
    • Objective: Begin Expeditions.
    • Rewards: Event Coins, Hero's Way Points.
    • Strategy: Start expeditions as often as possible. Try to time your expeditions so that they finish during the event to collect the maximum number of Event Coins and Hero's Way Points.
  7. The Way Up
    • Objective: Open chests in the Tower.
    • Rewards: Event Coins, Hero's Way Points.
    • Strategy: Use your tower runs effectively. Make sure to open as many chests as possible each day to maximize your rewards.

Maximizing Your Rewards in the Way of Eternity Event

To make the most out of the Way of Eternity event, focus on completing as many quests as possible. Prioritize quests that offer the highest rewards and align with your current in-game goals. Additionally, plan your resource usage, such as emeralds and energy, to ensure you can complete multiple quests efficiently.
