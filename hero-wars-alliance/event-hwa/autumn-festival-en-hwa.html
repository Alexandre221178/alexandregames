Autumn Festival Event strategies in Hero Wars Alliance

Participate in the Hero Wars Alliance Autumn Festival event to complete quests and earn valuable rewards, including Harunian Brew, rare gear, and powerful resources.

The Autumn Festival is an exciting three-day event in Hero Wars Alliance that rewards players for completing a series of quests. By participating in this event, players can collect Harunian Brew, a valuable event currency that can be used to obtain various resources. These include high-rarity heroes’ gear, such as purple and orange equipment, which can significantly enhance your heroes' performance in battle. The event provides an excellent opportunity to strengthen your team while enjoying the festive theme of the autumn season.

Event Overview

This special Autumn Festival event is featured in the Special Events tab within the game. You can find it listed separately under a unique tab, allowing easy access to the event quests and rewards. During the event, you will embark on a series of quest chains, each offering a chance to earn Harunian Brew. The event is designed to cater to a wide variety of play styles and game focuses, ensuring that all players have the opportunity to participate.

The event's structure revolves around completing specific tasks, such as logging into the game, spending resources like emeralds, gathering titanite, and obtaining soul stones for heroes. These activities, which are part of the game's daily progression, now offer the additional benefit of contributing to your event rewards.

The Importance of Harunian Brew

The Harunian Brew is the central reward currency for the Autumn Festival. Each bottle of Harunian Brew can be exchanged for various in-game items, which can range from rare equipment fragments to precious resources like Artifact Coins, Titan Potions, and even Gold. What makes the Harunian Brew especially valuable is its ability to offer rewards that may otherwise take a longer time to collect during standard gameplay. For players looking to optimize their heroes and overall account power, this event provides an efficient boost to progress.

Because the event is limited to just three days, it’s crucial to focus on completing as many quests as possible. The short duration creates a sense of urgency but also ensures that those who are active during the event can make significant progress in a short amount of time.

Event Quest Chains

The Autumn Festival offers several quest chains designed to target different aspects of gameplay. Here’s an in-depth look at each of the quest chains, including tips and strategies for completing them efficiently:

1. Welcome Guest

Objective: Log in to the game daily.

This is the simplest quest chain available and rewards players simply for being consistent in logging into the game. Each day that you log in during the event, you’ll receive a small amount of Harunian Brew. While this chain doesn’t require any special effort, missing a day could result in a loss of rewards, so it’s essential to make logging in part of your daily routine.

2. Special Invitation

Objective: Reach specific VIP levels.

This quest chain rewards players based on their VIP level within the game. Hero Wars Alliance utilizes a VIP system, which provides various benefits based on how much players have spent on in-game purchases. Each VIP level offers certain advantages, such as increased rewards or additional gameplay features.

3. Business Mindset

Objective: Spend emeralds to earn Harunian Brew.

Emeralds are one of the most important premium currencies in Hero Wars Alliance, used for a wide variety of in-game purchases. The Business Mindset quest chain rewards players for spending emeralds during the event. If you’ve been saving emeralds, this is the perfect time to spend them for extra rewards.

4. Crystal Depths

Objective: Obtain titanite to receive Harunian Brew.

Titanite is a resource that players collect in the dungeon by completing battles with their Titans. The Crystal Depths quest chain is ideal for players who are focusing on their Titan teams and dungeon progression. Completing dungeon levels efficiently will maximize your rewards.

5. Onwards and Upwards!

Objective: Open chests in the Tower to collect Harunian Brew.

The Tower is a core gameplay mechanic in Hero Wars Alliance. The Onwards and Upwards! quest chain incentivizes players to open these chests, which contain valuable rewards. Aim to open as many chests in the tower as possible during the event.

6. Team Morale

Objective: Obtain Hero Soul Stones to earn Harunian Brew.

The Team Morale quest chain rewards players for collecting Hero Soul Stones. These are essential for upgrading heroes. Make sure to focus on obtaining soul stones through campaign missions and events during the festival.

7. Incredible Tenacity

Objective: Spend energy to receive Harunian Brew.

Energy is used for completing campaign missions. The Incredible Tenacity quest chain rewards players for spending energy. To optimize this, spend energy wisely on quests that offer soul stones for your characters.

8. Trade Route

Objective: Start Valkyrie's Expeditions to gain Harunian Brew.

The Trade Route quest chain rewards players for initiating Valkyrie's Expeditions, offering opportunities to collect Harunian Brew. Expeditions are a valuable feature in Hero Wars Alliance, providing players with resources and rewards. Completing these expeditions during the Autumn Festival is a great way to maximize your rewards and enhance your heroes' strength.

Autumn Festival Reward Breakdown

The rewards for the Autumn Festival are diverse, ranging from Harunian Brew itself to high-value items such as Artifact Coins, Skin Stones, and Titan Potions. Here's a breakdown of the potential rewards and their drop rates:

  • Harunian Brew (2 units): 40.05% chance of obtaining.
  • Shop Coins (100 units): 13.18% chance of obtaining.
  • Skin Stones (50 units): 8% chance of obtaining.
  • Artifact Coins (150 units): 3.58% chance of obtaining.
  • Titan Chest: 13.48% chance of obtaining.
  • Rare Enchantment Rune: 3.43% chance of obtaining.
  • Great Enchantment Rune: 1.95% chance of obtaining.
  • Bottled Energy (1 unit): 0.63% chance of obtaining.
  • Titan Potion (1,000 units): 1.34% chance of obtaining.
  • Flawless Artifact Chest: 2.67% chance of obtaining.
  • Chaos Core (1 unit): 1.25% chance of obtaining.
  • Equipment Fragments (10 units): 7.8% chance of obtaining.
  • Gold (1,000,000, 500,000, or 250,000 units): 2.64% chance of obtaining.

Autumn Festival Conclusion

The Autumn Festival in Hero Wars Alliance is a dynamic event that challenges players to engage in various gameplay activities while offering substantial rewards. The event caters to a wide range of players, from casual participants to more dedicated players who invest time and resources into reaching higher milestones.

To maximize your rewards during the Autumn Festival, it’s essential to stay active and prioritize the quest chains that align with your current in-game goals. The event only lasts three days, so make the most of your time and resources to collect as much Harunian Brew as possible. With the right strategy, you’ll be able to walk away from the Autumn Festival with a stronger, more powerful team, ready to take on the next set of challenges in Hero Wars Alliance.
