Top Defense Teams for Polaris

Polaris is one of the most powerful and versatile heroes in Hero Wars Alliance. As a backline Control/Mage from the Way of Progress faction, Polaris thrives in defense teams that can support her stunning abilities and amplify her magical damage.

1. Julius, Judge, Isaac, Lara, Polaris

This team is an excellent mix of defense and energy control. Julius absorbs damage, Isaac boosts allies' physical attack while weakening enemy armor, and Judge and Lara provide AoE control. Polaris finishes off enemies with her crowd control and high burst damage.

2. Julius, Ninja Turtles, Judge, Isaac, Polaris

Adding the Ninja Turtles (a defensive pet-based team) boosts survivability while Polaris and Judge stun the enemy front and back. Isaac boosts allies, making this team a strong Arena wall.
