Somna Best 5 Combos and Teams - Hero Wars Alliance

In Hero Wars Alliance, few heroes have stirred as much excitement and strategic curiosity as Somna. Introduced as a Mystery Faction support with an unusual control-oriented kit, she immediately made waves among both competitive and casual players. Her ability to shut down physical attackers, stack debilitating effects, and transform enemies into harmless sheep turns the tide of battle in unexpected ways. But like any powerful hero, her true potential shines only when paired with the right allies.

In this article, we will explore the 5 best Somna combos and teams currently dominating the Hero Wars Alliance meta. Whether you are building for Arena offense, Guild War defense, or just looking to spice up your Adventure fights, these combinations blend creativity, synergy, and devastating power. By the end, you will see why Somna is quickly becoming a must-have hero and perhaps the most frustrating opponent your enemies will face.

Before we dive into the top 5 combos, it’s important to understand Somna’s core strengths and how her abilities influence team building. Somna specializes in Drowsiness, a debuff that reduces both physical damage and armor penetration. When enemies accumulate three stacks of this debuff, she can transform them into sheep, effectively removing them from the fight for several seconds. Combined with her Lullaby Veil sleep ability and the Silence of the Sheep passive, she creates an environment where enemy physical attackers struggle to function.

Her kit works best when paired with heroes who can exploit disabled opponents, extend debuffs, or generate massive area damage while enemies are helpless. Let’s break down the five most effective team compositions featuring Somna right now.

5 – Krista and Lars Combos

Krista and Lars have always been a terrifying duo in Hero Wars Alliance, thanks to their chain-freeze and storm synergy. Adding Folio for energy gain and Somna for battlefield control makes this combination even more dangerous. Somna’s sleep and sheep transformations keep enemies locked down, while Lars’ ultimate gathers them together for devastating combo bursts. Krista’s water marks and AoE damage ensure that once the crowd control is in place, enemies melt rapidly.

Example Teams:

  • Lian, Lars, Folio, Somna, Krista
  • Krista, Folio, Somna, Xe’sha, Krista

With Folio boosting energy gain, Somna disabling threats, and the twins delivering synchronized damage, these lineups dominate in both Arena offense and Guild War attacks. Against slower enemy teams, this combo feels almost unfair enemies barely get to act before they’re overwhelmed.

4 – Satori Combos

Satori recently received a powerful rework that significantly improved his burst damage potential. With Somna joining the Mystery faction, new possibilities opened up. Satori thrives against heroes who frequently gain energy or rely on basic attacks, and Somna’s debuffs keep them weakened and under control long enough for Satori to deliver his killing blow.

Example Teams:

  • Cascade, Folio, Somna, Satori, Astaroth
  • Peech, Folio, Somna, Satori, Rufus
  • Lian, Somna, Celeste, Satori, Rufus
  • Cascade, Amira, Somna, Satori, Astaroth

These teams leverage Satori’s Fox Fire marks to punish energy-gaining heroes, while Somna’s crowd control ensures they can’t retaliate. In particular, pairing with Rufus or Astaroth adds survivability, allowing the team to weather early aggression before unleashing decisive damage.

3 – Xe’sha Combos

Xe’sha’s Deadly Ray skill targets the enemy with the lowest magic defense which often ends up being Somna if she’s on the opposing team. But when they fight together, Xe’sha gains a reliable support partner. Somna’s crowd control creates windows where Xe’sha can safely unleash massive magic bursts without fear of being interrupted.

Example Teams:

  • Aidan, Somna, Xe’sha, Tempus, Astaroth
  • Aidan, Somna, Xe’sha, Tempus, Mushy

In these builds, Tempus synergizes perfectly with Somna by extending debuff durations meaning enemies stay under Drowsiness longer, and sheep transformations become even more punishing. Aidan provides sustain and shields, ensuring Xe’sha has the time to do her work.

2 – Kayla and Aidan Combos

Kayla’s aggressive leap into enemy lines pairs beautifully with Somna’s Drowsy Blessing. By placing Kayla directly in front of Somna, you ensure that she applies Drowsiness stacks to multiple enemies during her whirlwind assault. This combination can dismantle even the toughest frontlines in seconds.

Example Teams:

  • Aidan, Peech, Somna, Kayla, Julius
  • Aidan, Somna, Soleil, Tempus, Kayla
  • Aidan, Somna, Guus, Tempus, Kayla
  • Octavia, Aidan, Somna, Dante, Kayla

One standout variant uses Soleil to counter enemy control effects, protecting Kayla from being disabled. The Octavia + Dante version is excellent for dodge-based strategies, reducing incoming damage from Iris-heavy teams and turning battles into a war of attrition the enemy cannot win.

1 – Polaris Combos

Polaris is currently one of the deadliest partners for Somna. The combination of Tempus and Polaris with Somna creates a suffocating control chain that leaves enemies unable to respond. Tempus’ Chronostasis skill extends all debuffs including Somna’s while Polaris adds heavy damage and energy manipulation. Against unprepared teams, this lineup can lock down an entire battlefield.

Example Teams:

  • Peech, Polaris, Folio, Somna, Tempus
  • Aidan, Polaris, Somna, Kayla, Corvus
  • Polaris, Folio, Somna, Judge, Julius
  • Polaris, Folio, Somna, Iris, Tempus

The Peech + Polaris + Somna core is devastating for offense, while the Corvus variant is a nightmare for Iris players. In both cases, enemies spend most of the match either asleep, turned into sheep, or frozen in time. This level of control is rare in Hero Wars Alliance and it’s why Polaris combos take the number one spot.

Final Thoughts

Somna’s arrival in Hero Wars Alliance has reshaped the landscape for both PvP and PvE. Her crowd control, debuff stacking, and unique sheep transformation mechanic open up creative team-building opportunities that didn’t exist before. The five combos outlined here each highlight a different aspect of her kit from supporting burst damage dealers like Satori and Krista, to enhancing aggressive initiators like Kayla, to locking down entire teams alongside Polaris.

While these are the most effective combinations right now, the meta is always evolving. New hero releases, reworks, and balance changes could open up even more powerful partnerships for Somna in the future. The key to mastering her is understanding the timing of her abilities, positioning her correctly, and pairing her with allies who can capitalize on the chaos she creates.

If you haven’t built Somna yet, now might be the time. And if you already have her it’s time to test these combos and see just how far you can push her control-oriented playstyle. Whether in Arena, Guild War, or campaign adventures, one thing is certain: your opponents won’t forget the match where they spent more time as sheep than as heroes.
