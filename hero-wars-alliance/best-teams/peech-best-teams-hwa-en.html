Welcome, Heroes! Today, we’ll explore the best team compositions for the new character Peech in Hero Wars Alliance. Whether you’re an experienced player or just getting started, this guide provides tested strategies to maximize Peech’s potential.

These recommendations are based on recent game events, including brawl's event, and focus on optimal team synergies. Let’s dive in and find the strongest setups!

Why Peech is a Game-Changer

Peech is a versatile hero who brings unique abilities to the table. Her skills allow her to buff allies, deal significant damage, and synergize with a variety of team compositions. Whether you’re building a magic-based team or a physical damage squad, Peech can fit right in. Below, I’ll break down the top 7 team compositions that work exceptionally well with Peech.

7th Place: Peech, Orion, Mojo, Alvanor, Mushy, or Aidan

This team focuses on increasing both physical and magical damage. Mojo acts as a magic tank, becoming even stronger with Peech’s buffs. You can also swap Morrigan for Dark Star if you prefer. This team is particularly effective against Rufus teams, as Rufus is strong against Mushy. Overall, this is a solid team for Peech, especially if you’re looking for a nature-based composition.

6th Place: Jet, Peech, Sebastian, Oya, Astaroth or Jet, Peech, Sebastian, Qing Mao, Astaroth

This team is all about speed and critical hits. Jet and Sebastian provide critical hit support, while Oya or Qing Mao add damage and utility. Astaroth is the reliable tank here, keeping your team alive. This composition is great for players who enjoy fast-paced battles and high damage output.

5th Place: Peech, Lars, Jorgen, Krista, Astaroth

The twins, Lars and Krista, shine in this team. With Peech’s buffs, their synergy becomes even more deadly. Jorgen adds control by slowing down the enemy team, while Astaroth provides the necessary tanking. This team is perfect for players who love using the twins and want to maximize their potential with Peech.

4th Place: Octavia, Peech, Nebula, Dante, Corvus

This team combines the Eternity faction with Peech’s buffs. Octavia and Dante deal massive damage, while Nebula enhances their abilities. Corvus acts as the tank, and his altar ability can turn the tide of battle. This team is highly effective in both PvE and PvP scenarios.

3rd Place: Fafnir, Peech, Oya, Tristan, Astaroth

This team is a powerhouse of damage and support. Fafnir provides shields and buffs, while Oya and Tristan deal heavy damage. Peech enhances the team’s overall performance, and Astaroth keeps everyone alive. This composition is ideal for players who want a balanced team with strong offensive and defensive capabilities.

2nd Place: Aidan, Peech, Kayla, Tristan, Astaroth

This team is all about synergy and versatility. Aidan and Kayla work exceptionally well together, and adding Peech to the mix makes them even stronger. You can swap Tristan for Oya depending on your playstyle. This team is perfect for players who want flexibility and high damage output.

1st Place: Aidan, Peech, Oya, Tristan, Cleaver

This is my top pick for the best Peech team. Aidan and Peech provide incredible buffs, while Oya and Tristan deal massive damage. Cleaver acts as the tank, pulling enemies into the fray. This team is incredibly strong in both PvE and PvP, and it’s my personal favorite for dominating the battlefield.

If you prefer more defense and a chance to resurrect, you can swap Cleaver for Astaroth to add extra survivability.

Final Thoughts

Peech is a fantastic addition to Hero Wars Alliance, and her versatility allows her to fit into many team compositions. Whether you’re using her in a magic-based team or a physical damage squad, she can significantly enhance your team’s performance. I hope this guide helps you build the perfect team for Peech.
