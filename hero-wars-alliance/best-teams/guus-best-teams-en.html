Top 13 Best Guus Teams in Hero Wars Alliance (Meta Picks)

Welcome to another exciting guide through the world of Hero Wars Alliance. Today, we’re diving deep into one of the most underrated powerhouses in the game Guus. Known for his unique combination of healing and crit-boosting, Guus has slowly become a top-tier support hero that can fit into a surprising number of meta compositions.

But here’s the million-dollar question: Which teams truly unlock Guus’s full potential? Get ready to explore 13 of the best team compositions where Guus absolutely shines some of them will completely change the way you approach battles. Whether you're aiming to dominate Arena, Guild Wars, or Boss fights, there’s something here for everyone.

1. Aidan, Peach, Polaris, Guus, Kayla

Let’s kick off with a bang. Kayla, Aidan, and Peach already form an explosive damage-based trio. When you throw Guus into the mix, you’re stacking even more crit chances and healing. Add Polaris to stun the enemy front line and you’ve got one chaotic, fast-paced team that’s as aggressive as it is fun to watch.

2. Aidan, Kayla, Peach, Oya, Guus

This version builds on the crit-synergy concept. Oya boosts healing every time a teammate lands a crit. With Kayla and Peach consistently critting and Guus handing out his golden feathers, this team turns into an unstoppable healing wall with strong burst damage. Aidan adds further sustain and shields to keep everyone alive.

3. Lars, Krista, Polaris, Guus, Tempus

With their recent buffs, Lars and Krista are back in the meta. Their AoE burst potential is insane. Polaris stuns and provides magical crit boosts, while Guus adds feather buffs, and Tempest disrupts enemy timing with slows. A great setup for wiping both squishy and tanky enemies alike.

4. Polaris, Orion, Guus, Tempest, Julius

This team is a whirlwind of speed. Orion’s rapid-fire attacks perfectly align with Guus’s feather cooldown. Polaris and Tempest offer AoE disruption, and Julius holds the front line with defense and even more crit buffs. Fast, deadly, and hard to counter.

5. Polaris, Orion, Judge, Guus, Julius

A variation of the previous team but with Judge in the mix. His synergy with Polaris when it comes to AoE control and stuns elevates the magical burst potential. Guus boosts consistency, and Julius ensures survivability. An incredibly punishing team against slow-reacting enemies.

6. Aidan, Polaris, Xe'sha, Guus, Mushy

Nicknamed the “Hydra Team”, this lineup is horrifying when it clicks. Xe'sha stacks up poison, Mushy delivers magic bursts, and Aidan keeps them all alive. Now add Polaris for crit buffs and Guus for healing plus feather support this is a team that devastates in both Arena and boss fights.

7. Lian, Polaris, Guus, Satori, Astaroth

This team is all about control and counterplay. Lian charms enemies, Satori (now reworked!) punishes energy users, Astaroth tanks and revives, and Guus ties it together with crit buffs and healing. Polaris boosts everyone’s magic crit rate this setup shuts down most meta threats before they can react.

8. Fafnir, Guus, Keira, Andvari, Julius

Is it time for Keira’s comeback? With Jet falling behind, Guus might be her new best friend. He grants constant crit and healing enough to keep her aggressive style alive. Fafnir boosts her physical attack, Andvari offers protection, and Julius adds shields and more crit. A full crit hit physical comp with some new-age support.

9. Aidan, Peach, Tempest, Kayla, Guus

Arguably the most fun team to play right now. Aidan shields, Peach and Kayla deal tons of physical damage, Tempus controls space and slows, and Guus keeps everyone topped off with crit-boosted heals. You can swap in Soleil, Folio, or Octavia to add variety. The synergy feels seamless.

10. Sebastian, Ishmael, Guus, Tempus, Astaroth

Combining Ishmael’s lifesteal with Guus’s crit-buffed healing is just nasty. Once Ishmael starts critting, it’s game over. Sebastian ensures no control interference, Tempus slows the enemies, and Astaroth offers a revive. A top pick for Guild Wars where timing matters most.

11. Sebastian, Yasmine, Guus, Tristan, Julius

Yasmine already crits like a monster what she needs is survivability. That’s where Guus comes in. He keeps her alive longer and lets her finish off enemies. Tristan boosts anti-magic defense and Julius holds the line with shields. You’ll be surprised how long this team lasts under pressure.

12. Artemis, Oya, Guus, Tristan, Galahad

This team is still experimental, but it shows promise. Galahad benefits greatly from Oya’s healing and Tristan’s anti-magic shields. Guus adds crit boosts and healing, keeping Artemis viable. If you’re looking to test new lineups, this is a wild card team with solid defensive potential.

13. Helios, Aidan, Guus, Tempest, Kayla

Need to counter crit teams? This is your solution. Helios is the natural anti-crit pick, and with Guus ironically boosting your own team’s crits, you can double-dip on offense and defense. Aidan adds sustain, Tempus slows, and Kayla finishes with burst. A flexible team that punishes popular comps.

Conclusion: What’s Your Favorite Guus Team?

There you have it 13 incredibly powerful Guus team compositions that are either dominating or about to dominate the current Hero Wars Alliance meta. From control-heavy lineups to hyper-aggressive crit burst builds, Guus can adapt to almost any strategy thanks to his unique hybrid support style.
