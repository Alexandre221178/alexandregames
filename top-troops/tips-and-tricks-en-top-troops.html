When you merge troops, aim to merge five at a time to create a more powerful unit. Avoid merging in smaller numbers as it can lead to wasted potential and slower progression.

3. Top Troops Ascend Guide

To ascend your troops, you need extra troops, including duplicates of ones you already have. Only merge extra troops if possible, as you will need these duplicates for ascension. This ensures that you have the necessary resources to strengthen your troops without depleting your reserves prematurely.

  • In Top Troops ascend heroes require duplicates.
  • Merge extra troops only when necessary.
  • Plan your troop usage to ensure you have enough for ascension.

4. Complete Missions for Experience

Missions provide an excellent opportunity to earn experience and various rewards. These missions include upgrades, clearing land, adventures, and more. Look for the quest missions by clicking on the scroll icon in the upper left corner. Completing these missions will help you progress faster and unlock new features.

  • Missions provide experience and rewards.
  • Includes upgrades, clearing land, and adventures.
  • Access quest missions via the scroll icon in the upper left-hand corner.

6. Participate in Events

Events are a fantastic opportunity to earn free gold, gems, and sometimes experience. Check under the battle and quests events to participate in these limited-time activities. Events often provide unique challenges and rewards, making them a valuable part of your gameplay strategy.

7. Troop Productions

Always keep your troop productions active. If you have the goal, make sure you are continuously producing troops. This ensures that you have a steady supply of troops to use for merges, battles, and other activities.

  • Keep troop productions active.

8. Spend Gems and Arena Tickets Wisely

Gems and Arena tickets are valuable resources. Spend them on gold options to upgrade your buildings. Upgraded buildings enhance your gameplay and provide better troops and resources, giving you an edge in battles and progression.

  • Spend your gems and Arena tickets on valuable gold enhancements for optimal progression.

9. Adventure Quests and Energy Management

Adventure quests and other activities require energy. Do these quests until you run out of energy. Managing your energy efficiently ensures that you maximize your gameplay time and resource gains.

  • Adventure quests require energy.
  • Complete quests until energy is depleted.
  • Efficient energy management maximizes resource gains.

10. Merchant Visits

Every two hours, a Black Mage-looking Merchant appears with random free items. Click on the Merchant to collect these items. Regular visits to the Merchant can provide valuable resources and items to aid in your progression.

  • Merchant appears every two hours.
  • Provides random free items.
  • Visit regularly for valuable resources.

11. Level Up Your Main Team

Focus on leveling up your main team first, then other members. Ensure to check their talents, skills, and other attributes. Additionally, check the story mode for free gems, which can be used to further enhance your team.

  • Prioritize leveling up your main team.
