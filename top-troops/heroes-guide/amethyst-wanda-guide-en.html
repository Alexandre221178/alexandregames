Welcome to another Top Troops guide on Alexandre Games! Today, we’re diving deep into one of the most intriguing and powerful units in the game: Amethyst Wanda.

Whether you’re looking for tips on how to use her in battle, her best allies, or how to prioritize her evolution, this guide has got you covered. Let’s get started!

Who is Amethyst Wanda?

Amethyst Wanda is a legendary Mage from the Infernal faction. Her story is as fascinating as her abilities. She stole a secret book from her faction’s elders, unlocking the crystallization powers of the amethyst. Instead of sharing her discovery, she founded her own battalion, The Brotherhood of the Amethyst, specializing in crystallization. This makes her a unique and powerful unit in Top Troops, especially when used strategically.

Amethyst Wanda’s Skills

Understanding Wanda’s skills is key to maximizing her potential. Here’s a breakdown of her abilities and how to use them effectively in battle.

1. Amethyst Conjure (Main Skill)

Amethyst Conjure is Wanda’s primary skill. She spawns a sphere that casts 4 projectiles, targeting enemies with the most remaining health. Each projectile deals 230% of her Attack Damage at max level.

Evolution Priority: 1st
Why? This skill is your bread and butter. Increasing its damage output ensures Wanda can take down high-health enemies quickly.

2. Scholar Knowledge (Crystallized)

Each projectile from Amethyst Conjure has a 75% chance to Crystallize the enemy. Crystallized units take damage equal to the strongest attack they’ve received x2 at the end of the effect, plus 2% of their attacker’s Base Damage every second.

Evolution Priority: 4th
Why? While Crystallization is powerful, it’s more effective when paired with high-damage allies. Focus on upgrading this after boosting her damage output.

3. Crystal Burst

This skill increases the number of projectiles from Amethyst Conjure to 12 at max level.

Evolution Priority: 2nd
Why? More projectiles mean more damage and a higher chance to Crystallize multiple enemies. This makes Wanda a crowd-control powerhouse.

4. Crystal Resonance

When attacking a Crystallized unit, Wanda’s Critical Chance increases by 65% at max level.

Evolution Priority: 3rd
Why? This skill synergizes well with her Crystallization abilities, but it’s more effective after you’ve boosted her base damage and projectile count.

Gear Evolution Priorities

To make the most of Wanda’s abilities, prioritize her gear evolution as follows:

1st - Attack Gear:

Boosts her damage output, making her skills more lethal.

2nd - Critical Chance Gear:

Increases her chance to deal critical hits, especially useful with Crystal Resonance.

3rd - Cooldown Gear:

Reduces the time between skill uses, allowing her to spam Amethyst Conjure more frequently.

4th - Attack Speed Gear:

Helps her deal damage faster, though it’s less critical than the above stats.

Talents Priority

When it comes to talents, focus on these in order:

  1. Damage: Increases her overall damage output.
  2. Critical Chance: Synergizes with Crystal Resonance for devastating critical hits.
  3. Attack Speed: Helps her attack faster, though it’s secondary to damage and crit chance.
  4. Health: Adds survivability, but Wanda is best kept at a safe distance from the front lines.

Best Allies for Amethyst Wanda

Wanda shines when paired with units that complement her Crystallization abilities. Here are her best allies in Top Troops:

1. Amethyst Claymore

Amethyst Claymore is a perfect match for Wanda. Her Amethyst Cascade skill deals massive damage to Crystallized units and applies a second Crystallized effect. Additionally, her Crystal Shatter ability increases her Critical Chance when attacking Crystallized enemies, making her a deadly duo with Wanda.

2. Amethyst Aegis

Amethyst Aegis provides excellent support with his Crystal Barrier skill, which applies Crystallized to enemies within a radius. His Reflective Shine also increases allies’ Block Chance, while Amethyst Ward reduces the enemy's Block Chance. These abilities work together to enhance both the offensive and defensive capabilities of Amethyst Wanda.

3. Rainbow Chariot

Rainbow Chariot is another great ally, as its Spilling The Tea skill has a high chance to apply Crystallize in an area. This synergizes well with Wanda’s abilities, allowing your team to capitalize on Crystallized enemies.

Counters to Watch Out For

While Wanda is powerful, she’s not invincible. Be cautious of:

  • Assassins: Units like Samurai that can bypass your front lines and target Wanda directly.
  • Multi-population Units: These can overwhelm Wanda if she’s not properly protected.

Battle Tips

Here are some practical tips to dominate battles with Amethyst Wanda:

  • Positioning: Keep Wanda at the back of your formation to protect her from direct attacks.
  • Synergy: Pair her with high-damage units that can capitalize on Crystallized enemies.
  • Timing: Use her skills strategically to maximize damage and crowd control.

Final Thoughts

Amethyst Wanda is a versatile and powerful unit in Top Troops, especially when used correctly. By focusing on her evolution priorities, pairing her with the right allies, and protecting her from counters, you can unleash her full potential on the battlefield. Whether you’re climbing the ranks or just having fun, Wanda is a unit worth investing in.
