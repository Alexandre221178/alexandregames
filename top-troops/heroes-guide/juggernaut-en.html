Juggernaut Overview: The Immovable Object

Juggernaut is a Legendary Frontline Tank from the Militia faction with one simple job - to stand between your team and destruction. With his 3.5-ton shield (which he lifts like it's made of plastic!), he boasts:

  • Massive 362,221 HP at max level
  • 29.4% base Defense
  • Team-wide Block Chance buffs
  • Area stun capability

When building your Top Troops best troops lineup, Juggernaut shines in formations where you need a durable anchor that also provides crowd control and team protection.

Juggernaut Skills Breakdown & Evolution Priority

1. Stomp (Main Skill)

Effect: Deals 80% damage in 2.5 radius
Cooldown: 10s
Evolution Priority: 4th (Lowest)

While this is Juggernaut's basic attack, scaling the damage isn't as important as his utility. In your Top Troops ascend guide planning, this should be your last upgrade focus.

2. Defensive Formation

Effect: +10% Block Chance to allies in 4 radius
Evolution Priority: 3rd (Medium)

This support skill is excellent in compact formations but loses value if your team is spread out or ranged-heavy. When following Top Troops best units strategies, pair Juggernaut with other melee units to maximize this buff.

3. The Fortress

Effect: Increases Stomp radius by 4.2 (more than doubles it!)
Evolution Priority: 2nd (High)

This is where Juggernaut becomes a game-changer. The massive area increase means:

  • Hitting more enemies with each Stomp
  • Better synergy with Hard Stomp's stun effect
  • More battlefield control in team fights

4. Hard Stomp

Effect: Stuns enemies for 2 seconds
Evolution Priority: 1st (Highest)

The crown jewel of Juggernaut's kit. Area stuns are incredibly valuable in Top Troops, making this your top priority in any Top Troops ascend guide for Juggernaut. That 2-second disable can:

  • Interrupt enemy ultimates
  • Stop dangerous channeled abilities
  • Buy time for your damage dealers
