Introduction to Shadow Assassins

The Shadow Assassins are the latest addition to the Infernal faction in Top Troops, and they are nothing short of legendary. With a star rank of 15 and a population cost of 6, these assassins are designed to dominate the battlefield. Their story is as intriguing as their abilities: once on the brink of defeat, they were offered immense power in exchange for their souls. They refused until the mysterious shadow added designer blades to the deal, sealing their fate as eternal warriors.

Shadow Assassins are at their strongest when all six members of the unit are alive. Their power diminishes as they lose members, making them a high-risk, high-reward unit. If you're looking for a unit that can turn the tide of battle in your favor, the Shadow Assassins are a must-have in your Top Troops lineup.

Shadow Assassins Skills Breakdown

Understanding the skills of the Shadow Assassins is crucial to maximizing their potential. Here's a detailed breakdown of each skill and how to use them effectively in battle:

1. Shadow Pact - Main Skill

Skill Description: At the start of the battle, Shadow Assassins will jump to a mirror position. Their damage increases by 40% for each entity alive in the unit. Each unit will have its own boost.

Skill Level Up Priority: 1st

Battle Tips: This skill is the backbone of the Shadow Assassins' power. Always ensure that all six members are alive to maximize their damage output. Position them carefully at the start of the battle to avoid early casualties.

2. All As One

Skill Description: After defeating an enemy, Shadow Assassins become invisible for 2 seconds.

Skill Level Up Priority: 4th

Battle Tips: Use this skill to reposition or escape dangerous situations. The invisibility can be a game-changer, allowing your assassins to avoid incoming damage or set up for their next attack.

3. Clean Cut

Skill Description: Shadow Assassins increase their Critical Chance by 40%.

Skill Level Up Priority: 3rd

Battle Tips: This skill synergizes well with gear and talents that boost critical damage. Focus on increasing their critical hit chance to make them even deadlier in combat.

4. Last Fumes

Skill Description: Whenever an entity of Shadow Assassins dies, it will create an area of radius 1.5 that deals 75% damage to any enemy inside this area.

Skill Level Up Priority: 2nd

Battle Tips: This skill ensures that even in death, the Shadow Assassins contribute to the battle. Use it to weaken enemy units clustered together, making it easier for your remaining troops to finish them off.

Gear Priority for Shadow Assassins

Equipping the right gear is essential to maximize the effectiveness of the Shadow Assassins. Here's the recommended gear priority:

1st - Attack Gear

Boosting their attack power ensures that the Shadow Assassins can deal maximum damage, especially when all six members are alive.

2nd - Critical Hit Chance Gear

Increasing their critical hit chance synergizes with their Clean Cut skill, making them even more lethal.

3rd - Move Speed Gear

Faster movement allows the Shadow Assassins to reposition quickly and avoid enemy attacks.

4th - Attack Speed Gear

Increasing their attack speed ensures that they can deal damage more frequently, making them a constant threat on the battlefield.

Talents Priority for Shadow Assassins

Choosing the right talents can further enhance the Shadow Assassins' abilities. Here's the recommended talent priority:

  1. Damage: Boosting their damage output is always a top priority, especially for a unit that relies on dealing massive damage.
  2. Critical Chance: Synergizes with their Clean Cut skill, making them even more deadly.
  3. Move Speed: Allows them to reposition quickly and avoid enemy attacks.
  4. Attack Speed: Increases their damage output by allowing them to attack more frequently.

Best Allies for Shadow Assassins

  • El Bandolero: El Bandolero is a unique and powerful ally for the Shadow Assassins. His abilities revolve around gaining strength from fallen allies, making him a perfect match for the Shadow Assassins' high-risk, high-reward playstyle. Here’s why he’s a great fit:
    • Losing a Compadre (Main Skill): Every time a non-spawned unit in El Bandolero’s army dies, his damage increases by 35%, up to a maximum of 500%. Since the Shadow Assassins are a 6-population unit, losing even one member can significantly boost El Bandolero’s damage, turning him into a late-game powerhouse.
    • Steel Your Heart: When a non-spawned unit dies, El Bandolero heals for 15% of his maximum health. This skill ensures he stays alive longer, complementing the Shadow Assassins' need for sustained survivability.
    • Last Man Standing: After 10 non-spawned units die, El Bandolero becomes invulnerable for 8 seconds, with increased movement and attack speed. This skill synergizes perfectly with the Shadow Assassins, as their losses can trigger El Bandolero’s transformation into an unstoppable force.
    Together, El Bandolero and the Shadow Assassins create a deadly duo that thrives in chaotic battles, where every loss fuels their strength.

Counters to Shadow Assassins

While the Shadow Assassins are powerful, they do have their weaknesses. Here are some units and strategies that can counter them effectively:

  • AOE (Area of Effect) Units: Units that can deal damage to multiple targets at once can quickly reduce the number of Shadow Assassins, diminishing their power.
  • High-Damage Single-Target Units: Units that can quickly take out individual Shadow Assassins can weaken the unit significantly.
  • Crowd Control Units: Units that can stun or slow the Shadow Assassins can prevent them from dealing damage and repositioning effectively.

Conclusion

The Shadow Assassins are a powerful addition to any Top Troops lineup, but they require careful management to maximize their potential. By understanding their skills, prioritizing the right gear and talents, and pairing them with the best allies, you can dominate the battlefield with this legendary unit. Remember, their strength lies in their numbers, so keep them alive and watch as they cut through your enemies with ease.
