Welcome to another Top Troops guide on Alexandre Games! Today, we’re diving deep into one of the most ferocious melee fighters in the game: Ose.

Whether you’re looking for the best way to use him in battle, how to prioritize his evolution, or which allies and counters to pair him with, this guide has got you covered. Let’s unleash the wild instincts of this legendary Wildling!

Ose’s Story

Ose is a Wildling who once lived a life of luxury as the pet of the late King Wallace III. With a fluffy bed, three servants, and a polished armor, Ose was the epitome of refinement. However, the tides of war have forced him to abandon his pampered lifestyle and embrace his wild side once again. Now, he’s a solid melee fighter with a devastating area attack that becomes even more powerful when enraged.

Ose’s Stats and Overview

Ose is a Legendary unit with a population cost of 3. At max level (300) with 15 stars, his stats are impressive:

  • Army Power: 195k
  • Attack: 22.07k
  • Attack Speed: 2
  • Cooldown: 6s
  • Health Points: 257,523
  • Move Speed: 4

Ose’s lack of critical chance, block chance, and immunities might seem like a drawback, but his raw power and area damage more than make up for it.

Ose’s Skills and Evolution Priority

Ose’s skills make him a versatile and deadly unit on the battlefield. Here’s a breakdown of each skill and how to prioritize their evolution:

1. Great President of Hell

Main Skill (6s Cooldown): Ose deals area damage equal to 80% of his regular damage.

Level-Up Effect: Damage increases by 60%, 65%, 70%, 75%, and finally 80%.

Evolution Priority: 1st

Battle Tip: This is Ose’s bread and butter skill. Use it to clear groups of enemies quickly. Pair it with Rage effects to maximize damage output.

2. Duke of Claws

Passive Skill: When enraged, Ose’s damage increases by an additional 50%.

Level-Up Effect: Damage increases by 10%, 20%, 30%, 40%, and finally 50% when enraged.

Evolution Priority: 3rd

Battle Tip: This skill synergizes well with Rage effects. Focus on keeping Ose enraged to make the most of this damage boost.

3. Mysterious Operative

Passive Skill: Any Rage effect applied to Ose lasts an extra 7 seconds. At level 3, he also becomes immune to Stun during Rage.

Level-Up Effect: Rage duration increases by 2s, 4s, 5s, 6s, and finally 7s.

Evolution Priority: 2nd

Battle Tip: This skill is crucial for maintaining Ose’s Rage state. Pair him with units that can apply Rage to keep him in his most powerful form.

4. Tiger of Truth

Passive Skill: If Great President of Hell is activated while Ose is enraged, he also applies 5 stacks of Injury.

Injury Effect: Reduces the target’s Max Health by 1% of the attacker’s damage per stack.

Level-Up Effect: Injury stacks increase to x5, x10, x15, x20, and finally x30.

Evolution Priority: 4th

Battle Tip: This skill is excellent for taking down tanky enemies. Use it to whittle down high-health targets over time.

Gear and Talent Evolution Priorities

To maximize Ose’s potential, focus on the following gear and talent upgrades:

Gear Evolution Priority

1st - Attack:

Boosts Ose’s overall attack power.

2dn - Move Speed Gear:

Helps Ose close the gap on enemies faster.

3rd - Attack Speed Gear:

Increases the frequency of his attacks.

4th - Critical Hit Chance Gear:

Adds a chance for critical hits, though this is less of a priority.

5th - Cooldown Reduction Gear:

Reduces the cooldown of his skills.

Talent Evolution Priority

When upgrading the Ose's talents, prioritize the following:

  1. Damage: Further enhances Ose’s attack power.
  2. Attack Speed: Makes Ose even more lethal in battle.

Best Allies for Ose

Ose thrives when paired with units that can apply Rage effects. Here are some of the best allies for him:

Battle Drummer

Skill 1 - Battle Heartbeat: Creates an area that applies Rage to any ally inside it.

Skill 4 - Ear Worm: Units enraged by Battle Heartbeat stay enraged for 6 seconds after leaving the area.

Synergy: Battle Drummer’s Rage effects perfectly complement Ose’s skills, allowing him to stay enraged longer and deal more damage.

Berserker

Berserker

Skill - Rage: While the Berserker’s Rage skill does not directly buff Ose, the two form a strong duo that becomes even more powerful when paired with the Battle Drummer.

Counters to Ose

While Ose is a formidable unit, he does have some weaknesses. Here are a few units and strategies that can counter him:

High-Damage Ranged Units

Units with high ranged damage can take down Ose before he gets close enough to deal significant damage.

Crowd Control Units

Units with stun or freeze abilities can disrupt Ose’s attacks and prevent him from using his skills effectively.

Conclusion

Ose is a powerful melee fighter who excels in dealing area damage and taking down groups of enemies. By prioritizing his skills, gear, and talents, and pairing him with the right allies, you can unleash his full potential on the battlefield. Whether you’re climbing the ranks in Top Troops or just looking for the best units to add to your army, Ose is a solid choice.
