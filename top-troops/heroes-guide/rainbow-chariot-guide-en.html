Rainbow Chariot

Legendary

Population: x1

Hunter | Ranged

Tier: S+

Story

Remember when the King's not-so-beloved brother dyed all of our neighboring regions dark, accidentally giving birth to a new Adventure Map? Back then, he made it clear that he hates colors, so now, King's Bay is about to counterattack with a whole lotta those! The hunters have dusted off the old pride float and turned it into the Rainbow Chariot, the deadliest weapon against everyone who wants to make the world dark!

Rainbow Chariot will shoot single-target projectiles aimed at the closest unit on its regular attack. Its Sparkling Pride attack will shoot 3 projectiles at random enemies, however far they may be, and leave behind areas that inflict damage and might Crystalize.

Skills Evolution Priority for Rainbow Chariot

Skill 1 - Sparkling Pride (Main Skill)

Effect: Rainbow Chariot shoots 3 projectiles targeting random enemies at any distance, dealing 25% of Damage in an area with a radius of 2. The area lasts for 3 seconds.

Priority: 1st

Reason: This is Rainbow Chariot's main damage skill. Since it activates frequently and has AoE (area of effect) damage, upgrading this skill ensures higher consistent DPS (damage per second), making it the top priority.

Skill 2 - Missing Queen

Effect: Increases Critical Damage by 200% and Critical Chance by 35%.

Priority: 2nd

Reason: A huge boost in Critical Damage and Chance significantly enhances Rainbow Chariot’s overall damage output. After upgrading the main skill, increasing the critical potential ensures that each attack deals much more damage.

Skill 3 - Death Drop

Effect: Increases the radius of Sparkling Pride to 4.

Priority: 3rd

Reason: Expanding the radius of Sparkling Pride means hitting more enemies with each attack. This indirectly improves the effectiveness of the main skill but is not as impactful as boosting raw damage.

Skill 4 - Spilling The Tea

Effect: The area of Sparkling Pride has a 75% chance of applying Crystallize for 4 seconds.

Crystallized Effect: At the end of the effect, the target takes damage equal to the strongest attack it has received, multiplied by 2. Additionally, Crystallized units take 2% of their attacker's Base Damage per second.

Priority: 4th

Reason: While Crystallize adds extra damage, it is situational and depends on the enemy's strongest received attack. Since it relies on other attacks to be most effective, it is best upgraded last.

Final Evolution Order:

  1. Sparkling Pride (Main Skill for consistent DPS)
  2. Missing Queen (Critical boost for more damage)
  3. Death Drop (Expanding AoE for wider impact)
  4. Spilling The Tea (Situational damage, upgraded last)

Rainbow Chariot Gears Priorities: Focus on Main Stats

1st - Attack Speed:

Highest priority - Faster attacks mean more frequent Sparkling Pride activations (main skill has 7.2s cooldown). Synergizes with Crystallize for constant DoT application.

2nd - Cooldown Reduction:

Reduces Sparkling Pride's cooldown for more AoE bursts and Crystallize procs. Essential for skill-spam playstyle.

3rd - Critical Chance:

Boosts Missing Queen's potential (+35% base). Critical hits amplify both direct damage and Crystallize's final burst.

4th - Attack Power:

Increases base damage for all attacks and Crystallize's %-based ticks. Solid but less impactful than attack speed.

5th - Move Speed:

Helps reposition for optimal AoE coverage but lower priority than offensive stats.

Optional/Situational:

Life Steal:

Can provide sustain but conflicts with glass-cannon playstyle. Only consider if survivability is an issue.

Defense/HP:

Low priority - Rainbow Chariot should focus on damage output from safe range.

Block Chance:

Not recommended - Ranged units benefit more from positioning than mitigation.

Rainbow Chariot Ranged Talent Tree Priority

1st - Critical Chance

Advantage: Increases the probability of landing critical hits, which synergizes with Missing Queen to maximize damage output.

2nd - Attack Speed

Advantage: Faster attacks mean more frequent activations of Sparkling Pride, increasing overall DPS.

3rd - Damage

Advantage: Boosts the base damage of all attacks, enhancing the effectiveness of every skill.

4th - Health

Advantage: Provides better survivability, but since Rainbow Chariot is a ranged damage dealer, offensive stats take priority over durability.

Best Allies for Rainbow Chariot

Amethyst Claymore

Synergy: Her Amethyst Cascade skill applies Crystallize, which enhances the effect of Spilling The Tea. Her Crystal Shatter also increases Critical Chance against Crystallized units, further boosting damage potential.

Amethyst Wanda

Synergy: Wanda’s Amethyst Conjure launches multiple projectiles, increasing the chance of triggering Crystallize. Her Scholar Knowledge further enhances Crystallized effects, making her a perfect partner for Rainbow Chariot.

Other Support Units

Any unit that increases Critical Chance, Attack Speed, or applies Crystallize effects will synergize well with Rainbow Chariot’s skills, maximizing damage output and control over enemies.

Battle Tips & Counters

Battle Tips

  • Rainbow Chariot can flood the battlefield with zones that Crystallize enemies. Keep in mind that this effect duplicates the highest damage taken by the target while afflicted, so pairing it with high-burst units maximizes its potential.
  • It excels at countering Melee, Support, and Frontline units, but it’s vulnerable to Assassins and Ranged attacks proper positioning is key to keeping it alive.

Rainbow Chariot Guide Conclusion

The Rainbow Chariot proves to be a formidable force on the battlefield, leveraging its unique Crystallize mechanic to amplify damage and control enemy movement. By duplicating the highest damage instance received by affected foes, it becomes particularly effective against Melee, Support, and Frontline units. However, its vulnerability to Assassins and Ranged attackers means careful positioning and team coordination are essential for success.

To fully utilize this unit's potential, players should pair it with high-burst damage dealers while maintaining proper defensive formations. When used strategically, the Rainbow Chariot can single-handedly shift the tide of battle, transforming enemy engagements into opportunities for massive damage output. Mastering its mechanics will undoubtedly elevate any player's tactical approach to combat.
