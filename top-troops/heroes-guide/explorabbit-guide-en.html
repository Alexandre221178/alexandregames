In the world of Top Troops, few units blend chaos and firepower like the legendary Explorabbit. This Hunter-class Assassin transforms battles into a disastrous egg hunt, bombarding enemies with explosive chocolate mayhem. Perfect for players seeking the Top Troops best units, his unique mechanics make him both deadly and unpredictable. Mastering him requires understanding his explosive playstyle.

As a single-population Legendary, Explorabbit specializes in deleting clustered enemies with his egg-laying antics. His ability to target backline threats first makes him invaluable against mages and ranged compositions. This guide covers everything from skill priorities to optimal team setups. Whether you’re new to Top Troops or optimizing your roster, here’s how to unleash his full potential.

Why Explorabbit is One of the Top Troops Best Units

As a single-population Legendary, Explorabbit brings massive burst damage with his unique egg-laying mechanics. His ability to target backline threats like Mages and Ranged units first makes him invaluable in many Top Troops compositions. When built correctly, he can single-handedly wipe out clusters of enemies.

Explorabbit Skill Breakdown and Evolution Priority

1. Easter Joy (Main Skill)

Priority: Max this first

The core of Explorabbit's kit. He burrows underground and plants 8 explosive eggs in a radius, each dealing 30% of his attack damage. At higher levels, this becomes one of the hardest-hitting AoE skills in Top Troops.

Battle Tip: Position Explorabbit where enemies cluster - his eggs have a 2-radius splash effect.

2. Melting Chocolate (Burn Effect)

Priority: Second to max

Upgrades Easter Joy to inflict Burn (3% base damage per second) and reduce enemy Defense by 25%. This turns his explosions into armor-shredding tools that melt tanks.

Battle Tip: Pair with other AoE units to capitalize on the defense reduction.

3. Bunny Bite (Critical Chance Boost)

Priority: Third upgrade

Permanently increases Critical Chance to 75%, making his basic attacks and egg explosions devastating.

Battle Tip: Works exceptionally well with Critical Damage gear.

4. Digging Expert (Invisibility)

Priority: Last to upgrade

Provides 2.5 seconds of invisibility after using Easter Joy, helping him survive to plant more eggs.

Battle Tip: Use this to dodge enemy ultimates or tower aggro.

Gear Evolution Priorities

1st - Attack Gear:

Directly boosts Easter Joy's explosion damage and Burn effect. The #1 priority for any Top Troops ascend guide for Explorabbit.

2nd - Critical Chance Gear:

Synergizes with Bunny Bite to achieve near-guaranteed critical hits. Essential for maximizing his Top Troops best troops potential.

3rd - Attack Speed Gear:

Helps recharge Easter Joy faster between cooldowns. Important for consistent damage output.

4th - Cooldown Gear:

Reduces downtime between egg bombardments. Useful but less impactful than other stats.

Talents Priority

  • 1. Damage: Flat damage increase benefits all aspects of his kit.
  • 2. Attack Speed: More attacks = faster skill recharge.
  • 3. Move Speed: Helps position for perfect egg placements.
  • 4. Critical Chance: Good but less vital after Bunny Bite.

Best Allies for Explorabbit

When building your Top Troops best troops lineup, these units complement Explorabbit perfectly:

  • Tanks with Crowd Control: Units that group enemies together make his egg explosions hit multiple targets.
  • Defense Reduction Supports: Stack with Melting Chocolate for near-true damage.
  • Backline Protectors: Since Explorabbit targets enemy ranged units first, protectors help him survive counterattacks.

Explorabbit Counters

While powerful, Explorabbit struggles against:

  • High-Burst Mages: Can eliminate him before he sets up eggs.
  • Reveal Effects: Counters his Digging Expert invisibility.
  • Stun Teams: Interrupts his Easter Joy channeling.

Advanced Battle Tips

  • Against multipopulation units, aim Easter Joy at the center - the splash damage wrecks clustered foes.
  • Use his initial backline targeting to eliminate enemy damage dealers quickly.
  • Time his invisibility to dodge predictable high-damage abilities.
  • In Top Troops PvP, position him opposite enemy Mages/Ranged units.

Final Verdict

Explorabbit stands out as one of the most fun and powerful Assassins in Top Troops. His unique skill set requires smart positioning but rewards players with massive area damage. By following this Top Troops guide for skill priorities, gear choices, and team synergies, you'll turn this prankster into a battle-winning machine.

Remember: The key is maximizing his Easter Joy explosions while keeping him alive to plant more eggs. Do that, and you'll have one of the Top Troops best units in your roster.
