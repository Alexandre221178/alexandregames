Elemental Witch

Hero Rank: Legendary

Faction: Wildling

Class: Mage

Rumor has it that the Elemental Witch's mother, a powerful witch herself, was under threat and feared for her daughter's life, so she abandoned her in the forest when she was a baby. This could be a sad story, but the baby grew up happily amongst the creatures of the forest and is now one of their strongest fighters, as well as a champion berry tart eater.

Max Level Stats

Stats: level 300, 15 stars

Army Power: 195k

Attack: 27.59k

Attack Speed: 3

Health Points: 64,381

Range: 8

The Elemental Witch is a powerful ranged mage who deals massive area damage while knocking enemy units back.

Elemental Witch Skills and Upgrade Priority

1. Earth - Main Skill

Effect: Each regular attack creates an area of radius 3 that knocks back enemies and Stuns them for 1s.

Stun: Units can't move or attack.

Priority: Highest - This is your primary crowd control and damage ability that affects all attacks.

2. Nature Surge

Effect: Enemies affected by Elemental Witch's regular attack have a 50% chance of becoming Entangled for 2 seconds.

Entangled: Units will have their Movement Speed reduced to 0, but they can still attack.

Priority: High - Complements the main skill by adding another layer of crowd control.

3. Forest Trap

Effect: Enemies entering an area of radius 3 around Elemental Witch will have a 50% chance of becoming Entangled for 2 seconds.

Priority: Medium - Provides defensive crowd control but has limited range initially.

4. Tricky Terrain

Effect: The radius of Forest Trap becomes 7 and causes enemies to drop their Movement Speed by 50% and Attack Speed by 30%.

Priority: Medium-High (when unlocked) - Greatly enhances Forest Trap's effectiveness.

Recommended Upgrade Priority:

  1. Earth - Main Skill: Always prioritize first as it enhances your primary damage and crowd control.
  2. Nature Surge: Upgrade next to maximize crowd control potential on your attacks.
  3. Tricky Terrain: Once available, focus on this to significantly improve your defensive capabilities.
  4. Forest Trap: Upgrade last as its base form is already effective enough in early/mid game.

This prioritization focuses first on enhancing your offensive crowd control and damage, then improving your defensive capabilities, making the Elemental Witch a versatile threat at all ranges.

Elemental Witch - Optimal Gear Priorities

1st - Attack Speed:

Highest priority as it directly synergizes with Earth (Main Skill) and Nature Surge, allowing more frequent attacks that trigger area stuns and entanglement effects. Faster attacks mean more crowd control.

2nd - Attack:

Increases damage output for her area attacks. While CC is her strength, higher damage makes her knockbacks more threatening and helps clear waves faster.

3rd - Move Speed:

Helps position herself optimally to use Forest Trap's area effect and maintain safe distance. Also allows quicker repositioning after knockbacks.

4th - Health Points:

As a ranged unit with CC, survivability ensures she stays alive to keep applying her effects. Complements her lack of defensive stats.

5th - Cooldown:

Secondary benefit as most of her power comes from regular attacks, but helps with any active skills she might have.

Lower Priority Gears (Not Recommended):

  • Critical Chance/Damage: Her skills don't benefit from crits
  • Block Chance/Defense: Not optimal for a backline mage
  • Life Steal: Doesn't synergize with her kit

Gear Evolution Strategy: Focus first on maximizing Attack Speed to amplify her crowd control potential, then balance with Attack to increase threat. Move Speed and HP provide quality-of-life improvements. Ignore defensive stats as she shouldn't be taking direct hits.

Elemental Witch - Talent Tree Priority

1st - Damage

Why: Directly amplifies the effectiveness of her Earth (Main Skill) area attacks and overall DPS. More damage means her knockbacks become more threatening and helps clear waves faster. Synergizes perfectly with her role as an area control mage.

2nd - Attack Speed

Why: Increases frequency of her regular attacks which trigger both the stun (Earth skill) and entanglement (Nature Surge) effects. More attacks = more crowd control. Essential for maximizing her disruptive potential.

3rd - Health

Why: Provides survivability to maintain her position in battles. While she's ranged, some health ensures she survives occasional hits or AoE attacks to keep applying her crowd control effects.

Talent Synergy with Skills:

  • Damage + Earth Skill: Stronger knockback attacks that can potentially eliminate weakened enemies
  • Attack Speed + Nature Surge: More chances to trigger entanglement effects
  • Health + Forest Trap: Lets her survive longer to make use of her defensive entanglement zone

Talents to Avoid:

  • Critical Chance/Damage: Her skills don't benefit from critical hits
  • Defensive Talents (Block/Defense): Not optimal for a backline mage

Focus exclusively on talents that enhance her primary role as an area-control, crowd-focused mage.

Best Allies for Elemental Witch

Top Synergy Partners:

  • Frontline Tanks (Stone Guardian, Ice Blade): Keep enemies away while she applies crowd control from safety
  • Healers (Sacred Priest, Druid): Compensate her low defense with sustained healing
  • Single-Target DPS (Dark Assassin, Fire Archer): Eliminate high-value targets she stuns/entangles
  • Other AoE Mages (Stormcaller): Stack area control effects for maximum disruption

Synergy Breakdown:

  • Her knockbacks/stuns create perfect setups for melee allies
  • Entanglement effects enable ranged allies to focus fire
  • Slows from Tricky Terrain help position-dependent allies

Elemental Witch Counters

Strong Against:

  • Amethyst Wanda/Mages: Interrupts their channeling with frequent stuns
  • Frontline Tanks: Can knock them back to disrupt formations
  • Slow Melee Units: Keeps them permanently CC'd with proper timing

Weak Against:

  • Assassins (Shadow, Nightblade): Can bypass her CC and burst her down
  • Longer Range Snipers: Can outrange her knockback/stun area

Conclusion

The Elemental Witch excels as a disruptive backline mage specializing in area control. Her combination of:

  • Frequent stuns (Earth skill)
  • Movement impairment (Nature Surge/Forest Trap)
  • Position disruption (knockbacks)

Makes her ideal for:

  • Countering mage-heavy compositions
  • Disrupting frontline formations
  • Enabling focused fire from allies

Playstyle Tip: Position her safely behind tanks, prioritize attack speed to maximize crowd control, and pair with single-target damage dealers to capitalize on her disabling effects.
