If you're looking for one of the best units in Top Troops, look no further than Thor. This hammer-wielding powerhouse sits comfortably in the S-tier of our Top Troops tier list, and for good reason. In this Top Troops guide, we'll break down everything you need to know to maximize Thor's potential in battle.

Why Thor is a Must-Have Unit

As a Divine faction melee unit, Thor brings incredible damage and crowd control to your army. His ability to smash through enemy lines while pushing them back makes him invaluable for both offense and defense. According to our Top Troops ascend guide, Thor is one of the highest priority units to upgrade when you get him.

At max level (300/15 stars), Thor boasts:

  • 195k Army Power
  • 33.11k Attack
  • 191,301 HP
  • 4 Move Speed

Thor's Skills Breakdown

Shocking Volt (Main Skill)

Every 6 seconds, Thor smashes the ground dealing 70% damage in a 1 radius area. This is your bread and butter ability that makes Thor so powerful in the frontline.

Grounded

Increases Health by 20% and grants immunity to Electrocute at Level 3. While important for survivability, we recommend prioritizing this 4th in your Top Troops ascend guide for Thor.

Smite

This evolution increases Shocking Volt's radius to 3, making it hit more enemies. This should be your #1 priority when ascending Thor as it dramatically increases his effectiveness.

Electrifying Presence

Enemies hit by Shocking Volt become Electrocuted for 6 seconds. This debuff synergizes with many other powerful units, making it our 2nd priority for evolution.

Skill Evolution Priority

  1. Smite - Bigger area means more damage and more Electrocuted enemies
  2. Electrifying Presence - Enables powerful synergies with other units
  3. Shocking Volt - Base damage increase
  4. Grounded - While useful, the other skills provide more immediate impact

Gear Evolution Priorities

1st - Cooldown Gear:

Reducing Thor's skill cooldown means more frequent Shocking Volt smashes and more enemies Electrocuted. This directly increases both his damage and crowd control capabilities.

2nd - Attack Gear:

Straight damage increase makes Thor hit harder with both his skills and basic attacks. Essential for a frontline damage dealer.

3rd - Critical Chance Gear:

Thor's x2 critical damage multiplier makes crit chance valuable for bursting down tough enemies.

4th - Attack Speed Gear:

More attacks between skills means more consistent damage output.

5th - Life Steal Gear:

Helps Thor sustain in prolonged fights, though his high health pool means this is less critical.

Talents Priority

  1. Damage - Maximizes Thor's primary role as a damage dealer
  2. Attack Speed - More attacks between skills
  3. Health - Helps him survive longer in the frontline

Best Allies for Thor

Thor shines when paired with units that benefit from or enhance his Electrocute effect. Here are the best troops to pair with him:

Hermes (Legendary A+)

This Divine assassin gains 75% critical chance against Electrocuted enemies and can teleport behind enemy lines, creating perfect synergy with Thor's crowd control.

Storm (Legendary S)

Another Divine faction powerhouse, Storm can stun Electrocuted enemies and deals bonus damage to them, creating a devastating combo with Thor.

Wave Warrior Drenthest (Legendary S)

Deals 80% bonus damage to Electrocuted enemies affected by her main skill while reducing their defense by 30% - perfect for Thor's playstyle.

Thundershot (Legendary A+)

Her attacks slow Electrocuted enemies by 40% for 8 seconds, making it easier for Thor to smash them repeatedly.

Zeus (Legendary A+)

While not as directly synergistic as others, Zeus benefits from Thor's Electrocute and can help clear backline enemies Thor pushes around.

Counters to Thor

While Thor is one of the best units in Top Troops, he can be countered by:

  • High burst damage units that can take him down before he gets multiple Shocking Volts off
  • Crowd control specialists that can keep him from reaching optimal positions
  • Ranged kiting teams that can avoid his melee attacks while whittling him down

Final Verdict

Thor absolutely deserves his spot in the S-tier of any Top Troops tier list. His combination of area damage, crowd control, and durability makes him one of the most versatile frontline units in the game. When following our Top Troops ascend guide for Thor and pairing him with the right allies, he can carry your team through most content.

Remember to prioritize his Smite evolution first, focus on cooldown reduction gear, and build a team around his Electrocute synergy. Do this, and you'll have one of the best troops in Top Troops smashing through your enemies like the God of Thunder he is!
