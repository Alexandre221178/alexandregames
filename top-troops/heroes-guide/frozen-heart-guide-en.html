Introduction

Rarity: Legendary

Faction: Militia

Class: Mage

Population: x1

After being dethroned and exiled from her kingdom, Frozen Heart sought asylum in King's Bay. It was given to her under the condition that she joined the militia. She's not friendly with any of the soldiers, but she's the only one who can stand close to Frost Widow for over 10 minutes without dying of hypothermia.

This cold-hearted queen specializes in freezing frontline targets over a large area. Refrain from freezing her back, or she will counterattack even more fiercely! Her unique abilities make her one of the most powerful crowd control specialists in Top Troops, capable of turning the tide of battle with well-timed freezes.

Frozen Heart Skills Upgrade Priority in Top Troops

1. The Cold Shoulder - Main Skill (Priority #1)

How it works: Frozen Heart releases a cold wind wave that freezes all enemies in a 10-radius area for 1 second (+6 max range). Frozen units cannot move or attack.

Upgrade reason: This is your core crowd control ability - upgrading it first improves your initiation and teamfight potential. The freeze is crucial for setting up other skills and protecting your team.

2. Long Winter (Priority #2)

How it works: Increases The Cold Shoulder's freeze duration by 4 seconds (total 5s freeze).

Upgrade reason: Extending the freeze duration dramatically improves your crowd control. A 5-second AoE freeze is game-changing and synergizes with Biting Cold.

3. Biting Cold (Priority #3)

How it works: Deals 10% of Frozen Heart's damage per second to frozen units (scales with your damage).

Upgrade reason: While important, it relies on freezing enemies first. Upgrade after enhancing your freeze capabilities. Becomes deadly with longer freezes from Long Winter.

4. Room Temperature (Priority #4)

How it works: Prevents Frozen Heart from being frozen - instead, freezing attacks boost her damage by 10%.

Upgrade reason: Valuable defensive skill, but lower priority than offensive capabilities. Upgrade last unless facing many freeze enemies.

Frozen Heart Gear Priorities

1st - Cooldown Reduction:

Top priority to spam her game-changing Main Skill "The Cold Shoulder" more frequently. More freezes = more crowd control and more damage through "Biting Cold".

2nd - Attack:

Directly boosts "Biting Cold" damage (10% of her ATK) and her overall DPS. Synergizes with "Room Temperature" damage buff when enemies try to freeze her.

3rd - Move Speed:

Essential for positioning her AoE freeze optimally. Helps maintain distance from threats while her skills recharge.

4th - Health Points:

Provides survivability since she lacks defensive skills (except "Room Temperature"). A living mage deals more damage than a dead one.

5th - Attack Speed:

Secondary DPS boost between skill casts, though less valuable than raw ATK for her kit.

6th - Defense:

Added protection against physical damage dealers who might reach her.

7th - Critical Chance:

Low priority as her damage primarily comes from skills rather than basic attacks.

8th - Block Chance:

Minimal value for a backline mage who shouldn't be taking many hits.

9th - Life Steal:

Nearly useless since she deals most damage through freeze effects (Biting Cold) which don't trigger lifesteal.

Frozen Heart Talent Tree Priority

1st - Damage

Benefits for skills: Directly increases all damage including "Biting Cold" (scales with 10% of ATK) and basic attacks. Maximizes damage potential when "Room Temperature" triggers.

2nd - Attack Speed

Benefits for skills: Helps charge Main Skill faster and provides more DPS between freezes. Works well with her increased range from "The Cold Shoulder".

3rd - Critical Chance

Benefits for skills: Adds occasional damage spikes to "Biting Cold" and basic attacks. Lower priority as her freeze-based kit doesn't rely heavily on crits.

4th - Health

Benefits for skills: Provides survival to maintain crowd control. While "Room Temperature" prevents freeze, she still needs HP against burst damage.

Best Allies for Frozen Heart

Eira (Legendary - S+ Assassin)

Synergy: Her battlefield teleport and freeze area (3.25s) combos perfectly with Frozen Heart's abilities. Eira's critical chance boost against frozen units (+75%) works great with Frozen Heart's "Biting Cold" damage-over-time effect.

Frozen Bowman (Legendary - S+ Ranged)

Synergy: His normal attacks create freeze areas (1.5s) that set up Frozen Heart's "Biting Cold". His defense reduction (-8%) makes Frozen Heart's damage more effective against frozen targets.

Other Freeze Synergy Notes:

All these allies benefit from Frozen Heart's "Long Winter" (+4s freeze duration) which extends their freeze-based effects. Frozen Heart's "Room Temperature" damage boost (+10%) becomes more valuable when allies trigger additional freeze effects.

Frozen Heart Counters

High Burst Assassins

Reason: Can eliminate Frozen Heart before she sets up her freeze combos (like Samurai Guide or Explorabbit Guide).

Long-range Snipers

Reason: Units that outrange her (like Rainbow Chariot) can attack before she positions for her freeze.

Final Verdict: The Ultimate Freeze Dominator

Frozen Heart stands as one of Top Troops' most powerful crowd control specialists when properly supported. Her ability to lock down entire enemy teams for up to 5 seconds (with Long Winter upgrade) creates game-changing opportunities for your damage dealers.

Key Strengths: - Best AoE freeze duration in game - Scales well with attack stats through Biting Cold - Unique anti-freeze mechanic with Room Temperature - Perfect synergy with Militia freeze comps

Ideal Usage: 1. Pair with Eira/Frozen Bowman for freeze chains 2. Position carefully to maximize her 10-radius freeze 3. Upgrade Main Skill first for maximum control 4. Use cooldown gear for more freeze uptime

While vulnerable to fire units and burst damage, proper team composition and positioning make Frozen Heart an A+ tier mage for any freeze-focused strategy. Her exile's revenge becomes your enemy's nightmare when mastered.
