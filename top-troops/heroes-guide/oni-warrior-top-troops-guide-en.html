Story of Oni Warrior: The Infernal Prodigy

The Oni Warrior wasn't always the disciplined powerhouse you see today. Once a student at Samurai School, she failed her anger management test so disastrously that her return to the Underworld was celebrated as a triumph. Her faction, recognizing her fiery potential, rewarded her with a custom-designed weapon, perfectly suited to channel her unrelenting rage into devastating attacks.

With her explosive temper and unparalleled strength, the Oni Warrior quickly became a legend among her Infernal allies. Her ability to deal massive single-target damage has earned her a fearsome reputation, making her an indispensable unit in both offense and defense.

Skills of Oni Warrior and Tips for Using Them in Battles

Heaven-Piercing Slam (Main Skill)

  • Cooldown: 5 seconds
  • Effect: Slams her weapon into the ground, dealing 200% of her attack damage.
  • Level-Up Effects: Damage increases by increments of +120%, +140%, +160%, +180%, and +200%.

Battle Tips: Heaven-Piercing Slam is Oni Warrior's bread and butter for delivering heavy single-target damage. Use this skill to take down high-priority enemies like tanky front-liners or key DPS units. Pair it with gear that reduces cooldowns to increase its frequency and maximize impact.

Hot-Blooded Brawler (Rage Skill)

  • Effect: Increases movement and attack speed by 25% and boosts damage by 15%.
  • Trigger: Has a 50% chance of activating for 4 seconds whenever Oni Warrior takes damage.
  • Level-Up Effects: Rage chance increases up to +50%.

Battle Tips: This skill makes Oni Warrior a fearsome brawler, especially when surrounded by enemies. Deploy her in the frontline to absorb damage and trigger Rage frequently. Pair her with healers or defensive units to keep her alive while she wreaks havoc.

King of Fighters (Passive Skill)

  • Effect: Boosts attack damage by 50%.
  • Level-Up Effects: Additional damage increases in increments of +10%, +20%, +30%, +40%, and +50%.

Battle Tips: King of Fighters amplifies Oni Warrior’s raw damage potential, making her one of the deadliest melee units. Prioritize leveling this skill early to maximize her output. Equip attack-boosting gear to synergize with this passive ability.

Conqueror's Will (Critical Skill)

  • Effect: Increases the chance of Heaven-Piercing Slam dealing a critical hit by 75%.
  • Level-Up Effects: Critical chance increases in increments of +30%, +40%, +50%, +60%, and +75%.

Battle Tips: Critical hits are the key to turning Oni Warrior into an unstoppable force. Focus on building her critical hit chance with gear and talents to make Conqueror’s Will truly shine. This skill is particularly effective against tanky opponents, allowing her to cut through defenses with ease.

Evolution Priorities for Oni Warrior

When upgrading Oni Warrior, focus on the following priorities to align with her core strengths:

  1. Heaven-Piercing Slam: Boost her primary damage skill first to maximize single-target damage.
  2. King of Fighters: Increase her attack power to amplify all other abilities.
  3. Conqueror's Will: Enhance her critical hit potential for devastating strikes.
  4. Hot-Blooded Brawler: Upgrade last to make her more resilient and mobile in prolonged battles.

Gear Priorities for Oni Warrior

5th - Attack Speed:

Talent Priority

When upgrading the Oni Warrior’s talents, prioritize the following:

  • Attack Power: Increases her overall effectiveness in combat. Directly enhances the damage of skills like Heaven-Piercing Slam and synergizes with King of Fighters to maximize her raw damage output.
  • Attack Speed: Boosts her ability to deal consistent damage quickly. Works well with Hot-Blooded Brawler, allowing her to capitalize on the speed boost and increase her damage during Rage.

Best Allies for Oni Warrior

Pairing Oni Warrior with the right allies can significantly increase her effectiveness. Here are some of the best units to team up with her:

  • Healers (e.g., Meiga, Sekhmet, Herbalist): These units help keep Oni Warrior alive longer, allowing her to maximize her damage potential over time.
  • Buffers (e.g., Meiga, Inferno Shredder): Units that enhance critical chance (like Meiga) or increase allies' damage (like Inferno Shredder) synergize perfectly with Oni Warrior's skills.
  • Tanks (e.g., Holy Protector): High-defense units that boost Max Health and Block Chance provide a solid frontline, giving Oni Warrior the space she needs to operate effectively.

Counters to Oni Warrior

Despite her power, Oni Warrior has some weaknesses that can be exploited:

  • Crowd Control Units (e.g., Frozen Heart, Elemental With): Stuns, Freezes or slows can neutralize her high damage potential.
  • High DPS Ranged Units (e.g., Amethyst Wanda, Rainbow Chariot): Can whittle her down from a distance before she reaches them.

How to Use Oni Warrior in Different Compositions

  • Infernal Faction Teams: Pair her with other Infernal units to benefit from faction synergy buffs.
  • Balanced Teams: Combine her with tanks, healers, and ranged DPS for a well-rounded lineup.
  • Aggressive Teams: Use her as the centerpiece of an all-out offense strategy, backed by attack buffs and cooldown reduction.

FAQs

What makes Oni Warrior a legendary unit in Top Troops?
Oni Warrior's unique combination of massive single-target damage, critical hit potential, and Rage mechanics make her one of the most versatile and powerful melee units in the game.
How do you maximize Oni Warrior’s damage?
Focus on leveling up her Heaven-Piercing Slam and King of Fighters skills, and equip gear that boosts attack power and critical chance.
What is the best way to counter Oni Warrior?
Use crowd control units to stun or slow her, and deploy high DPS ranged units to eliminate her from a distance.
Should I prioritize critical hit chance for Oni Warrior?
Yes, critical hit chance is essential to maximizing her damage output, especially with Conqueror’s Will enhancing critical potential.
What faction does Oni Warrior belong to?
Oni Warrior is part of the Infernal faction, known for their fiery, high-damage units.
Is Oni Warrior worth investing resources into?
Absolutely! Her versatility and power make her a valuable addition to any team composition.
