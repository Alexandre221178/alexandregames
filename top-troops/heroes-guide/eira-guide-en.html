If you're searching for a Top Troops best unit to shred backline enemies while controlling the battlefield, Eira is your frosty answer. This agile assassin teleports behind enemy lines, freezing high-value targets like Mages and Rangers making her a game-changer in both PvP and PvE. Let's break down how to maximize her potential!

Why Eira is a Must-Have Unit

During her time as a spy in the Far North, Eira mastered icy daggers and polar bear diplomacy (seriously!). Now in your army, she:

  • ✅ Teleports instantly to mirror her starting position at battle start
  • ✅ Freezes groups of enemies for 3.25 seconds (longer than most stuns!)
  • ✅ Deletes frozen targets with +75% crit chance against them
  • ✅ Gets 3 seconds of invisibility to avoid initial focus fire

No wonder players hunting a Top Troops ascend guide prioritize Eira she's one of the best disruptive assassins.

Eira's Skills: Priority & Battle Tips

1. Tip of the Iceberg (Main Skill)

Priority: MAX THIS FIRST

Why prioritize: This is Eira's core identity as one of the Top Troops best units. The teleport-and-freeze combo does three critical things:

  • 🔄 Positioning Control: Instant backline access disrupts enemy formations
  • ❄️ Crowd Control: 3.25s freeze is longer than most stuns in the game
  • ⏱️ Cooldown Synergy: Base 6s cooldown means frequent reapplications

Advanced Tip: The teleport mirroring works both vertically and horizontally - place her opposite enemy carries.

2. Feel The North

Priority: 2nd

Why prioritize: Doubling the freeze radius (2→4) transforms her from single-target to teamfight monster because:

  • 📈 Value Scaling: Hits 4x more area (πr² math!)
  • 🔄 Combo Potential: Enables multi-unit executes with Cold Hearted
  • 🛡️ Defensive Utility: Can freeze approaching melee units mid-dash

Tip: This upgrade makes her freeze impossible to dodge in clustered fights.

3. Cold Hearted

Priority: 3rd

Why prioritize: The +75% crit against frozen targets seems simple but has hidden depth:

  • 💥 Damage Multiplier: Effectively a 1.75x damage amp when combo'd
  • ⚔️ Execute Threshold: Lets her kill targets she couldn't otherwise
  • 🧊 Synergy Value: Works with ANY freeze source (allies/items)

Note: Prioritize this lower because it needs other skills/enablers to shine.

4. Snow Camouflage

Priority: Last

Why deprioritize: While invisibility sounds great, it has limitations:

  • ⏳ Duration Issues: 3s is often shorter than enemy engage animations
  • 🎯 Targeting Logic: Enemies may retarget to your squishies instead
  • 🔄 Opportunity Cost: Other skills provide more consistent value

Tip: Only upgrade this after core freeze mechanics are maxed.

Gear Evolution Priorities

Gear choices amplify Eira's strengths as a burst assassin. Here's the Top Troops guide priority list:

1st - Cooldown Gear:

More freezes = more control. Reducing her 6s freeze cooldown lets her lock down enemies constantly.

2nd - Attack Gear:

Raw damage ensures frozen targets don't survive her crit onslaught.

3rd - Critical Chance Gear:

Synergizes with Cold Hearted for massive execute damage.

4th - Attack Speed Gear:

Helps stack damage between freezes.

Talents Priority

  1. Damage: More burst = faster kills on frozen priority targets.
  2. Critical Chance: Capitalizes on her anti-freeze passive.
  3. Move Speed: Helps reposition after teleporting.
  4. Attack Speed: Good but less impactful than raw damage/crit.

Eira's Best Team Comps: Top Freeze Synergies in Top Troops

Eira dominates when paired with these powerful allies that amplify her freeze mechanics and assassination potential:

1. Frozen Heart - The Freeze Amplifier

This legendary mage is Eira's perfect partner. His massive area-of-effect freeze (10-range!) sets up Eira for easy kills. The synergy gets even more brutal because:

  • His freeze triggers Eira's +75% crit chance against frozen targets
  • He deals bonus damage to frozen enemies too - doubling the punishment
  • They share the Militia faction bonus for extra stats

2. Frozen Bowman - The Icy Executioner

This S+ tier archer creates deadly freeze zones that make Eira unstoppable:

  • His attacks have 50% chance to freeze, giving Eira more crit opportunities
  • Reduces enemy defense by 8% in freeze zones - making Eira hit even harder
  • Starts battle invisible like Eira, creating a sneaky backline duo

3. Ymir - The Frozen Melee

This melee damage dealer becomes terrifying when paired with Eira because:

  • Freeze-Enhanced Attacks: Ymir's 2nd skills deal bonus damage to frozen enemies (even though he can't freeze them himself)
  • Melee Pressure: While Eira freezes the backline, Ymir smashes through frontline enemies caught in her freeze zones

Ultimate Freeze Comp Strategy

  1. Position Eira to teleport onto enemy backline
  2. Have Frozen Heart drop his massive AOE freeze
  3. Let Frozen Bowman reduce enemy defenses
  4. Watch Eira delete frozen targets with 75% crits
  5. Ymir hits frozen enemies harder - use him to finish off targets Eira freezes.

Pro Tip: This comp works best against teams relying on fragile backline damage dealers. Against heavy frontline comps, consider swapping Ymir for another damage dealer.

Counters to Watch For

Even the best Top Troops units have weaknesses. Eira struggles against:

  • Anti-Assassin Tanks: Units that punish melee attackers can burst her down.
  • Frozen Heart's Ice Amplification: Just like her "Ice attacks against frozen enemies deal +10% damage" passive, some units can have abilities that punish freeze effects. This means Eira's own freeze can backfire if the enemy team has these anti-freeze specialists.
  • High-Burst AOE: She's fragile if caught in crossfire.

Final Verdict: Is Eira Worth It?

Absolutely. Whether you're following a Top Troops ascend guide or just want a versatile assassin, Eira delivers. Her unique teleport-and-freeze combo disrupts enemy strategies while deleting high-value targets. Prioritize her skills and gear as outlined, pair her with synergistic allies, and watch enemies shiver in fear!

Pro tip: Try starting her on different battlefield tiles in practice mode to master her teleport mirroring it’s the key to unleashing her full potential as one of the Top Troops best units.
