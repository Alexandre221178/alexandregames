Introduction to the Samurai

The Samurai is a Legendary unit in Top Troops, belonging to the Militia class and specializing as an Assassin. With a population cost of just 1, this unit is a powerful addition to any army. Known for their honor and unique combat style, Samurai are not your typical assassins.

Instead of sneaking behind enemies, they charge forward, slashing through their foes with precision and reducing their max Health. If you're looking for a unit that combines strength, strategy, and style, the Samurai is a must-have.

Samurai Skills Explained

Understanding the Samurai's skills is key to maximizing their potential in battle. Here's a breakdown of each skill and how to use them effectively:

1. Oni-Giri - Main Skill

Oni-Giri is the Samurai's primary ability. When the Samurai reaches their first opponent, they teleport behind the enemy and perform an area attack dealing 300% of their Attack Damage. This attack also applies a Stun effect for 1 second, giving your team a brief advantage.

Skill Evolution Priority: 1st. This skill is the backbone of the Samurai's combat effectiveness, so prioritize upgrading it first.

2. Song of the Lion

Song of the Lion applies an Injury effect, reducing the target's max Health by 1% of the attacker's Damage. This effect can stack, and Oni-Giri applies 100 stacks of Injury to enemies. This makes the Samurai a great unit for weakening tough opponents over time.

Skill Evolution Priority: 2nd. This skill is crucial for dealing with high-health enemies, so upgrade it after Oni-Giri.

3. Dance of the Dragon

Dance of the Dragon increases the vertical range of Oni-Giri to 12. This allows the Samurai to hit more enemies in a wider area, making them even more effective in crowd control.

Skill Evolution Priority: 4th. While useful, this skill is less critical than others, so focus on it last.

4. Rush of the Bull

Rush of the Bull makes the Samurai invisible for 2 seconds after performing Oni-Giri. This allows them to avoid enemy attacks and reposition themselves for the next strike.

Skill Evolution Priority: 3rd. This skill provides excellent survivability, so upgrade it after Song of the Lion.

Best Allies for Samurai

Pairing the Samurai with the right allies can make your army unstoppable. Here are some of the best units to team up with:

  • Ose: Applies Injury to enemies, complementing the Samurai's Song of the Lion skill.
  • Girtablilu: Deals ranged damage and applies Injury, making it a great partner for the Samurai.

Counters to Watch Out For

While the Samurai is a powerful unit, they do have weaknesses. Be cautious when facing:

  • High-DPS Units: Units with high damage output can quickly take down the Samurai.
  • Crowd Control Units: Enemies with stuns or freeze abilities can disrupt the Samurai's attacks.

Final Tips for Using Samurai

To get the most out of your Samurai, keep these tips in mind:

  • Always prioritize upgrading Oni-Giri and Song of the Lion first.
  • Pair the Samurai with units that can apply Injury or deal ranged damage.
  • Use gear and talents to boost Attack and Attack Speed for maximum effectiveness.
  • Be mindful of counters and adjust your strategy accordingly.
