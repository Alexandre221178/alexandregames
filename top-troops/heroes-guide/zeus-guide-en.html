Introduction to Zeus

Meet Zeus, the self-proclaimed "God of Glitz" in Top Troops. This Legendary Divine Mage is a force to be reckoned with on the battlefield. With his golden bracelets and jewelled beard, Zeus struts around like a gilded peacock, claiming every shiny thing as his "divine right."

But don't let his smug demeanor fool you Zeus is a powerhouse who can rain thunderbolts on his enemies and turn the tide of any battle.

In this guide, we'll dive deep into Zeus's skills, evolution priorities, best allies, and counters. Whether you're looking for a Top Troops guide or specifically want to master Zeus, this article has you covered.

Zeus's Skills and Evolution Priorities

Zeus's skills make him a versatile and deadly unit in Top Troops. Here's a breakdown of each skill and how to prioritize their evolution:

1. Divine Storm - Main Skill

Cooldown: 8s

Zeus unleashes one lightning bolt attack on the closest enemy target. The number of lightning bolts increases by 1 (up to 6) for every electrocuted enemy. Each bolt deals 500% of Zeus's damage.

Skill Evolution Priority: 2nd

Battle Tip: Use this skill in crowded battles to maximize the number of lightning bolts. Pair Zeus with allies who can apply Electrocute for maximum damage.

2. Olympian Pressure

Entities affected by Divine Storm have their Attack Speed reduced by 40% for 8 seconds.

Skill Evolution Priority: 3rd

Battle Tip: This skill is excellent for slowing down high-damage enemies. Use it strategically to cripple enemy DPS units.

3. Call me Piter, Ju Piter

Each of Zeus's regular attacks has a 75% chance of applying Electrocute.

Electrocute Effect: Targets receive 2% of the attacker's base damage every second and an additional 5% for every attack received.

Skill Evolution Priority: 1st

Battle Tip: This skill is Zeus's bread and butter. Prioritize evolving it first to maximize his damage output and synergy with other Electrocute-based allies.

4. Titan Slayer

Zeus's Attack Speed increases by 25%.

Skill Evolution Priority: 4th

Battle Tip: While this skill is useful, it's less critical than the others. Focus on it after upgrading the core skills.

Gear Evolution Priorities

Choosing the right gear for Zeus is crucial to maximizing his potential. Here's the priority list for gear evolution:

1st - Attack Gear

Boosting Zeus's attack power directly increases the damage of his lightning bolts and Electrocute effects.

2nd - Attack Speed Gear

Faster attacks mean more chances to apply Electrocute and trigger Divine Storm.

3rd - Cooldown Gear

Reducing cooldowns allows Zeus to use Divine Storm more frequently.

4th - Move Speed Gear

While not a priority, increased move speed can help Zeus reposition during battles.

Talents Priority

Here's how to prioritize Zeus's talents to complement his skills:

  1. Damage: Increases overall damage output, making his lightning bolts and Electrocute effects even deadlier.
  2. Attack Speed: Enhances his ability to apply Electrocute and trigger Divine Storm.
  3. Health: Provides survivability, ensuring Zeus stays on the battlefield longer.

Best Allies for Zeus

Zeus thrives when paired with allies who can amplify his Electrocute effects and provide crowd control. Here are his best allies:

Storm:

Storm is a perfect match for Zeus. Her skills synergize well with his Electrocute effects:

  • Doudensei: Chains lightning bolts to multiple targets, spreading Electrocute.
  • Raiu: Deals massive damage to Electrocuted enemies, complementing Zeus's Divine Storm.
  • Kandenshi: Adds more Electrocute chances, enhancing Zeus's damage output.
  • Mahi: Stuns Electrocuted enemies, providing crowd control.

Strong Against

Zeus has a strong advantage against certain enemy types due to his powerful lightning abilities. He is particularly effective against:

Single Frontliners

Zeus' Divine Storm increases in power as more enemies are Electrocuted. Against single frontliners, he can stack multiple lightning strikes quickly, dealing massive burst damage.

Melee Units

Zeus excels against melee units because his ranged attacks allow him to strike from a distance, preventing them from closing in. Additionally, Olympian Pressure reduces their attack speed, making them less effective in battle.

Counters to Zeus

While Zeus is a powerful unit, he has his weaknesses. Here are some counters to watch out for:

Amethyst Claymore

Amethyst Claymore's Amethyst Conductivity makes her immune to Electrocute. If she receives an attack that would Electrocute her, her Damage increases by 20% for 6 seconds. This effect can stack, making her a significant threat to Zeus.

Multipopulation Ranged Units

Zeus struggles against ranged units with multiple entities, as his single-target lightning strikes are less effective against swarms. These units can overwhelm him by spreading out the damage and avoiding concentrated Electrocute effects.

Final Thoughts

Zeus is a game-changing unit in Top Troops, especially when used strategically with the right allies and gear. By prioritizing his skills, gear, and talents, you can unleash his full potential and dominate the battlefield. Whether you're following a Top Troops ascend guide or looking for the best units, Zeus is a must-have in your lineup.
