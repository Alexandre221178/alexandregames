While hero availability varies from player to player, some epic heroes are consistently valuable across different setups. The following heroes are especially effective for F2P players looking to strengthen their teams.

Archers (Hunters): These ranged heroes are critical in reaching enemy backlines, targeting support characters like healers that can prolong a battle. Their ability to deal damage from a distance makes them essential for any F2P strategy.

Electricians and Conductors

Conductors and Electricians: Heroes that deal elemental damage, such as electricity, are powerful assets. They can paralyze enemies, allowing your team to strike without retaliation. Their ability to spread status effects makes them highly valuable in long battles.

Golems and Tanks

Golems: As some of the most durable heroes in Top Troops, Golems have enormous health pools and can absorb damage for your entire team. Pair them with healers for an almost impenetrable frontline defense.

Healers

Healers: Every team needs a reliable healer. Their ability to keep your front line alive is critical in long engagements. Invest heavily in at least one healer to ensure your team can withstand heavy damage.

Trolls

Trolls: Trolls are essential for adding sustainability to your team. They regenerate health during battle and, when paired with a healer, can outlast stronger foes. Developing multiple trolls can make your team nearly unstoppable.
