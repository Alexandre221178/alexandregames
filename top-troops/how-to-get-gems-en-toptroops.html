What are Gems in Top Troops

Gems are the premium currency in Top Troops, and they play a crucial role in enhancing your gaming experience. Whether you want to unlock powerful troops, speed up your progress, or acquire exclusive items, having a good stash of gems can make all the difference. In this comprehensive guide, we will explore all the ways to earn gems in Top Troops, ensuring you make the most out of your gameplay.

Daily Activities

1. Collect the Summon Pack Every Day

One of the simplest ways to accumulate gems is by collecting the Summon Pack daily. This activity may seem trivial, but over time, the gems you collect can add up significantly. Make it a habit to log in daily and claim your Summon Pack to ensure a steady flow of gems.

2. Watch Ads for Extra Gems

Top Troops offers various opportunities to earn gems by watching advertisements. Specifically:

  • Summon Pack Ad: Watching an ad after collecting your daily Summon Pack can give you additional rewards, including gems.
  • Merchant Ad: Occasionally, the merchant will offer an ad that, when watched, provides a chance of earning gems. These opportunities are random but worth taking advantage of whenever available.

Seasonal and Event Rewards

3. Level Up in the Season Pass

The Season Pass is a fantastic way to earn gems as you progress through the game. By leveling up and achieving milestones within the Season Pass, you can unlock gem rewards. Focus on completing tasks and objectives to maximize your level and, consequently, your gem earnings.

4. Earn 45 Stars in Adventure Mode

Adventure mode is another reliable source of gems. When you accumulate 45 stars in Adventure mode, you receive gem rewards. Aim to complete as many adventure levels as possible and gather stars to benefit from these rewards.

5. Participate in Events

Events are a periodic feature in Top Troops that offer players the chance to earn extra gems. These events vary in nature and can include tasks such as completing specific challenges, defeating certain enemies, or participating in special game modes. Keep an eye out for these events and participate actively to earn bonus gems.

Arena and Tower Rewards

6. Arena Season End Rewards

Competing in the Arena can be highly rewarding, especially when the season ends. Depending on your rank and performance, you will receive gem rewards. To maximize your gem earnings, strive to achieve higher ranks in the Arena by honing your strategies and building a formidable team.

7. Tower of Fate and Tower of Faction Rewards

The Tower of Fate and Tower of Faction are challenging game modes that offer significant rewards, including gems. Completing these towers can be tough, but the gem rewards make the effort worthwhile. Focus on strengthening your troops and strategies to conquer these towers and claim your gem prizes.

Mission and Quest Rewards

8. Complete Current and Main Missions

Missions are a core aspect of Top Troops, and completing them not only advances your progress but also provides gem rewards. Both current missions (daily, weekly, or monthly) and main missions (storyline or long-term goals) can yield gems. Prioritize completing these missions to continually add gems to your collection.

Special Locations

9. Chamber of Destiny and Magic Island

The Chamber of Destiny and Magic Island are special locations within Top Troops that offer opportunities to earn gems. These locations typically involve completing specific challenges or tasks. Make sure to visit and participate in these locations regularly to maximize your gem earnings.

Purchasing Gems

10. Buying Gems Outright

While there are numerous ways to earn gems for free, the option to purchase gems outright is always available. Various offers and packages are available for players who prefer to buy gems to speed up their progress or obtain exclusive items quickly. However, for those who enjoy the challenge of earning gems through gameplay, focusing on the previously mentioned methods is recommended.

Conclusion

Earning gems in Top Troops doesn't have to be a daunting task. By incorporating the methods outlined in this guide, you can ensure a steady accumulation of gems to enhance your gaming experience. Remember to log in daily, participate in events, complete missions, and take advantage of all opportunities to watch ads for additional rewards. Whether you're a casual player or a dedicated gamer, these strategies will help you make the most out of your Top Troops journey.

If you have any other tips or know of additional ways to earn gems, please share them in the comments below. Happy gaming!

By following this guide, you can systematically increase your gem count in Top Troops, ensuring you have the resources needed to unlock new troops, speed up your progress, and enjoy all the exclusive content the game has to offer. Happy collecting!
