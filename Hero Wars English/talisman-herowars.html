How to get a Talisman


Currently, talismans in Hero Wars Alliance can only be acquired in special events.

In the future, there may be other ways to acquire talismans other than at special events, or it may be that talisman events are repeated. At the moment, it is not yet known how this will happen.

New players or players who do not yet have the talisman need to have the chance to get them. So I believe that soon the developers will create something so that those who do not yet have the talisman can acquire them.


How to activate the Talisman


The talisman is only activated if the character is at level 120, then, the EXP level changes to talisman level and goes up to level 50.

To be able to level up the talisman, you need to acquire the character's talisman, and each character in Hero Wars Alliance has a unique and exclusive talisman that gives them additional stat points to increase their powers.

How to Level Up the Talisman



To level up the talisman, you need to acquire mastery crystals. Mastery crystals, at the moment, can only be acquired in special events, perhaps in the future we will have new types of events and missions to obtain them.

To increase a character's talisman level from 0 to 50, you need a total of 18375 mastery crystals, and they are very difficult to obtain.

How to reroll talismans



Talismans have 1 main stat slot that gives 2000 points of Strength, Agility, or Intelligence depending on the character's class. And 3 special stat slots, which can be, physical attack, magic attack, armor, critical hit chance, magic defense, armor penetration, magic penetration, or Health.


Talisman reroll in Hero Wars Alliance

The three Reroll slots can be changed up to the maximum level of each talisman's stats. To do the Reroll it is necessary to acquire Metacubes or use emeralds. Metacubes can be obtained for free from daily captain battles, and 100 metacubes are required for each Reroll.

The table below shows the probabilities of rewards received in port battles. The developers really want to make metacubes scarce and difficult to get, and the chances of getting a good Reroll with 100% of the talisman's statistics are very difficult.

Tips for Harbor Battles


It is important to note that you need to level up all three captains for defense, as the defense captain is random, and there is no option to select the defense captain.

My suggestion is to focus on the attack and prioritize leveling up the strongest captain, Captain Whiskur. This increases your chances of winning.
