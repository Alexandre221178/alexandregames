Glyph Class Skill Guide Hero Wars Alliance

  • By: Alexandre Domingos. .

Unlocking Class Abilities and Maximizing Power

Are you ready to take your Hero Wars Alliance battles to the next level? With the introduction of Glyph Class Skills, you can enhance your heroes' abilities and make your strategies even more competitive. In this guide, we'll walk you through everything you need to know about Glyph Class Skills and how to maximize their power.

1. Understanding Glyph Class Skills

Glyph Class Skills are special abilities that become available to heroes once they reach level 40 in all their current glyphs. These abilities are tied to specific classes such as Tank, Warrior, Marksman, Mage, Healer, Support, and Control. Once unlocked, Class Abilities can be utilized by any hero belonging to that particular class, providing strategic advantages in battles.

2. Unlocking Class Abilities

To unlock Class Abilities for a specific class, you need to ensure that all heroes belonging to that class have reached level 40 in their respective glyphs. Once this requirement is met, the Class Glyph will unlock, granting access to powerful Class Abilities.

3. Leveling Up Class Glyphs

The power of Class Abilities increases with the level of the Class Glyph. Class Glyph levels are shared among all heroes belonging to the same class. This means that each hero's contribution to leveling up the Class Glyph makes the abilities stronger for all heroes in that class. Aim to have multiple heroes of the same class with maxed-out glyphs to maximize the potency of Class Abilities.

4. Acquiring Runic Spheres

Runic Spheres are the new resource introduced to increase the maximum cap of current glyphs by 5 levels, up to a maximum level cap of 80. These spheres can be obtained from the upgraded Highwaymen mode in Outland, which now features new Orange-grade opponents and higher rarity chests. Keep participating in the Highwaymen mode to collect Runic Spheres and enhance your glyphs.

5. Glyph Upgrade Resources

With the introduction of Glyph Class Skills, the enchantment rune system has been revamped. Small, Common, Rare, and Great Enchantment Runes have been converted into Runic Stones, which are now used for glyph upgrades. You can acquire Runic Stones from various sources such as Heroic Chests, Hero’s Way, daily quests, and other in-game activities. Stock up on Runic Stones to upgrade your glyphs and unlock powerful Class Abilities.

6. Strategic Approach

When planning your hero lineup, consider the synergies between Class Abilities and your overall strategy. Each class offers unique advantages, so choose heroes that complement each other to create a well-rounded team. For example, pairing Tank heroes with strong defensive Class Abilities can create an impenetrable frontline, while combining Support and Healer abilities can ensure your team's survivability.

7. Prioritize Class Glyphs

As you progress in the game, prioritize leveling up Class Glyphs for classes that align with your preferred playstyle or current meta. Investing resources into Class Glyphs that enhance the strengths of your team composition can give you a significant edge in battles. Keep track of which heroes contribute to each Class Glyph and focus on leveling up the glyphs with the most impactful abilities for your strategy.

8. Continuous Improvement

The introduction of Glyph Class Skills is just the beginning of a new era in Hero Wars Alliance. Stay informed about updates and changes to the glyph system to adapt your strategies accordingly. Keep experimenting with different hero combinations and Class Abilities to discover new synergies and optimize your team's performance.

Analysis of Class Glyph Mechanism: Astaroth Example

In the realm of Hero Wars Alliance, the introduction of Glyph Class Skills has brought forth a new layer of depth to strategic gameplay. Astaroth, renowned as a stalwart Tank hero, serves as an exemplary illustration of how the Class Glyph system operates.

Astaroth's arsenal consists of five Glyphs: Magic Attack, Armor, Health, Magic Defense, and Strength. Upon reaching the pinnacle of advancement at level 40 in all Glyphs, the coveted Class Glyph is unlocked, granting access to the Tank Class Ability. This Ability, when activated, bestows passive enhancements upon all Tank heroes enlisted in the player's roster, fortifying their resilience and effectiveness on the battlefield.

However, the journey towards mastery does not end with the mere unlocking of the Class Glyph. To unleash the full potential of the Tank Class Ability, players must embark on a quest for ascension through the utilization of Runic Spheres. These mystical spheres serve as catalysts for the augmentation of Astaroth's Glyphs, incrementing the Tank Class Glyph by +1 with each application.

Through the process of Rune Ascend, the Tank Class Ability undergoes a metamorphosis, growing in potency with each subsequent level. Moreover, the level cap of Astaroth's Glyphs is elevated by increments of 5, offering an opportunity for further empowerment. As each ascended Glyph reaches its zenith, players can once again invoke the Ascending ritual, pushing the boundaries of their heroes' capabilities to ever greater heights.

Nevertheless, the ascent towards Glyph supremacy is not without its limits. The Tank Class Glyph's ascent culminates at level 75, marking the zenith of Astaroth's potential. Beyond this threshold, the Glyph reaches a state of equilibrium, its power reaching a harmonious balance with the hero's innate prowess.

In summary, the Class Glyph mechanism, as exemplified by Astaroth's journey, embodies the essence of progression and empowerment in Hero Wars Alliance. Through meticulous strategy and the judicious application of resources, players can unlock the true potential of their heroes, shaping the course of battles and etching their names into the annals of legend.

Guide to Class Abilities in Hero Wars Alliance

In Hero Wars Alliance, heroes are categorized into different classes, each with unique abilities tailored to their roles on the battlefield. Understanding these abilities is crucial for forming effective teams and strategies. Below is a comprehensive guide to the class abilities along with examples of heroes belonging to each class.

Tank Class Abilities

Description: When an ally's HP falls below a certain threshold, this hero redirects incoming damage to themselves for a limited duration. This effect can trigger once per ally every few seconds.

Passive Skill Tank Heroes
Damage Redirection Aurora, Chabba, Mushy & Shroom, Luther, Julius, Corvus, Astaroth, Cleaver, Ziri, Rufu

Warrior Class Abilities

Description: With each auto-attack, the warrior's physical attack increases for a short duration for every opponent hit, stacking up to a certain limit.

Passive Skill Warrior Heroes
Fierce Assault Elmir, Yasmine, Galahad, Ishmael, Qing Mao, Tristan, K’arkh, Kayla

Marksman Class Abilities

Description: When the target's HP drops below a certain percentage, the marksman deals increased damage with their attacks for a brief period. This effect can trigger periodically.

Passive Skill Marksman Heroes
Precision Shot Jhu, Dark Star, Artemis, Daredevil, Fox, Ginger, Astrid & Lucas, Keira, Dante, Cascade

Mage Class Abilities

Description: Upon entering the battlefield, the mage gains an initial boost of energy and continues to gain additional energy with each spell cast.

Passive Skill Mage Heroes
Arcane Surge Kai, Mojo, Cornelius, Orion, Iris, Lilith, Lars, Xe’Sha, Heidi, Satori

Healer Class Abilities

Description: Whenever healing or shielding effects are applied to an ally, incoming damage is converted into additional healing for a limited duration. This effect can activate periodically.

Passive Skill Healer Heroes
Healing Conversion Thea, Markus, Morrigan, Dorian, Aidan, Maya, Martha

Support Class Abilities

Description: Whenever this hero applies a buff to an ally or damages enemy teams, opponents receive a debuff that reduces their Armor and Magic Defense for a certain duration. This effect can stack multiple times.

Passive Skill Support Heroes
Debilitating Aura Alvanor, Fafnir, Helios, Sebastian, Jet, Nebula, Amira, Celeste

Control Class Abilities

Description: Extends the duration of all crowd control effects such as Marks, Stun, Charm, Blind, and Silence for a certain duration.

Passive Skill Control Heroes
Persistent Control Isaac, Arachne, Judge, Phobos, Faceless, Peppy, Jorgen, Krista, Andvari, Lian

Understanding these glyph abilities is crucial for optimizing hero performance and crafting effective team compositions in Hero Wars Alliance.

Hero Wars Glyph Class Conclusion

Glyph Class Skills are set to revolutionize the way you approach battles in Hero Wars Alliance. By unlocking and maximizing the power of Class Abilities, you can gain a significant advantage over your opponents. Focus on leveling up glyphs for heroes of the same class, gather Runic Spheres to increase glyph caps, and collect Runic Stones to upgrade glyphs efficiently. Prepare your heroes for the upcoming challenges and dominate the battlefield like never before!
