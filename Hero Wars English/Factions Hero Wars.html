Factions


In Hero Wars Mobile all heroes are part of a faction and in the game we have six different types of factions. Currently, the existing six factions are. Eternity, Chaos; Mystery, Progress, Honor, Nature.

The factions provide synergy in the abilities of the heroes, and provide special bonuses when they are together at the same time, for example, the Julius tank of the progress faction has the ability to increase the attack speed of allies by 10%, but if the ally is the Ginger who is from the same faction bonus efficiency will be 20%.

Joining allies from the same faction can yield extra bonuses in battles, but it is not a must, there are several teams and heroes from different factions that complement each other and fit together, generating great teams for defense and offense.

Currently, the Honor faction teams known as FART are the hardest to win in Hero Wars, there are counters, but the diversity varies according to the type of tank they are using, and for this reason victory becomes uncertain.
