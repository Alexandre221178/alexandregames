Types of Hydras and Levels in Hero Wars Alliance: A Comprehensive Guide

Hydras are formidable opponents in Hero Wars Alliance, presenting various challenges that require strategic planning and a well-equipped team to overcome. Understanding the different types of Hydras and their respective levels is crucial for maximizing your chances of success in battle. In this guide, we'll delve into the six types of Hydras and the five levels they come in, along with essential tips and rewards associated with battling them.

Analysis Types of Hydras in Hero Wars

Earth Hydra:

  • Basic Attack: Deals physical damage.
  • Elemental Breath: Also deals physical damage and has the additional effect of stunning the first hero affected and nearby heroes in a small area.
  • Analysis: The Earth Hydra focuses on physical damage and crowd control, making it important to have heroes with high physical resistance and crowd control immunity in your team to counter its attacks effectively.

Water Hydra:

  • Basic Attack: Inflicts physical damage.
  • Elemental Breath: Deals physical damage, with damage scaling based on the proximity of the target. Affected enemies experience a significant slowdown.
  • Analysis: This Hydra poses a threat with its ability to slow down heroes drastically. It's essential to maintain distance and mobility to minimize the impact of its Elemental Breath.

Fire Hydra:

  • Basic Attack: Causes physical damage.
  • Elemental Breath: Inflicts physical damage and applies burns to affected heroes, reducing their armor and dealing ongoing physical damage.
  • Analysis: The Fire Hydra's burns can weaken your team's defenses and cause sustained damage over time. Prioritize heroes with high health or healing abilities to counteract the burn effect.

Light Hydra:

  • Basic Attack: Deals magic damage.
  • Elemental Breath: Inflicts pure damage and blinds affected heroes, reducing their accuracy.
  • Analysis: The Light Hydra's ability to blind heroes can significantly reduce their effectiveness in combat. Heroes with strong AOE abilities or healing effects can help mitigate the impact of blindness.

Wind Hydra:

  • Basic Attack: Inflicts physical damage.
  • Elemental Breath: Deals magic damage and knocks back heroes. The first affected hero is pushed further than others and experiences a significant slowdown.
  • Analysis: The Wind Hydra's ability to knock back and slow down heroes can disrupt your team's positioning. Heroes with crowd control immunity or mobility skills control like Andvari can help counteract this effect.

Darkness Hydra:

  • Basic Attack: Deals magic damage.
  • Elemental Breath: Causes magic damage. Affected heroes gain the Cursed Flame penalty, preventing any healing.
  • Analysis: The Darkness Hydra poses a unique challenge with its ability to prevent healing. Heroes with strong burst damage or debuff removal abilities can help overcome this obstacle.

Each Hydra presents its own set of challenges and requires a specific approach to defeat. Understanding their abilities and weaknesses is crucial for devising effective strategies and assembling a team capable of emerging victorious against these formidable foes.

Levels of Hydras:

  • Common Hydra: The most basic form of Hydra, suitable for beginners and lower-level teams.
  • Uncommon Hydra: Slightly stronger than the Common Hydra, presenting a moderate challenge to seasoned adventurers.
  • Rare Hydra: With increased potency and additional abilities, the Rare Hydra demands a well-rounded team and strategic approach to defeat.
  • Epic Hydra: A formidable foe, the Epic Hydra boasts considerable strength and resilience, requiring a highly skilled and coordinated team to overcome.
  • Legendary Hydra: The pinnacle of Hydra strength, the Legendary Hydra is a fearsome adversary that tests the limits of even the most powerful heroes. Only the bravest and most experienced warriors dare to face this legendary beast.

Tips for Battling Hydras:

Hydras are immune to Crowd Control (CC) effects such as stunning, knocking down, or charming. Keep this in mind when selecting heroes for battle, focusing on damage output and survivability rather than crowd control abilities.

Hydra Rewards:

Rewards for defeating a Hydra vary based on its level and the number of heads defeated. You can check the potential rewards by accessing the Hydra selection menu and tapping the three stripes button. Possible rewards include Fairy Dust, used to level up titanic artifacts, and Elemental Spheres, which can be exchanged for artifact shards at the Altar of the Elements.

By understanding the types of Hydras, their levels, and implementing effective strategies, you can increase your chances of victory and reap valuable rewards in Hero Wars Alliance. Prepare your team, sharpen your skills, and embark on epic battles against these formidable foes!

The Golden Horn Crate

The Golden Horn Crate can be obtained from the Altar of Elements by summoning Titan Elemental Spheres. When you open a Golden Horn Box, you generously gift a Golden Horn of Summoning to each member of your Guild. Additionally, a notification will appear in the Guild chat, announcing the opening of a Golden Horn Box.

Extra Hydra Battles

Each Golden Horn provides an opportunity to launch an attack against the Hydra, even if you have already exhausted your daily allotment of free Summoning Horns. It's advisable to reserve at least 6 extra Golden Summoning Horns for special events, as they do not expire.

How the Crystal Fairies Work

It is now possible to gift your Golden Horns to the fairies, but the Crystal Fairies are only accessible after all the hydra heads have been defeated.

In the end-game, your Guild friends will likely have most of their heroes at maximum level. If the Guild manages the attacks, many will be able to complete the daily mission of fighting the Hydras.

The Crystal Fairies serve as a solution for those Guild mates who cannot receive their summoning sphere prizes if all the hydras are defeated, and they have no opportunity to attack the hydras. This way, they can still receive the summoning spheres from daily missions.

If you have already used your three daily attacks and offer your Golden Horns to the fairies, you will not receive anything in return; you will only spend your Golden Horns. However, if you complete it, the fairies will give you back the daily quest as a reward for the gift.

Maximizing Aidan's Potential Against Magical Hydras

If you've encountered magical Hydras in Hero Wars Alliance, you've likely witnessed the prowess of Aidan. While he's not yet capable of defeating Hydras alone, with the right team, he becomes an indispensable asset. Effectively utilizing Aidan's strengths not only ensures your personal success but also significantly contributes to the growth and prosperity of your guild. In this guide, we'll delve into Aidan's optimal team composition for tackling magical Hydras and enhancing his performance to achieve outstanding damage output.

Aidan's Best Team 2024 for Magical Hydras:

  • Martha: Martha's healing abilities and speed provide crucial support, ensuring the team's sustainability during prolonged battles against magical Hydras.
  • Aidan: Aidan's devastating attacks are particularly potent against magical Hydras, positioning him as the linchpin of the team's offensive strategy due to his sacrificial abilities that grant buffs to Xe'sha.
  • Faceless: Faceless complements Aidan's prowess by replicating his potent abilities, amplifying the team's damage potential with magic penetration artifacts, specifically tailored for combating magical Hydras.
  • Xe'sha: Xe'sha's damage increases when allies sacrifice health, aligning perfectly with Aidan's health-sacrificing skills, allowing the team to focus on dealing maximum damage to the Hydra.
  • Mush and Shroom: Mush and Shroom's synergy with Martha and Aidan's healing abilities enhances the team's damage output, making them invaluable allies in the battle against magical Hydras.

Recommended Team Strategy:

  1. Positioning: Position Aidan in the back line to maximize his damage potential, with Martha providing support from the rear.
  2. Focus Fire: Coordinate your team's attacks to concentrate damage on the Hydra's weak points, exploiting Xe'sha's ability to deal massive damage with each strike.
  3. Utilize Crowd Control: Strategically employ Faceless's passive magic defense skill to decrease the Hydra's attacks and minimize damage to your team.
  4. Maintain Healing: Prioritize Martha's healing to ensure the team's longevity, particularly during prolonged battles against powerful magical Hydras.
  5. Synergize Abilities: Ensure that Faceless and Mush and Shroom synchronize their abilities with Aidan's attacks, maximizing damage output and efficiency.

Expected Damage Output:

With the recommended team composition and strategic approach, you can anticipate dealing significant damage to magical Hydras, ranging from 70 million to 220 million points.

By assembling Aidan's optimal team and implementing the suggested strategies, you can harness his formidable power to confidently overcome magical Hydras and significantly contribute to your guild's success in Hero Wars Alliance. Prepare for epic battles, coordinate with your teammates, and unleash Aidan's full potential to emerge victorious against these formidable foes!

Best Hydra Team for Beginners in Hero Wars 2024

If you're just starting out in Hero Wars and looking to tackle Hydras effectively, Jhu and Mojo are an excellent duo to consider. They are easy to acquire and can form a solid foundation for battling Hydras. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to optimize their performance for Hydra battles:

1. Acquiring Jhu and Mojo:

  • Both Jhu and Mojo are relatively easy to obtain heroes, making them accessible choices for beginners.
  • Focus on acquiring their Soul Stones through various in-game activities such as Campaign, Store, Boxes and Events.

2. Understanding Their Synergy:

  • Jhu and Mojo synergize well together, offering a potent combination of damage and utility.
  • Jhu's abilities benefit greatly from Mojo's Hex skill, which continues to deal damage even if Mojo is defeated in battle.
  • Mojo's abilities complement Jhu's kit, providing crowd control and additional damage output.

3. Leveling Priorities:

  • Prioritize leveling up Mojo's Hex ability, as it synergizes exceptionally well with Jhu's attacks.
  • Focus on upgrading Jhu's first ability, which renders him invincible, boosts his attack speed, deals pure damage, and restores his health.

4. Team Composition:

  • Build a team around Jhu and Mojo, focusing on heroes that enhance their strengths and cover their weaknesses.
  • Consider adding support heroes like Martha and Dorian that can further amplify their damage output or provide additional survivability.

5. Utilizing in Other Modes:

  • Apart from Hydra battles, Jhu and Mojo can also excel in other game modes such as Tower, Outland, and various missions.
  • Leverage their versatility to tackle different challenges and progress further in the game.

6. Continuous Improvement:

  • As you progress in Hero Wars, continue to invest in Jhu and Mojo's development by leveling up their abilities, equipping them with powerful artifacts, and refining their team composition.
  • Stay updated on game updates and new heroes that may complement or enhance Jhu and Mojo's effectiveness in Hydra battles.

By following these steps and harnessing the strengths of Jhu and Mojo, you can build a formidable Hydra team even as a beginner in Hero Wars. Experiment with different strategies, adapt to the evolving game meta, and enjoy the journey of becoming a Hydra-slaying hero!
