Types of Hydras and Level


We have 6 types of Hydras Earth, Water, Fire, Light, Wind, and Dark and these Hydras have five levels, Common Hydra, Ancient Hydra, Ancient Hydra, Dread Hydra, and Legendary Hydra. Each level increases the Hydra's power, and you will need more power in your team to defeat them.

Tip for Hydras


Hydras are immune to CC control. The hydra cannot be stunned, knocked down, or charmed. It has magical and physical protection and has no energy. Before choosing a hero for Hydra, remember this tip.



Hydra Rewards


You receive a reward based on the Hydra's level and the number of heads defeated. You can view the reward by tapping the three stripes button in the Hydra selection menu. Possible rewards include Fairy Dust used to level up titanic artifacts and Elemental Spheres used to obtain artifact shards at the Altar of the Elements.



How the Golden Horn Box Works


The Golden Horn Crate can be acquired from the Altar of Elements when you Summon Titan Elemental Spheres. You give a Golden Horn of Summoning to each Guild member if you open a Golden Horn Box. There will also be a message in the Guild chat saying that a guild member has opened the Golden Horn Box.

Each Golden Horn grants an attempt to attack the Hydra, even if you've spent all of your daily free Summoning Horns. Save at least 6 extra golden Summoning Horns for special events as they do not expire. Below are some of my videos to help you with the Hydras.
