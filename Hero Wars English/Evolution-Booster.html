How to use the Evolution Books


The evolution booster books can be used to increase the Star level, Skills, and EXP (experience) of your Hero Wars Alliance heroes more quickly.

Books or Boosters help you upgrade your heroes and their characteristics. You can purchase them from the Global Championship Store for 1,500 Trophies received from the Global Championship.

They are great and help a lot with your progress in Hero Wars Mobile. That's why you must participate in the global championship war to receive these rewards and use them to increase the remaining levels of EXP, star, and skill levels.


Star Evolution Booster


>
Evolution Booster Book

Make sure to farm soul-stone fragments at events as much as possible. These new soul stones grant you star power, and the more stars the more power. Unlike other hero collection games, you don't unlock these heroes just by buying them randomly but use event prizes as one of the reward methods.

The evolution book “Evolution Booster” increases the evolution level of the chosen hero by one star. Absolute Star is the maximum level you can level your evolution.

You can evolve from 1 to 2 stars, from 2 to 3 stars; 3 to 4 stars; 4 to 5 stars; and 5 to 6 stars.


Evolution Books in Hero Wars Mobile

If you have “Soul Stones” from your chosen hero and leveling up requires less than the maximum stars at star level, you will receive some Global Championship Trophies back in your inventory.

The more Soul Stones you have, the more trophies you will receive back. If you have half of the soul stones from the current star level, you will receive half of the global championship trophies back.

It is important to remember that this “Booster” Reinforcement does not work for the hero Jet, where all soul stones must be purchased in the Guild store.

EXP Evolution Booster



>
EXP Booster Book

The EXP Booster maximizes the level of a selected hero for your current team level. The maximum level of heroes is 120. Make sure you have at least one hero at level 120 or maximum level before using the EXP boost.

If the hero's level is greater than one, or you haven't reached your team's maximum level yet, you get some Global Championship Trophies back in your inventory. The lower the hero's level to reach the maximum level, the more trophies you get back.

Skill Enhancer Boost



>
Skill Booster Book

The skill booster “Skill Booster” increases according to the skills of the chosen hero up to its maximum level. The first and second skills have a maximum level of 120. The third skill has a maximum level of 100 and the fourth skill has a maximum level of 80.

If you've already put some points into your chosen hero's abilities, you'll receive some Global Championship Trophies back in your inventory. The more level points you put into the skill, the more trophies you will receive back.

It is important to pay attention that the return of trophies is related to the amount of gold spent and not to the skill level.


Verdict on Booster Books


With the constant arrival of new heroes in Hero Wars Alliance, it's always good to have Booster Books saved to quickly level up your new heroes.

For beginners, it is important to use the books to quickly assemble a strong team, to get a good position in the arena, and to get better emerald rewards daily to get more resources in the game's events.

The best book to use is Stellar Reinforcement. I recommend using it to go up from 4 to 5 stars, and from 5 to 6 stars. I've used them all to test, and I can say with certainty, that the best is star-level evolution.

And the worst reinforcement book is the EXP evolution book. It really is a waste. You can get EXP in stores, do daily missions, or spend energy on events.
