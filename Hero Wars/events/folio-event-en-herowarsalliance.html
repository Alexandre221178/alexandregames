Strategies for the Chronicle of Dominion: Folio Event

Chronicle of Dominion Introduction

The Chronicle of Dominion: Folio Event is an exciting opportunity for players to delve into the mysteries of Dominion with a new hero, Folio. This event spans 28 days and is divided into two parts, each lasting 14 days. Both parts feature identical tasks, but you can benefit from careful planning and resource management to maximize rewards. This guide will provide a comprehensive strategy for navigating the event, focusing on daily objectives, resource allocation, and event-specific missions.

1. Understanding Folio

Before diving into the event’s mechanics, it's crucial to understand the new hero introduced in this event:

  • Hero Name: Folio
  • Main Stat: Intelligence
  • Class: Mage
  • Faction: Way of Mystery

Folio's Background: Folio is an ancient yet youthful magical book brought to life by a wizard's mistake. Although it harbors powerful spells, Folio is more like a child, driven by curiosity to discover its purpose. By joining the Guardians of Dominion, Folio aims to unveil the mystery of its origins.

2. Event Overview

The Chronicle of Dominion event features several daily and cumulative missions that players need to complete to earn valuable rewards, including Folio’s Soul Stones, Rune Stones, Avatars, and powerful artifacts. Given the 28-day duration and the division into two 14-day segments, the event offers a second chance to accomplish the same objectives and claim rewards.

3. Strategic Breakdown by Event

3.1 Day after Day

Objective: Log in daily for 14 days.

Rewards: Rune Stones, 10 fragments of Folio's weapon artifact (Archmage's Feather), and one Avatar.

Strategy: Ensure consistent daily logins throughout both parts of the event. Missing a day can result in lost rewards, which are essential for advancing Folio's power. Set reminders or alarms to log in every day if necessary.

3.2 Value Exchange

Objective: Spend emeralds to earn Folio’s Soul Stones and other valuable items.

Reward Tiers:

  • 200 emeralds: 1 Soul Stone
  • 500 emeralds: 2 Soul Stones
  • 1000 emeralds: 3 Soul Stones
  • 1500 emeralds: 4 Soul Stones
  • 2100 emeralds: 5 Soul Stones
  • 3000 emeralds: 7 Soul Stones
  • 5000 emeralds: 9 Soul Stones
  • 7500 emeralds: 10 Soul Stones
  • 11,000 emeralds: 12 Soul Stones
  • 16,000 emeralds: 14 Soul Stones + Talisman of Knowledge
  • 22,000 emeralds: 16 Soul Stones
  • 30,000 emeralds: 20 Soul Stones
  • 40,000 emeralds: 25 Soul Stones
  • 50,000 emeralds: 40 Soul Stones

Total Soul Stones and Emeralds

You can gain a total of 168 soul stones by spending 50.000 emeralds during the firs part of the event.

Note: You need a total of 630 soul stones to reach absolute star level 6 for the character.

Additional Details

  • As the levels increase, it becomes harder to earn soul stones.
  • Besides soul stones, at each level, you can also earn rewards such as Mastery Crystal, Meta Cube, and Rune Sphere.
  • After spending 50,000 emeralds, you no longer receive soul stones of Folio.
  • It is advisable to save for the second stage of the event since after 14 days, all missions reset, starting the second part of the event.
  • You can complete these missions throughout the event.

Strategy: Given the increasing cost of Soul Stones with each tier, plan your emerald expenditure carefully. It's advisable to save emeralds during the first part of the event and maximize spending towards the end of the second part when you have a clearer idea of your overall resources. Aim to reach the 50,000 emeralds tier if possible, as it offers the highest rewards, but ensure that you save enough for other event tasks.

3.3 Generous Souls

Objective: Accumulate VIP points during the event.

Rewards: Large Skin Boxes and Rune Spheres.

Strategy: Focus on achieving VIP points daily, as this will allow you to unlock higher-tier rewards, which include essential items for leveling up your heroes. Since these rewards can only be claimed once per event part, make sure you reach the desired VIP level within the first 14 days and repeat the process in the second part.

3.4 Energy of Evolution

Objective: Spend up to 8000 energy daily to earn runes and Folio's Soul Stones.

Reward: 1 Soul Stone for every 400 energy spent.

Strategy: Prioritize spending at least 400 energy daily to secure 1 Soul Stone of Folio each day. You can gain energy passively (per minute) and through daily logins (180 energy) and ads (40 energy). Additionally, consider buying energy (120 energy for 50 emeralds twice a day) to complete this task with minimal expenditure. If resources are limited, focus on reaching the 400 energy threshold daily.

3.5 Secrets of Outland

Objective: Open Outland chests to earn Large Skin Boxes.

Strategy: Open the 5 free Outland chests daily and consider purchasing 3 additional chests at a discounted rate (50 emeralds each). This should be done every day during the event to maximize rewards, but consider saving most of your emeralds for more significant spending opportunities later in the event.

Note: If there is a Skin+ event running concurrently, save up your Outland chest openings for that, as it offers a higher chance of obtaining valuable skins.

3.6 Head Hunter

Objective: Win battles in Outland.

Rewards: Large Skin Boxes and Folio’s Soul Stones (15 total).

Strategy: You have 14 days to win 70 Outland battles, which averages to 5 battles per day. Use your free daily battles and consider purchasing additional ones if needed to ensure you reach the target. The rewards include 15 Folio Soul Stones, which are crucial for unlocking and upgrading Folio.

3.7 Lord of the Arena

Objective: Participate in Arena battles.

Rewards: 1 Folio Soul Stone and Hero Artifact Boxes per battle.

Strategy: Participate in your 5 daily Arena battles, focusing on your Arena reward times to maximize rewards. You can also spend emeralds to get extra battles, but remember that additional battles only offer Hero Artifact Boxes, not extra Soul Stones.

3.8 The Way Up

Objective: Open chests in the Tower.

Reward: 1 Folio Soul Stone for every 10 chests opened.

Strategy: Open the 15 free Tower chests daily. While you can open more chests, the rewards beyond the first 10 chests are less impactful in this event. Focus on getting the 1 daily Soul Stone from Folio without overextending your resources.

4. Additional Event: Great Archivist

Objective: Level up Folio to earn additional rewards.

Tasks:

  • Leveling Folio’s Skin: Earn Skin Stones.
  • Upgrading Hero Artifacts: Earn Hero Artifacts in return.
  • Star-Leveling Ring, Book, and Weapon Artifacts: Receive summoning materials.
  • Upgrading Equipment (Green to Orange+4): Earn gold and energy.
  • Leveling Folio (0 to 120): Earn EXP.
  • Upgrading Glyphs: Earn runes.
  • Upgrading Gift of the Elements: Receive Spark of Power.

Strategy: This event is an excellent opportunity to level up Folio quickly. The rewards, while not 100% of your investment, significantly help to boost Folio’s stats and effectiveness. Focus on the tasks that align with your current resources, but prioritize tasks that give back essential items for future upgrades, such as Spark of Power and runes.

5. General Tips for Success

  • Resource Management: Efficiently manage your emeralds, energy, and other resources throughout the event. Prioritize spending on tasks that give you the highest returns, and save where possible for the second part of the event.
  • Daily Commitment: Ensure daily participation to maximize rewards. Even small daily gains can accumulate to significant rewards by the end of the event.
  • Monitor the Blog: Stay updated with the Hero Wars Alliance blog (Alexandre Games) to catch any additional events or changes that may impact your strategy.

Conclusion

The Chronicle of Dominion: Folio Event is an extensive and rewarding event if approached with a well-thought-out strategy. By following the guidelines outlined in this strategy, you can maximize your gains and unlock the full potential of the new hero, Folio. Remember, the key to success lies in consistency, smart resource allocation, and timely execution of event missions. Good luck, Guardians!
