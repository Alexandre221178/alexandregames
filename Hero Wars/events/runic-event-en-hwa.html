Strategic Guide for Maximizing Growth in the Rune Event in Hero Wars Alliance

Overview of Runic Events

Runic Events are special occasions in Hero Wars Alliance where you can earn valuable resources such as Rune Spheres and Rune Stones to upgrade Glyphs.

There are two primary events:

  1. Alliance of Forces (available at team level 90)
  2. Runic Millstone (available at team level 30)

Both events last three days and feature multiple quest chains to complete for rewards.

Event Details

Alliance of Forces:

  1. Day After Day:
    • Rewards: Rune Spheres
    • Task: Log in daily during the event.
  2. Competent Investment:
    • Rewards: Rune Spheres
    • Task: Spend Rune Spheres.
  3. Energy of Evolution:
    • Rewards: Rune Spheres
    • Task: Spend energy.
  4. The Way Up:
    • Rewards: Rune Spheres
    • Task: Open chests in the Tower.

Runic Millstone:

  1. Day After Day:
    • Rewards: Rune Stones
    • Task: Log in daily during the event.
  2. Wanting More:
    • Rewards: Rune Stones
    • Task: Spend Rune Stones.
  3. Value Exchange:
    • Rewards: Rune Stones and other resources
    • Task: Spend emeralds.
  4. Journey:
    • Rewards: Rune Stones
    • Task: Start expeditions.

Tips for Preparing for the Rune Event

Before the Event:

  1. Save Resources Throughout the Month:
    • Rune Spheres: Avoid spending your Rune Spheres during the month. Accumulate them so you have a significant amount to spend during the "Competent Investment" quests in the event.
    • Rune Stones: Similarly, save your Rune Stones during the month for the "Wanting More" quests. Efficiently saving resources ensures you can complete more quests over the three-day period. Additionally, make sure to level up a low-level Glyph to complete the daily missions without using up too many resources.
  2. Efficient Glyph Upgrades:
    • Focus on upgrading Glyphs with lower levels first. This strategy uses fewer Rune Stones, allowing you to maximize the number of Glyphs upgraded for the same amount of resources.
    • Save high-level Glyph upgrades for future events where you may have more resources.
  3. Daily Mission Completion:
    • Continue to complete your daily missions throughout the month without spending your Rune Spheres and saving Rune Stones. This helps in accumulating resources for the event while still progressing in the game.
  4. Plan Ahead:
    • Anticipate the Runic Events and prepare accordingly. Make a plan for how you will spend your resources during the event to maximize your gains.

Tips for During the Rune Event

  1. Log in Daily:
    • Ensure to log in every day during the event to complete the "Day After Day" quests. This guarantees you earn the initial set of Rune Spheres and Rune Stones.
  2. Efficient Resource Spending:
    • Spend your saved Rune Spheres during the "Competent Investment" quests gradually to complete all quest stages.
    • Spend your saved Rune Stones during the "Wanting More" quests efficiently to maximize quest completions.
  3. Manage Your Energy:
    • During the "Energy of Evolution" quests, make sure to manage your energy wisely. Use energy potions or wait for natural energy regeneration to avoid spending too many resources at once.
  4. Optimize Chest Openings:
    • Plan your Tower runs to coincide with "The Way Up" quests. Opening more chests during the event period will maximize your Rune Sphere earnings.
  5. Expeditions Planning:
    • Start expeditions strategically to align with the "Journey" quests, increasing the total rewards earned.
  6. Emerald Spending Strategy:
    • If participating in the "Value Exchange" quests, plan your emerald spending. Use emeralds for essential upgrades and tasks that contribute to your overall progress in the game.

An In-Depth Conclusion of the Runic Event Strategy

By following these strategic tips, you can maximize your growth during the Rune Events in Hero Wars Alliance. Efficient resource management and task completion will help you earn the maximum possible rewards, contributing to your heroes' overall strength and progression.
