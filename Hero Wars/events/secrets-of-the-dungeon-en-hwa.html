Strategies for Secrets of the Dungeon Hero Wars Alliance

Discover the Secrets of the Dungeon event in Hero Wars Alliance! Complete quests to earn Titan Skin Stones, Hero's Way Points, and more. Join the adventure now!

The Secrets of the Dungeon event becomes available at team level 35 and lasts three days. This event offers a fantastic opportunity to collect Titan Skin Stones by completing various quests. Here's a strategic guide to maximize your rewards during the event.

Accessing the Event

You can find the Secrets of the Dungeon event in a separate tab within the Special Events window.

Quest Chains

The event features several quest chains, each providing specific rewards. Here’s how you can complete them effectively:

Day After Day

Objective: Log in to the game daily.
Rewards: Titan Skin Stones and Hero's Way Points.
Strategy: Ensure you log in every day of the event to collect these rewards. Set a reminder if necessary.

Value Exchange

Objective: Spend emeralds.
Rewards: Titan Potions, Titan Skin Stones, and Hero's Way Points.
Strategy: Plan your emerald spending in advance. Focus on essential purchases or wait for special offers that coincide with the event.

Hydra Hunter

Objective: Battle any Hydra or give Horns of Summoning to the Fairies.
Rewards: Titan Skin Stones and Hero's Way Points.
Strategy: Participate in Hydra battles daily. Collect Horns of Summoning beforehand and use them during the event to maximize your rewards.

Energy of Evolution

Objective: Spend energy.
Rewards: Titan Skin Stones and Hero's Way Points.
Strategy: Save up your energy before the event starts. Use your energy efficiently by completing high-reward quests and campaigns.

Titanic Contribution

Objective: Collect titanite in the Dungeon.
Rewards: Titan Skin Stones.
Strategy: Focus on collecting titanite daily. You can also purchase 75 titanite at the VIP Shop for 300 emeralds (VIP level 7 required).

Fellows in Arms

Objective: Earn Guild Activity points.
Rewards: Titan Skin Stones and Hero's Way Points.
Strategy: Ensure you are active in your guild. Upgrade a glyph of any hero daily to earn 850 guild points. If you are low on runes, focus on heroes with glyphs close to level zero to minimize rune expenditure.

Additional Tips for Secrets of the Dungeon Event

  • Plan Ahead: Familiarize yourself with the quests and plan your actions in advance to make the most of the event.
  • Resource Management: Manage your resources, such as emeralds and energy, wisely to ensure you can complete as many quests as possible.
  • Guild Participation: Actively participate in your guild activities to maximize your Fellows in Arms quest rewards.
  • Event Timing: Pay attention to the event duration and ensure you are logging in and completing quests daily.

An In-Depth Conclusion of the Secrets of the Dungeon Event

The in Hero Wars offers a unique opportunity for players to earn valuable rewards such as Titan Skin Stones and Hero's Way Points. By completing a series of engaging quests, players can enhance their Titans and heroes, thereby strengthening their overall team. The event is designed to encourage daily participation and strategic resource management, ensuring that players remain active and invested throughout its duration.

To maximize the benefits from this event, players should plan their activities in advance. Efficient use of resources like emeralds, energy, and titanite can significantly impact the rewards earned. Engaging in guild activities and coordinating with guild members can further amplify the gains from the Fellows in Arms quest chain. By focusing on these strategic elements, players can optimize their performance and achieve substantial progress in a short period.

Overall, the Secrets of the Dungeon event is an exciting and rewarding experience for Hero Wars players. It not only provides substantial in-game rewards but also fosters a sense of community and collaboration among players. By following a well-thought-out strategy and actively participating in the event, players can unlock the full potential of their heroes and Titans, setting the stage for future successes in the game.
