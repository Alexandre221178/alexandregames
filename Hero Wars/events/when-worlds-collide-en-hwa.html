Tips for the When Worlds Collide Event in Hero Wars Alliance

The When Worlds Collide event in Hero Wars Alliance is ideal for beginners to gather resources for upgrading Titans and their artifacts. The event becomes available at team level 35 and lasts for three days.

You can find this event in a separate tab in the Special Events window. The event features several quest chains:

Day After Day

Log in daily to receive Titan Skin Stones and Hero's Way Points.

Generous Soul

Earn VIP Points to receive Light & Dark Artifact Chests.

Value Exchange

Spend emeralds to receive Light & Dark Titan Chests, Titan Skin Stones, and Hero's Way Points.

Master of Fire

Complete quests in the Master of Fire special event to earn Araji's Soul Stones, Light & Dark Artifact Chests, and Hero's Way Points.

Titan Plains

Complete quests in the Titan Plains special event to earn Eden's Soul Stones, Light & Dark Artifact Chests, and Hero's Way Points.

Father of the Ocean

Complete quests in the Father of the Ocean special event to earn Hyperion's Soul Stones, Light & Dark Artifact Chests, and Hero's Way Points.

Strategies to Conquer When Worlds Collide Events

Here are tips for completing the specific events within When Worlds Collide: Master of Fire, Titan Plains, and Father of the Ocean.

Master of Fire Tips

This event focuses on upgrading Fire Titans. Here's how to maximize your rewards:

Log In Daily

  • Quest: Day After Day
  • Reward: Fire Titans' Soul Stones
  • Tip: Ensure you log in every day to claim the daily rewards. Set a reminder if necessary to avoid missing out.

Get VIP Points

  • Quest: Generous Soul
  • Reward: Araji's Soul Stones and Fire Titan Artifact Chests
  • Tip: Invest in small purchases to increase your VIP level and complete this quest. The rewards will significantly boost your Fire Titans.

Spend Energy

  • Quest: Energy of Evolution
  • Reward: Fairy Dust and Fire Titan Artifact Chests
  • Tip: Plan your energy usage. Focus on spending energy on missions that offer the best value for rewards.

Win Outland Battles

  • Quest: Head Hunter
  • Reward: Chests of Seals
  • Tip: Use your strongest team to win Outland battles efficiently. Check your opponentâ€™s strength before engaging.

Complete Summons

  • Quest: Power Unleashed
  • Reward: Titan Skin Stones
  • Tip: Save your Summoning Spheres and complete summons during the event to maximize rewards.

Start Expeditions

  • Quest: Journey
  • Reward: Fire Titan Artifact Chests and Demonic Sparks
  • Tip: Start as many expeditions as possible. Expeditions can run in the background while you complete other tasks.

Titan Plains Tips

Focused on upgrading Earth Titans, this event includes:

Log In Daily

  • Quest: Day After Day
  • Reward: Earth Titans' Soul Stones
  • Tip: Consistent daily logins will ensure you gather all the Earth Titan Soul Stones available.

Get VIP Points

  • Quest: Generous Soul
  • Reward: Eden's Soul Stones and Earth Titan Artifact Chests
  • Tip: Similar to the Master of Fire event, small purchases can help you gain VIP points needed for this quest.

Open Heroic Chests

  • Quest: Heroic Discovery
  • Reward: Fairy Dust and Earth Titan Artifact Chests
  • Tip: Save your keys and open as many Heroic Chests as you can during the event period.

Complete Missions

  • Quest: Stories of Victors
  • Reward: Earth Titan Artifact Chests
  • Tip: Focus on completing story missions to quickly gather the required number of completions.

Collect Titanite

  • Quest: Titanic Contribution
  • Reward: Titan Skin Stones
  • Tip: Join an active guild and participate in Titanite farming to contribute efficiently.

Open Tower Chests

  • Quest: The Way Up
  • Reward: Earth Titan Artifact Chests and Mandrake Roots
  • Tip: If possible, a day before the event starts, save your Tower keys and open chests during the event to maximize your rewards.

Tips for Success

Daily Participation: Ensure you log in every day to maximize your rewards from the Day After Day quest chain. Consistent participation is key to gathering a large number of resources.

Focus on VIP Points: If you're aiming for Light & Dark Artifact Chests, focus on earning VIP Points through the Generous Soul quest chain. This can significantly boost your progress.

Efficient Use of Emeralds: Plan your emerald spending wisely. The Value Exchange quest chain offers valuable rewards for spending emeralds, so prioritize purchases that will help you in the event.

Targeted Quest Completion: For Master of Fire, Titan Plains, and Father of the Ocean quests, focus on completing the specific tasks required. This will ensure you earn the maximum rewards, including soul stones for Araji, Eden, and Hyperion.

Conclusion of the When Worlds Collide Event

Participating in the When Worlds Collide event is a fantastic opportunity for beginners to enhance their Titans and their artifacts. By completing specific quests such as Master of Fire, Titan Plains, and Father of the Ocean, players can gather vital resources to strengthen their teams.

Make sure to take full advantage of this event, as it only lasts for three days. Prioritize your tasks and efficiently use your resources to maximize your gains. With these tips, you'll be well on your way to boosting your Titans and achieving greater success in Hero Wars Alliance.
