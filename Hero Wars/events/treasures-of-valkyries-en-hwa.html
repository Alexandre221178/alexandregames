Tips for the "Treasures of Valkyries" Event in Hero Wars

Event Duration: Three days

Required Team Level: 20

Overview:

The "Treasures of Valkyries" event is an excellent opportunity to gather resources for upgrading Hero Artifacts. By completing various quests, you can earn valuable rewards that will significantly enhance your Heroes' power.

Accessing the Event:

You can find the "Treasures of Valkyries" event in a separate tab within the Special Events window.

Quest Chains and Rewards:

  • Day After Day:
    • Objective: Log in daily during the event.
    • Rewards: Emeralds and Hero's Way Points.
  • Journey:
    • Objective: Start Expeditions.
    • Rewards: Artifact Coins, emeralds, and Hero's Way Points.
  • Value Exchange:
    • Objective: Spend emeralds.
    • Rewards: Artifact Coins, Chaos Cores, and Hero's Way Points.
  • Fusion of Souls:
    • Objective: Obtain Soul Stones of any hero.
    • Rewards: Artifact Essence Chests.
  • Valkyrie's Artifacts:
    • Objective: Open Artifact Chests.
    • Rewards: Artifact Scroll Chests.
  • Energy of Evolution:
    • Objective: Spend energy.
    • Rewards: Artifact Metal Chests.
  • The Way Up:
    • Objective: Open chests in the Tower.
    • Rewards: Artifact Coins and Hero's Way Points.

Tips for Maximizing the Event:

  • Daily Logins: Ensure you log in every day to complete the "Day After Day" quest chain. This will provide you with consistent rewards of emeralds and Hero's Way Points.
  • Plan Your Expeditions: Start expeditions as soon as possible to maximize your gains in the "Journey" quest chain. This will help you accumulate Artifact Coins and additional rewards efficiently.
  • Spend Emeralds Wisely: Use your emeralds strategically to complete the "Value Exchange" quest chain. This will grant you Artifact Coins, Chaos Cores, and Hero's Way Points.
  • Focus on Soul Stones: During the event, aim to gather as many Soul Stones as possible to complete the "Fusion of Souls" quest chain. This will earn you valuable Artifact Essence Chests.
  • Open Artifact Chests: Utilize your Artifact Keys or other resources to open Artifact Chests and complete the "Valkyrie's Artifacts" quest chain, earning Artifact Scroll Chests.
  • Spend Energy Efficiently: Use your energy to complete missions and quests that align with the "Energy of Evolution" quest chain. This will reward you with Artifact Metal Chests.
  • Climb the Tower: Make sure to open chests in the Tower daily to complete "The Way Up" quest chain, securing Artifact Coins and Hero's Way Points.

An In-Depth Conclusion of the "Treasures of Valkyries" Event

During the three-day "Treasures of Valkyries" event, it's crucial to focus on spending energy on missions that provide Hero Soul Stones. This strategy maximizes your gain of Weapon Artifacts while simultaneously earning Bronze Artifacts to level up Ring Artifacts.

Key Points:

Spending Energy Wisely:

  • Focus on missions that provide Hero Soul Stones to maximize the gain of Weapon Artifacts.
  • Spending energy on these missions will also earn you Bronze Artifacts, which are essential for leveling up Ring Artifacts.

Obtaining Scroll Artifacts:

  • Scroll Artifacts, necessary for upgrading Book Artifacts, can be a bit more challenging to acquire.
  • If you haven't saved the Artifact Chests obtained from daily mission rewards, you might need to spend emeralds to open more Artifact Chests.

Final Tip:

Ensure you log in daily, complete expeditions, and strategically spend your resources to make the most of this event. By focusing on the quests that yield the most valuable rewards, you can significantly enhance your Hero Artifacts and improve your team's performance. Happy upgrading and enjoy the "Treasures of Valkyries" event!
