Introduction

The Talisman Keepers event in Hero Wars Alliance is a highly anticipated three-day event designed to enhance the power of several heroes by adding Talismans. Completing event quests allows players to earn Talismans and resources necessary for upgrading them. Available exclusively to players at team level 120, the event offers a range of quest chains, each rewarding players with valuable Mastery Crystals, Meta Cubes, and Talismans. This comprehensive guide aims to provide strategic tips and insights for each mission within the event, helping players maximize their rewards and efficiency.

Key Considerations for Beginners

Before diving into the specific missions, it is crucial for beginners to understand a few important aspects:

  • Team Level Requirement: The event is only available to players who have reached team level 120. If you are not at this level yet, focus on leveling up your team by completing daily quests and participating in various game modes.
  • Hero Level Requirement: Only heroes that have reached level 120 can acquire Talismans. Ensure that the heroes you wish to equip with Talismans are at the required level before the event ends.
  • EXP Potions: To quickly level up a hero to 120, purchase EXP potions daily from the City Shop, VIP Shop, Soul Shop, or by spending energy. Spending energy not only provides a significant amount of EXP but also rewards gold and equipment needed for hero upgrades.

Accessing Event Quests

You can find the event quests by tapping the event entrance on the main screen or by opening the Quests menu. The event features several quest chains, each designed to test different aspects of your gameplay. Below is a detailed analysis and strategy for each quest chain.

Quest Chains and Strategies

1. Chosen by the Keepers

Objective: Log in to the game daily to receive Mastery Crystals and a Talisman.

Strategy:

  • Consistency: Ensure you log in every day of the event. Set a reminder if necessary.
  • Maximize Rewards: This is the easiest quest chain, requiring no additional effort beyond daily login. Claim your rewards immediately to avoid missing out.

2. Value Exchange

Objective: Spend emeralds to receive Mastery Crystals and a Talisman.

Strategy:

  • Plan Your Spending: Prioritize spending emeralds on essential items that will benefit your overall progress, such as EXP potions, gold, or rare equipment.
  • Budget Management: Allocate a specific amount of emeralds for this event to ensure you do not overspend. Track your emerald usage to meet the quest requirements without depleting your resources. Remember that some Talismans can only be obtained by spending 60,000 emeralds, so plan your budget accordingly.

3. Generous Soul

Objective: Score VIP points to receive Mastery Crystals and a Talisman.

Strategy:

  • VIP Levels: Higher VIP levels offer more rewards and benefits. Some talismans can only be acquired with 10,000 VIP points, which is quite expensive. Unfortunately, these are often the best talismans available. Consider purchasing small packages that provide good value for the VIP points they offer.
  • Daily Offers: Take advantage of daily offers and bundles that give VIP points. These often provide a cost-effective way to accumulate points.

4. Energy of Evolution

Objective: Spend energy to receive Mastery Crystals and a Talisman.

Strategy:

  • Energy Management: Use your energy efficiently by focusing on campaign missions that provide hero soul stones to max out their star levels. These missions also offer valuable equipment needed for character progression. Additionally, the Soul Shop has the best orange equipment items available for purchase.
  • Daily Energy Bonuses: Make sure to claim your free daily energy bonuses and participate in events that reward extra energy.

5. Transforming Talismans

Objective: Reroll talisman slots to receive Mastery Crystals and a Talisman.

Strategy:

  • Optimize Rerolls: Focus on rerolling Talismans for your most powerful or frequently used heroes. This ensures that the resources spent on rerolls have a significant impact on your overall team strength.
  • Resource Allocation: Be mindful of the resources required for rerolling and plan accordingly. Do not exhaust all your resources on unnecessary rerolls.

6. Trials for the Keepers

Objective: Win a PvP battle using a hero that has a Talisman equipped to receive Meta Cubes and a Talisman.

Strategy:

  • PvP Preparation: Equip Talismans on your strongest heroes before entering PvP battles. This will increase your chances of winning and completing this quest chain.
  • Team Composition: Ensure your team is well-balanced with a mix of offensive and defensive heroes. Synergy between heroes is crucial for success in PvP.

7. Talismans Grow in Power

Objective: Level up certain heroes' Talismans to receive Meta Cubes.

Strategy:

  • Focus on Key Heroes: Prioritize leveling up Talismans for heroes that are central to your team’s strategy. This will enhance their abilities and overall effectiveness in battles.
  • Resource Management: Balance your resources between leveling up Talismans and other hero upgrades to maintain a strong team.

8. Fellow in Arms

Objective: Level up certain heroes' Talismans to receive Meta Cubes.

Strategy:

  • Hero Synergy: Level up Talismans for heroes that work well together. This creates a more cohesive and powerful team.
  • Long-term Planning: Consider the long-term benefits of leveling up certain Talismans. Focus on heroes that will be useful in various game modes, not just during the event.

Additional Tips

  • Resource Allocation: Efficiently manage your resources such as emeralds, energy, and gold. Prioritize spending in ways that align with event requirements and overall team improvement.
  • Daily Activities: Participate in daily activities and missions to gather additional resources. This will help you progress in the event and strengthen your heroes.
  • Event Preparation: Before the event begins, stockpile essential resources and plan your strategy. This preparation will allow you to complete quests more efficiently and maximize your rewards.
  • Community Engagement: Join a guild or community group where you can share tips and strategies with other players. Collaborative efforts can provide valuable insights and enhance your event experience.

An In-Depth Conclusion of the Talisman Keepers event in Hero Wars Alliance

The Talisman Keepers event in Hero Wars Alliance is a golden opportunity for players to enhance their heroes with powerful Talismans. By understanding the event's mechanics and strategically approaching each quest chain, players can maximize their rewards and strengthen their teams significantly. Remember to focus on resource management, daily participation, and community engagement to make the most of this event. With careful planning and execution, you'll be well on your way to achieving top-tier performance in Hero Wars Alliance.
