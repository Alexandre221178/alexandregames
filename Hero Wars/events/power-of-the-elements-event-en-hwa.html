Discover strategies for the Power of the Elements event in Hero Wars Alliance. Maximize your rewards with Alexandre Games detailed quest tips!

Accessing the Event

The Power of the Elements event in Hero Wars Alliance is an excellent opportunity to gather resources for upgrading your titans' artifacts. The event is available at team level 35 and lasts for three days. Here's a guide with tips for each quest chain to help you maximize your rewards.

You can find the Power of the Elements event in a separate tab in the Special Events window.

Quest Chains

1. Day After Day

Objective: Log in to the game daily.

Tips:

  • Consistency is Key: Simply logging in each day will earn you Titan Artifact Chests and Hero's Way Points. Set a reminder on your phone or calendar to ensure you don’t miss a day.
  • Log in Early: Log in early in the day to ensure you collect the rewards and have time to complete other quests.

2. Hydra Hunter

Objective: Battle any Hydra or give Horns of Summoning to the Fairies.

Tips:

  • Prepare Your Team: Make sure your team is strong enough to take on Hydras. Prepare your team to face the Hero Wars Hydra before the event starts.
  • Save Horns: If you have Horns of Summoning, save them for this event to maximize your rewards.
  • Frequent Battles: Engage in Hydra battles regularly to accumulate rewards quickly.

3. Fellows in Arms

Objective: Earn Guild Activity points.

Tips:

  • Stay Active: Participate actively in guild activities. Engage in Guild Wars, complete guild quests, and assist your guildmates.
  • Communicate: Coordinate with your guild to ensure everyone is contributing. A well-coordinated guild can earn points more efficiently.
  • Guild Shop: Use your Guild Activity points in the Guild Shop to further boost your progress in the event.

4. Value Exchange

Objective: Spend emeralds.

Tips:

  • Plan Ahead: Decide in advance how many emeralds you’re willing to spend during the event. Budgeting can help you avoid overspending.
  • Wait for Discounts: Look out for special offers and discounts in the shop during the event to make your emeralds go further.
  • Prioritize Needs: Spend emeralds on resources that will benefit your titans and heroes the most, such as artifact components or hero soul stones.

5. Good Deal

Objective: Purchase any products in the Dungeon Shop.

Tips:

  • Save Dungeon Coins: Prior to the event, save up your Dungeon Coins so you can make multiple purchases during the event.
  • Focus on Value: Buy items that offer the best value for your Dungeon Coins, Purchase items that offer the best value for your Dungeon Coins, such as Fairy Dust components and Titan Skin Stones
  • Daily Purchases: Make sure to check the Dungeon Shop daily for new items and deals.

6. Generous Soul

Objective: Get VIP points.

Tips:

  • Small Purchases: If you're close to the next VIP level, consider making small purchases to reach it and earn extra rewards.
  • Events and Offers: Take advantage of any special offers or events that provide extra VIP points.
  • VIP Benefits: Remember that increasing your VIP level not only helps with the event but also provides long-term benefits like increased energy regeneration and additional daily rewards.

Power of the Elements Event Conclusion

By following these strategies, you can efficiently complete the Power of the Elements event quests and gather valuable resources to enhance your titans and heroes.

Consistent participation, smart planning, and coordination with your guild will ensure you maximize your rewards and progress.

Dive into the event prepared and make the most of every opportunity. Good luck, and may your heroes and titans grow stronger!
