A Strategic Guide for the Event Gift of the Elements in Hero Wars Alliance

The "Gift of the Elements" event in Hero Wars Alliance is a highly anticipated event that offers players the opportunity to significantly enhance their Titan armies. The event becomes available at team level 30 and lasts three days, providing various quests that reward valuable resources for upgrading Titans. This guide will walk you through each quest chain, offering tips and strategies to maximize your rewards and progress.

Gift of the Elements Event Overview

The Gift of the Elements event features several quest chains, each designed to provide different resources and rewards. Here's a breakdown of the quests you'll encounter:

  • Assemble: Log in daily to receive Titan Potions and Hero's Way Points.
  • Unbreakable Shield: Spend energy to earn Chests of Defender Titans and Hero's Way Points.
  • Combat Support: Open chests in Outlands to obtain Chests of Support Titans.
  • Value Exchange: Spend emeralds to get Chests of Supertitans, Hero's Way Points, and Summoning Spheres.
  • Celestial Hall: Collect Soul Stones of any hero to receive Chests of Marksman Titans and Hero's Way Points.
  • Fulfilling Dreams: Complete summons in the Summoning Circle to earn Titan Potions.
  • Titanic Contribution: Collect titanite to gain Titan Potions and Hero's Way Points.

Each quest chain has its unique rewards and challenges. Let's dive into detailed tips and strategies for each task.

1. Assemble

Task: Log in daily to receive Titan Potions and Hero's Way Points.

Tips:

  • Consistency is Key: Make sure to log in every day during the event. This task is straightforward but requires daily commitment.
  • Set Reminders: If you tend to forget, set reminders on your phone or calendar to log in.
  • Maximize Rewards: Log in at the start of the day to ensure you don't miss out due to time zone differences or server reset times.

2. Unbreakable Shield

Task: Spend energy to earn Chests of Defender Titans and Hero's Way Points.

Tips:

  • Energy Management: Plan your energy usage to coincide with the event. Don't spend all your energy before the event starts.
  • Prioritize Missions: Focus on missions that provide the best energy-to-reward ratio.
  • Use Energy Potions: Save and use energy potions during this event to maximize your energy expenditure.

Optimal Defender Titans Priorities

  • Sigurd: Excellent for the dungeon with his shield and healing artifacts.
  • Angus: High area damage, useful for advancing in the dungeon.

3. Combat Support

Task: Open chests in Outlands to obtain Chests of Support Titans.

Tips:

  • Efficient Chest Opening: Plan your chest openings to align with the event. Donâ€™t open chests just before the event starts.
  • Daily Quests: Complete daily quests and activities that reward Outland Coins to use on chest openings.

Support Titans Priorities

  • Avalon: Best choice due to healing artifacts and a strong shield, excellent for the dungeon.
  • Ignis: More suited for the arena; consider for long-term planning.
  • Mairi: Requires fewer stars as her abilities scale with percentage, upgrade her slowly without focusing on star levels.

4. Value Exchange

Task: Spend emeralds to get Chests of Supertitans, Hero's Way Points, and Summoning Spheres.

Tips:

  • Emerald Efficiency: Spend emeralds wisely. Look for the best deals and avoid unnecessary spending.
  • Daily Deals: Check the shop for daily deals that might offer more value for your emeralds.

Supertitans Priorities

  • Hyperion: High healing power through both artifacts and abilities.
  • Eden and Araji: Both are excellent, with Eden strengthening your Earth team and Araji delivering strong damage in your Fire team.

5. Celestial Hall

Task: Collect Soul Stones of any hero to receive Chests of Marksman Titans and Hero's Way Points.

Tips:

  • Soul Stone Collection: Focus on daily and event-specific activities that reward Soul Stones.
  • Heroic Chest Openings: Consider opening Heroic Chests if you have the resources, as they often contain Soul Stones.

Marksman Titans Priorities

  • Nova: Best option due to her stunning abilities, useful for both dungeon and arena.
  • Vulcan and Sylva: Less effective in both dungeon and arena; only upgrade them minimally for collection purposes.

  • Potion Usage: Focus on upgrading tank and healing Titans first, but ensure all Titans are leveled enough to not hinder your progress in the dungeon.

7. Titanic Contribution

Task: Collect titanite to gain Titan Potions and Hero's Way Points.

Tips:

  • Daily Dungeon Runs: Make sure to complete your dungeon runs daily to collect titanite.
  • Dungeon Team Setup: Ensure your dungeon team is strong and balanced to maximize titanite collection.
  • Guild Participation: Work with your guild to reach titanite collection goals. Active guild participation can significantly boost your rewards.

An In-Depth Conclusion of the Gift of the Elements Event

The Gift of the Elements event in Hero Wars Alliance is an excellent opportunity to strengthen your Titan team. By following the tips and strategies outlined in this guide, you can maximize your rewards and progress efficiently. Remember to plan your resources, focus on the most beneficial Titans, and participate actively in your guild to make the most of this event. Happy gaming!
