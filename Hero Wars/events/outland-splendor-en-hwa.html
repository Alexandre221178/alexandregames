Advanced Strategy Guide for Outland Splendor

The Outland Splendor event becomes available at team level 25 and lasts three days. Complete quests to get Skin Stone Chests and Outland Coins!

Event Overview

You can find this event in a separate tab in the Special Events window. The event has several quest chains, each offering valuable rewards. Below is a breakdown of each quest chain and strategies to maximize your rewards.

Day After Day

Objective: Log in daily to receive Large Skin Stone Chests and Hero's Way Points.

Strategy:

  • Consistency is Key: Ensure you log in every day during the event. Set reminders or alarms to avoid missing a day.
  • Claim Rewards Early: As soon as you log in, claim your rewards. This ensures you donâ€™t forget later in the day.

Outland Treasures

Objective: Make purchases to receive Skin Stone Chests and Hero's Way Points.

Strategy:

  • Plan Your Purchases: If you were planning to make any in-game purchases, do so during this event to maximize your rewards.
  • Focus on Value Packs: Look for special value packs or offers that give you the most bang for your buck, ensuring you get the highest number of Skin Stone Chests and Hero's Way Points.

Successful Fighter

Objective: Win with different heroes in the Arena to receive Skin Stone Chests and Hero's Way Points.

Strategy:

  • Diverse Team Composition: Prepare multiple teams with different heroes to ensure you can rotate and win with various heroes.
  • Study Your Opponents: Analyze your opponents' teams in the Arena and strategically select heroes that counter them to increase your chances of winning.
  • Use Refresh Battles: If available, use refresh battles with emeralds to find easier opponents, ensuring more victories with different heroes.

Generous Soul

Objective: Score VIP Points to receive Large Skin Stone Chests.

Strategy:

  • Accumulate VIP Points: Focus on activities and purchases that earn VIP Points. This might include buying VIP packages or spending on certain in-game items.
  • Maximize Spending: If youâ€™re close to the next VIP level, consider making the necessary purchases during this event to hit the threshold and earn more rewards.

Secrets of Outland

Objective: Open Outland chests to receive Large Skin Stone Chests, Outland Coins, and Hero's Way Points.

Strategy:

  • Save Your Keys: If you have been saving emerads for opening Outland chests, now is the time to use them.
  • Efficient Chest Opening: Open the highest-tier chests you can to get the best rewards and Skins. This might require you to progress further in the Outland challenges.
  • Daily Chest Opening: Make sure to open chests daily to spread out your rewards and ensure you maximize your daily potential.

General Tips

  • Resource Management: Plan your resources and spending wisely throughout the event to ensure you can complete as many quests as possible.
  • Monitor Progress: Regularly check your progress in each quest chain to stay on track and adjust your strategy if needed.
  • Team Up: If your game allows, collaborate with your guild or friends to exchange tips and help each other complete quests.

An In-Depth Conclusion of the Outland Splendor

By following these strategies, you can maximize your rewards during the Outland Splendor event. The key to success lies in meticulous planning and consistent participation. Logging in daily ensures you never miss out on valuable rewards, while strategic purchasing and efficient use of resources can significantly enhance your overall gains. Remember, the event is designed not only to test your skills but also to reward your dedication and strategic thinking.

As you navigate through the various quest chains, keep a close eye on your progress and adjust your strategies as needed. Collaborating with guild members or friends can provide additional insights and support, making it easier to complete challenging quests. The Outland Splendor event is an excellent opportunity to strengthen your team, acquire rare items, and showcase your prowess in the game.

Ultimately, the event is not just about the rewards, but also about enjoying the journey and the camaraderie of your fellow players. Take advantage of this exciting event to explore new strategies, experiment with different heroes, and immerse yourself in the dynamic world of Mobile Legends: Bang Bang. Good luck, and may your efforts be richly rewarded!
