The Event Way of Honor

The "Way of Honor" is a faction-themed event in Hero Wars Alliance, focusing on a specific faction and its featured hero. This recurring event lasts three days, during which players can earn event coins to spend in a special shop. The shop offers the featured hero and resources for upgrading the heroes of the event faction.

Overview of the Honor Faction

The Honor faction, often referred to by the acronym F.A.R.T., is known for its formidable presence on the battlefield. The popular FART. team consists of Fafnir, Artemis, Tristan, plus two tanks or Oya. The newest addition to this team is the hero Soleil. Each hero brings unique strengths to the team:

  • Soleil: A new hero who excels against control abilities and weakens enemy defenses.
  • Fafnir: Provides team buffs and shields, enhancing survivability.
  • Artemis: Delivers high critical damage, especially when buffed by the F.A.R.T. composition.
  • Tristan: Known for high damage output, armor penetration, and draining energy from enemy mages.
  • Galahad: Becomes incredibly powerful when used in the Honor team.
  • Luther: A formidable tank that leaps into the enemy team, causing chaos.
  • Helios: Counters critical damage.
  • Cornelius: Counters mages with the highest intelligence stat.
  • Ishmael: Has great synergy with Soleil due to their combined abilities to weaken enemies.

Tips and Strategies for Event Quests

The "Way of Honor" event features several quest chains, each offering valuable rewards:

1. Day After Day

Earn Event Coins and Hero's Way Points by logging in daily.

Strategy:

  • Consistency is Key: Make sure to log in every day during the event. Setting a reminder can help ensure you don't miss a day.
  • Plan Ahead: If you know you'll be busy, log in early in the day to avoid missing the login rewards.

2. Value Exchange

Receive Event Coins, Artifact Coins, and Hero's Way Points for spending emeralds.

Strategy:

  • Smart Spending: Plan your emerald spending in advance. Use emeralds for essential upgrades and purchases that align with your long-term goals.
  • Discounts and Offers: Look for special offers and discounts that may occur during the event to maximize your emerald efficiency.

3. Energy of Evolution

Gain Event Coins for spending energy.

Strategy:

  • Energy Management: Use your energy wisely. Focus on campaign levels that drop valuable items or hero soul stones you need.
  • Energy Boosts: Utilize energy potions and other energy-boosting items strategically to maximize the number of Event Coins you can earn.

4. Generous Soul

Obtain Event Coins for scoring VIP points.

Strategy:

  • VIP Points Accumulation: Focus on activities that give VIP points. Purchasing small packages that you need can also contribute to your VIP points.
  • Daily Quests: Complete daily quests and missions that reward VIP points to maximize your gains.

5. Secrets of Outland

Earn Event Coins by opening chests in Outlands.

Strategy:

  • Chest Opening: Save Outland chests specifically for the event. Plan to open the maximum number of chests each day to get the most Event Coins.
  • Outland Bosses: Focus on defeating Outland bosses that drop the most valuable rewards for your current needs.

6. Journey

Collect Event Coins and Hero's Way Points by beginning Expeditions.

Strategy:

  • Expeditions: Start your expeditions as soon as the event begins. Expeditions that take longer but offer better rewards should be prioritized.
  • Completion Timing: Plan the timing of your expeditions to ensure they finish while youâ€™re online, allowing you to start new ones immediately.

7. The Way Up

Get Event Coins and Hero's Way Points for opening chests in the Tower.

Strategy:

  • Tower Strategy: If you're a beginner, focus on climbing as high as possible in the Tower. Higher floors offer better rewards and allow you to earn more Event Coins.
  • Chest Prioritization: When opening chests, focus on the most valuable orange item chests and prioritize those that are harder to obtain to maximize your rewards.

8. Promote Hero

Receive Event Coins and Hero's Way Points for promoting the event hero.

Strategy:

  • Hero Promotion: Focus on promoting the event hero during the event to maximize your Event Coins and Hero's Way Points.
  • Resource Allocation: Allocate your resources to ensure you have enough to promote the hero effectively during the event.

Tips for the Event Shop

In the event shop, you can purchase various items, including equipment, Artifacts, Demon Dolls, which provide a range of items like hero soul stones, gold, and artifacts chests.

Recommended Purchases

  • Artifact Boxes: Investing in buying artifact fragments, especially the orange ones, is highly recommended. When you open these boxes, you have a chance to obtain items needed to upgrade artifacts to the orange level, which are rarer and more valuable.
  • Event-Specific Artifacts: Focus on acquiring the event heroâ€™s weapon, book, and ring artifacts, as these are rarer and more difficult to obtain compared to other artifact upgrade items. While ring artifacts are important, they can be obtained more easily during hero events by opening Titan summoning spheres.

Items to Avoid

Avoid purchasing blue, purple, and orange hero equipment in the event shop as they are more efficiently acquired through energy spending. Only buy these if absolutely necessary.

An In-Depth Conclusion of the Way of Honor Event

The "Way of Honor" event offers a unique opportunity to strengthen your Honor faction heroes and acquire valuable resources. By focusing on rare soul stones, strategically spending event coins, and following the quest chains, you can maximize your gains and bolster your team for future battles. Plan ahead, save your resources, and make the most of this exciting event in Hero Wars Alliance.

With this comprehensive guide, you'll be well-equipped to navigate the Way of Honor event and emerge victorious, ensuring your heroes are ready for any challenge that comes their way.
