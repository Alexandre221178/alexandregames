Efficient Strategies for Using Lian in Hero Wars Alliance

Lian is a powerful heroine in Hero Wars Alliance, with unique abilities that can turn the tide of a battle. Her mastery over enchantment is unparalleled, making her a valuable choice, especially against opponents who rely on area attacks and mass damage. In this tutorial, we will explore effective strategies for utilizing Lian to her fullest potential.

Get to Know Lian's Abilities

Before diving into the strategies, it's crucial to understand Lian's abilities:

  • Enchantment: This ability enchants all enemies, putting them to sleep for 7 seconds. Enemies wake up when taking damage, but Lian's attack doesn't wake them up.
  • Hypnotic Dance: Lian fires a magic ball at the nearest enemies, dealing pure damage wherever it passes. This ability can cause a total of 54,226.5 damage.
  • Inconstant Lights: She creates 5 magic spheres and launches them at the nearest enemy, causing magical damage. Each sphere can cause up to 32,535.9 damage.
  • Reconciliation: This is a passive ability. When Lian takes damage, the enemy who dealt the damage is enchanted for 4 seconds.

Usage Strategies

  1. Crowd Control

    Lian shines when it comes to crowd control. Use her Enchantment ability to put all enemies to sleep for 7 seconds. This can be crucial to interrupt devastating combos from enemy heroes, especially those relying on area attacks.

  2. Devastating Combos

    Combine Lian's abilities with those of her allies to create devastating combos. For example, synchronize Lian's Enchantment with other heroes' area damage abilities to maximize crowd control and damage potential.

  3. Efficient Counter-Attack

    Take advantage of Lian's Reconciliation passive ability to perform efficient counter-attacks. When she takes damage, the enemy who dealt it will be enchanted for 4 seconds. Use this time to launch powerful attacks and tilt the battlefield in your favor.

  4. Focus on Primary Targets

    Prioritize your attacks on the most important targets. Use Inconstant Lights to cause concentrated damage to the nearest enemy. This can be particularly useful for eliminating long-range threats or enemy healers supporting their team.

  5. Adaptation to the Situation

    Be prepared to adapt your strategies based on the enemy team composition and battle conditions. Lian is versatile and can play different roles depending on the situation. Be flexible and willing to adjust your tactics as needed.

Lian's Talisman

Lian is a very strong heroine with an incredible Ultimate, and now with her Talisman, she has become even stronger. She is not a hero focused on causing a lot of damage, but rather on using her skills to enchant enemies.

Leveling up Lian's Talisman is not a priority for beginners, but she certainly gets very strong with the talisman.

Guide: Best Teams with Lian in 2024

Lian is a versatile heroine in Hero Wars Alliance, capable of integrating into different teams and contributing to victory with her unique abilities. In this guide, we'll present some of the best team combinations involving Lian. Each team listed below has been carefully selected based on skill synergies and proven effectiveness in various battle contexts.

1. Lian, Astrid, Isaac, Judge, Julius

This team stands out for its crowd control capability and concentrated damage. Astrid and Isaac provide additional support, while Judge and Julius ensure resilience and protection.

2. Lian, Jorgen, Mojo, Alvanor, Mushy

A balanced team with a strong focus on buffs and debuffs. Jorgen and Mojo excel at controlling the battlefield, while Alvanor and Mushy deal significant damage to enemies.

3. Aidan, Lian, Xe'sha, Kayla, Cleaver

An aggressive team with great survival capability. Lian provides crowd control, while Aidan and Cleaver are the main attackers. Xe'sha and Kayla offer additional support and healing.

4. Aidan, Lian, Jorgen, Kayla, Cleaver

Similar to the previous team, but with the addition of Jorgen to increase crowd control and debuffs. This team is effective both offensively and defensively.

5. Lian, Sebastian, Ishmael, Tristan, Ziri

A team centered around Lian for crowd control and damage. Sebastian and Ishmael provide additional damage, while Tristan ensures armor penetration and Ziri guarantees protection and sustain.

6. Lian, Faceless, Nebula, K'arkh, Astaroth

A team focused on buffs and massive damage. Faceless and Nebula boost K'arkh's attack power, while Astaroth provides resilience and protection.

7. Lian, Faceless, Jorgen, Celeste, Aurora

A balanced team with a focus on crowd control and support. Jorgen and Faceless ensure control over the battlefield, while Celeste and Aurora offer healing and buffs.

8. Lian, Amira, Celeste, Satori, Astaroth

A team with strong healing capability and concentrated damage. Amira and Satori are the main attackers, while Celeste provides healing support and Astaroth protection.

9. Lian, Jorgen, Celeste, Satori, Astaroth

Similar to the previous team, but with the addition of Jorgen for more crowd control and debuffs. This team is effective at prolonging battles and wearing down enemies.

10. Lian, Faceless, Celeste, Satori, Astaroth

A versatile team focusing on crowd control and concentrated damage. Faceless and Celeste offer additional support, while Satori and Astaroth handle the damage.

11. Martha, Lian, Celeste, Satori, Astaroth

A team emphasizing sustainment and crowd control. Lian and Astaroth provide additional protection, while Celeste and Martha take care of healing, and Satori deals damage.

12. Lilith, Peppy, Dorian, Phobos, Lian

A unique team focusing on buffs and debuffs. Lilith, Peppy, and Phobos confuse enemies, while Dorian provides healing support and Lian controls crowds.

Conclusion of the Lian Guide

Lian is a valuable addition to any team in Hero Wars Alliance, thanks to her crowd control abilities and ability to inflict significant damage. By mastering her skills and implementing effective strategies, you can make the most of Lian's potential and ensure victory for your team. Experiment with different combinations and tactics to discover what works best in various battle situations. May your adventures with Lian be filled with success and glory in Hero Wars Alliance!
