Lian History


Hero Wars Alliance History: Following the path of love, the wild cat became a beautiful young woman. But her happiness was short-lived, as her love died in battle. Devastated but still strong in spirit, Lian decided to use the power of sincere feelings to fight evil.

Strategy on how to use Lian

Lian is a charming character with the ability to enchant enemies. That's right, her enchantment with the Ultimate ability puts all enemies to sleep for 7 seconds when it is activated, and during this time they are unable to counterattack.

The Ultimate Enchantment and Conciliation skill makes Lian the strongest counter to Keira and Ginger in Hero Wars Alliance. The conciliation skill makes enemies fall asleep for 4 seconds whenever they cause damage to Lian. Keira's basic damage ability, ricochets, causing damage to all enemies, and thus receives the enchantment.

Lian is strong against all enemies that cause area damage and often, as a support, he ends up being better than a healer. Better than healing allies is putting the enemy team to sleep, not allowing them to attack or receive energy.


Positive and Negative Points

Lian Positive Points

  • Ultimate puts all enemies to sleep for 7 seconds
  • Deals pure damage
  • Whenever Lian takes damage, she will paralyze the enemy for 4 seconds

Lian Negative Points

  • Does low damage
  • If an ally hits a sleeping enemy, it will immediately wake them

Lian vs Hydra

Lian is not a great hero for the Hydra. As a support, she does little magical damage in her skills.

Lian Best Counters

Counters What happens

Corvus
When Lian uses his Hypnotic Ball or Shifting Lights skill, she can deal a good deal of damage to frontline enemies and the Corvus Altar reciprocates with pure damage.

Cornelius
Cornelius attacks the enemy with the highest intelligence, causing a lot of physical damage with a huge monolith.

Phobos
Phobos tortures the enemy with the highest Magic Attack, causing magic damage. While torturing the enemies, Phobos does not attack them. This way, Lian cannot charm him.

Max level Skills

1st Enchantment

Charms all enemies, putting them to sleep for 7.0 seconds. Sleeping enemies wake up when damaged.
Lian's attacks do not wake enemies. Charm chance lowered if the target's level is higher than 120.

2nd Hypnotic Ball

Fires a magical ball at the nearest enemies, then brings it back, dealing pure damage on the way.
Damage: 41326 (50% Mag. atk. + 6000).

3rd Wandering Ligths

Creates 5 magical spheres and launches them at the nearest enemy, dealing magical damage.
Magic sphere damage: 24795 (30% Mag. atk. + 3600).

4th Conciliation

Passive skill. When Lian is damaged, the enemy who dealt damage is charmed for 4.0 seconds.
Charm chance lowered if the target's level is higher than 120.
