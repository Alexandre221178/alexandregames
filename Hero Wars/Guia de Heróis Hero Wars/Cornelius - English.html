Strategy on how to use Cornelius

Cornelius is the best Mage Counter in Hero Wars and is definitely worth investing in, his artifact provides team-wide magic defense when activated.

Cornelius has skill that gives powerful magic defense shield to allies, has skill that reduces the magic damage of the strongest enemy and curses a random enemy reducing Ultimate damage, in addition, Cornelius' ultimate deals physical damage against mage heroes and the damage is proportional to the intelligence attribute of the enemy causing powerful mortal damage with no chance to enemies.

So, Cornelius manages to protect the whole team against magic damage using his shield, after Rufus, Cornelius is the hero with the highest magic resistance, he is just not more resistant than Rufus because Rufus is invincible against mages, however, Rufus is a tank and Cornelius is a Mage Killer, if a Mage suffers a single attack from Cornelius' Ultimate the chance to survive is 0.5% even at lower levels of the early game!!!!


Positive and Negative Points

Positive Points

  • High physical damage on mages with his Heavy Wisdom skill
  • Lots of Magic Defense
  • Magic Shield for Allies
  • Reduces Enemies' Magic Attack
  • Curses a random enemy by lowering Ultimate's level

Negative Points

  • Weak armor defense
  • Weak against agility attribute heroes
  • Does not deal magic damage

Stats Evolution Priority

Glyphs Priority

In Cornelius' priority glyphs for magic attack and intelligence, the more magic attack the more magic protection, as Cornelius has enough magic defense for him and his allies in skills, then prioritize health and defense.

  1. Magic Attack
  2. Intelligence
  3. Health
  4. Magical Defense
  5. Armor

Artifacts Priority

First level up the book to gain magic attack and health, then the ring to gain more magic defense and magic attack, and lastly the weapon to gain bonus magic defense for the whole team.

  1. Book
  2. Weapon (Magical Defense)
  3. Ring

Skins Priority

In Cornelius skins, prioritize leveling up, first magic attack and health to gain resistance, then again magic attack and intelligence, and lastly magic defense.

  1. Magic Attack
  2. Health
  3. Magic Attack
  4. Intelligence
  5. Magical Defense

Cornelius vs Hydras

Cornelius is a good hero against Hydras and can help his team by protecting with his shield absorbing magic damage, Cornelius can be a good ally against Magic Hydras making his team resist longer; mostly for novice players

Max level Skills

1st Heavy Wisdom

The target of Cornelius' monolith is the highest Intelligence enemy on the enemy team, the monolith deals physical damage proportional to the enemy target's Intelligence level. The damage dealt by the monolith is the enemy's Intelligence value divided by 100, take that result and multiply it by the damage of the Heavy Wisdom skill.
Damage per 100 points of target's Intelligence: Max {21020} Skill Formula: (20% Magic Attack + 30 * Skill Level) Keywords: debuff

2nd Rune of Suppression

Cornelius casts a spell on the enemy with the highest Magic Attack and reduces their Magic Attack for 12 seconds.
Temporary Magic Attack Reduction: Max (49395) Formula: (45% Magic Attack + 85 * Skill Level)

3rd Protective Dome

Cornelius creates a protective barrier in turn that increases the magic defense of all allies for 6 seconds.
Temporary Magic Defense Increase: Max (31530) Skill Formula: (30% * Magic Attack + 40 * (Skill Level + 20) + 600) Keywords: buff

4th Memory Loss

: At the start of the battle, Cornelius chooses a random enemy and casts a curse. The curse reduces the level of the enemy target's first skill, this skill only disappears if Cornelius dies or is removed by curse removal skills. Example: Celeste and Sebastian.
The target's first skill level decreases by Max (95) Skill Formula: (1 * (Skill Level + 40) -25) or (120-95= level 25) Keywords: curse
