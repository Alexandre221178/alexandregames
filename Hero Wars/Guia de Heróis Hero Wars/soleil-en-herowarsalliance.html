Hero Wars Alliance: Complete Guide to Soleil

Soleil is a powerful priest who has managed to defeat even a deity, and now he is ready to join the ranks of the Guardians in the battle against chaos. Reflecting control effects, weakening enemies, and strengthening allies make Soleil a vital part of any team.

Artifacts:

  • Prison of the Ancient God: Magic Attack
  • Tome of Arcane Knowledge: Magic Attack + Health
  • Ring of Intelligence: Intelligence

Soleil Skills:

1ª Curse of the Ancient God

Description: Casts the Curse of the Ancient God on the nearest enemy for a certain amount of time, stealing Armor and Magic Defense from them and dealing damage. The Armor and Magic Defense stolen from the enemy are temporarily and evenly distributed among all allies.

Tips: Use this skill to debilitate strong enemy tanks and boost your team's overall defense. Timing this skill correctly can significantly weaken enemy frontline heroes, making it easier for your damage dealers to penetrate their defenses.

2ª Halo

Description: Temporarily envelops the frontmost ally in a magical halo, reducing the damage they take. The ally with the halo cannot be stunned, blinded, or silenced. If an enemy tries to stun, blind, or silence the haloed ally, the effect will rebound onto the enemy themselves for 3 seconds.

Tips: Use Halo on your main tank or any crucial frontline hero to ensure they stay in the fight longer and avoid being controlled. This skill is particularly effective against teams that rely heavily on control abilities.

3ª Mark of the Ancient God

Description: Applies the Mark of the Ancient God to the central enemy for a certain amount of time. The enemy takes damage with each use of their abilities and takes additional damage for using control abilities against heroes.

Tips: Target the enemy’s main damage dealer or control hero with this mark to punish them for using their abilities. This skill can disrupt the enemy's strategy and force them to rethink their approach.

4ª Blessing of the Ancient God

Description: Temporarily increases damage from physical and magical attacks, as well as healing, to all allies in front of Soleil. Additionally, increases damage and healing for each control ability applied by enemies since the beginning of the battle.

Tips: Position Soleil strategically so that the maximum number of allies benefits from this buff. This skill scales with the number of control abilities used by the enemy, making it more effective in prolonged battles.

Strategies for Using Soleil:

Synergy with Allies

Pair Soleil with heroes like Qing Mao, Markus, and Ishmael to maximize the synergy within the Way of Honor faction. These heroes can benefit greatly from Soleil’s buffs and protection.

Positioning

As a middle-line hero, Soleil should be placed where he can support both the frontline and backline heroes effectively. Ensure he is protected but still close enough to apply his buffs and debuffs.

Maximize Artifact Usage

Focus on boosting Soleil’s Intelligence to enhance his magic attack power and overall effectiveness. Prioritize leveling up his artifacts to make his skills more impactful.

By leveraging Soleil’s unique abilities to weaken enemies and strengthen allies, you can turn the tide of battle in your favor. Use his skills strategically to disrupt enemy plans and empower your team, making Soleil an indispensable part of your Hero Wars Alliance lineup.

Comprehensive Analysis of Soleil's Talisman

Soleil's Talisman is a game-changer in Hero Wars Alliance, significantly enhancing his magical and physical defenses. The substantial boost in intelligence not only increases his magical attack but also enhances his magical defense, making him a more versatile hero capable of both offense and defense. The additional armor points from the Reroll ability further solidify his role as a resilient defender.

The combination of increased intelligence and armor makes Soleil a formidable opponent in battles. His ability to deal substantial magical damage while withstanding both magical and physical attacks ensures he can hold his ground and protect his allies effectively. This makes him an invaluable asset in various team compositions, particularly those that require a balance of offense and defense.

How Soleil Counters Each Control Hero

Utilize Soleil’s abilities to counter enemy control tactics. Skills like Halo and Mark of the Ancient God can reflect and punish enemy control abilities, disrupting their strategy and giving your team an advantage.

Isaac:

Ability: Isaac can push enemies and reduce their armor with control effect.

Counter: Use Mark of the Ancient God to make Isaac takes additional damage for using control attempts to your ally, the effect will rebound onto Isaac, disrupting his strategy.

Arachne:

Ability: Arachne can stun and knock up enemies.

Counter: Apply Halo to protect your frontline hero from Arachne's stuns and knock-ups. This will reflect these effects back onto Arachne, reducing her effectiveness.

Judge:

Ability: Judge can stun and slow enemies.

Counter: With Mark of the Ancient God, you can target Judge to punish him for using his control abilities, making him take additional damage and think twice before using his control skills.

Phobos:

Ability: Phobos can fear and control enemy heroes.

Counter: Halo can prevent fear effects on your main tank or frontline heroes. Any attempt by Phobos to instill fear will be reflected back onto him.

Faceless:

Ability: Faceless can stun and copy abilities.

Counter: Curse of the Ancient God can be used to weaken Faceless, reducing his ability to control your team effectively. The stolen defenses boost your team’s resilience against his copied abilities.

Peppy:

Ability: Peppy can charm and control enemies.

Counter: Halo will protect against Peppy's charm, reflecting it back onto her. Mark of the Ancient God can also be used to ensure Peppy takes damage for using her control skills.

Jorgen:

Ability: Jorgen can steal energy and apply crowd control effects.

Counter: Use Halo to prevent energy theft and control effects on your key heroes. Mark of the Ancient God punishes Jorgen for using his control abilities, causing him to take additional damage.

Andvari:

Ability: Andvari can control and stun enemies with Stone Grasp.

Counter: Halo protects against the stun and bind effects of Stone Grasp, reflecting them back onto Andvari. Curse of the Ancient God reduces his defenses, making him easier to eliminate.

Lian:

Ability: Lian can charm and sleep enemies.

Counter: Halo shields your heroes from charm and sleep effects, reflecting these back onto Lian. Mark of the Ancient God targets Lian to make her take extra damage for using her control abilities.

Soleil Guide Conclusion

Soleil’s skills provide robust counters against each control hero, using Halo to reflect and nullify control effects, Mark of the Ancient God to punish control abilities, and Curse of the Ancient God to weaken and steal defenses. By leveraging these abilities strategically, Soleil can significantly reduce the impact of control heroes in Hero Wars Alliance.
