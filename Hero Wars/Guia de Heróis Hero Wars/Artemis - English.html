Strategy on how to use Artemis

Artemis is a great assassin as a ranged sniper attacker and is among the best Meta teams of 2023 for Hero Wars Mobile. Recently with its rework Artemis is very strong mainly in combination with heroes of the honor faction like Tristan that can help her with armor penetration.


  • Advertisement
     

In addition, the new hero Fafnir who is also an ally of the honor faction grants a shield making it even more difficult to attack an enemy's life; since her 2022 rework Artemis has become very competitive and will start showing up on top teams.

Artemis has a lot of chance of critical attack in its statistics, which eliminates the need to use Jet. In this way, there is space on your team for other good supports that will give a lot of work to the enemies; you can use a Cornelius who is a hero of honor and will give a lot of magic defense to Artemis.

Artemis's stun and blind skills are great! It's worth trying, there are no invincible heroes in the end-game, so if you liked Artemis you can invest without fear…


Positive and Negative Points

Positive Points

  • Critical Damage
  • Gain buff on high health team
  • Damage to all enemies
  • Over Power

Negative Points

  • Depends on allies with armor penetration
  • Depends on the amount of health of allies to gain buff
  • Low defense

Stats Evolution Priority

Glyphs Priority

In glyphs focus on armor penetration and critical damage chance, then agility to increase critical damage chance, in fourth physical attack Artemis already gains enough physical attack buff on skills, and health last.

  1. Armor Penetration
  2. Critical Hit Chance
  3. Agility
  4. Physical Attack
  5. Health

Artifacts Priority

In Artemis artifacts prioritize the book to gain more armor penetration and physical attack, then the ring to gain physical attack, armor and also increase the chance of critical damage. Finally, the weapon to give physical attack to the whole team.

  1. Book
  2. Ring
  3. Weapon (Physical Attack)

Skins Priority

In skins priorize critical hit chance and agility, then health for survivability, and lastly physical attack and magic defense.

  1. Critical Hit Chance
  2. Agility
  3. Health
  4. Physical Attack
  5. Magical Defense



Artemis vs Hydra

Artemis is a good hero against hydras, but hydras are immune to blind and stun, which causes Artemis to lose his extra damage received from blind and stun effects against Hydras.

Max level Skills

1st Deadly Shower

Artemis launches a barrage of arrows, hitting all enemies. Fires an extra arrow each time an ally uses their first ability, or two if your ally hero is an honor faction hero.
Each arrow does Max(13995) - (15% Physical Attack + 50 * Damage level)

2nd True Light

Shoots a light that damages all enemies and blinds them in 5.0s. Artemis deals extra damage to blinded or stunned enemies.
Flash Damage: Max(8330) - (10% Physical Attack + 25 * Skill Level) Extra damage: Max(8930) - (10% Physical Attack + 30 * Skill Level) Chance: Chance to blind reduced if target's level is above MAX skill level (120 = 1 * Skill Level)

3rd Smart Arrow

Whenever an enemy hero is stunned or blinded, Artemis attacks them once more. If there are more than one of these enemies, the farthest one is the target. Can be triggered once every 4.0 seconds.
Damage: Max(56900) - (100% Physical Attack) + 30 * (Skill Level + 20)

4th Punishing Arrows

Passive Skill: Artemis increases your Physical Attack. Physical attack boost increases based on the current total health of Artemis's allies.
Physical Attack Buff: Max(1) - (1% * (Skill Level + 40))
