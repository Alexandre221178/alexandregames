Positive and Negative Points

Positive Points

  • Weak against physical damage teams

  • Can be removed from the middle of the enemy team Faceless

Stats Evolution Priority

Glyphs Priority

In Kayla's glyphs, you need to prioritize physical attack and life, as Kayla needs these two attributes to increase the powers of her skills, then agility to gain more physical attack and armor. Lastly, focuses on Armor and lastly, Magic Defense.

  1. Physical attack
  2. Health
  3. Agility
  4. Armor
  5. Magic Defense

Artifacts Priority

In artifacts prioritize the ring to gain more physical attack and armor, then the book, and lastly the weapon to gain physical attack for all allies.

  1. Ring
  2. Book
  3. Weapon (Physical Attack)

Skins Priority

  1. Agility



Kayla vs Hydra

As you may have noticed, Kayla is a great hero against the magical hydras, but is not being used much, as her brother Aidan in combination with other heroes has a better combination with Martha, Aidan, Faceless, Xe`sha, Mushy and Shroom , capable of dealing over 200 million damage and annihilating a hydra's head with just one attempt.

Strategy on how to use Kayla

Kayla and her brother Aidan are the new heroes of the 2023 Chaos faction, they arrived with excellent synergy and with power comparable to the combo of the twins Krista and Lars, Kayla is a warrior with the power to resurrect Aidan every time he uses his skill special and Aidan can also resurrect Kayla whenever he uses his ultimate, so the two become almost immortal in battle.

Kayla has the skill with the sacrifice keyword, and every time she uses it she will be providing a buff to Xe'sha's fourth ability High Might that grants her a 25% attack bonus with each life sacrifice with skills .

She can also regenerate her own life making her more durable in battles, Kayla can make a great combo with Luther jumping with him to the center of the enemy team while doing pure damage to all enemies.
