Strategy on how to use Mojo

Mojo is a great hero against hydras, in combination with Jhu's 1st skill that makes him immortal while his 4th skill restores his health; damage mainly if used together with the hero Mojo that causes magic damage in his 4th skill even after being dead.

That's right, hydras are not immune to Mojo's mark and you just need to have a Purple level Mojo for this perfect combo to cause a lot of damage in all hydras; Mojo is the only hero in the game that has an exclusive ability to cause negative affect at hydras, and it's definitely worth it; invest.

Positive and Negative Points

Positive Points

  • Deals damage to all enemies
  • Stuns Enemies
  • Healer
  • High damage at hydras even dead

Negative Points

  • His first skill can be countered with damage in area AOE (Keira, Ginger)
  • Weak against teams of Cornelius, Iris Phobos

Mojo at Max Level in Hero Wars Mobile

Max level Skills

1st Totem of Wrath

Creates a totem that shoots magic projectiles at the two closes enemies for 4.0 seconds. The skill lasts for 0.15 seconds longer for each Wrath of the Spirits charge. Mojo cannot attack or use skills during this time.
Damage per missile: Max(16772) - (20% Magic Attack + 15 * Skill Level)

2nd Healing Spirits

Heals allies. If an ally has been fully healed by this skill, they receive 10% energy, while MOjo himself receives a Wrath of the Spirits charge. Healed Nature Heroes receive 10% extra energy.
Maximum healing: Max(42030) - (30% * Magic Attack + 30 * Skill Level + 1000)

3rd Cursed Bones

Shoots a projectile at a random enemy, dealing magic damage and stunning the target. If an enemy burns or steals an ally's energy, Mojo uses this skill on that enemy, dealing extra damage for every Wrath of the Spirit's charge.
Damage: Max(18572) - (20% Magic Attack + 30 * (Skill Level + 20)) Extra Damage for each Wrath of the Spirits charge: Max(18572) - (20% Magic Attack + 30 * (Skill Level + 20)) Keywords: Stun

4th Hex

Curses the enemy every 2.0 seconds, applying a Hex mark to the Hero that took the most damage during that time. The Mark channel's damage taken within 2.0 seconds is equal to the current percentage of Mojo's Health. No less than 20% and no more than 80%. If an enemy dies to this skill, Mojo gets 3 Wrath charges. The skill remains active after Mojo dies, dealing an extra 20% of the damage the target has taken.
Maximum Mark damage: Max(80%) - (0.5 * (Skill Level + 40) + 20) of damage received by the target Keywords: Mark
