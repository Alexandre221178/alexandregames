Strategy on How to Use Mojo in Hero Wars Alliance

Mojo is a versatile and powerful hero in Hero Wars Alliance, capable of turning the tide of battle with strategic use. Not only is Mojo effective in various game modes like campaign missions and arena battles, but it also proves invaluable in specific tasks such as defeating Brog Missions in Outland and tackling Hydra encounters. In this article, we'll delve into the intricacies of using Mojo effectively and explore its synergies and strengths.

Mojo's Role in Hero Wars Alliance:

Mojo serves multiple roles within Hero Wars Alliance, making it a valuable asset in any team composition. Its abilities as a Mage and Support hero allow it to fulfill roles ranging from dealing damage to providing healing and utility support. Understanding Mojo's skills and how they synergize with other heroes is crucial to maximizing its potential on the battlefield.

Utilizing Mojo in Campaign and Outland Missions:

One of Mojo's primary strengths lies in its versatility, making it a valuable addition to any team in campaign missions and Outland activities. Its Totem of Wrath skill, which deals magic damage to enemies, can help clear waves of opponents efficiently. Additionally, Mojo's Healing Spirits skill provides much-needed support by healing allies and granting energy, making it indispensable in sustaining your team throughout challenging encounters.

Defeating Brog Missions:

Brog Missions in Outland present a significant challenge for beginner players, but Mojo can prove to be a valuable ally in overcoming these obstacles. With its ability to deal magic damage and provide healing support, Mojo can help sustain your team through prolonged battles and mitigate the threat posed by Brog's stuns. By strategically timing its skills and coordinating with other heroes, Mojo can turn the tide in your favor and secure victory against Brog's forces.

Synergizing with Nature Faction Allies:

Mojo's effectiveness is further enhanced when paired with Nature Faction allies. The synergy between Mojo and other Nature heroes amplifies its abilities, making it even more potent in battle. Nature heroes often benefit from Mojo's Healing Spirits skill, receiving extra energy and healing, which can be crucial in maintaining their presence on the battlefield. Moreover, Mojo's Hex ability, which curses enemies and deals damage based on Mojo's health percentage, synergizes well with Nature heroes' abilities, creating devastating combos that can turn the tide of battle in your favor.

Combatting Hydras:

One of Mojo's standout abilities is its effectiveness against Hydras, particularly in combination with other heroes like Jhu. Mojo's Totem of Wrath skill, when paired with Jhu's immortality-granting ability, creates a formidable combo that deals significant damage to Hydras. Additionally, Mojo's Hex ability applies a mark to Hydras, allowing it to deal extra damage over time. This makes Mojo a pivotal hero in Hydra encounters and a valuable asset in any Hydra-focused team composition.

Investing in Mojo:

Considering its versatility, effectiveness against Hydras, and synergy with Nature Faction allies, investing in Mojo is a wise decision for any Hero Wars Alliance player. With its unique abilities and strategic utility, Mojo proves to be a valuable addition to any team composition, capable of turning the tide of battle and securing victory in challenging encounters.

A Guide to Understanding Mojo's Abilities in Hero Wars Alliance

In the mystical realm of Hero Wars Alliance, where battles are fought and destinies are forged, Mojo stands as a powerful force to be reckoned with. As a versatile Shaman, Mojo possesses a diverse array of skills that can turn the tide of battle in favor of allies and strike fear into the hearts of enemies. In this article, we'll delve into the intricacies of Mojo's abilities, shedding light on how to wield this enigmatic hero to maximum effect.

1st Totem of Wrath

Mojo's first skill, Totem of Wrath, conjures a mystical totem that unleashes a barrage of magic projectiles upon the two closest enemies for a duration of 4.0 seconds. As Mojo's connection with the spirits grows stronger, the skill's duration extends by 0.15 seconds for each Wrath of the Spirits charge accumulated. It's important to note that during this time, Mojo is unable to launch further attacks or employ other skills. The damage inflicted by each projectile is determined by a formula incorporating Mojo's Magic Attack and skill level, ensuring devastating effects on the battlefield.

2nd Healing Spirits

The second skill in Mojo's arsenal, Healing Spirits, showcases the Shaman's benevolent side. This ability allows Mojo to heal allies, providing them with much-needed sustenance in the heat of battle. Moreover, if an ally is fully healed by this skill, they receive a 10% energy boost, while Mojo himself gains a Wrath of the Spirits charge. Notably, Nature Heroes healed by Mojo receive an additional 10% energy, enhancing their combat prowess and resilience.

3rd Cursed Bones

Cursed Bones, Mojo's third skill, embodies the Shaman's ability to unleash potent mystical forces upon enemies. With this skill, Mojo hurls a projectile at a random foe, dealing magic damage and stunning the target. However, the true power of Cursed Bones lies in its ability to strike back against those who would dare to harm Mojo's allies. If an enemy inflicts burns or steals an ally's energy, Mojo targets them with Cursed Bones, dealing additional damage for each Wrath of the Spirits charge accumulated. This skill not only inflicts pain but also serves as a potent deterrent against those who would threaten Mojo's allies.

4th Hex

Finally, Mojo's fourth skill, Hex, invokes a dark curse upon enemies, marking the Hero who suffers the most damage within a 2.0-second interval. This cursed mark channels damage to the afflicted Hero based on the current percentage of Mojo's health, ranging from 20% to 80%. Notably, if an enemy succumbs to this curse, Mojo gains three Wrath charges, empowering him further. Even in death, Mojo's Hex remains active, dealing additional damage based on the harm inflicted upon the marked target.

Mojo's versatility

Mojo's abilities make him a formidable asset in the ever-evolving battles of Hero Wars Alliance. From unleashing devastating barrages of magic to healing and protecting allies, Mojo's versatility knows no bounds. By mastering the intricacies of his skills and understanding their synergies, players can unleash the full potential of this enigmatic Shaman, turning the tide of battle in their favor and securing victory on the battlefield.

Mojo Guide Conclusion

In conclusion, Mojo stands as a formidable hero in Hero Wars Alliance, offering versatility, strategic utility, and synergy with Nature Faction allies. Whether it's clearing campaign missions, defeating Brog in Outland, or combatting Hydras, Mojo proves to be an invaluable asset capable of turning the tide of battle in your favor. By understanding its skills, synergies, and strategic applications, players can harness the full potential of Mojo and achieve success in Hero Wars Alliance.
