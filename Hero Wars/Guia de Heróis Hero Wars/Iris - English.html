Strategy on how to use Iris

Iris is a great hero worth investing in. Iris will normally be seen on top end-game teams in both server wars and global wars, with the right allies Iris can become very strong, particularly against teams of Satori and the twins Krista and Lars.


  • Advertisement
     

My honest opinion about Iris: Iris is very strong against twins and Satori, but since we already have Cornelius and Phobos, Iris serves more to help the eternity allies against debuffs; Phobos was weak against Amira and Iris didn't help Phobos having his attacks blocked by Amira, and unfortunately I got better results from Iris with Cornelius; Iris has low magic penetration and will do better with Faceless.


Positive and Negative Points

Positive Points

  • Strong against Satori and the twins
  • Reduces enemies' attack
  • Reduces enemies' magic defense
  • Immune to debuffs
  • Increases the time of allied debuffs

Negative Points

  • Low defense and health
  • Weak against Amira
  • Weak against Altar of Corvus
  • Very weak against physical attack teams

Iris vs Hydra

Iris can be used against hydras. Hydras are immune to debuffs, but Iris is also immune to debuffs applied by hydras.

  • Hydras that cause debuffs: Fire; Darkness; Light.

Max level Skills

1st Burning of the Soul

When any ally or enemy receives Buff Iris, Iris will receive pure soul charges. Thus, when Iris uses the skill, she will deal damage to all enemies proportional to the amount of pure souls captured, thus eliminating everyone who has life below the integrity limit of the execution. Base Damage: Max(37551) - Skill Formula: (40% * Magic Attack + skill level * 100)
Damage per charge of Pure Soul: Max(3793) - Skill Formula: (5% * Magic Attack + skill level * 5) Execution Integrity Limit: Max(37551) - Skill Formula: (40% * Magic Attack + skill level * 100)

2nd Creature from another world

Iris throws Bitey towards the enemy team, Bitey pierces the enemy line and reduces enemies' magic defense for 5 seconds, and extends the time of any debuffs, no matter if they are already active or inflicted while using the ability.
Temporary Magic Defense Reduction: Max(14575) - Skill Formula (20% * Magic Attack + skill level * 15) Keywords: debuff

3rd Mysterious Pact

Iris cannot receive debuffs, and if a debuff or Mark is applied to an ally. Iris will debuff them for 7 seconds by lowering their Physical Attack and Magic Attack.
Temporary Physical Attack Reduction: Max(8787) - Skill Formula (10% * Magic Attack + (skill level + 20) * 20) Temporary Magic Attack Reduction: Max(8787) - Skill Formula (10% * Magic Attack + (skill level + 20) * 20) Keywords: debuff

4th Inner Fire

Whenever an ally or Iris applies a debuff to any enemy. Iris will burn them with the Inner Fire skill, while the debuff is in use this debuff will still deal damage. If the debuff is applied by an Eternity faction hero, the fire charge deals double damage.
Up to 5 charges stack on a target. Damage dealt per second for each charge of Inner Fire: Max(4393) - Skill Formula (5% * Magic Attack + (Skill Level + 40) * 10).
