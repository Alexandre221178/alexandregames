Strategy on how to use Isaac

Isaac is a great hero, especially if used in combination with teams from the progress faction, currently he is forming an excellent shield combo with Julius, Judge, Sebastian with Astrid and Lucas or Ginger, it is a very strong team and is worth the effort worth investing.


  • Advertisement
     

Isaac is not only great with progress heroes, but also with other physical damage heroes that position themselves in the backline like Danada, Fox, Darkstar.


Positive and Negative Points

Positive Points

  • Shield for allies
  • Physical attack buff
  • Magic Defense Artifact for the team
  • Armor penetration buff for Allies of Progress
  • Heals Progress allies

Negative Points

  • Heals only Progress allies
  • Has little synergy with diverse teams
  • Increases the physical attack only for heroes fighting in the backline: Ginger, Danada, Astrid and Lucas.

Isaac vs Hydras

Isaac is not a great hero against Hydras but he can be useful to help Ginger and Danada teams against Hydras.

Issac can also be used on physical hydras to protect your allies with the shield, I use it on earth hydra so Mojo doesn't die in the hydra's first attack.

  • Isaac's team for Earth Hydra: Martha, Isaac, Mojo, Jhu, Celeste (Damage: 17M to 22M)

Max level Skills

1st Tes'Lin's Overdrive

Tes'Lin launches an electrical charge forward, dealing physical damage to all enemies affected.
Damage: Max(36727) (60% Physical Attack + 30 * Skill Level)

2nd Observer Drones

Isaac launches drones that increase Physical Attack for him and his allies behind for 8.0 seconds. Observer Drones also increase Armor Penetration for Progress Heroes.
Physical Attack Bonus: Max(16803 (25% Physical Attack + 25 * Skill Level)) Armor Penetration increase: Max(8521) (25% Physical Attack + 25 * Skill Level) Keywords: Buff

3rd Shield Drones

Isaac launches drones that cast a shield over him and his allies ahead. The shield absorbs both physical and magical damage until its destruction. Shield Drones also heal Engineers under their protection.
Shield Health: Max(61212) - (100% Physical Attack + 50 * (Blue Skill Level + 20)) Healing: Max(14521) - (10% Physical Attack + 75 * (Blue Skill Level + 20))

4th High-Voltage Module

Tes'Lin's Overdrive knocks back all enemies, crushing their armor. Enemies knocked back to have their armor reduced for 5.0 seconds. The closest enemy to Isaac gets knocked back twice.
Temporary Armor Reduction: Max(2156 - (20% Magical Attack + 10 * (Violet Level + 40))) Keywords: Knockback, Debuff
