Strategy on how to use Ishmael

Ishmael is a good hero from early to late levels and is common to find him on top teams; when Ishmael uses his awakening ability he becomes very strong and causes a lot of damage to enemies, especially tanks, which will make Ishmael a great ally for Astrid and Lucas or Dante.

Talisman of Ishmael

With the talisman, Ishmael receives an increase in his Agility statistic, receiving more Physical Attack and Armor. At the maximum level, he will increase his physical attack by +18,000 points, and another 2,000 points of armor, receiving another 20170 points of protection against armor penetration, stopping the physical damage to enemies, so he will become quite a tank.

With the physical attack bonus from the talisman, Ishmael will be able to do more critical physical damage to enemies and will certainly be difficult to stop if your team doesn't have a strong tank like Ziri, for example.

Ishmael vs Hydra

Ishmael is not a great hero against hydras, but his vampirism ability can make him stay alive longer against hydras and deal good damage.
