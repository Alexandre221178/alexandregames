Strategy on how to use Ishmael

Ishmael is a good hero from early to late levels and is common to find him on top teams; when Ishmael uses his awakening ability he becomes very strong and causes a lot of damage to enemies, especially tanks, which will make Ishmael a great ally for Astrid and Lucas or Dante.

Ishmael vs Hydra

Ishmael is not a great hero against hydras, but his vampirism ability can make him stay alive longer against hydras and deal good damage.

Ishmael at Max Level in Hero Wars Mobile



Max level Skills

1st Awakening

Assumes a demonic form that increases critical hit chance and attack speed. Ishmael starts to gradually lose energy, when it reaches 0, the demonic form dissipates.
Critical hit chance increase: Max(1200) - (10 * Skill Level) Attack speed increase: 200% Keywords:Buff

2nd Mirage

Dodges physical attacks for 4 seconds.
Chance to dodge lowered if the target's level is higher than skill level Max(120 = Skill Level) Skill Cooldown: 12s

3rd Dark Craft

Passive skill. Increases vampirism.
Vampirism: Max(120) - (Skill Level + 20)

4th Storm Blades

Every 4th hit stuns the opponent for 1.0 seconds.
Stun Chance: Chance to stun is lowered if the target's level is above skill level Max(80 = Skill Level + 40) Keywords: Stun
