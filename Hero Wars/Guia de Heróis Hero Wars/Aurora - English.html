Aurora History


Hero Wars Alliance History: As a child, Aurora decided to follow the light. She left home and set out on a long journey to earn the title of Paladin of Riversar. So, she became the protector and light of her people.

Strategy on how to use Aurora

Aurora is the main tank of the nature faction and is very strong, one of its main advantages is the ability to absorb 80% of the magic damage received with a percentage equal to 10% of its life, preventing that damage from being directed towards the allies, and after absorbed, returns to the enemy team in the form of damage.


  • Advertisement
     

Aurora also has the ability to stun nearby enemies, that is, she can stun enemy frontline tanks giving her team time to gain energy and attack.

In Hero Wars Alliance Aurora is not as feared as in the web version, but even so, she is among the strongest heroes to invest in and have on your team.

Aurora's Talisman

With the Aurora Talisman, she will receive, 80000 more health points and 6000 more dodge points, leaving Aurora with 14131 dodge points.

In fact, Aurora will be very strong, returning to making good prints and combos with dodge times with Dante.

Stats Evolution Priority

Glyphs Priority

In Aurora's glyphs, prioritize dodge and then strength, as strength is your main stat and the calculation of percentage of dodge is based on the amount of the hero's main attribute plus the amount of dodge. Third, level up Health to gain more durability and also make your fourth skill stronger. Lastly go up magic penetration and magic attack.

  1. Dodge
  2. Strength
  3. Health
  4. Magic Penetration
  5. Magic Attack

Artifacts Priority

In Aurora's artifacts, prioritize the book to gain more health and dodge, then the ring to gain more health and raise the main attribute and finally the weapon to give dodge to the whole team when activated.

  1. Book
  2. Ring
  3. Weapon (Dodge)

Skins Priority

For Aurora skins, follow the same glyph evolution priorities.

  1. Dodge
  2. Strength
  3. Health
  4. Magic Penetration
  5. Magic Attack

Aurora vs Hydra

Aurora is not a great hero against hydras, but can help on secondary teams against magical hydras, being able to do reasonable and good damage.
