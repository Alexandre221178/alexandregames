Strategy on how to use Tristan

Underestimating Tristan is a mistake, as Tristan in the right team can become a very strong S+ rank hero.

Tristan is good against teams of mages, when he uses his skill (Sorcerer's Judge) he targets heroes with the highest magic penetration stats, burning the enemy's energy with higher magic penetration, in addition, he gives magic penetration armor on his artifacts, making it a great combo with Cornelius who is also of the same faction, and Artemis and Cornelius needs Tristan's artifact armor penetration to deal more damage to enemies.

Tristan also combos with Galahad gaining double energy and giving Galahad armor penetration, lack of armor penetration is Galahad's weakness. Tristan also makes a good combo with Artemis and Fafnir, Tristan can create very strong teams and it is worth investing.


Positive and Negative Points

Positive Points

  • Gains a lot of energy
  • Armor Penetration Artifact
  • Counter for mages with high magic penetration
  • Burns the energy of mages with high magic penetration

Negative Points

  • Acts more as a support for Artemis than a really warrior
  • Weak against hydras

Tristan vs Hydra

Tristan is a great hero for Hydras if used on Artemis teams, and can deal a lot of damage, providing quick armor penetration for Artemis on her artifacts.

  • Tristan's team suggestion for Water Hydra: Fafnir, Dorian, Artemis, Tristan, Galahad
