Markus Guide Hero Wars Alliance

Markus is one of the most exciting healing and supporting heroes, and he also excels in the Hydra because even after his death, he continues to heal allies for 42 seconds. His first ability has the power to heal the hero with less health and grant a shield that absorbs all damage.

Markus is the only support hero in the game with the ability to help his team even after death.

In Hero Wars, Markus and Luther form a strong combination. When Luther breaks into the enemy formation, Markus maintains the tank position for the allied team while protecting Luther with a shield and healing.

Even after his Rework and with all these qualities, he is not normally found in solid teams in Hero Wars Alliance battles.

Markus's Abilities:

  1. Holy Shield: Markus casts a shield on the ally with less health. The shield blocks any damage for 5.0 seconds and heals the ally.
    • Keywords: Shield, Healing: (130% of magic attack + 13200).
  2. Righteous Light: Markus launches a wave of light forward and backward, healing allies and dealing damage to enemies.
    • Allies healing: (35% of magic attack + 4800) Damage: (35% of magic attack + 4800).
  3. Compassion: Heals the ally with less health.
    • Healing: (65% of magic attack + 15500).
  4. Ascension: After dying, Markus temporarily becomes a ghost and can still use all available skills.
    • Enemies can't target the ghost. Spirit form duration: 42 seconds.

Advanced Strategies:

  1. Synergy with Luther: As mentioned earlier, Markus and Luther form a powerful duo. Luther can break enemy defenses while Markus protects and heals the team. Make sure to position Markus strategically to maximize Luther's protection and the team's healing.
  2. Maximizing the use of Holy Shield: Markus's "Holy Shield" ability is a crucial tool for protecting allies with less health. When using it, prioritize heroes who are at the greatest risk of being eliminated. This may include your tanks or other high-impact heroes who are under enemy focus.
  3. Managing Righteous Light: Markus's "Righteous Light" ability can heal allies and deal damage to enemies. Use this ability strategically to heal wounded allies while simultaneously dealing damage to enemies. This can be especially effective in moments when you need to keep your team alive while weakening enemy ranks.
  4. Timing of Ascension: Markus's unique ability, "Ascension," allows him to continue supporting his team even after his death. Use this to your advantage, especially in prolonged battles. Knowing the right time to sacrifice Markus can ensure that your team has a crucial advantage even after his fall.
  5. Building Teams Around Markus: Consider building teams that complement Markus's healing and support abilities. Heroes that can increase team resilience or cause crowd control can be particularly effective in combination with Markus.

Analysis of Markus' First and Second Talismans in Hero Wars Alliance

1. First Talisman: Talisman of Compassion

The first talisman, the Talisman of Compassion, provides intelligence and health. These attributes enhance Markus' survivability, allowing him to resist both physical and magical damage. The intelligence boost slightly increases his magical attack, improving his healing abilities. However, the overall impact on his healing power is limited compared to the second talisman. This talisman is more defensive, focusing on keeping Markus alive for extended periods in battle.

2. Second Talisman: Talisman of Salvation

The second talisman, the Talisman of Salvation, offers a significant improvement in Markus’ performance. It provides magical attack and armor, enhancing both his healing potential and survivability on the frontline. With up to three slots available for magical attack, this talisman greatly increases Markus' ability to heal his team effectively. The added armor also helps Markus withstand physical damage from basic attacks, which are prevalent in the game. This talisman is more balanced for a frontline healer, addressing both his defensive needs and boosting his offensive healing capabilities.

Comparison and Recommendation

  • First Talisman (Talisman of Compassion): Focuses on survivability through health and intelligence, making Markus more durable but offering only moderate improvements to his healing abilities.
  • Second Talisman (Talisman of Salvation): A more impactful option for Markus. The combination of increased magical attack and armor significantly enhances his healing effectiveness and frontline durability.

Overall, the Talisman of Salvation is the superior choice for Markus. Its ability to provide both higher magical attack and armor gives him a much-needed boost, allowing him to heal more effectively and withstand physical damage. This talisman could potentially make Markus a more viable option in the meta, improving his utility and presence in teams.

Markus vs Hydra

Markus Guide Conclusion

In conclusion, Markus emerges as a valuable piece in the tactical puzzle of Hero Wars Alliance. His unique ability to heal and protect allies, even after his own downfall, adds a crucial strategic dimension to the battlefield. Though underestimated by some, Markus offers a set of skills that can turn the tide of a battle when used wisely.

By deeply understanding the nuances of his abilities and applying advanced strategies, players can harness Markus' potential to the fullest. From strengthening alliances with Luther to precise management of the holy shield and strategic timing of ascension, there are countless ways to integrate Markus into winning teams.

Therefore, the next time you're assembling your team for a crucial battle, don't underestimate the tireless support of Markus. With him by your side, you'll be one step closer to achieving victory in Hero Wars Alliance.
