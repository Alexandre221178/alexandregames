Markus's History


Hero Wars Alliance History: Hero Wars Alliance Stories: An Order healer who miraculously survived a demonic attack on his monastery. A divine power that came from the heavens and helped him defeat his enemies and heal his dying allies. Since then, Markus has traveled the world distributing life.

Strategy on how to use Markus

Markus is one of the most exciting healing and supporting heroes, and he is also great on the hydra because even after death he still spends 42 seconds healing allies. His first ability has the power to heal the hero with less health and give a shield that absorbs all damage.

Markus is the only support and hero in the game with the ability to help his team even after death.

In Hero Wars, Markus and Luther form a strong combo. When Luther breaks into the enemy formation, Markus maintains the tank position for the allied team while protecting Luther with a shield and healing.

Even after his Rework and with all these qualities, he is not normally found in solid teams in Hero Wars Alliance battles.


Positive and Negative Points

Positive Points

  • Shield for allies with less health
  • Heals ally with less health
  • Can use all skills after death

Negative Points

  • Frontline support
  • Low resistance
  • After-death skill only lasts 42s

Markus vs Hydra

The ability to continue supporting the team even after his death makes Markus a good hero to attack the Hydras. This way, he will always heal his allies until the end of the battle, even though he has little hero power.

  • Markus' team suggestion for Light Hydra: Cornelius, Nebula, Alvanor, Qing Mao, Markus. Damage: 5M to 17M

  • Markus' team suggestion for Wind Hydra: Phobos, Orion, Alvanor, Satori, Markus. Damage: 5M to 9M

  • Markus' team suggestion for Water Hydra: Mojo, Jhu, Celeste, Maya, Markus. Damage: 15M to 19M

  • Markus' team suggestion for Fire Hydra: Martha, Lilith, Xe'sha, Mojo, Markus. Damage: 35M to 49M
