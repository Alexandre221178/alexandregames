Strategy on how to use Ziri

Ziri is the tank with the most resistance to physical damage in Hero Wars Alliance, being one of the main tanks used against physical damage teams that attack from the front line, like Ishmael and Astrid & Lucas.

When Ziri uses her ultimate ability, she increases her physical and magical defense by 150% as she has a lot of defense against armor penetration. This increase makes her almost immortal.

Ziri is a tank that buries itself to regenerate health and at that moment your team is left without a tank, making it ideal for use with a second tank or a Blender tank like Andvari. It is also often used in twin teams, as Krista has tanking skills for the team.

Talisman of Ziri

With the Talisman, Ziri gains another 2,000 points of strength, which will give her another 80000 extra health, another 2,000 points of physical attack, and more points of armor. Which will make her the most resistant tank in Hero Wars against all types of physical damage.

Stats Evolution Priority

Glyphs Priority

In Ziri's glyphs, prioritize leveling up, firstly, armor, life, and magical defense to gain resistance quickly, then strength to gain more life, and finally physical attack.

Priority Glyphs
1st Armor
2nd Health
3rd Magic Defense
4th Strength
5th Physical Attack

Artifacts Priority

In artifacts, the priority is the book to gain armor and magical defense, then the weapon artifact, because when Ziri uses the Ultimate she increases her own defense, benefiting from the artifact's buff. Finally, climb the ring to receive more life and physical attack.

Priority Artifacts
1st Book
2nd Weapon (Armor)
3rd Ring

Skins Priority

In Ziri's skins, prioritize armor, strength, and Health. I suggest strength before health because it helps reduce the critical hit chance and enemies' dodge in addition to increasing life and physical attack.

Ziri vs Hydra

Ziri is not found in the best hydra teams, but as it is a tank with a lot of defense, it can be used in your secondary teams to help it do more damage to the physical hydras.

Ziri in Battles

Strong Against

Ziri is strong against physical and critical damage teams, especially against Ishmael and Daredevil, and is also strong against Cornelius, as his Ultimate deals physical damage and cannot eliminate Ziri.

  • Ishmael, Daredevil, Artemis, Keira, Jet, Jhu, Cascade, Fox, Cornelius, Sebastian

Ziri Counters

Ziri is weak against teams that deal magic damage, even increasing her maximum defense she doesn't have enough. Qing Mao has the ability to reduce the enemy's armor with each hit, and in this way, he can reduce Ziri's armor. Celeste can cut the regeneration effect by magical damage. Phobos, at the end of the battle, prevents her from receiving energy and deals a lot of magical damage to Ziri.

  • Qing Mao, Aurora, Satori, Orion, Mojo, Tristan, Arachne, Phobos

Ziri Best Teams

The best defense teams for Ziri in 2023 will be the reworked Heidi or Krista and Lars with Talisman. For attack, we will have good teams with Artemis, Ishmael, Cascade and Keira.

Hero stats at max level 2023



Ziri with Champions Skin in Hero Wars Alliance

Max level Skills

1st Focus of Hatred

Ziri temporarily increases her armor and magic defense and taunts the enemy team, forcing them to attack her for 8.0 seconds.
Armor bonus and magic defense bonus: 150% Keywords: Buff.

2nd Reflection of Pain

Ziri activates an ancient curse. The curse reflects 100% of physical damage received by Zir back at the attacker for 8.0 seconds.
Chance to reflect damage decreases if the enemy's level is higher than 120.

3rd Shelter of Sands

If Ziri's health is below 30% (but only once every 15.0 seconds), she burrows into the ground for 7.0 seconds, avoiding enemy attacks and restoring health.
Recovered health: 62674 (10% Health + 48000) during 7.0 seconds.

4th Fury of Akhreb Ummi

Now, when Ziri unburies herself, she stuns and throws back any nearby enemies and uses the second skill.
Chance to stun is lowered if the target's level is above 120 Keywords: Stun, Knockback.
