DareDevil History


Hero Wars Alliance History: Danada: what is life? Life is an endless war. How to be happy in this life? Just enjoy the war, of course. Danada doesn't care about the lost arm, because the new one, made of steel, is much cooler. She goes all out in the war. This one knows how to enjoy life!

How to use Danada in Hero Wars

Danada's second ability is unique in that it has a 100% chance of critical damage, and can be crucial in gaining an advantage over your opponents.

Accompany Danada with characters who offer support, and with healers with vampirism like Dorian. This will ensure he stays alive to continue doing critical hit damage throughout the battle.

Danada has great synergy with Dorian and Elmir, with Elmir jumping to the backline close to Dorian's vampirism and, at the same time, activating the armor penetration artifact to help Danada deal more damage to enemies.


Talisman of Daredevil Guide

With the increased agility stat received from the talisman, Danada's 2nd skill will benefit from increased armor penetration and will cause more damage to enemies.

The armor penetration received from Talisman will make Danada very strong, eliminating enemy tanks and frontline killers very quickly.

Which Allies have Best Synergy with Daredevil?

Ally Skill Synergy

Dorian
Dorian increases the vampirism of nearby allies by 105% and every time Daredevil causes critical damage she recovers all health.

Elmir
When Elmir uses Ultimate skill he jumps to the backline and gets closer to Dorian's vampirism, starts doing damage from a distance, and, at the same time, activates the armor penetration artifact for allies, favoring Daredevil's damage.

Who is Daredevil Strong against in Battle?

Defeated Enemies How Daredevil Beats Enemies

Keira

How to Counter Daredevil

Counters
 How enemies defeat Daredevil

Helios
Helios protects allies against physical damage and counterattacks with high magical damage to enemies that deal critical damage to allies.

Oya
Oya's Hunter ability targets enemies with less health, as Daredevil has little health and can be Oya's primary target.

Ziri
Ziri is the tank with the highest armor points in Hero Wars and even hovers with Elmir and Talisman. Daredevil doesn't have enough armor penetration to defeat Ziri.

Amira
Amira has an ability that turn critical damage into critical failures, but this ability does not work when Daredevil uses the second skill. Therefore, Amira is a moderate counter.
