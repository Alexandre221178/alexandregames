Stats Evolution Priority

Glyphs

In Amira's glyphs focus first on health for her to have more survivability than magic penetration to do more damage, then magic attack and defense;

  1. Health
  2. Magic Penetration
  3. Magic Attack
  4. Armor
  5. Intelligence

Artifacts

Prioritize leveling up the book artifact first as it will increase magic piercing and magic attack, then intelligence to gain more magic attack and magic defense, and lastly the weapon.

  1. Book
  2. Ring
  3. Weapon (Magic Defense)

Skins

Amira has three priority skins; first magic penetration to deal the most damage, then intelligence, and lastly magic defense.

  1. Magic Penetration
  2. Intelligence
  3. Magic Defense

Amira stats at max level 2023





Max level Skills

1st Careless Wish

Amira buries her enemies under mounds of cursed gold. Enemies have the curse for 10s, then the curse is removed from the gold and deals damage proportional to health. Each time an enemy deals damage, its amount of gold takes the same amount of damage. Each Mystery ally increases Gold Health by 10%. With the exception of Amira.
Gold Max Life (272564) & Gold Damage Max Life(460705) - (400% Magic Attack + 48,000) & (500% Magic Attack + 36,000) damage.

2nd Fair Settlement

Amira makes a "fair deal" with those enemies, with Agility as her main stat. Your critical strike chance turns into critical misses for 9.0 seconds and increases your critical strike chance. Critical misses do only half the damage of a normal attack.
Critical Hit Chance Bonus: Max(13628) - (20% magic attack + 2400).

3rd Genius Sponsorship

Genie's Patronage: Amira activates the Genie's Patronage skill, protecting her allies for 8s. During the time that Genie's Sponsorship is in use, Amira's allies will not take magic damage from enemies that have the main attribute Intelligence. If the ally is from the Mystery faction, Genius Sponsorship is always active.
Patronage Protection: Max (62141 - (70% * Magic Attack + 6000)) damage is not dealt to allies.

4th Resistance Test

Amira reduces the total maximum energy of enemies by 5 percent, with Strength as her main stat.
If her max health is below Max: 569128 - (800% magic attack + 120,000), she depletes 50% of her max energy.



Strategy on how to use Amira

Amira is one of the main heroes of the Mystery Faction. She makes a great hero when fighting heroes that have a core stat of Agility, Intelligence, or Strength.

Amira is often seen on high end-game teams with other mystic heroes: Martha, Celeste, Rufus, Satori, and Ziri, and we can see some really cool and strong team compositions formed with Amira and other mystic heroes in the war and in the arena.

Amira is the best counter to Artemis her ability to make deals with agility heroes reducing her damage makes her an excellent counter to critical damage heroes, even better than Helios. Amira is very strong, she deals a lot of damage and unlike Helios she provides magical defense and is really worth investing in.
