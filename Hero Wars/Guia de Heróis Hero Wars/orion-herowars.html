Orion History


Hero Wars Alliance History: He is the true mystery of the world of supremacy. No one knows which galaxy he comes from, or what he is doing in these lands. Some people consider him a great danger, a messenger from hell. Others glorify him as a savior. What are the true motives of this great warrior from a distant planet?

Strategy on how to use Orion

Orion is one of the most beloved characters in Hero Wars, with excellent skills. This robot created by Julius is undoubtedly very strong. Famous for being very fast, he is always the first hero in the match to use his Ultimate. This is because he gains extra energy with each attack he makes and, with just two basic attacks, he is already using his Ultimate.

Orion makes combos with many heroes and one of the most important is with Nebula. Due to Orion's passive skill, he gains extra energy. As soon as Nebula puts the balance skill on Orion, he already makes his first attack with Nebula's buff, causing a lot of magical damage to the enemies. For this combo to work, Orion needs to be above level 100.

Another essential combo to use with Orion is Helios and Dorian. Dorian is most used to giving vampirism to the fast robot. However, when the three are together with Dorian's aura, Helios has the ability to absorb physical and critical damage by applying magical damage. As Helios has little magical penetration, he ends up causing more damage, benefiting from Orion's weapon artifact.

Currently, with new shield heroes added to the game, Orion is better used in combos with Nature, Mojo, Alvanor, and Mushy & Shroom teams. Without a doubt, it is one of the best teams, and with Orion giving magic penetration all the time, activating the weapon artifact for the whole team to cause more magic damage.

Positive and Negative Points

Positive Points

  • Orion is the fastest character in Hero Wars
  • Receives extra energy with each attack
  • Deals area damage and slows enemies for 4s
  • Stuns 1 nearby target for 4s

Negative Points

  • Attacks enemies with more health, does not finish enemies and activates their artifacts
  • Weak against Satori marks

Stats Evolution Priority

Glyphs Priority

In Orion's glyphs, prioritize leveling up magic penetration and magic attack first to deal more damage. Then life and intelligence, and finally magical defense.

Priority Glyphs
1st Magic penetration
2nd Magic attack
3rd Health
4th Intelligence
5th Magic Defense

Artifacts Priority

In artifacts, first level up the book to gain magical attack and magical penetration, then the weapon to gain more magical penetration. Finally, level up the ring to gain more of the magical attack and magical defense.

Priority Artifacts
1st Book
2nd Weapon
3rd Ring

Skins Priority

A very important detail: Orion does not have a Magic Penetration Skin. Therefore, in skins, we prioritize magical attacks, and in glyphs and artifacts, prioritize magical penetration.

Priority Skins
1st Magic attack
2nd Magic attack
3rd Intelligence
4th Armor
5th Magic defense

Orion in Battles

Strong Against

  • Keira, Danada, Ginger, Astrid & Lucas

The combo of Helios, Dorian, and Orion is very strong against Keira's and critical damage teams, this synergy between them is very strong. Orion is also very strong against Progress teams in the Alvanor, Mushy, and Mojo combo.

Orion Counters

  • Satori, Martha, Rufus, Corvus, Aurora, Jorgen, Lian, Jhu

Because Orion attacks enemies with higher health, it ends up becoming weak against tanks, especially Rufus, in addition to Martha, who is a backline tank that heals and speeds up the team.

Corvus' altar can finish off Orion very quickly if he doesn't have Dorian's vampirism. Satori applies many marks to Orion, as with each attack he receives extra energy.

Orion Best Teams

Orion's best teams for 2023 are the nature heroes, Alvanor, Cogu, Mojo, and Aurora. He is very strong, especially against Julius' teams, both for defense and attack.
