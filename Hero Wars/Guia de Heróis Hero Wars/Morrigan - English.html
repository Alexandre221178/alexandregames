Morriganvs Hydra

Morrigan is not a great hero for hydras, but as a healing ally it ends up being important on secondary teams. Morrigan has the power to heal all allies and invoke a shield of magical and physical defense, this shield grants twice as much resistance to allies of eternity.

  • Suggested Morrigan Teams for all Hydras: Fafnir, Keira, Morrigan, Julius, Corvus (Damage: 3M to 8M)

Max level Skills

1st Army of Graven

Morrigan summons 6 skeletons, treated as Eternity Heroes, to her side to fight in the front row. She can control up to 10 skeletons at any given time. The skeletons have the same Armor and Magic Defense stats as Morrigan when they are summoned.
Skeleton health: Max(45123) - (60% Magic Attack + 20 * Skill Level) Skeleton attack damage: Max(16272) - (6% Magic Attack + 100 * Skill Level) Keywords: Can't be copied

2nd Bone Armor

Morrigan summons 2 skeletons and grants temporary defense to allied Eternity Heroes. It increases their Armor and Magic Defense for 8.0 seconds, as well as healing them. The skill's effectiveness is halved for allies from other factions.
Armor and Magic defense bonus for Eternity Heroes: Max(16041) - (20% Magic Attack + 15 * Skill Level + 20) Healing: Max(47602) - (50% Magic Attack + 100 * Skill Level) Keywords: Buff

3rd Empathy of Eternity

Morrigan summons 2 skeletons and applies the Empathy effect to allied Eternity Heroes for 10.0 seconds. The effect spreads a portion of damage taken evenly among all skeletons.
Damage spread: Max(83205) - (100% Magic Attack + 100 * (Skill Level + 20))

4th Necromancy

Each time an enemy dies, Morrigan summons 1 skeleton from their body. Cancels any resurrection attempt on the battlefield. Whenever an enemy attempts resurrection, Morrigan receives 1 Soul Essence charge. When an ally dies, Morrigan spends 1 Soul Essence charge to resurrect them.
Health after the resurrection: Max(130807) - (150% Magic Attack + 200 * (Skill Level + 40)) Keywords: Resurrection

Strategy on how to use Morrigan

Morrigan is a great hero, especially in Eternity Faction compositions. His violet ability Necromancy is a great counter to Astaroth's Last Word ability and also to Rufus, Aidan and Kayla, and now he can also resurrect his allies from other factions.

Also makes a good combo with Cornelius against magic teams, giving Cornelius armor penetration for more damage, or, with Phobos healing and increasing defense.

She is definitely worth investing in and experimenting with, Morrigan can increase the defense of all allies and heal them.
