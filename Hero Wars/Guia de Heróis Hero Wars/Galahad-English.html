Galahad History


Hero Wars Alliance History: Galahad is a fearless knight driven by victory. He forged his own path to glory. He is a fighter and a true leader who commands the army of Harun, the land of warriors.

Strategy on how to use Galahad

Galahad is the protagonist hero of Hero Wars Alliance, his tank stats make him a strong warrior for the campaign, tower, hydra, and even the arena.

Galahad is very strong on Honor teams with the Artemis, Fafnir, and Tristan combo.

The weapon artifact skill (physical attack) increases Artemis's physical damage and being an Honor faction tank helps Artemis gain an extra arrow bonus on Death Bath ultimate.

Now, with the critical damage talisman, Galahad received additional stats and will grow stronger and deal more damage. That way it will increase your vampirism because Galahad has the ability to recover life from every damage he does.

Galahad's Talisman

With Talisman Galahad will receive, 80000 more health points, and 2000 more points of physical attack, plus 6000 points of critical hit chance. While a casual critique by itself isn't helpful or enough, if he's paired with Sebastian or Tristan it will be a much more effective effect.

In damage, the Talisman really is going to affect his ultimate. If Galahad uses his ultimate with Sebastian present in his team, or with Tristan's armor penetration enabled, Galahad will be able to destroy entire enemy lines, as well as increase his vampirism.
