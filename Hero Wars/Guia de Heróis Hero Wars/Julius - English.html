Strategy on how to use Julius

Julius is a great hero and definitely worth investing in, his artifacts provide critical damage for the whole team and can be used in combination with agility heroes like Keira, Artemis, Daredevil, etc.

Julius is able to protect himself and the entire team from all kinds of damage with his shield. Heroes with the keyword description â€œshieldâ€ in their skills have better synergy with Julius's skills as listed below:

Julius vs Hydra

Julius is a good hero against Hydras and can help his team by protecting with his shield absorbing all kinds of damage, this makes Julius a good tank for all Hydras.

  • Julius' team for Hydra: Fafnir, Morrigan, Keira, Julius, Corvus

Max level Skills

1st Meowsterpiece of Defense

Casts a shield in front of his team that absorbs any damage. After the shield is destroyed, all heroes in the team are healed. Progress heroes are healed twice as much.
Healing: Max(33114) - (75% * Physical Attack + 80 * Skill Level) Shield Strength: Max(162765) - (500% Physical Attack + 50 * Skill Level) Timers and Distances: Animation Delay:0.6 Range:400

2nd Copycat Device

Passive: Whenever Julius gains a shield, his physical attack is boosted by 8% until the end of the battle. During the battle, this boost doesn't change. Active: Julius casts a shield on himself that absorbs any damage. If it's not destroyed in 5.0 seconds, the remaining shield is copied onto each ally.
Shield: Max(68706) - (200% Physical Attack + 50 * Skill Level) Timers and Distances: Initial Timeout:10 Skill Timeout:14 Duration:5 Animation Delay:0.64

3rd Nine-Lives Engine

Whenever a shield is destroyed or deactivated on Julius or a teammate, that hero gains bonus armor and magic defense for 1.5 seconds. All their debuffs are removed.
Armor Bonus: Max(11805) - (30% * Physical Attack + 20 * (Skill Level - 20)) Magic Defense Bonus: Max(159165) - (30% Physical Attack + 20 * (Skill Level - 20)) Timers and Distances: Duration:2

4th Purrfect Reflexes

Passively boosts the speed of attacks and skills for all allies protected by a shield. The bonus is increased for Progress heroes.
Speed-Up: Max(6) - (10% * (Skill Level - 40) - 2) Speed-Up for Progress Heroes: Max(28) - (40% * (Skill Level - 40) - 4) Timers and Distances: Range:400
