Max level Skills

1st Dance of Death

Yasmine paralyzes the last enemy who attacked her for 3 seconds, appears behind their back, hits 7 times, and applies the Assassin's Mark. While Dance of Death is active, Yasmine is immune to debuffs. When it ends, she returns to the previous position and continues attacking the marked enemy with basic attacks.
Damage: Max(32972) - (60% Physical Attack + 20 * (Skill Level))
Keywords: Mark

2nd Assassin's Instinct

When Dance of Death is activated, Yasmine's Critical Hit Chance and Dodge are increased for 10 seconds.
Extra Critical Hit Chance and Dodge: Max(24129) - (45% Physical Attack + 10 * (Skill Level))

3rd Embrace of Pain

Yasmine's critical hits poison the enemy. When the poison is re-applied to a poisoned enemy, the duration of the poison debuff is refreshed, and the damage stacks up to 10 times.
One poison debuff deals: Max(13698) - (25% Physical Attack + 8 * (Skill Level + 20)) pure damage per 5.0 seconds

4th Unknown Toxin

Blocks a portion of healing received by enemies poisoned by Yasmine.
Points of Healing blocked: Max(15388) - (25% Physical Attack + 20 * (Skill Level + 40) + 250) per heal



Strategy on how to use Yasmine

Yasmine is a great hero and is great at dealing insanely lethal critical damage to enemies. She is often found on attacking and defending teams in global wars due to her dodging abilities and high critical damage she provides.

Yasmine's Ultimate when activated will attack the enemy, with lower armor, being mostly support, damage and healing mages, making this attack deadly, because when she uses this skill with its high critical damage power, it also paralyzes, poisons, and blocks enemy healing.
