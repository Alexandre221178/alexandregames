Positive Points

Hero stats at max level 2023





Max level Skills

1st Icy Vengeance

Hits enemies one by one with 5 ice crystals, dealing area damage.
Damage: Max(24187) - (25% * Magic Attack + 50 * Skill level)

2nd Chains of Frost

Krista hits the enemies in the center of an enemy's team, temporarily reducing their magic defense and applying a Mark of Water to them.
Temporary Magic Reduction: Max(4837) - (5% * Magic Attack + 10 * Skill level) Chance: The chance to apply the skill is lowered if the target's level is above skill level Max120 = Skill Level) Keywords: Debuff, Mark

3rd Frozen Needles

Freezes the ground under the feet of an enemy's team, filling the zone with ice thorns. Any movement through the area deals damage to enemies. When the thorns melt, all heroes standing on them get a Mark of Water.
Damage upon movement: Max(11729) - (12% * Magic Attack + 25 * (Skill level + 20)) Keywords: Mark

4th Crystallization

When in danger of taking damage, Krista summons an ice block that protects the caster for 2.0 seconds by partially absorbing any incoming damage.
Ice Armor absorbs: Max(82) - (40% * (Skill level + 40) + 34)



Strategy on how to use Krista

Krista is a very good hero and is often seen in the end-game forming teams that are difficult to beat, and is very strong when used in combination with Lars. Krista and Lars are a perfect match and are considered one of the best matches in the game.

Krista is commonly found on top teams alongside Lars and is definitely worth investing in.
