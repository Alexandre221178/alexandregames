Skills Breakdown

Tempus possesses a set of time-bending abilities that can disrupt enemy strategies while empowering his team. Hereâ€™s a breakdown of his core skills and how to use them effectively:

1. Chronostasis

Skill Description: Tempus stops time for all enemy Heroes, freezing their movement, skill usage, and evasion. During this time, enemy Armor and Magic Defense are reduced, and the cooldown of all their skills is halted. This ability also prolongs the duration of all Curses and Debuffs affecting the enemies.

Strategy: Chronostasis is a game-changing ability that can turn the tide of battle. 2. Withering

Skill Description: Tempus temporarily weakens distant enemies in front of him, reducing their Physical Attack and max Health.

Strategy: Withering is particularly effective against physical attackers and frontline enemies. This skill allows Tempus to mitigate the damage from opponents who rely on Physical Attack, while also reducing their maximum Health. Use this to soften up enemy tanks or damage dealers, giving your team a clear advantage. This is especially effective when Tempus is positioned against teams with strong physical damage output.

3. Temporal Anomaly

Skill Description: Tempus sends the enemy Hero with the highest Armor Penetration into the Temporal Anomaly, where they are slowed down, lose energy, and take continuous magic damage over time.

Strategy: Temporal Anomaly targets the enemy with the highest Armor Penetration, making it an ideal counter against opponents designed to shred through your team's defenses. By isolating and weakening this target, Tempus ensures that your teamâ€™s tanks and frontline heroes stay protected longer. Additionally, the magic damage over time can help wear down high-priority enemies.

4. Time Paradox

Skill Description: Tempus summons a giant clock in the center of the battlefield, creating an area of effect (AoE) that slows down enemies while speeding up allies within its range.

Strategy: Time Paradox is Tempusâ€™ ultimate control ability. The AoE affects both enemies and allies, slowing enemies down while boosting the speed of Tempus' team. This can be used to control the pace of battle, allowing your allies to quickly outmaneuver and overpower opponents. In close matches, the ability to manipulate time for both teams can be the difference between victory and defeat. Utilize Time Paradox to keep enemies from reaching key objectives or to ensure your team executes attacks with deadly precision.

  3. Target Key Opponents: Use Temporal Anomaly to neutralize high-priority targets like enemy heroes with strong Armor Penetration. By removing these threats from the battlefield temporarily, you give your team a significant advantage.
