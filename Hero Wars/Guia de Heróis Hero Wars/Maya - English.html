The Talisman of Blooming focuses on improving Maya's magical offense, particularly boosting her magic attack and intelligence. This makes her counterattack, using the flowers' pure damage, much more aggressive against middle and backline enemy heroes. With stronger magic defense, she becomes more resilient, while her increased magic attack helps her deal heavy damage.
