Maya's Story in Hero Wars Alliance


Story: Maya is a child born against the laws of nature. Impossible love between a human and a titan fell as tears on the fertile ground, and gave birth to such a beautiful, and yet, powerful creature.

Strategy on how to use Maya

Maya's skills have synergy with the Refluence skill of Cascade, which provides extra damage to Mystery heroes, and thus Maya gains the power to deal extra pure damage in double.


  • Advertisement
     

Maya is a good healer, but as a healer she is no better than Celeste, Martha, Dorian; She is especially strong with some heavy tanks on the front lines and can form a great combo with new hero Iris.

Maya deals a lot of pure damage to enemies. Maya is also very strong against Hydras, and it's worth investing in Maya to use with the new Cascade character.

Maya's Talisman

With talisman, Maya gains 21,000 more magic attack, and 2,000 more magic defense.

Maya will gain in her stats more than 33000 magic defense and more than 100000 magic attack, which makes her counterattack of the flowers with pure damage even more aggressive towards enemy heroes of the middle line and the back line weaker.

Maya vs Hydra

Maya is used a lot on Hydra teams and can help the team with healing and causing a lot of pure damage to Hydras.

  • Maya's team suggestion for Hydra of Light: Alvanor, Maya, Nebula, Cornelius, Fox (Damage: 22M)
