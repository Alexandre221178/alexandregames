Keira History


Hero Wars Alliance History: Captain Keira once was one of the best royal guards who has fallen in battle against Morth Chrone. The demon of war has resurrected young guard and turned her into a ghost foredoomed to suffer and wander about till the end of times.

Strategy on how to use Keira

Keira is a hero who has an ultimate Over-Power, and this damage is increased if used with heroes that grant critical damage to the team. Keira is typically seen on top end-game teams in both server wars and global wars and is well worth investing in. With the right allies, Keira can become very strong, especially if used with Eternity Faction allies.

Currently, a bug has been fixed. When Keira was used with Sebastian the fourth skill was favored causing extra damage, and to compensate for this Nerf the developers increased Keira's damage in the fourth skill by 1.5X, so the new damage formula was (15% Physical Attack + 75 x level).

After adjusting the fourth skill, Keira became stronger to use with other heroes, mainly with heroes from the Eternity Faction, in addition to this adjustment, Keira also received a Super Skin of physical damage, and Keira became very strong in 2023.

Even with new heroes being added to the game and receiving new skins it is not enough for Keira to stop being the old favorite and favorite Keira of the players. Keira's Talisman

With the talisman, Keira receives an increase in her Agility stat, receiving more Physical Attack and Armor. At the maximum level, it will increase physical attack by +18,000 points, and another 2,000 points of armor, adding up to a total of 17,449 points of protection against armor penetration, reducing the enemies' physical attack damage, so it will become quite strong.

With the talisman's physical attack bonus, Keira will be able to do more physical damage to enemies, and it will certainly be difficult to stop her when she launches her whirlwind of blades at the enemy team.

Keira Stats Evolution Priority

Glyphs Priority

In Keira's glyphs, level up armor penetration first to deal more damage. Then, prioritize health to survive, especially against Corvus' altar. Then physical attack and agility to increase damage in skills, and finally armor.

Priorities Glyphs
1st Armor Penetration
2nd Health
3rd Physical Attack
4th Agility
5th Armor

Artifacts Priority

In artifacts, the priority is the book because Keira gains more armor penetration, and she needs a lot of armor penetration to cause a lot of damage. Then climb the ring to gain agility which will increase your physical attack points and armor. Lastly leveling up Keira's weapon artifact because it increases the physical attack of Keira and her allies.

Priorities Artifacts
1st Book
2nd Ring
3rd Weapon (Physical attack)

Skins Priority

In Keira's skins, start by leveling up armor penetration, then the super skin, because it increases physical attack and is worth two skins. Third: physical attack, and fourth agility to gain more physical attack and armor.

Lastly, level up the magical defense skin, Keira is powerful against wizard teams with this skin, especially if combined with Fafnir's shield, it allows her to survive Satori's attack, gaining energy and launching her Ultimate at enemies.

Priorities Skins
1st Armor Penetration
2nd Physical Attack (Super skin)
3rd Physical Attack
4th Agility
5th Armor
6th Magic Defense
