Strategy on how to use Keira

Keira is a hero that has an ultimate Over-Power, and this damage is increased if used with heroes that grant critical damage to the team. Keira is typically seen on top end-game teams in both server wars and global wars, and is well worth investing in. With the right allies, Keira can become very strong, especially if used with Eternity Faction allies .

Currently, a bug has been fixed that when Keira was used with Sebastian the fourth skill was favored causing extra damage, and to compensate for this Nerf the developers increased keira's damage in the fourth skill by 1.5X, so the new damage formula was (15% Physical Attack + 75 x level).

After adjusting the fourth skill, Keira became stronger to use with other heroes, mainly with heroes from the Eternity Faction, in addition to this adjustment, keira also received a Super Skin of physical damage, and Keira became very strong in 2023.

Even with new heroes being added to the game and receiving new skins it is not enough for Keira to stop being the old favorite and favorite keira of the players, Keira was once the best and most feared assassin of Hero Wars, but she is still very strong and worth investing in... and Keira is Alexandre Games' favorite hero.


Positive and Negative Points

Positive Points

  • High damage (Ultimate Over Power)
  • It has a lot of speed
  • Pure Damage in passive skill

Negative Points

  • Weak against stun and control
  • Can interrupt her ultimate if she takes damage at the start of the cast.
  • Weak against Altar of Corvus.
  • Very weak against Over Power mage teams
  • Depends on other heroes for critical damage
