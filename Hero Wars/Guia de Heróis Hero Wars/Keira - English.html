Comprehensive Guide: How to Utilize Keira in Hero Wars Alliance

Keira is a powerful heroine in Hero Wars Alliance, capable of dealing significant damage on the battlefield when used correctly. With versatile abilities and the ability to synergize with different heroes and factions, Keira is a popular choice for high-level teams. In this guide, we'll explore how to make the most of Keira's potential, including her ideal combinations, team strategies, and usage tips.

Introduction to Keira

Keira is an agile assassin who stands out for her ultimate ability, Over-Power, capable of dealing massive amounts of damage to enemies. Her effectiveness is even greater when combined with heroes that grant critical damage bonuses to the team. With the recent adjustment to her fourth ability and the introduction of a new physical damage skin, Keira has become an unstoppable force on the battlefield.

Powerful Combos

1. Fafnir and Keira

A classic combo, Fafnir provides protection to Keira through his shield, making her more resilient and able to survive longer on the battlefield. This is especially useful when facing enemy teams with high damage potential.

2. Jet and Keira

Jet is a valuable ally for Keira, as his abilities add critical damage to Keira's skills, thus increasing her damage potential. This combination makes Keira even deadlier, especially in direct confrontations.

3. Dorian, Fafnir, and Keira

This combo offers both protection and sustain to Keira. While Fafnir provides the protective shield, Dorian offers vampirism to the team, allowing Keira to heal herself while dealing damage to enemies. It's a highly effective combination in prolonged battles of attrition.

4. Eternity Faction Allies

Keira benefits greatly from being used with allies from the Eternity Faction, such as Morrigan and Corvus. Their synergies further increase Keira's damage and Valor potential, making her a formidable choice for Chaos faction-focused teams.

Usage Strategies

Positioning: Strategically position Keira on the battlefield, preferably behind tanks or more resilient heroes. This helps protect her from enemy attacks while she deals long-range damage.

Timing of Ultimate Ability: Keira's ultimate ability, Over-Power, is crucial for dealing massive amounts of damage. Wait for the right moment to activate it, ideally when enemies are grouped together or when a crucial enemy hero is unprotected.

Utilize Synergies: Maximize Keira's potential by leveraging synergies with other heroes on your team. Heroes that grant critical damage buffs, protection, or healing can significantly increase her effectiveness on the battlefield.

Counter-Attack: Keira is most effective when used as part of an aggressive strategy. Use her to counter-attack and eliminate vulnerable enemy heroes, dealing as much damage as possible before they have a chance to react.

Keira's Abilities Guide in Hero Wars Alliance

1st Ability: Blade Whirlwind

Hit Damage:

  • Maximum (25181): The maximum damage caused by the ability.
  • Formula: 30% of Physical Attack + (50 * Skill Level).

This ability is the cornerstone of Keira's arsenal, allowing her to deal significant damage to her enemies with a whirlwind of sharp blades.

2nd Ability: Soul Piercer

Attack and Skill Speed Increase:

  • Maximum (290%): The maximum increase in attack and skill speed.
  • Formula: (Skill Level + 170).

This ability grants Keira an additional boost in her attack and skill speed, making her a formidable force on the battlefield. It's important to note that this ability functions as a buff, temporarily enhancing Keira's capabilities.

3rd Ability: Dancing Blades

Ricochet Chance:

  • Reduced Chance if Target Level is Above:
    • Maximum (120): The maximum level the target can have to reduce ricochet chance.
    • Formula: (Skill Level + 20).

This ability grants Keira the ability to make her blades ricochet between enemies, dealing additional damage. However, the effectiveness of this ricochet is reduced if the target's level is much higher compared to Keira's skill level.

4th Ability: Piercing Pain

Extra Damage:

  • Maximum (18591): The maximum extra damage caused by the ability.
  • Formula: 15% of Physical Attack + (75 * (Skill Level + 40)).

This ability allows Keira to inflict extra damage on her enemies, adding an additional layer of destruction to her arsenal. It's a crucial ability for overcoming resilient enemies and ensuring victory.

Comparative Analysis of Keira's Talismans in Hero Wars Alliance

Keira’s first talisman, the Talisman of Loyalty, grants agility, which significantly boosts her physical attack and armor. This enhances both her offensive capabilities and her ability to withstand some physical damage.

In addition, the talisman provides three physical attack slots, each of which can reach up to 4400 physical attack points. This makes Keira more dangerous offensively, as she can deal more damage with each attack, while also gaining a slight increase in defense due to the additional armor.

Keira’s second talisman, the Talisman of Valor, focuses more on defensive attributes. It increases her health and magic defense, making it ideal for situations where she faces magic-heavy teams. This talisman offers up to 110,000 health points and 6600 magic defense per slot, which enhances her resilience against mages.

Keira has historically struggled against magic-damage teams, particularly when paired with heroes like Faceless, who can throw her out of position and lead to her quick demise. With the Talisman of Valor equipped, she can reduce the impact of magic attacks and survive longer in battles, increasing her chances of outlasting these threats.

Comparison and Strategy

Talisman of Loyalty (First Talisman): Best used when Keira’s primary role is to deal damage. The increase in physical attack from the agility boost directly enhances her offensive output, making her a major threat on the battlefield. However, this talisman offers less in terms of survivability, as it only boosts her physical defense slightly.

Talisman of Valor (Second Talisman): Prioritizes Valor, especially against magical teams. By granting additional health and magic defense, it helps Keira withstand the pressure from mages, a role she often struggles with.

Although it sacrifices some of her damage potential, the increased health and magic defense make her much more durable, which is crucial for her to stay in the fight longer. This talisman is especially useful against heroes like Faceless, who can stun her and leave her vulnerable to magic burst damage.

Damage Analysis

When using the Talisman of Loyalty, Keira’s damage output increases noticeably:

  • Her pure damage increases to 22,000 when using her ultimate ability.
  • Her basic attacks from her first skill deal around 32,000 physical damage per hit.

In contrast, when using the Talisman of Valor, her damage decreases slightly:

  • Her ultimate’s pure damage drops by 2000 points, bringing it to 20,000.
  • Her basic attack damage falls to 27,000 physical damage per hit, which is around a 4000-point reduction per attack.

Despite the reduction in damage, Keira becomes much harder to eliminate with the Talisman of Valor equipped, especially when fighting against mages or teams with high magical output.

Final Thoughts

Both of Keira’s talismans are powerful in their own right:

  • Talisman of Loyalty is ideal for maximizing her physical damage output and is suited for fights where offense is key.
  • Talisman of Valor is perfect for enhancing her survivability, especially against magic-heavy teams, allowing her to last longer in battles.

Ultimately, the choice between the two talismans should depend on the team composition you are facing. If Keira’s Valor is a concern, especially against magic, the Talisman of Valor is the better option. If your priority is maximizing her damage, the Talisman of Loyalty will ensure she hits harder.

Keira Best Teams in Hero Wars Alliance

Keira is a versatile and powerful hero in Hero Wars Alliance, capable of dealing significant damage when paired with the right heroes. Below are some of the best Keira team combinations that include Keira, highlighting their synergies and effective usage strategies:

1. Fafnir, Judge, Keira, Julius, Corvus

This team combines the protection of Fafnir and Julius with Keira's explosive damage, complemented by Judge's control and Corvus's support.

2. Fafnir, Judge, Keira, Julius, Astaroth

The addition of Astaroth offers an additional layer of defense and crowd control, while Judge and Julius help divert enemies' attention.

3. Fafnir, Cornelius, Morrigan, Keira, Corvus

This team focuses on boosting Keira's defense with the help of Morrigan, Cornelius, and Fafnir, while Corvus provides support and control.

4. Fafnir, Dorian, Keira, Qing Mao, Andvari

The combination of Dorian's healing, along with Fafnir and Andvari's defense, allows Keira and Qing Mao to wreak havoc while staying sustained.

5. Fafnir, Dorian, Keira, Andvari, Corvus

This team combines the resilience of Fafnir and Andvari with Dorian and Corvus's support, allowing Keira to deal consistent damage while receiving protection and healing.

6. Jet, Sebastian, Morrigan, Keira, Corvus

Jet and Sebastian increase Keira's critical damage, while Morrigan and Corvus provide additional support and control for the team.

7. Jet, Sebastian, Keira, Andvari, Astaroth

This formation prioritizes defense with Astaroth and Andvari, while Jet and Sebastian increase Keira's critical damage, making her an even greater threat.

8. Phobos, Faceless, Morrigan, Keira, Corvus

This formation focuses on confusing and controlling enemies with Phobos and Faceless, while Morrigan and Corvus increase Keira's damage.

Experiment with different combinations and adapt them according to the challenges faced on the battlefield. Remember to adjust your strategy as needed to maximize Keira and your team's potential.
