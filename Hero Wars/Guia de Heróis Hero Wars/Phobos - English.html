Phobos vs Hydra

Phobos is a good hero against hydras if used in combination with Mojo, because while Phobos is dealing magic damage per second Mojo uses his 4th skill Enchantress and deals damage continuously.

Phobos team suggestion for Hydra:

  1. Thea, Phobos, Mojo, Jhu, Astaroth (Fire Hydra)
  2. Martha, Phobos, Mojo, Jhu, Andvari (Wind Hydra)

Strategy on how to use Phobos

Phobos is one of the heroes of the eternity faction, with the power to cause a lot of damage through his first skill, in addition to neutralizing the energy of the strongest mages and transferring it to himself.

Phobos has a lot of magic damage and also has a lot of magic penetration which makes him a hero that doesn't depend on others to deal damage, Phobos is considered the mage counter of the best high end teams today and he is really worth investing in and level up.
