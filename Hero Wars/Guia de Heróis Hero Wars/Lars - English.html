Strategy on how to use Lars

Lars is a very good hero and is often seen in the end-game forming teams that are difficult to beat, and is very strong when used in combination with Krista. Krista and Lars are a perfect match and are considered one of the best matches in the game.

Lars can also be used on Karkh's teams to contain Andvari's protection, when Lars pushes Andvari back only Andvari will be displaced and Andvari's allies will be behind the shield and have lost protection, so Karkh is free to attack and finish off enemies.

Lars is commonly found on top teams along with Krista, he's the only hero in the game that has an exclusive rare skin, sure it is a good investment.
