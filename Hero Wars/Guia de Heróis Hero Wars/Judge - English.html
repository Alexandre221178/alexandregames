Strategy on how to use Judge

Judge is a hero similar to Nebula, however, instead of increasing the damage to nearby allies he gives a very strong shield that protects two allies to the left and right from any type of damage.

In his artifact Judge provides increased magic damage for the entire team when activated, and in his skills provide: shield, stun, and slow the speed of enemies.

Judge and is a hero that has synergy to be used on all types of teams with his shield and skills, the second skill of Judge Field of Force generates an ally shield that has synergy with the skills of the hero Julius reinforcing the shield, Judge as well is seen in teams of twins Krista and Lars positioning themselves in the middle of them and giving protection to both, forming a very strong team for defense.

Therefore, currently in Hero Wars, new heroes have appeared that provide shields that absorb all types of damage and for the entire team, examples: Fafnir and Julius. And in this way, Juiz was a little sidelined because it only offers protection for two allies, but if used with Julius, this shield becomes very strong, Juiz is a great hero and is still worth investing in 2023.


Positive and Negative Points

Positive Points

  • Shield for two allies (last and first)
  • Shield for all kinds of damage
  • Stun enemies
  • Slows the speed of enemies

Negative Points

  • Low Armor
  • The shield protected only two allies
  • Depends on activating the shield to protect the team

Judge vs Hydra

Judge is a good hero against Hydras and can help two allied heroes that are left and right of the Judge on your team protecting with his shield and absorbing all kinds of damage, this makes Judge a useful hero for all Hydras; but not the best for the late game.
