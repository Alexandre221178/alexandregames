Positive and Negative Points

Positive Points

  • Ultimate reduces armor, magic defense, and dodge for enemies, and increases defense reduction by 10% for Eternity allies
  • Increases magical and physical attack for Eternity allies
  • Conjures an altar that deals pure damage to enemies

Negative Points

  • 4th Royal Defense Skill is the most useless in Hero Wars Mobile game
  • Has low defense compared to other tanks
  • Depends on Morrigan to be stronger



Stats Evolution Priority

Glyphs

On Corvus glyphs focus first on health and strength to increase survivability, then armor and magic defense, lastly physical attack.

  1. Health
  2. Strength
  3. Armor
  4. Magical Defense
  5. Physical Attack

Artifacts

In Corvus artifacts prioritize the ring to give more life and physical attack, then the book to increase defense and lastly the weapon to give bonus armor to the whole team.

  1. Book
  2. Ring
  3. Weapon(Armor)

Skins

In Corvus skins focus on armor and strength to gain resistance, then physical attack to give more power to skills and lastly magic defense.

  1. Armor
  2. Strength
  3. Physical Attack
  4. Magical Defense



Corvus vs Hydra

Corvus is a good tank against Hydras and can help your team by dealing pure damage and can be used both on Jhu and Mojo combo teams and on eternity teams.

Corvus is not among the best teams for Hydra, but it can be used with allies of the eternity faction and deal good damage to Hydras.

Corvus team for Hydra:

  • All Hydras: Fafnir, Keira, Morrigan, Julius, Corvus (damage: 3M to 8M)
  • Hydra of Darkness: Martha, Cornelius, Mojo, Jhu, Corvus (Damage: 28M to 30M)
  • Wind Hydra: Martha, Cornelius, Mojo, Jhu, Corvus (Damage: 29M to 32M)

Hero stats at max level 2023





Max level Skills

1st Strike of the Damned

Corvus deals physical damage to enemies near him, and lowers their defenses for a duration: (Dodge, Armor, Magic Defense).
Damage: Max(44952)—(100% Physical Attack + 100 * Skill Level) Defense Decrease: Max(6295)—(10% Physical Attack + 25 * Skill Level) Chance: Total defense decrease and damage dealt is increased by 10% for each Eternal ally on the team.

2nd Unit of the Damned

Corvus boosts physical and magical attacks for 10 seconds for Eternity Path allies.
Physical and Magic Attack Bonus: Max(10038)—Skill Formula: (25% Physical Attack + 15 * Skill Level) Keywords: buff

3rd Altar of Souls

Corvus throws an altar into the backline as a sixth ally. Whenever an ally of Corvus takes any damage, the Altar responds with pure damage. The altar has health and its health decreases whenever it deals damage to enemies.
Altar Damage: Max(15173) – Skill Formula: (1.2% Health + 10 * (Skill Level + 20)) Altar Health: Max(774644)—(90% Health + 400 * (Skill Level) + 20))

4th Royal Defense

When an ally of Corvus drops below 20% health, Corvus picks up that ally and quickly throws them into the center of the team. This skill isn't very useful, being one of the worst in the game.
Can be used once every 8 seconds. Protection Chance: The chance to move an ally is reduced if the target's level is above the Max skill level (80 = 1 * (Skill Level + 40))



Strategy on how to use Corvus

Corvus seems to be a very strong tank and can make great synergies with the other heroes of the eternity faction, especially with his daughter Morrigan, and now with his new armor skin and Magic Defense Corvus is very strong. One thing for sure is that he is definitely worth trying out, his altar ability does a lot of pure damage to enemies, making Corvus the most damaging tank in the game.

Your weakness is the last Royal Defense skill which is good for nothing and has to be the worst skill in the game.
