Strategy on how to use Astaroth

Astaroth is a good Tank against the Hydras and can help your weaker allied heroes against the physical damage Hydras, mainly for early-game players up to end-game beginners. In the end-game, Astaroth is little used against Hydras, but until you dismiss your Hydra Astaroth it will take a lot of time and investment in the game.

Astaroth is a character worth investing in, at the beginning, he is weak and does not deal much damage, but after he gets strong he is great. It is also recommended to use him as the best tank for the tower, for the campaign, and for the hydra due to your ability to resurrect.

Even with the addition of new heroes and tanks in the game, Astaroth is still one of the most used tanks for team building in the Royal League. For that reason, it is present among the strongest teams in the game.

Astaroth's Talisman

With Talisman Astaroth will receive an additional, 80000 points of health and 12000 points of magic defense, leveling the defense with most of the magic penetrations in the game.

So, with more magic defense and health, Astaroth can defend his team against any mage that doesn't have magic penetration, making him strong against mage teams.

Stats Evolution Priority

Glyphs Priority

Astaroth is a tank, so you need to focus on the armor and health glyph first, then magic defense and health, and lastly magic attack.

  1. Armor
  2. Health
  3. Magical Defense
  4. Strength
  5. Magic Attack

Artifacts Priority

In Astaroth's artifacts focus on the book to increase armor and magic defense, then the ring to gain more life and finally the weapon.

  1. Book
  2. Ring
  3. Weapon(Armor Bonus)

Skins Priority

In Astaroth's skins, you should prioritize armor and health to gain resistance, and then magic defense, health, and lastly magic attack.

  1. Armor
  2. Health
  3. Magical Defense
  4. Strength
  5. Magic Attack
