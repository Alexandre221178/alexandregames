Positive and Negative Points

Positive Points

  • Physical shield for all allies
  • Burns some of the enemy's energy from the bottom line
  • Absorbs part of the physical damage from the ally with the lowest health
  • Resurrect

Negative Points

  • Resurrect does not work against Morrigan
  • Resurrect does not work if morrigan is allied
  • The shield protects only physical damage and is not a strong shield



Stats Evolution Priority

Glyphs

Astaroth is a tank, so you need to focus on the armor and health glyph first, then magic defense and health, and lastly magic attack.

  1. Armor
  2. Health
  3. Magical Defense
  4. Strength
  5. Magic Attack

Artifacts

Artifacts In Astaroth's artifacts focus on the book to increase armor and magic defense, then the ring to gain more life and finally the weapon.

  1. Book
  2. Ring
  3. Weapon(Armor Bonus)

Skins

In Astaroth's skins, you should prioritize armor and health to gain resistance, and then magic defense, health, and lastly magic attack.

  1. Armor
  2. Health
  3. Magical Defense
  4. Strength
  5. Magic Attack



Astaroth vs Hydras

Astaroth is a good Tank against Hydras and can help your weaker allied heroes against physical damage Hydras, mainly for early game players up to end-game beginners. In the end-game Astaroth is little used against Hydras; but until you dismiss your Hydra Astaroth it will take a lot of time and investment in the game.

  • Astaroth Team for Hydra: Martha, Mojo, Nebula, Jhu, Astaroth

Strategy on how to use Astaroth

Astaroth is a good Tank against the Hydras and can help your weaker allied heroes against the physical damage Hydras, mainly for early game players up to end-game beginners. In the end-game Astaroth is little used against Hydras; but until you dismiss your Hydra Astaroth it will take a lot of time and investment in the game.

Astaroth is a character worth investing in, at the beginning he is weak and does not deal much damage, but after he gets strong he is great, it is also recommended to use him as the best tank for the tower, for the campaign and for the hydra due to your ability to resurrect.

Even with the addition of new heroes and tanks in the game Astaroth is still one of the most used tanks for team building in the Royal league and for that reason it is present among the strongest teams in the game.
