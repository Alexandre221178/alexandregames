Strategy on how to use Jet

Jet is a hero who provides the critical damage attribute for the team. Heroes like Keira and Ginger who are agility stats but do not have this attribute when they are in Jet teams are able to deal critical damage and deal a lot of damage while Jet is alive.

Jet also has the ability to lower the armor of the enemy hero with the highest armor. If it is an enemy tank, this ability is very good against the new hero Julius who has little armor in his attributes.

Jet also has the ability to heal and increase the physical attack of the ally with the highest physical attack, in addition to preventing enemies from healing.

It is a difficult hero to farm for those who are beginners who need to choose between Jets soul stones or items to level up other heroes in the soul stone store. Although we have new meta heroes in the game Jet is still a good hero, but in my humble opinion, it is not an advisable hero for those who are free-to-play.


Jet's Talisman

With more intelligence and magical attack, Jet will increase the critical hit chance for allies who do not have this statistic, such as Keira. Remember that it only uses 2.5% magical attack as a bonus to critical damage, but with the talisman providing an increase of 24000 points of magical attack, there is another 600 points of increased critical chance for allies.
