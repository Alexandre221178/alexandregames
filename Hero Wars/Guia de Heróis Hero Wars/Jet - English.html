Strategy on how to use Jet

Jet is a hero that provides the critical damage attribute for the team, heroes like Keira and Ginger who are agility but do not have this attribute when they are in Jet teams are able to deal critical damage and deal a lot of damage while Jet is alive.

Jet also has an ability to lower the armor of the enemy hero with the highest armor, and it is enemy tanks, this ability is very good against the new hero Julius who has little armor in his attributes.

Jet also has the ability to heal and increase the physical attack of the ally with the highest physical attack, in addition to preventing enemies from healing.

It is a difficult hero to farm for those who are beginners need to choose between Jet's soul stones or items to level up other heroes in the soul stone store, although we have new meta heroes in the game Jet is still a good hero, but in my humble opinion it is not an advisable hero for those who are free-to-play.
