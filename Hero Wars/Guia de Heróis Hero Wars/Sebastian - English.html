Strategy on how to use Sebastian

Sebastian is a great ally for agility heroes who have the critical damage attribute, providing an increase in the team's critical hit chance, even with pure damage, that is, without defense for enemies.

Another great advantage of Sebastian is having an ability that reduces the armor of tanks or frontline enemies at the beginning of the game and can only be removed when he dies. Now with the physical attack super skin, it will increase this armor reduction, and also improve the other skills.

Sebastian also has a shield in his ultimate that provides defense for the team. The shield has life and while it is active, the team is protected against curse and stun. Including this shield releases the curse that Cornelius provides to his ally at the beginning of the game, but only when the ultimate is activated.

Sebastian's Talisman Guide

With Talisman, Sebastian receives more Armor and Physical Attack from the Agility stat, improving the shield, increasing pure damage and the chance of critical damage for his allies. In addition to power, receive another armor stat increase bonus with Reroll improving your battle durability.

Sebastian vs Hydra

Sebastian is a good hero against Hydras mainly in combination with Danada teams and can do a lot of damage, it can also be used with Jhu and Mojo teams, Sebastian's shield helps a lot on Hydras, in addition to increasing critical damage.

Sebastian's team suggestion for Hydras:

  1. Earth Hydra: Andvari - Yasmine - Maya - Sebastian - Jet (damage: 3M to 8M)
  2. Hydra of Darkness: Galahad - Sebastian - Daredevil - Fox - Dorian (Damage: 26M to 30M)
  3. Water Hydra: Astaroth - Elmir - Sebastian - Daredevil - Dorian (damage: 10M to 23M)
  4. Wind Hydra: Astaroth - Sebastian - Daredevil - Fox - Dorian (damage: 19M to 23M)
