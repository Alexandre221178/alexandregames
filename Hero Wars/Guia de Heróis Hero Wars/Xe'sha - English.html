Mastering Xe'sha: A Comprehensive Guide

In the realm of Chaos, where battles are won and lost with every move, Xe'sha emerges as a formidable hero, capable of turning the tide of even the most dire encounters. With her unique set of skills and synergies, Xe'sha is a cornerstone in end-game teams, wielding immense power that can overwhelm adversaries. In this guide, we'll delve into the strategies and tactics to unleash the full potential of Xe'sha on the battlefield.

Understanding Xe'sha's Abilities

Before delving into strategies, it's crucial to grasp the intricacies of Xe'sha's abilities:

  1. Deadly Ray (1st Skill):
    • Xe'Sha drains a percentage of max health from allies over 25% health, then attacks the enemy with the lowest magic defense.
    • The damage dealt depends on both the damage dealt to allies and Xe'Sha's magic attack.
    • If the target is defeated, allies recover double the health lost.
    • Basic attacks deal magic damage scaling with Magic Attack.
  2. Spiritual Power (2nd Skill):
    • Xe'Sha empowers allies, damaging them if their current health is above 25%.
    • Empowered heroes gain a Magic Attack bonus for a duration, doubled for Chaos heroes.
  3. Crimson Mist (3rd Skill):
    • Xe'Sha shrouds the battlefield, causing enemy basic attacks to miss for a limited duration.
  4. Soaring Might (4th Skill):
    • Xe'Sha gains a permanent magic attack bonus whenever an ally loses health from allied skills.

Strategies for Xe'sha Mastery

  1. Synergy with Sacrifice Keyword Heroes:

    Xe'sha excels when paired with Chaos faction heroes possessing the Sacrifice keyword. Her abilities synergize well with sacrificing allies, amplifying her damage potential and providing sustain for the team.

  2. Targeting Enemies with Low Magic Defense:

    Leveraging Deadly Ray's ability to target enemies with the lowest magic defense is crucial. Prioritize opponents vulnerable to magic damage to maximize effectiveness.

  3. Empowering Chaos Faction Heroes:

    Utilize Spiritual Power to empower Chaos heroes, capitalizing on the doubled Magic Attack bonus. This amplifies their damage output significantly, especially in crucial moments of battle.

  4. Timing Crimson Mist for Defensive Maneuvers:

    Crimson Mist can be a game-changer when timed correctly. Use it to mitigate incoming damage from enemy basic attacks, providing a window of opportunity for your team to strike.

  5. Capitalizing on Soaring Might's Bonus:

    Position allies to trigger Soaring Might's bonus effectively. The permanent magic attack bonus gained can exponentially increase Xe'sha's damage potential as the battle progresses.

Xe'sha's Talisman Guide: Understanding the Power Boost

Equipped with her talisman, Xe'sha experiences a significant augmentation in her magical prowess. Specifically, she acquires an additional 21,000 magical attack points, heightening her offensive capabilities, alongside a bolstering of 2,000 magical defense points, enhancing her resilience against magical assaults.

This augmentation translates into a formidable increase in Xe'sha's magical attack statistics, rendering her Ultimate ability exceptionally potent. The surge in her magical potency not only amplifies her damage potential but also solidifies her position as a force to be reckoned with on the battlefield.

Xe'sha vs Hydra

As you may have noticed, Xe'sha is a great hero against the magical hydras. Her prowess in battle allows her to face these formidable foes with confidence, often emerging victorious with just a single attempt. This not only showcases her strength but also proves invaluable in aiding the growth of your guild. Each triumphant encounter with a hydra brings not only glory but also valuable resources and recognition within the realm.

To ensure Xe'sha's success against magical hydras, assembling the right team is crucial. Here's a recommended lineup:

Each member of this team contributes their unique skills and abilities to complement Xe'sha's strengths, resulting in a formidable force capable of taking on any magical hydra that crosses their path. With determination and strategic planning, victory is within reach.

Rufus
Rufus is immortal against magical damage, and his first skill Rakashi Barrier creates a shield that absorbs all magical damage received by his allies. This way, Xe'sha was unable to eliminate anyone protected by Rufus' shield.

If Xe'sha is the enemy with the most intelligence, when Cornelius attacks with the ultimate he will deal a lot of physical damage with a huge monolith. Additionally, Cornelius creates a shield that increases magical defense for all allies.

If Xe'sha is the enemy with the highest magical attack when Phobos uses his third Dark Binds skill, he will connect to her and transfer all energy gained to him for 10 seconds. This way, Xe'sha will not be able to use her Ultimate.

Building the Ultimate Xe'sha Team

To harness Xe'sha's full potential, consider the following team composition:

  • Chaos Faction Heroes: Prioritize including Chaos heroes with Sacrifice keywords to maximize synergy.
  • Support Heroes: Include support heroes that complement Xe'sha's abilities, providing additional utility and sustain for the team.
  • Damage Dealers: Supplement Xe'sha's damage output with other high-damage heroes to pressure opponents effectively.
