Hero stats at max level 2023





Max level Skills

1st Deadly Ray

Xe'Sha drains 3% from the max health of any allies over 25% of their health currently remaining, then attack the enemy with the lowest magic defense. The damage dealt depends on the damage dealt to allies and Xe'Sha's magic attack. If the target is killed as a result, allies recover double the health they lost. Xe'Sha's basic attacks deal magic damage and scale with Magic Attack.
Damage: Max(76065) - (125% Magic Attack + 40 * (Skill Level) + 3% of allied max health)
Keywords: Sacrifice, Can't be copied

2nd Spiritual Power

Xe'Sha fills her allies with power, damaging them by 3% of their max health if their current health is above 25%. The empowered heroes gain a Magic Attack bonus for 4.0 seconds. The bonus is doubled for Chaos heroes.
Magic Attack Bonus: Max(32106) - (50% Magic Attack + 30 * (Skill Level))
Keywords: Sacrifice, Buff

3rd Crimson Mist

Xe'Sha shrouds the battlefield in mist for 4.0 seconds, causing enemy basic attacks to miss.
Chance: The chance to affect an enemy is lowered if the target's level is above skill level Max(120 = Skill Level + 20)

4th Soaring Might

Whenever an ally loses health from allied skills, Xe'Sha gains a permanent magic attack bonus of 25% of the health lost by the ally.
Chance: The chance to trigger the skill is lowered if the ally's level is above skill level Max(120 = Skill Level + 40)



Strategy on how to use Xe'sha

Xe'sha is a great hero and is often seen in the end-game forming teams that are difficult to beat, and is very strong dealing above normal OP damage if used in combination with Chaos faction heroes that have the sacrifice keyword in the skills.

Xe'sha can also deal a lot of damage to hydras if used with chaos faction heroes.

Fully found on top end-game Xe'sha teams and definitely worth investing in.
