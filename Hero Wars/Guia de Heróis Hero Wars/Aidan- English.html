Strategy on how to use Aidan

Aidan and his sister Kaila are the new heroes of the Chaos faction of 2023, arrived with a great synergy with power comparable to the combo of twins Krista and Lars, Aidan has the power to resurrect Kayla every time he uses his special ability, and Kayla also can resurrect Aidan whenever she uses her ultimate, that way the two become unstoppable.

In addition, his skills have a lot of sacrifice keywords, which means that all the time he sacrifices his life for his team and that makes Xe'sha very strong, as her fourth skill Highborne Strength causes her to gain a 25% magic attack bonus whenever that an ally sacrifices life using skills and Aidan with skills makes the whole team sacrifice life, and in this way, this combo is a success in the Hydras of Hero Wars Mobile.

Aidan can also be a good healer even if used with other factions, but his true potential is with the Chaos faction, especially alongside her sister Kayla and her ally Xe`sha.


Positive and Negative Points

Positive Points

  • Sacrifices life to heal allies
  • Great against magical hydras in combination with Xe'sha
  • Can resurrect Sister Kayla multiple times

Negative Points

  • Weak against physical damage teams
  • Resurrect does not work against the Morrigan
  • Deals little damage against enemies
  • The ability to resurrect only works for her sister Kayla

Priorities of Aidan's Glyphs, Artifacts, and Skins Evolution

In this guide, we will explore specific strategies to maximize the potential of the hero Aidan in Hero Wars Alliance, focusing on the evolution of his glyphs, artifacts, and skins. With these tips, you can optimize Aidan's performance and make him a dominant force in your battles.

1. Glyphs

Glyphs play a fundamental role in strengthening Aidan's statistics and abilities. Here is a table with the level-up priorities for Aidan's glyphs:

Priority Glyphs
1st Magic Attack
2nd Health
3rd Intelligence
4th Magic Defense
5th Armor

Glyphs Priorities:

  • Magic Attack: Increases Aidan's offensive power, making his abilities more devastating.
  • Health: Ensures Aidan's survival in combat, allowing him to remain active for longer.
  • Intelligence: Contributes to increasing Aidan's magic attack, improving his performance in confrontations.
  • Magic Defense: Essential for resisting magic attacks, especially in battles against Hydras.
  • Armor: Prioritize if your goal is to shine in the arena, providing solid defense against physical attacks.

2. Artifacts

Artifacts are another important tool to strengthen Aidan and his team. Here are the level-up priorities for Aidan's artifacts:

Priority Artifacts
1st Book
2nd Ring
3rd Weapon (Magic Defense)

Artifacts Priorities:

  • Book: Prioritize this artifact to increase Aidan's magic attack and health, improving his ability to deal damage and survive in combat.
  • Ring: Second, level up the ring to provide additional benefits to Aidan's team.
  • Weapon (Magic Defense): Lastly, focus on the weapon to increase the magic defense of all allies, which is especially useful in battles against magical Hydras.

3. Skins

Skins not only enhance the aesthetics of your hero but also grant significant benefits. Here are the level-up priorities for Aidan's skins:

Priority Skins
1st Magic Attack
2nd Intelligence

Skins Priorities:

  • Magic Attack: Level up this skin first to increase Aidan's healing ability, making him more effective in sustaining your team during battles.
  • Intelligence: Next, focus on the intelligence skin to gain more magic attack and magic defense, further increasing Aidan's offensive and defensive power.

General Evolution Strategy for Aidan

In addition to prioritizing Aidan's evolution through glyphs, artifacts, and skins, it is important to consider a general strategy for your team. Here are some additional tips:

  • Assemble a team that complements Aidan's abilities, including healers and heroes that boost magic damage.
  • Know Aidan's strengths and weaknesses and adjust your strategy according to the type of battle you are engaging in.
  • Train Aidan and his team regularly to maximize their potential and unlock new abilities and improvements.
  • Stay alert to updates and changes in the game to adjust your strategy as needed.

With these tips and priorities in mind, you will be well-equipped to lead Aidan and his team to victory in the battles of Hero Wars Mobile Hydras. Good luck, and may glory be on your side!

1st Phoenix Ritual

Final Conclusion of Aidan's Guide in Hero Wars

Aidan and his sister Kayla emerged as the new heroes of the Chaos faction in 2023, bringing exceptional synergy and power comparable to the legendary combo of twins Krista and Lars. With Aidan's unique ability to resurrect Kayla and vice versa, their duo becomes unstoppable, ensuring a dominant presence on the battlefield.

Furthermore, Aidan's abilities are permeated with the keyword "sacrifice," demonstrating his commitment to sacrificing his own life for the good of his team. This mechanic aligns perfectly with heroes like Xe'sha, whose fourth ability, High Altaneira, grants a 25% magic attack bonus whenever an ally sacrifices life using abilities. The success of this combo in battles against the Hero Wars Mobile Hydras is evident, solidifying its position as a victorious strategy.

Although Aidan can play an effective role as a healer in other factions, his true potential is unlocked alongside his sister Kayla and allies like Xe'sha in the Chaos faction. His ability to sacrifice, resurrect, and heal make him an invaluable asset to any team seeking glory in the lands of Hero Wars.
