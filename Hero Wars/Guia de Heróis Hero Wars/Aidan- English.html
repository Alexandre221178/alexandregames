Strategy on how to use Aidan

Aidan and his sister Kaila are the new heroes of the Chaos faction of 2023, arrived with a great synergy with power comparable to the combo of twins Krista and Lars, Aidan has the power to resurrect Kayla every time he uses his special ability, and Kayla also can resurrect Aidan whenever she uses her ultimate, that way the two become unstoppable.

In addition, his skills have a lot of sacrifice keywords, which means that all the time he sacrifices his life for his team and that makes Xe'sha very strong, as her fourth skill Highborne Strength causes her to gain a 25% magic attack bonus whenever that an ally sacrifices life using skills and Aidan with skills makes the whole team sacrifice life, and in this way, this combo is a success in the Hydras of Hero Wars Mobile.

Aidan can also be a good healer even if used with other factions, but his true potential is with the Chaos faction, especially alongside her sister Kayla and her ally Xe`sha.

The Ashes Talisman for Aidan in Hero Wars Alliance

The Ashes Talisman is a powerful enhancement for Aidan, significantly boosting his performance on the battlefield. Aidan can receive up to three slots of 6000 points of magic attack each, in addition to the initial benefits provided by the talisman.

Initial Benefits

  • Intelligence Increase: Aidan gains 2000 intelligence points.
    • Magic Attack Boost: Each point of intelligence adds 3 points to Aidan's magic attack. Therefore, the 2000 intelligence points translate to a total increase of 6000 points in magic attack.
    • Magic Defense Boost: Each point of intelligence also contributes 1 point to Aidan's magic defense, resulting in a total of 2000 points of magic defense.
    • Physical Attack Bonus: Although primarily a magic attacker, Aidan gains an additional 1 point of physical attack per intelligence point, adding 2000 points of physical attack, which enhances his basic attacks.

Additional Slots

Aidan can further enhance his magic attack with three additional slots, each providing 6000 points of magic attack. This brings the potential total additional magic attack from the talisman to 18000 points.

Healing and Shielding Enhancement

Aidan's increased magic attack greatly enhances his healing capabilities. With higher magic attack, Aidan can heal much more effectively while providing a shield to the ally with the lowest health. This improvement makes him an even more valuable support character, capable of keeping his team alive longer in battles.

Synergy with Kayla

The talisman reinforces Aidan's synergy with his sister, Kayla. Both siblings have abilities that allow them to resurrect each other, and the increased magic attack from the talisman makes their resurrection skills more potent. This unique synergy can turn the tide of battle by bringing back key characters at critical moments.

Chaos Faction Synergy

The Ashes Talisman also enhances Aidan’s synergy with characters from the Chaos faction, particularly those with Sacrifice abilities. These characters can benefit from the increased magic attack and defense provided by Aidan’s talisman, allowing for more effective sacrifices and overall team strategy.

Talisman Statistics

  • Slot 0: Intelligence +2000
  • Slot 1: Magic Attack +6000
  • Slot 2: Magic Attack +6000
  • Slot 3: Magic Attack +6000
  • Additional Slots: Aidan can further enhance his capabilities with three more slots, each potentially adding up to 6000 points of magic attack.

Significance of the Ashes Talisman

The Ashes Talisman significantly boosts Aidan’s overall performance by enhancing his magic attack, magic defense, and even providing a bonus to his physical attack. These improvements make Aidan a more formidable healer and support character, capable of providing better shields, stronger heals, and reinforcing his synergy with both his sister Kayla and other Chaos faction members. With the potential for three additional slots each adding up to 6000 points of magic attack, this talisman is an excellent choice for players looking to maximize Aidan’s potential in Hero Wars Alliance.

Priorities of Aidan's Glyphs, Artifacts, and Skins Evolution

In this guide, we will explore specific strategies to maximize the potential of the hero Aidan in Hero Wars Alliance, focusing on the evolution of his glyphs, artifacts, and skins. With these tips, you can optimize Aidan's performance and make him a dominant force in your battles.

1. Glyphs

Glyphs play a fundamental role in strengthening Aidan's statistics and abilities. Here is a table with the level-up priorities for Aidan's glyphs:

Priority Glyphs
1st Magic Attack
2nd Health
3rd Intelligence
4th Magic Defense
5th Armor

Glyphs Priorities:

  • Magic Attack: Increases Aidan's offensive power, making his abilities more devastating.
  • Health: Ensures Aidan's survival in combat, allowing him to remain active for longer.
  • Intelligence: Contributes to increasing Aidan's magic attack, improving his performance in confrontations.
  • Magic Defense: Essential for resisting magic attacks, especially in battles against Hydras.
  • Armor: Prioritize if your goal is to shine in the arena, providing solid defense against physical attacks.

2. Artifacts

Artifacts are another important tool to strengthen Aidan and his team. Here are the level-up priorities for Aidan's artifacts:

Priority Artifacts
1st Book
2nd Ring
3rd Weapon (Magic Defense)

Artifacts Priorities:

  • Book: Prioritize this artifact to increase Aidan's magic attack and health, improving his ability to deal damage and survive in combat.
  • Ring: Second, level up the ring to provide additional benefits to Aidan's team.
  • Weapon (Magic Defense): Lastly, focus on the weapon to increase the magic defense of all allies, which is especially useful in battles against magical Hydras.

3. Skins

Skins not only enhance the aesthetics of your hero but also grant significant benefits. Here are the level-up priorities for Aidan's skins:

Priority Skins
1st Magic Attack
2nd Intelligence

Skins Priorities:

  • Magic Attack: Level up this skin first to increase Aidan's healing ability, making him more effective in sustaining your team during battles.
  • Intelligence: Next, focus on the intelligence skin to gain more magic attack and magic defense, further increasing Aidan's offensive and defensive power.

General Evolution Strategy for Aidan

In addition to prioritizing Aidan's evolution through glyphs, artifacts, and skins, it is important to consider a general strategy for your team. Here are some additional tips:

  • Assemble a team that complements Aidan's abilities, including healers and heroes that boost magic damage.
  • Know Aidan's strengths and weaknesses and adjust your strategy according to the type of battle you are engaging in.
  • Train Aidan and his team regularly to maximize their potential and unlock new abilities and improvements.
  • Stay alert to updates and changes in the game to adjust your strategy as needed.

With these tips and priorities in mind, you will be well-equipped to lead Aidan and his team to victory in the battles of Hero Wars Mobile Hydras. Good luck, and may glory be on your side!

Aidan Guide on Teams 1 to 6 Hero Wars

1. Aidan, Kayla, Oya, Tristan, Cleaver

Cleaver is the tank that synergizes well with Chaos allies. Oya pulls enemies forward, causing damage to the frontline along with Cleaver. Tristan supports with buffs, enhancing Aidan and Kayla's damage output.

2. Aidan, Kayla, Oya, Tristan, Astaroth

Astaroth protects Kayla when she jumps into the middle of the enemy team and can also resurrect her if she dies. Tristan continues to support with buffs, and Oya adds additional crowd control and damage.

3. Aidan, Kayla, Oya, Tristan, Luther

Luther can dive into the enemy backline, similar to Kayla, creating disruption and chaos. This duo can overwhelm the enemy's backline while Tristan and Oya provide support and control. Aidan offers consistent damage, complementing the burst damage from Kayla and Luther.

4. Fafnir, Aidan, Kayla, Tristan, Astaroth

Fafnir provides shields and boosts physical attack, making Kayla even more resistant at the start of the battle. Astaroth ensures survivability with protection and resurrection, while Tristan enhances physical damage output. This team is well-rounded, focusing on both offense and defense.

5. Julius, Kayla, Sebastian, Aidan, Jet

This team features a strong combination of control and damage. Julius provides tanking and crowd control, Kayla offers burst damage, Sebastian boosts critical hit chances and cleanses, and Jet enhances critical damage and heals. Aidan adds consistent damage, making this team versatile and resilient.

6. Luther, Tristan, Kayla, Aidan, Fafnir

Luther and Kayla both dive into the enemy backline, creating a high-pressure situation. Tristan provide buffs and Fafnir shields, ensuring survivability and maximizing damage output. Aidan offers steady damage, making this team aggressive and durable in prolonged fights.

Final Conclusion of Aidan's Guide in Hero Wars

Aidan and his sister Kayla emerged as the new heroes of the Chaos faction in 2023, bringing exceptional synergy and power comparable to the legendary combo of twins Krista and Lars. With Aidan's unique ability to resurrect Kayla and vice versa, their duo becomes unstoppable, ensuring a dominant presence on the battlefield.

Furthermore, Aidan's abilities are permeated with the keyword "sacrifice," demonstrating his commitment to sacrificing his own life for the good of his team. This mechanic aligns perfectly with heroes like Xe'sha, whose fourth ability, High Altaneira, grants a 25% magic attack bonus whenever an ally sacrifices life using abilities. The success of this combo in battles against the Hero Wars Mobile Hydras is evident, solidifying its position as a victorious strategy.

Although Aidan can play an effective role as a healer in other factions, his true potential is unlocked alongside his sister Kayla and allies like Xe'sha in the Chaos faction. His ability to sacrifice, resurrect, and heal make him an invaluable asset to any team seeking glory in the lands of Hero Wars.
