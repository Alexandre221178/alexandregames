Strategy on how to use Aidan

Aidan and his sister Kaila are the new heroes of the Chaos faction of 2023, arrived with a great synergy with power comparable to the combo of twins Krista and Lars, Aidan has the power to resurrect Kayla every time he uses it. Her special ability and Kayla can resurrect Aidan whenever she also uses her ultimate, that way the two become unstoppable.


  • Advertisement
     

In addition, his skills have a lot of sacrifice keywords, which means that all the time he sacrifices his life for his team and that makes Xe'sha very strong, as her fourth skill Highborne Strength causes her to gain a 25% magic attack bonus whenever that an ally sacrifices life using skills and Aidan with skills makes the whole team sacrifice life, and in this way, this combo is a success in the Hydras of Hero Wars Mobile.

Aidan can also be a good healer even if used with other factions, but his true potential is with the Chaos faction, especially alongside her sister Kayla and her ally Xe`sha.


Positive and Negative Points

Positive Points

  • Sacrifices life to heal allies
  • Great against magical hydras in combination with Xe'sha
  • Can resurrect Sister Kayla multiple times

Negative Points

  • Weak against physical damage teams
  • Resurrect does not work against the Morrigan
  • Deals little damage against enemies
  • The ability to resurrect only works for her sister Kayla

Stats Evolution Priority

Glyphs Priority

In Aidan's glyphs, prioritize magic attack and health, as Aidan needs these two attributes to increase the powers of his skills, then intelligence to gain more magic attack and magic defense for Hydras. Lastly, focus on magic defense if your goal is hydras and armor if your goal is the arena.

  1. Magic attack
  2. Live
  3. Intelligence
  4. Magic Defense
  5. Armor

Artifacts Priority

In artifacts, prioritize the book to gain more magic attack and life, then the ring, and lastly the weapon to gain magic defense for all allies, especially in magical hydras.

  1. Book
  2. Ring
  3. Weapon (Magic Defense)

Skins Priority

  1. Intelligence

Aidan stats at max level 2023


Max level Skills


1st Phoenix Ritual

Aidan sacrifices 20% of his maximum health. Then he increases allies' maximum and current health and sets them on fire. Burning heroes take damage over time equal to the health bonus for 8.0 seconds. If Kayla in the allied team is dead, she gets resurrected. Aidan's abilities do not deal damage to heroes on his team if their health is less than 25%.
Aidan's Health Sacrifice: Max(54844) - (20% * Health) Allies' Health Increase: Max(57244) - (20% * Health) + (Skill level * 20)
Keywords: Sacrifice

2nd Fire Embrace

Heals the hero with the lowest health on the team and all the heroes on the team who lost health from friendly skills since the last time this skill was applied. Chaos Heroes gain more healing.
Healing: Max(22818) - ((30% * Magic Attack) + (Skill Level * 40)) Healing to each Chaos Hero: Max(45637) - ((60% * Magic Attack) + (Skill Level * 80)) Timers and Distances: Skill Timeout:12

3rd Bonds of Fire

Aidan deals damage to the ally with the highest health, as well as all other allies with more than 80% health, equal to 2.5% of their maximum health, increasing his maximum and current health by the value sacrificed by the allies, and heals them.
Healing: Max(14025) - (50% * Magic Attack) + ((Skill Level + 20) * 100) Timers and Distances: Initial Timeout:6 Skill Timeout:12 Animation Delay:0.98 Range:3500

4th Flame Shield

When Aidan or an ally that he started the fight with has less than 30% health left, Aidan creates a powerful shield around that hero that absorbs physical and magical damage for 4.0 seconds. The shield durability depends on the total maximum health of the team's heroes. This skill can be only applied once every 15.0 seconds to each ally.
Shield Durability (% of team max health): Max(24)% - ((Skill Level + 40) * 20%)% Keywords: Shield Timers and Distances: Skill Timeout:15 Duration:4
