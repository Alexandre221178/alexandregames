Strategy on how to use Dante

Dante's support skills are quite interesting, however you may prefer other characters when it comes to snipers that deal damage or use him with a hero that gives armor penetration to your team in the first artifact. Dante's main feature is to reduce the main attribute of enemies, in this way they become weak, tanks reduce health, mages reduce magic defense and magic attack, and agility heroes lose physical attack and armor.

  • Advertisement
     

Another advantage of Dante in reducing the main attributes of the enemy team is to increase the chance of dodging and critical damage, as these characteristics are calculated according to the main attributes of the heroes.

Dante can be used strategically in the attack to reduce the main attributes of the enemies or in the defense mainly with allies that reduce the armor of the tanks like Sebastian and Jet, because these allies together with Dante leave the enemies with little life and little armor, in this way, will be easily eliminated.


Positive and Negative Points

Positive Points

  • Has Skill that gives extra dodge bonus to allies
  • Reduces the main attribute (Strength, Agility and Intelligence) of enemies every time you throw a Spear of Destiny
  • Pushes enemies back away from allies
  • Dodge Artifact

Negative Points

  • Pushes enemies into Dorian's vampirism aura
  • Low magic defense
  • As a marksman hero it has no armor penetration and ends up becoming a support with little damage

Dante vs Hydra

Dante is not a great hero against Hydras and is not among the best Hydras teams, but because he has a lot of dodge for both him and the team he can be useful on secondary teams for physical damage hydras: earth, water and fire .

Dante cannot reduce the main stats of Hydras because Hydras are immune to debuffs, but it can be useful giving dodge bonuses if used on Eternity teams on Hydras.

Max level Skills

1st Instrument of Destiny

Dante throws 4 Spectral Spears at enemies and knocks them back, dealing physical damage to targets hit.
Damage: Max(54618) - (95% Physical Attack + 85 * Skill Level - 50) Keywords: Knockback

2nd Prediction

All Dante allied heroes have their Dodge increased for 7 seconds.
Dodge Bonus: Max(7680) - (10% Physical Attack + 25 * Skill Level) Keywords: buff

3rd Retribution

When Dante or an ally deflects physical attack damage, Dante summons a Spectral Spear and hurls it at the enemy in front. The spear pushes the target back and deals physical damage.
Damage: Max(46278) - (90% Physical Attack + 40 * (Skill Level + 40) - 650) Keywords: Knockback

4th Shackles of Weakness

Every time the Spectral Spear from Instrument of Fate or Retribution skills hits an enemy, Dante lowers the target's main stat for 7 seconds.
Enemy intelligence, agility, and strength have Max reduction (5208) - (6% physical attack) + 20 * (skill level + 40). Keywords: debuff

Dante tips from the Hero Wars devs!


Dante is the only Hero who can reduce his opponent's main stat.


This Guardian is also unique as he is the only one who can perform an action while dodging, namely throwing a spear at the enemy with his third skill.


Among Heroes, only Dante can increase allies' dodge.


Our Hero Dante has the highest HP among Guardians with the main stat "Agility".
