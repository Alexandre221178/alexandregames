Strategy on how to use Faceless

Faceless is among the best Heroes in Hero Wars, as he is a character, with abilities that quickly adapt to all teams.

Now with his talisman ability, Faceless will become even stronger with more magic attacks, more defense, and more health.

Faceless is the only character who can copy skills, and for that reason, he always causes doubts in enemies, as it is never possible to know the exact result that can result from battles.

In addition to having the ability to stun enemies, it also reduces the enemies' physical attack and increases the allies' magic defense, it is just having Faceless on the team and activating the violet skill that your whole team gains a magic defense bonus.

It is common to see Faceless in teams of K'arkh because when he uses the ability Power Throw simultaneously, K'arkh uses the ability Deadly Tendrils, dealing double damage to targets.

Faceless Talisman

With Talisman, Faceless gains 6,000 more magic attack, plus 2,000 magic defense, and 165,000 more health.

With more damage and more health, Faceless will become more resistant in PvP battles, both for wars and in the arena, being now able to cause more difficulty to enemy times.

For Hydra, the Faceless talisman can mean a small loss of income, since with more magic defense it will suffer less damage and activate less of the ultimate and its artifacts.

Artifacts Priority

In Faceless artifacts prioritize Intelligence, for each point of intelligence, he gains 3 points of magic attack, 1 point of magic defense, and 1 point of physical attack.

Then, if you decide to use Faceless on teams of Satori or other mages, prioritize the weapon to give more magic penetration, if you are going to use Eternity teams with Keira and Dante, prioritize the book to gain more magic attack and magic penetration.
