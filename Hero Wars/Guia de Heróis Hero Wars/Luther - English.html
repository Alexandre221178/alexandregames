Strategy on how to use Luther

A year ago, Omen was the first and Sentence the second skill. Switching their places was enough to make this Hero join the ranks of the most beloved and popular Guardians among players.


  • Advertisement
     

Luther is one of the few Heroes whose skills can change his own basic characteristics. When you level up the Doctrine of Faith skill, the Hero's Magic Defense will also increase in Stats.

Based on the previous point, maxed-out Luther has the highest Magic Defense in the game. Luther jumping into the opponents' team, turns enemies to himself, which allows for example to knock opponents up despite having Andvari on their team.


Positive and Negative Points

Positive Points

  • Advances into the enemy team and deals heavy damage to backline supports
  • Deals damage and stuns enemies
  • Increases magic defense

Negative Points

  • Advance and leave the team without a tank
  • Must be used together with a second tank
  • Faceless removes him from the enemy's team

Luther vs Hydra

Luther isn't a great hero for Hydras, but as a tank he can be support at hydra for secondary teams.

Luther at Max Level in Hero Wars Mobile

Max level Skills

1st Sentence

Stuns nearby enemies and damages them.
Damage: Max(#) - (60% Physical Attack + 50 * Skill Level)
Keywords: Stun, Can't be copied

2nd Omen

Luther moves deep into the enemy team, dealing damage to nearby enemies.
Damage: Max(#) - (10% Physical Attack + 50 * Skill Level)
Keywords: Knockback

3rd Gehenna

Landing after using Omen, Luther creates a fire wave that deals damage and stuns enemies ahead.
Damage: Max(#) - (20% Physical Attack + 25 * (Skill Level + 20)) Chance: Chance to stun is lowered if the target's level is above skill level Max(120 = Skill Level + 20)
Keywords: Stun

4th Doctrine of Faith

A passive skill. Increases magic defense.
Magic Defense: Max(#) - (10 * Skill Level + 15 * (Skill level + 40))
