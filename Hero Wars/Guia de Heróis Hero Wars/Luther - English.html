Strategy on how to use Luther

A year ago, Omen was the first and Sentence the second skill. Switching their places was enough to make this Hero join the ranks of the most beloved and popular Guardians among players.

Luther is one of the few Heroes whose skills can change his own basic characteristics. When you level up the Doctrine of Faith skill, the Hero's Magic Defense will also increase in Stats.

Based on the previous point, maxed-out Luther has the highest Magic Defense in the game. Luther jumping into the opponents' team, turns enemies to himself, which allows for example to knock opponents up despite having Andvari on their team.
