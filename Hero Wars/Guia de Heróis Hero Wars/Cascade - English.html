Advanced Strategies for Using Cascade in Hero War Alliance

In Hero War Alliance, each hero has their own distinct abilities that can be explored to the fullest with the right strategies. Cascade, a young titan, is one of those versatile heroes whose abilities can be enhanced with the right synergy. In this article, we'll explore the best strategies for utilizing Cascade, especially when working in conjunction with other heroes like Maya, Satori, and Heidi.

Synergy with Maya, Satori, and Heidi

One of Cascade's most powerful synergies is with the heroes Maya, Satori, and Heidi. Here's how they work together:

  • Maya and Heidi: Cascade's third ability, called "Reflux," deals additional damage for each portion of damage inflicted on an enemy while active. Maya and Heidi possess many skills with periodic damage, which is multiplied by Cascade's effect. This means that if Maya and Heidi attack enemies with pure periodic damage, Cascade's third ability will hit enemies with even purer damage.
  • Satori: Cascade is effective with Satori due to its ability to deal extra damage for each mark. Satori can mark enemies, and Cascade can capitalize on these marks to cause even more damage.

Why do Cascade's basic attacks deal 2 types of damage at the same time (magic and physical attacks)?

It's an exclusive feature of Cascade. Additionally, the young titan can cause 3 types of damage: magic, physical, and pure! This unique characteristic allows Cascade to be versatile on the battlefield, dealing significant damage to enemies regardless of their defenses.

Can Cascade defeat Rufus?

The ability to defeat Rufus depends on the battle circumstances and Cascade's abilities. Since Cascade can cause different types of damage, its effectiveness against Rufus may vary. However, its versatility makes it a formidable threat against a wide range of opponents.

Understanding Cascade's 4th Ability

One of Cascade's most striking features is its fourth ability, which gives it remarkable adaptability on the battlefield. This ability allows Cascade to adjust to the different types of damage present in a team, whether magical or physical. If it receives a magical penetration bonus, it will also receive armor penetration with the same value, and vice versa. This ability to adjust to circumstances makes Cascade a valuable addition to teams with predominance of damage in a specific type.

Enhancing with Artifacts

In addition to Cascade's natural abilities, its artifacts also play a crucial role in its performance on the battlefield. Its artifact weapon offers it two penetration buffs of each type, which accumulate to further increase its effectiveness. With a double effect on itself, Cascade can apply up to 38,000 armor penetration, making it even more formidable against resistant opponents.

Focus on Basic Attacks and Support Skills

While Cascade is capable of causing a variety of damage, it's important to recognize that its most powerful attack is concentrated in its basic attacks. Its skills are more geared towards team support and control, while its basic attacks are where it inflicts the majority of its damage. In ideal situations, Cascade can even cause triple damage with each basic attack, making it a force to be reckoned with on the battlefield.

More Than Just a Hero

Cascade is much more than just a hero with attack abilities. Its versatility, synergy with other heroes, and adaptability make it one of the most powerful characters in Hero War Alliance. By understanding its abilities and usage strategies, players can maximize Cascade's potential and lead their team to victory. Experiment with different team combinations, artifacts, and strategies to discover the true power of this young titan.

Cascade Talisman Guide

In the Hero Wars Alliance universe, the character Cascade's talisman plays a crucial role in improving his abilities and combat power. This mystical talisman has been carefully designed to enhance the specific characteristics of the Cascade, focusing primarily on the Agility stat.

By strengthening Agility, the talisman not only grants greater resistance to the Cascade, but also enhances your offensive capabilities. The increase in Physical Attack points results in more powerful strikes, allowing Cascade to deal significant damage to his opponents.

Furthermore, the synergistic effect of the talisman goes beyond simply increasing stats. Improvements to Physical Attack directly contribute to increasing the power of Cascade abilities. Your abilities become more lethal, capable of inflicting considerable damage and weakening enemies' armor and magic defenses.

In the heat of battle, Cascade becomes a formidable force, taking advantage of the advantages conferred by the talisman. His extraordinary agility not only makes him more evasive but also enhances his offensive abilities. The result is a more effective warrior, capable of tipping the balance in his team's favor, weakening enemy defenses, and wreaking havoc on the battlefield.

Thus, the Cascade talisman plays a crucial role in the character's combat power, improving his skills and elevating him to a higher level of power in the Hero Wars Alliance universe.

Max level Skills

1st Hydrokinesis

Unleashes a heavy downpour, damaging enemies every second and inflicting status effects depending on their main stat. Strength: Deals physical damage, decreasing armor and magic defense. Agility: Deals magic damage, blinding the target. Intelligence: Deals physical damage, silencing the target.
Keywords: Debuff, blinding
Damage Over Time: 1616(Depends on physical attack) per second.
Armor and Magic Defense decrease: 5265(Depends on physical attack)

2nd Tidal Wave

Cascade dashes through enemy lines, dealing magic damage and knocking back enemies. Then he returns to the initial position.
Keywords: Knock back
Damage: 11363 of Magic Damage
Formula: (20% physical attack + 1200)

3rd Refluence

After casting the “TIDAL WAVE” skill, Cascade inflicts the Sodden Armor status effect on the enemies hit on his way back. This effect lasts for 8.0 seconds. Whenever the afflicted enemy takes damage, Sodden Armor deals extra damage of the same type.
If the damage was dealt by an allied Mystery Hero, the afflicted enemy takes increased damage.
Extra damage: 5657(7% physical attack + 2400)
Extra damage from Mystery Heroes: 12422 (15% physical attack + 4800)

4th Elemental Surge

Cascade’s Magic Defense penetration is always equal to his armor penetration value. When one of these stats is affected in battle, the other changes as well.
The type of damage from Cascade's basic attacks changes with the target’s defense stats. If the target's Armor is greater then their Magic Defense, they will take magic damage, and if not, they will take physical damage instead.
If the hero's basic attack penetrates the target’s Armor or Magic Defense, the target will also take the other type of damage in an extra attack. If the extra attack penetrates the associated defense, the target also takes pure damage.
Consecutive attack damage: 100%
