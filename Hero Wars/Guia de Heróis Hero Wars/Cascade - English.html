Cascade History


Hero Wars Mobile History: Cascade, a young titan, with power over water currents. Skillfully fights against any opponent, no matter what the type of armor.

Positive and Negative Points

Positive Points

  • Extra damage for Mystery Heroes
  • Reduces enemies defense
  • Damage enemies by stats
  • Artifact weapon with two skills Armor and Magic Penetration

Negative Points

  • Weak Against Corvus

Stats Evolution Priority

Glyphs

On Cascade glyphs, prioritize Health for survival if dying fast; and Agility to gain physical defense armor and improve skills.
After going up the glyph of Health and Agility, go up Physical Attack to gain the skills buff, lastly go up with Armor Penetration to deal more damage and Armor for more physical defense.

  1. Health
  2. Agility
  3. Physical Attack
  4. Armor Penetration
  5. Armor

Artifacts

In Cascade's artifacts, prioritize the weapon because it is a unique artifact that gives magical and physical penetration to the whole team.

  1. Weapon (Magic Penetration and Armor Penetration)
  2. Ring
  3. Book

Skins

At the moment Cascade only has Agility Skin.

  1. Agility



Cascade vs Hydra

Cascade is not a great hero against hydras, as hydras are immune to debuffs.

Max level Skills

1st Hydrokinesis

Unleashes a heavy downpour, damaging enemies every second and inflicting status effects depending on their main stat. Strength: Deals physical damage, decreasing armor and magic defense. Agility: Deals magic damage, blinding the target. Intelligence: Deals physical damage, silencing the target.
Keywords: Debuff, blinding
Damage Over Time: 1616(Depends on physical attack) per second.
Armor and Magic Defense decrease: 5265(Depends on physical attack)

2nd Tidal Wave

Cascade dashes through enemy lines, dealing magic damage and knocking back enemies. Then he returns to the initial position.
Keywords: Knock back
Damage: 11363 of Magic Damage
Formula: (20% physical attack + 1200)

3rd Refluence

After casting the “TIDAL WAVE” skill, Cascade inflicts the Sodden Armor status effect on the enemies hit on his way back. This effect lasts for 8.0 seconds. Whenever the afflicted enemy takes damage, Sodden Armor deals extra damage of the same type.
If the damage was dealt by an allied Mystery Hero, the afflicted enemy takes increased damage.
Extra damage: 5657(7% physical attack + 2400)
Extra damage from Mystery Heroes: 12422 (15% physical attack + 4800)

4th Elemental Surge

Cascade’s Magic Defense penetration is always equal to his armor penetration value. When one of these stats is affected in battle, the other changes as well.
The type of damage from Cascade's basic attacks changes with the target’s defense stats. If the target's Armor is greater then their Magic Defense, they will take magic damage, and if not, they will take physical damage instead.
If the hero's basic attack penetrates the target’s Armor or Magic Defense, the target will also take the other type of damage in an extra attack. If the extra attack penetrates the associated defense, the target also takes pure damage.
Consecutive attack damage: 100%



Strategy on how to use Cascade

Cascade is the newest hero of the mystery faction, has great synergy with Maya and Heide to increase pure damage, in addition to great synergy with Satori, one of the best assassin mages in the Hero Wars Mobile game.

Cascade also counters of Luther and Kayla when he uses his ultimate ability Hydrokinesis pushes Luther and Kayla back towards the enemy team.

It's still too early to say how strong Cascade is, but my advice is that new heroes are always strong and worth investing in.

Tips from the Hero Wars devs!


Q:How does the synergy with Maya, Satori and Heidi work?

A: Sodden Armor effect of Cascade's third ability deals additional damage for every damage portion dealt to an enemy while it is active. Maya and Heidi have many abilities with periodic damage that is multiplied by Cascade's effect. So if Maya and Heidi attack enemies with periodic pure damage, Cascade's third ability will hit enemies with even more pure damage. Similarly, Cascade is effective with Satori, as he gives extra damage for each mark.


Q: Why do Cascade's basic attacks deal 2 types of damage at the same time (magical and physical attacks) in the event?

A: It's a Cascade-exclusive feature. In addition, the young titan can deal 3 types of damage: magical, physical and pure!


Q: With his 4th skill, if he gets a magic penetration bonus, will he get armor penetration of the same value and vice versa?

A: Yes, its strong quality is adaptability, Cascade will be a good addition to teams with a predominance of magic and physical damage.


Q: His artifact weapon gives him two penetration buffs of each type. Would they stack with your 4th skill (19,000*2) or just 19,000 each?

A: Cascade applies a double effect to itself, which is 38,000.


Q: Can Cascade kill Rufus?

A: The ability to kill Rufus is highly dependent on the circumstances Cascade will be in, as he attacks with different types of damage.


Q: Cascade's power attack is more focused on basic attacks than abilities, right?

A: Yes, his skills are more focused on support and team control, and his basic attacks are where the damage is contained. Under the best of circumstances, Cascade can deal triple damage with each attack.
