Strategy on how to use Ginger

Ginger is a very strong marksman in the progress faction, mainly due to her fourth ability Ranging Shot, with the power to increase the physical attack for progress heroes with each basic attack.

  • Advertisement
     

His physical damage power allied to the shield protection synergy of the progress faction make Ginger a deadly marksman, and worth investing to have on your team.

A good strategy is to keep Ginger positioned as the hero furthest from the back line, that way she receives the Judge's protection shield from the beginning of the battle, being protected from physical and magical damage.


Positive and Negative Points

Positive Points

  • Deals damage in area
  • Physical attack bonus for Progress heroes
  • Back Line Marksman

Negative Points

  • Low physical defense
  • Weak against mage teams
  • Only strong on progress teams

Stats Evolution Priority

Glyphs Priority

In Ginger's glyphs, prioritize armor penetration to deal more damage to enemies, then physical attack and agility, lastly health and magic defense.

  1. Armor Penetration
  2. Physical attack
  3. Agility
  4. Health
  5. Magical Defense

Artifacts Priority

In Ginger's artifacts, prioritize the book, as it increases armor pentration and physical attack, then agility to gain more physical attack and armor, and finally the weapon that will increase physical attack when activated.

  1. Book
  2. Agility
  3. Weapon (Physical Attack)

Skins Priority

Ginger's skins prioritize armor penetration to increase damage, then agility to increase physical attack and armor, then the two physical attack skins, and lastly magic defense.

  1. Armor Penetration
  2. Agility
  3. Physical attack
  4. Physical attack
  5. Magic Defense


Ginger vs Hydra

Ginger isn't a great hero against hydras, but can deal decent damage to hydras if used in the right team.
