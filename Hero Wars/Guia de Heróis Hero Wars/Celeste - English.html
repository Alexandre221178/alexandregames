Strategy on how to use Celeste

Celeste is one of the three rarest heroes (Celeste, Cleaver, Jet) and you can only acquire Soulstones from hero chests or during Special Events. Her skill set requires a lot of attention, considering she has a light form and a dark form, or the ability to block healing from enemies and heal allies, attacking with great effectiveness, is great to use against teams of healers.

  • Advertisement
     

Celeste can remove all kinds of negative effects from her allies with the Limbo skill, and that's why she is always among the best heroes in the game, I like to use this skill against teams of Q.Mao, Sebastian for that it removes the Curse applied to the tank.

Celeste is often found on top teams. If you don't have Martha, Celeste is a great hero to invest in Hero Wars Mobile.

Celeste's Talisman

With the Talisman Celeste will gain an additional 2000 points of intelligence which will give her an additional 6000 points of magical attack and 2000 points of magical defense. With Reroll she can gain an additional 19800 points of armor and will be more resistant on the front line against physical damage.

Celeste really needs this talisman to become strong, normally she is very weak and even at maximum level she dies quickly, now with this talisman she will become stronger and be able to face the meta teams.

Celeste Stats Evolution Priority

Glyphs Priority

In Celeste glyphs, prioritize leveling up, magical penetration, magical attack, and intelligence to increase damage and healing, then health for more survivability. And lastly, magical defense.

Priorities Glyphs
1st Magic Penetration
2nd Magic Attack
3rd Intelligence
4th Health
5th Magic Defense

Artifacts Priority

In Celeste's Artifacts, the priority is to level up the book to increase magical penetration and magical attack. Then the weapon increases the magic attack for Celeste and the whole team, remembering that Celeste activates the artifact with 50% energy and lasts half the time. This way, she is activating the artifact all the time and healing and blocking healing from enemies. And lastly, the ring to gain more magical attack and magical defense.

Priorities Artifacts
1st Book
2nd Weapon (Magic attack)
3rd Ring

Skins Priority

In Celeste's skins prioritize the Super Skin to increase magical penetration, then magical attack and intelligence to increase damage. And lastly, magical defense and armor, remembering that for Celeste to tank the legendary hydras, the defense skins must be at least level 55.

Priorities Skins
1st Magic Penetration (Super Skin)
2nd Magic Attack
3rd Intelligence
4th Armor
5th Magic Defense

Celeste vs Hydra

Celeste is an excellent hero for hydras and is usually used as a tank, but in order for her to be used on hydras as a tank, you must first make her very strong. On Legendary Hydras Celeste will only have resistance after raising its defense to maximum, this applies to skins, glyphs and artifacts.

  • Celeste's team for Earth Hydra: Martha, Isaac, Mojo, Jhu, Celeste (Damage: 17M to 21M)
