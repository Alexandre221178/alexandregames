Strategy on how to use Martha

Martha's healing powers and support abilities make her one of the best healers and supporters in the game.


  • Advertisement
     

She arguably ranks alongside Celeste and Aidan among the best healers in the game, and Martha's secondary role as a Tank makes her much more durable in battle.

Martha is a great hero overall, is very commonly found on top teams, is used to counter Jorgen and Jhu teams, and is definitely a good character to level up.


Positive and Negative Points

Positive Points

  • Healer
  • Increases team speed
  • A strong tank against enemies that attack in the back line;

Negative Points

  • It takes to much time to heal allies
  • Deals little damage

Martha vs Hydras

Martha is a great hero against hydras she can heal the team a lot with her teapot and also speed up her allies, it's a great combo to help with the Jhu and Mojo combo against hydras, and also help with the Xe'sha and Aidan combo.

Max level Skills

1st Foremother's Oath

Increases all allies' speed by 6.0 seconds.
Increases ally's speed by Max(240%) - (Skill Level + 120). Keywords: Buff

2nd Secret of Longevity

Martha's basic attacks heal her when a projectile hits an enemy. The farther the projectile travels, the more health it will restore.
Projectile health restored up to: Max(155963) - (24% Health + 400 * Skill Level) health.

3rd Tea Party

Conjures a totem in front of the character that restores allies' health. Enemies can't target the totem, but it can take AOE damage.
Health regen for each ally: Max(70682) - (60% Magic Attack + 250 * (Skill Level + 20)) for 8.0 seconds.

4th Healing Brew

While the totem is active, the ally with the least health now receives additional healing.
Health regeneration per second: Max(105477) - (15% Health + 400 * (Skill Level + 40) - 10,000) for 8 seconds.
