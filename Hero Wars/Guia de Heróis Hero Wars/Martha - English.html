Strategy on how to use Martha

Martha's healing powers and support abilities make her one of the best healers and supporters in the game.

She arguably ranks alongside Celeste and Aidan among the best healers in the game, and Martha's secondary role as a Tank makes her much more durable in battle.

Martha is a great hero overall, is very commonly found on top teams, is used to counter Jorgen and Jhu teams, and is definitely a good character to level up.
