How to Level Up Jorgen in Hero Wars Alliance: Strategies and Tips

Jorgen is one of the most versatile and useful heroes in Hero Wars Alliance. His main role is to control the battlefield, preventing enemies from using their special abilities and providing crucial support to his team. In this guide, we'll explore how to maximize Jorgen's potential and take him to the next level in terms of power and effectiveness.

Understanding Jorgen's Abilities

Before delving into leveling strategies, it's essential to understand Jorgen's abilities and how they can be maximized:

  • Torment of Impotence (First Ability): Jorgen summons a skull that deals magical damage to nearby enemy heroes. Additionally, affected enemies cannot gain energy for 9 seconds. This ability is crucial for interrupting enemies' special abilities, giving your team a significant advantage.
  • Energy Cycle (Second Ability): This ability protects an ally with a magical shield, allowing the ally to gain energy at double the rate until the shield is destroyed. This is especially useful for heroes who rely on energy for their special abilities, ensuring they can cast abilities more frequently.
  • Leprosy (Third Ability): Jorgen curses all enemies, causing the furthest one to become the target of the curse. For 10 seconds, all physical damage received by enemies is redirected to the cursed target. This ability is excellent for diverting damage away from your more fragile heroes and focusing on a single enemy.
  • Contaminated Wounds (Fourth Ability): With each attack, Jorgen steals energy from the affected enemy. This ability is essential for keeping enemies low on energy, limiting their ability to cast special abilities.

Strategies for Leveling Up Jorgen

Now that we understand Jorgen's abilities, let's discuss some strategies for leveling him up and maximizing his effectiveness on the team:

  1. Prioritize Control Abilities: When leveling up Jorgen, prioritize his control abilities like "Torment of Impotence" and "Leprosy." These abilities are essential for interrupting enemies and controlling the battlefield.
  2. Invest in Resistance and Magic Power: Since Jorgen is a support and control hero, it's crucial to invest in attributes that increase his resistance and magic power. This will make him more durable and effective in prolonged combat.
  3. Pair Him with Heroes That Benefit from Energy Reduction: Jorgen works best when paired with heroes who benefit from enemies' energy reduction. Heroes like Cleaver, who rely on powerful special abilities, can wreak havoc when enemies are low on energy due to Jorgen's abilities.
  4. Use Him in Various Team Compositions: Jorgen is a versatile hero who fits well in various team compositions. Experiment with different combinations to find the one that best suits your playstyle and the specific strategy you want to implement.
  5. Maximize His Abilities with Suitable Equipment and Artifacts: Make sure to equip Jorgen with items that increase his control and support attributes. Artifacts that boost resistance, magic power, and energy reduction will be especially useful for him.

Analysis of Jorgen's Talismans

Talisman of Corruption:

The Talisman of Corruption primarily enhances Jorgen's Strength, providing him with additional physical attack and health. With this talisman, Jorgen gains +2000 Strength, which translates into increased physical attack power, along with +19,800 Magic Defense distributed across three slots. The significant boost to Magic Defense makes Jorgen particularly resilient against mage teams, helping him withstand magical assaults.

Attributes of the Talisman of Corruption:

  • Slot 0: Strength +2000 (provides +80,000 Health and +2000 Physical Attack)
  • Slot 1: Magic Defense +6600
  • Slot 2: Magic Defense +6600
  • Slot 3: Magic Defense +6600

However, the physical attack boost from this talisman is somewhat redundant since Jorgen's primary role is not as a physical attacker. His abilities are more aligned with supporting and disrupting enemy teams rather than dealing direct damage. Therefore, while the Talisman of Corruption does improve his defense against magic, its physical attack boost is less impactful for his overall utility.

Talisman of Sacrifice:

The Talisman of Sacrifice focuses on enhancing Jorgen's Magic Attack and Armor. With this talisman, Jorgen gains +12,000 Magic Attack and +19,800 Armor, making him more formidable against physical threats. The Magic Attack boost directly benefits two of Jorgen's abilities:

Attributes of the Talisman of Sacrifice:

  • Slot 0: Magic Attack +12,000
  • Slot 1: Armor +6600
  • Slot 2: Armor +6600
  • Slot 3: Armor +6600

Given that Jorgen's effectiveness in battle is closely tied to his ability to disrupt and weaken the enemy team, the Talisman of Sacrifice's focus on Magic Attack is highly beneficial. It enhances his offensive capabilities, making him a more formidable force on the battlefield, especially when facing teams with high magic or physical damage output.

Summary of Jorgen Talismans

Talisman of Corruption: Best suited for players who want to bolster Jorgen's defenses against magical teams. The increased Magic Defense makes him harder to take down by mages, but the boost to Physical Attack is less useful given Jorgen's role.

Talisman of Sacrifice: Superior for players who want to enhance Jorgen's offensive capabilities and shield strength. This talisman significantly boosts his Magic Attack, making him more effective in both offensive and defensive roles, especially when facing mixed-damage teams.

Keep in mind that only one talisman can be equipped at a time, so it's important to make a wise choice. In summary, while both talismans have their strengths, the Talisman of Sacrifice generally offers more versatility and power, particularly in battles where Jorgen's abilities need to shine. It's the better choice for maximizing Jorgen's potential in Hero Wars Alliance.

How to Counter Jorgen

Best Counters What happens

Sebastian
Jorgen's Leprosy skill curses the enemy team and redirects all physical damage received by the enemy team to the farthest target. However, Sebastian's Ballad of Tenacity skill conjures a shield that protects against Jorgen's curse and thus breaks the curse.

Satori
The fourth skill, Jorgen's passive, steals some energy from the enemy that receives damage with each attack. In this way, Satori applies the marks to Jorgen and can cause mortal damage.

Nebula
Nebula's second ability, Calm, in addition to healing, clears negative effects from the two nearby allies who are receiving the buff from the balance ability. Therefore, when Jorgen uses Skill 1, Torment of Impotence, it prevents enemies from gaining energy. Nebula will clear this negative effect from the two nearby allies.

Martha
When Jorgen redirects all the enemy team physical damage to the furthest target and that target is Martha, she will resist, as Martha is a backline tank. In addition, she will gain energy and activate her Ultimate more times, accelerating all her allies.
