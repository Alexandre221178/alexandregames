Strategy on how to use Jorgen

Jorgen's main attribute is his strength, but his main utility as a character is his Control and Support abilities. Jorgen steals energy from enemies and this prevents them from using special skills (first green skill); that way it also prevents them from using their accumulated, and Jorgen is really great, a Jorgen with 60k of power is enough for you to leave the enemy team on the back foot.

Jorgen is a great ally for any team composition and is definitely worth investing in. He is very commonly found on top teams due to his great skills and defensive abilities.

Jorgen's Talisman

With the talisman, Jorgen will gain an additional 80,000 health points and 12,000 magical defense points, making him stronger for defense, especially against wizard teams.

Jorgen is the type of hero that even without leveling up you can get the most good results, and therefore many players have doubts about leveling up their defense skills. But the truth is that Jorgen at maximum level with Talisman will have insane power, and it will be worth investing.

How to Counter Jorgen

Best Counters What happens

Sebastian
Jorgen's Leprosy skill curses the enemy team and redirects all physical damage received by the enemy team to the farthest target. However, Sebastian's Ballad of Tenacity skill conjures a shield that protects against Jorgen's curse and thus breaks the curse.

Satori
The fourth skill, Jorgen's passive, steals some energy from the enemy that receives damage with each attack. In this way, Satori applies the marks to Jorgen and can cause mortal damage.

Nebula
Nebula's second ability, Calm, in addition to healing, clears negative effects from the two nearby allies who are receiving the buff from the balance ability. Therefore, when Jorgen uses Skill 1, Torment of Impotence, it prevents enemies from gaining energy. Nebula will clear this negative effect from the two nearby allies.

Martha
When Jorgen redirects all the enemy team physical damage to the furthest target and that target is Martha, she will resist, as Martha is a backline tank. In addition, she will gain energy and activate her Ultimate more times, accelerating all her allies.
