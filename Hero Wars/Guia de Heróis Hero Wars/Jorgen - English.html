Max level Skills

1ª Torment of Powerlessness

Jorgen summons a skull to the enemy’s front line, which deals magic damage to nearby heroes. Affected enemies can not gain energy for 9 seconds.
Damage: Max(32490) - (60% Magic attack + 100 * Skill Level)

2ª Cycle of Energies

Protects an ally with a magic shield. The ally will gain energy at a double rate until the shield is destroyed.
The shield can absorb: Max(38905) - (70% Magic attack + 125 * Skill Level) damage to any type"

3ª Leper

Jorgen curses everyone on the enemy team, making the furthest enemy the target of the curse. Physical damage taken by heroes on the enemy team will be redirected to the target of the curse for 10 seconds.
Chance: This skill redirects all physical damage to enemies below level Max(120 = Skill Level + 20). Only a part of the damage is redirected to higher-level enemies. Keywords: Curse

4ª Tainted Wounds

With every attack, Jorgen steals some of the affected enemy's energy.
Energy stolen: Max(100) = (Skill Level+40) - 20)

Strategy on how to use Jorgen

Jorgen's main attribute is his strength, but his main utility as a character is his Control and Support abilities. Jorgen steals energy from enemies and this prevents them from using special skills (first green skill); that way it also prevents them from using their accumulated, and Jorgen is really great, a Jorgen with 60k of power is enough for you to leave the enemy team on the back foot.

Jorgen is a great ally for any team composition and is definitely worth investing in. He is very commonly found on top teams due to his great skills and defensive abilities.
