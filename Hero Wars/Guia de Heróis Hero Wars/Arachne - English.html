Positive and Negative Points

Positive Points

  • Poisons enemies on all attacks even with basic attacks
  • Stuns enemies for 5 seconds on first and third skill
  • Focuses on the enemy with the lowest health, stuns, damages, and poisons
  • 80% vampirism

Negative Points

  • Low physical and magical defense
  • Low magic penetration
  • Not a great damage dealer



Stats Evolution Priority

Glyphs

Focus first on magic penetration, magic attack, and intelligence to deal more damage, then health and armor for defense.

  1. Magic Penetration
  2. Magic Attack
  3. Intelligence
  4. Health
  5. Armor

Artifacts

In artifacts you have to prioritize the book as it increases life and magic attack, then intelligence to gain more magic attack and magic defense and finally the weapon to give armor to the whole team.

  1. Book
  2. Ring
  3. Weapon (Armor Bonus)

Skins

On Arachne's skins, focus first on health if available, then intelligence to raise your main attributes, and lastly focus on more magic attacks and armor.

  1. Health
  2. Intelligence
  3. Magic Attack
  4. Armor
  5. Magic Attack



Arachne vs Hydra

Arachne is not a great hero against hydras, hydras are immune to stun and poison.

Max level Skills

1st Demolition Robot

Summons a spider monster that attacks the first enemy it sees in front of it, exploding and stunning enemies for 5.0 seconds.
Explosion damage: Max(22638) - (25% Magic Attack + 40 * Skill Level) Keywords: Stun

2nd Teleport

Leaps forward at target enemy with lower health, slashing at them with claws, then leaps back.
Damage: Max(92223) - (120% Magic Attack + 55 * Skill Level)

3rd Underground Strike

Attacks the nearest rival enemy dealing damage, and stunning the target and all enemies around it for 3 seconds.
Damage: Max(53547) - (70% Magic Attack + 30 * (Skill Level + 20)) Chance: Stun chance lowered if the target's level is above skill level (Max is 120 = 1 * (Skill Level + 20)) Keywords: Stun

4th Neurotoxin

Arachne poisons enemies for 3.0 seconds every time she deals damage to them.
Keywords: Stun



Strategy on how to use Arachne

Arachne is one of the heroes of the progress faction and has the ability to cause stun in the area, she is very strong against teams of Keira and Karkh, and other agility heroes, now, with the arrival of Fafnir in Hero Wars.

Arachne becomes a great ally against Fafnir teams with her abilities to stun for 5 seconds and also to poison enemies in any type of damage she causes, it is certainly a good choice, I am always surprised with strong Arachne in special events, but she is not often seen on end-game teams, maybe it will become important with the arrival of Fafnir.
