Strategy on how to use Lilith

Lilith is a great replacement for Martha when you need a backline tank because she has a lot of health, Lilith is also a marksman who deals a lot of damage.

Lilith has great and strong damage skills, and using the Seal of the Underworld skill, she can also heal herself. Additionally, it can be found on strong Chaos Faction teams and will do good combos with Xe`sha, Aidan, and Kayla.


Positive and Negative Points

Positive Points

  • Increases maximum health
  • Deals damage to enemies fighting in the middle line
  • Reduces enemies' magic defense
  • Sacrifice 5% of life to increase magic attack
  • It's a tank and plays in the back line

Lilith's Talisman Guide

With the Talisman, Lilith gains 2000 more points of strength which will give her, 80000 more extra life and 2000 more points of physical attack, and more points of armor.

The stat boosts gained with Talisman will make Lilith a very resistant tank for the backline in Hero Wars against all types of physical damage, especially damage from Jorgen allies redirected to the backline.

Lilith vs Hydra

Lilith is very strong against Hydras and can be seen on some chaos faction teams that do a lot of damage to Hydras. Now with the new heroes Aidan and Kayla, it will do even more damage, being a good option for both Hydras and battles.

Lilith at Max Level in Hero Wars Mobile

Max level Skills

1st Underworld Seal

Applies a demonic seal to the central enemy. The seal explodes, damaging the target and nearby enemies. Lilith grows in size, increasing her max and current health by 3% for each enemy damaged and for each allied Chaos Hero.
Damage: Max(20632) - (40% Magic Attack + 50 * Skill Level)

2nd Demonic Fury

Lilith lashes out in Demonic Fury for 10 seconds, attacking twice with each basic attack. Her attacks also target the central enemy and deal extra magic damage in an area of effect.
Extra AOE Damage: Max(25974) - (30% Magic Attack + 120 * Skill Level + 600)

3rd Fire Breath

Lilith breathes fire onto the central enemy, reducing the target and nearby enemies' magic defense by 8.0 seconds.
Magic Defense Reduction: Max(16645) - (25% Magic Attack + 60 * (Skill Level + 20) + 300) Keywords: Curse, Debuff

4th Sacrifice

If Lilith's health is above 25%, she sacrifices 5% of her max health before using each skill, increasing get magic attack by the sacrificed amount of health for 10 seconds.
Magic Attack increase: Max(31216) - (5% Health) Chance: Chance to trigger the skill is reduced if Lilith's level is above 120 Keywords: Buff
