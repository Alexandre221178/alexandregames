Strategy on how to use Jhu

Jhu is one of the heroes of the Nature faction, with critical damage marksman skill he is very strong against hydras and many other Hero Wars heroes. Now, with his recent buff he is even better for hydras and will help you and your Guild get more titan artifact summoning orbs is definitely a good choice to invest in Jhu to be your secondary hero.

  • Advertisement
     

In addition to Jhu being a great combo on Hydras with Mojo you can still use to reinforce the combo Martha to give speed, Thea even dead increases the speed of Jhu, Fafnir to increase damage and defense, Nebula, Celeste, Markus, Cornelius, Faceless, Orion, Rufus, Andvari, Astaroth are examples of (suggested limit reached) that you can use to increase the damage of this combo against hydras, you can see more on the channel page.


Jhu's Talisman

Jhu with Talisman will receive, 80000 additional health points and 2000 additional physical attack points, in addition to 6000 critical hit chance with Reroll. This way, Jhu will become stronger for both, battles and hydra. Even so, he will still remain a more useful hero against Hydras.

Jhu vs Hydra

Jhu is a great hero against hydras, Jhu's 1st skill makes him immortal for seven seconds while his 4th skill restores his health; especially if it is used with the hero Mojo that causes damage in his 4th skill even after being dead, that's right, the hydras are not immune to Mojo's mark and you only need to have a Mojo level Purple in combination for this perfect combo to cause a lot of damage in all hydras.

Max level Skills

1st You will not stop me

Jhu becomes enraged for 7 seconds, significantly increasing his attack speed. While enraged, Jhu cannot die.
Speed Bonus: Max(180) - (Skill Level + 60) Keywords: Buff

2nd I will take your life

Each of Jhu's attacks deals additional pure damage to the target while sacrificing 2% of their own current health.
Extra damage: Max(4.4%) - (0.02 * Skill Level + 2) of the enemy's current health Keywords: Percent-based damage, Sacrifice

3rd I see you

For a period of time, Jhu increases his attack power and focuses his attacks on a ranged enemy.
Extra damage: Max(16363 - (25% * Physical Attack + 60 * (skill level + 20))) Duration: Max(10) - (0.025 * (Skill Level+20) + 7) seconds Keywords: Buff

4th The spirits will heal me

If Jhu is not under control effects after the first skill is used, he regenerates some health.
Recovered health: Max(130306) - (200% Physical attack + ((Skill Level+40)*500) -3,000)
