Heidi History


Hero Wars Alliance History: Heidi's bravery once brought peace to the animal kingdom. Now the fluffy assassin and his best friend, the snake Koba, have joined the Guardians of the Dominion. A master of poisons and toxic herbology, Heidi commands poisonous spores, staying at a safe distances. His enemies will fall in three, two, one...

Strategy on how to use Heidi

With the 2023 Rework, Heidi becomes a King Kong. She is very strong against heroes who have shielding abilities and becomes a counter to all of them, especially Julius.

Heidi applies toxins to enemies that accumulate with each base damage. If an enemy uses a shield or tries to increase health (Aidan), the toxins are removed from him and applied to allies who received the shield or increased health.

Nebula, Celeste, or Julius also cannot remove Heidi's toxins. Whenever an enemy tries to remove the negative effects of the toxins, they will also receive damage from the toxins that were applied to the ally.

Heidi vs Hydra

Heidi is not a great hero against hydras, since hydras are immune to toxins and hydras also do not have shields to be penalized.

Heidi in Battles

Strong Against

  • Shield: Julius, Aidan, Judge, Isaac, Rufus, Astaroth, Fafnir, Andvari
  • Supporters: Nebula, Celeste, Julius

Heidi is strong against all heroes who have shield abilities, and is also strong against support heroes who remove negative effects, and backline heroes on shield teams.

Heidi Counters

  • Phobos, Cornelius, Iris, Morrigan, Corvus, Keira, K'arkh

    • Heidi is weak against the main mage counters of the Hero Wars Alliance. The best tank to face him is Corvus and Morrigan, with Phobos, Iris, or Cornelius. If Heidi's team is made up of Ziri and Nebula, it is recommended to use Phobos, as he is counter to Nebula and Ziri.

Heidi Best Teams

Heide's best teams after the 2023 Rework are the combo of Heidi, Amira and Ziri. The other two heroes must be chosen according to the team you plan to attack. Ziri is a super strong tank from the Mystery faction who takes all the damage for herself, and Amira manages to prevent the team from getting low magic damage.

Heidi Skills in Hero Wars

1st Deathflower

Summons a flower in the middle of the enemy team that spreads poisonous pollen and applies.
5 Stacks of toxin to enemies in the effective area, then deals damage to the affected targets, scaling with the stacks of toxin on all enemies in the battle.

2nd Poison mastery

The hero's basic attacks apply one stack of toxin to the opponent whenever they hit or are absorbed by a shield.
Every stack of toxin removed from an opponent before its full duration deals damage. if an opponent removes a stack, they take the same damage as well. Enemies take extra damage for each stack, scaling with the number of allied mystery heroes.

3rd Snake charmer

Releases a snake that removes all stacks of toxin from the nearest enemy, then attacks the furthest enemy and applies the same number of stacks of toxin to them.
The snake also applies half as many stacks to any opponents it passes by.

4th Detrimental effect

Whenever an opponent affected by stacks of toxin uses a skill that applies a shield or increases health on another enemy hero, all stacks of toxin are removed from them.
When those stacks are removed, the same number of stacks is applied to every target of the skill.
