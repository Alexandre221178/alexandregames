Hero stats at max level 2023



###### with skin ###### in Hero Wars Alliance

Max level Skills

1st Deathflower

Summons a flower in the middle of the enemy team that spreads poisonous pollen and applies.
5 Stacks of toxin to enemies in the effective area, then deals damage to the affected targets, scaling with the stacks of toxin on all enemies in the battle.

2nd Poison mastery

The hero's basic attacks apply one stack of toxin to the opponent whenever they hit or are absorbed by a shield.
Every stack of toxin removed from an opponent before its full duration deals damage. if an opponent removes a stack, they take the same damage as well. Enemies take extra damage for each stack, scaling with the number of allied mystery heroes.

3rd Snake charmer

Releases a snake that removes all stacks of toxin from the nearest enemy, then attacks the furthest enemy and applies the same number of stacks of toxin to them.
The snake also applies half as many stacks to any opponents it passes by.

4th Detrimental effect

Whenever an opponent affected by stacks of toxin uses a skill that applies a shield or increases health on another enemy hero, all stacks of toxin are removed from them.
When those stacks are removed, the same number of stacks is applied to every target of the skill.
