Strategy on how to use Dorian

Dorian is one of the best healers in Hero Wars. He is described as a Mage, his skill set has support written all over it. Dorian has synergy with several strong heroes like Daredevil, Orion, Helios, Peppy, Ginger, and even Keira and Fafnir make good combos with Dorian. You just need to level him up and promote him according to your need, get him on your team!

He is rarely found on top teams, because you only need to invest a little for Dorian to be strong, so players invest in other heroes first. But they all have a Dorian ready to use in offense, It's worth investing, and you may need him on your team.


Dorian's Talisman

With the talisman, Dorian will receive more points for magical attack and magical defense, and a lot of magical penetration in the Reroll, in terms of damage and improving support skills for the team. Little will change with Dorian's talisman, as his abilities depend more on percentages and not on increasing stats.

Dorian vs Hydra

Dorian is a great hero against the Hydras providing a lot of healing with his vampirism, it is very common to see Dorian in the Hydras teams, from the beginning of the game and with little strength Dorian should already be used to help in the hydras because his aura healing with vampirism works even when Dorian is dead.

Dorian team suggestion for Hydras:

  1. Water Hydra: Fafnir, Dorian, Artemis, Tristan, Galahad (average damage: 40M)
  2. Dark Hydra: Fafnir, Dorian, Artemis, Tristan, Galahad (average damage: 64M)
  3. Fire Hydra: Fafnir, Dorian, Artemis, Tristan, Galahad (average damage: 100M)
  4. Hydra of Light: Martha, Lilith, Faceless, Xe`sha (average damage: 100M)
  5. Wind Hydra: Dorian, Fox, DarkStar, Damned, Andvari (average damage: 34M)
  6. Earth Hydra: Dorian, Fox, DareDevil, Elmir, K'arkh (average damage: 27M)
