Strategy on how to use Satori

Satori is a great mage hero who applies marks to enemies and then deals a lot of damage based on the amount of marks applied to enemies.

He can deal a lot of damage after applying the marks, but because he is weak in defense Satori needs to be well-protected by tanks before attacking.

Now with his new magic defense and magic penetration skin, Satori teams will be a lot of work to defeat.

Satori is usually seen on strong end-game teams, especially with various mage combinations like Amira, Celeste, Martha, Faceless, Jorgen, Aurora, etc.

Satori's Talisman

With the talisman, Satori will receive more magical defense, more magical attack, and a lot of magical penetration, which will make him very strong and capable of destroying an entire team in seconds. And when the glyph class arrives in Hero Wars Alliance where mage class characters will receive extra energy, Satori will be a strong counter to all mages.

Satori vs Hydra

Satori isn't a great hero against hydras, but his vampirism ability can make him stay alive longer against hydras and deal good damage.

  • Satori's team suggestion for Wind Hydra: Helio, Dorian, Orion, Satori, Rufus (Damage: 4M to 6M)

Satori in Battles

Strong Against

Satori is strong in especially against heroes that receive extra energy like Orion, Jorgen, Andvari, and K'arkh, and also enemies that have little magical defense.

Satori can be strong against Iris' teams if Celeste is on her team. As Celeste will clear, the negative effect applied by Iris and Satori will use her Ultimate quickly, causing a lot of damage to enemies, which is why it is very common to see Satori's teams with Celeste.

  • Orion, Andvari, Krista & Lars, K`arkh, Martha, Hélio, Tristan, Keira, Jorgen(on physical teams)

Counters

Satori is weak against Nebula and Celeste, as these characters have abilities that can remove any type of negative effect, and thus remove Satori's marks from allies. Jorgen can be a counter to Satori if he is with mages like Cornelius or Rufus, who increase magical defense, and thus, Jorgen prevents Satori from being able to use his Ultimate by stealing energy.

  • Cornelius, Phobos, Iris, Rufus, Celeste, Nebula, Jorgen(in magic teams)

1st Ravenous Pack

Damage for each mark: Max(50620) - (50% Magic Attack + 150 * Skill Level) Keywords: Mark

2nd Spirit Banishment

Satori attacks all enemies, dealing damage and applying a Fox Fire mark.
Damage for each mark: Max(29096) - (40% Magic Attack + 25 * Skill Level) Chance: The chance to apply Fox Fire marks is lowered if the target's level is above skill level Max(120 = skill level) Keywords: Mark

3rd Centurial Wisdom

Each successful base attack steals intelligence from the enemy and restores the hero's health by 200% of the damage dealt. The intelligence steal is active until the battle ends and can't be canceled.
Intelligence Steal: Max(240) - (200% * (Skill Level + 20)) Chance: The chance to steal intelligence and restore health is lowered if the target's level is above skill level Max(120 = skill level)
