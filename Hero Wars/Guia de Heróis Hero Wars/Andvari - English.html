Stats Evolution Priority

Glyphs Priority

In the glyphs of Andvari level up health and strength first for him to have more survivability, then armor, lastly armor penetration and physical attack.

  1. Life
  2. Strength
  3. Armor
  4. Armor Penetration
  5. Physical attack

Artifacts Priority

In the Artifacts, priority ring to gain more life and physical attack, then the weapon to gain Armor and lastly the book.

  1. Ring
  2. Weapon(Armor)
  3. Book

Skins Priority

Andvari has super armor skin, but we should focus on strength first, then super skin if possible, and physical attack and magic defense last.

  1. Strength
  2. Armor - Skin+
  3. Physical Attack
  4. Magic Defense

Andvari vs Hydra

    Andvari is a great hero against Light hydra protecting the ally with less health from pure damage, it is also very efficient against wind hydra protecting your team against displacement and preventing them from stopping attacking during displacement caused by wind hydra.

  • Andvari team for wind hydra: Martha, Dorian, Mojo, Jhu, Andvari

Strategies for using Andvari

Andvari is a great hero that protects the team against displacement caused by enemies and then causes stun. It is worth investing in.

Andvari is often seen on top teams in the end-game, and especially with various combinations against Karkh teams. Andvari is one of the best counters for Karkh teams in combinations with Keira teams and other teams.
