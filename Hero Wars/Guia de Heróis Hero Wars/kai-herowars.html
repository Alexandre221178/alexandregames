Kai History


Hero Wars Alliance History: There's nothing in the world but magic, and Kai is a true magical genius. Rejected by his own race, the mage left his home. Everybody who thinks Kai prides himself too much is just jealous!

Strategy on how to use Kai

Kai is a great hero to use with K'arkh in battles. It's only worth investing in Kai if you want to invest in K'arkh and this combo can be beneficial until the final levels and is commonly found in top attacking teams.

When Kai launches enemies into the air, K'arkh automatically attacks with his passive and deals double damage to all enemies, this ability makes Kai an excellent combo with K'arkh and can cause a lot of damage to enemies.

Kai is used less than Faceless in this synergy with K'arkh precisely because Kai needs to receive energy and use the ability to launch enemies into the air. While Faceless uses the passive ability to launch into the air first and causes enemies to be stunned. And for this reason, we see more Faceless than Kai in K'arkh teams.

Kai vs Hydra

Kai is not a great hero against hydras, but his vampirism ability can make him stay alive longer against hydras.

It is also possible to create a Kai + Dorian + Orion combo on the hydras, as Kai's magic attack bonus in combination with Orion's magic penetration can cause good damage.
