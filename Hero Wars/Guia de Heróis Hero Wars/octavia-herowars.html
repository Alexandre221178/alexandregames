Octavia History


Hero Wars Alliance History: Former empress of dominion, who uses the insidious magic of the mirror of truth to achieve undeniable victory for her allies. Additionally, strengthens the team and deals damage to opponents when dodge is activated.

Strategy on how to use Octavia

Octavia arrived very strong in Hero Wars, bringing back the shine of the Dodge ability, and not only physical damage, but her allies can dodge all types of damage, including the unique ability to dodge magical damage.

Octavia's Ultimate can reflect all dodged damage from allies back to enemies, plus whenever an ally dodges she applies a buff that increases their armor penetration, which is incredible. One of Dante's weaknesses in Hero Wars Alliance is that he has little armor penetration and ends up not doing much damage. Now, with the synergy between Dante and Octavia, he will be able to do more damage.

It also has synergy with K'arkh, as Karkh has an ability that blocks enemy physical attacks and receives extra energy for each attack held, so even if you dodge K'arkh will receive extra energy.

The Imperial Guard skills make Octavia a nightmare for enemy teams .Whenever her allies dodge any type of damage, she receives extra energy, be it physical or magical damage.

Octavia cannot be targeted by skill and attacks, but can receive AOE (area damage) from heroes skills like Keira, Ginger, and Lars. Because of that Octavia is often the last battle hero to die.

Octavia Positive and Negative Points

Positive Points

  • Reflect all types of damage to enemies
  • Applies Buff to forward allies
  • Increases armor penetration
  • Cannot be targeted by attacks

Negative Points

  • Can receive AOE (area damage)
  • May be the target of Satori's marks
  • Receives watermark from Krista and Lars

Octavia Stats Evolution Priority

Octavia Glyphs Priority

In Octavia's glyphs, physical attack and agility should be prioritized to increase the power of your skills and provide increased armor penetration and dodge for allies. Then health to increase the damage reflected by the mirror, and finally dodge and magical defense, because she does not suffer to much damage while her allies are alive.

Priority Glyphs
1st Physical Attack
2nd Agility
3rd Health
4th Dodge
5th Magic Defense

Octavia Artifacts Priority

In Octavia's artifacts, the priority is to level up the ring to increase the armor penetration and dodge buffs in Octavia's skills. Then level up the weapon to give the whole team more dodging. As the more your allies dodge, the more they gain energy, and the more your allies receive a buff. And finally, the book increases health and mirror damage with health in her Ultimate.

Priorities Artifacts Stat Increase
1st Weapon Dodge
2nd Book Dodge x2, Life
3rd Ring Agility

Octavia in Battles

Octavia is Strong Against

  • Astrid, Kayla and Ainda, Jhu, Ishmael, Artemis(Artemis cannot target Octavia, but undead usually lose)

With all the tests I did with Octavia in battles, the best results were with Dante, with an increase in Dodge Buff and Armor Penetration, and thus, increasing energy gain in battle. For defense, the best results are with Nebula, as with the gain of extra energy, Octavia can be the target of Satori's marks and Nebula can remove Satori's marks.

Octavia Best Counters

Counters What happens

Satori
Satori can apply the marks to Octavia, as the marks are penalties, different from skills. This way Satori applies marks to all of Octavia's allies who receive extra dodge energy.

Ginger
Ginger deals AOE area damage in her ultimate and can deal damage to Octavia.

Lars
Lars deals area damage AOE dragging all enemies to the backline and in combination with Krista can deal damage to Octavia by applying watermarks.

Keira
Keira deals basic area damage and can damage Octavia.

Octavia tips from the Hero Wars devs!

"Distortion of Fate. How does the magic mirror work?"

The mirror does not have its own health, so it cannot be broken, and it will remain effective throughout the entire duration.

There can be only one mirror on the battlefield. If you use the skill while a mirror is already summoned, the duration of its presence in the battle will be extended. Once the mirror disappears, the next time the skill is used, its duration will be standard again. If Octavia‚Äôs allies dodge frequently, she will be able to ult more often thanks to the bonus energy from the skill ‚ÄúImperial Guard‚Äù. So the mirror can stand until the end of the battle without disappearing.

With each ally‚Äôs dodge, the mirror inflicts physical damage equal to what the enemy dealt (but it cannot go over the limit specified in the skill). Enemy's armor can reduce this damage. Therefore this skill becomes more effective with the increase of armor penetration from the skill "Moment of Concentration" or with the allied artifacts (Morrigan's, for example).

2nd Favorite of the empress

Octavia applies a buff to furthest forward ally that she started the battle with, increasing their Dodge for 6.0 seconds. Eternity Heroes receive a stronger buff.
Passive effect: Octavia's allies have a chance to dodge even magic damage. Bonus to Dodge: 5413 (10% Phys. atk. + 1200) Bonus to Dodge on allied Eternity Heroes: 15040 (30% Phys. atk. + 2400) Keywords: Buff.

"Favorite of the Empress: more details about this buff."

The active effect of the skill will work even on those allies who do not have innate Dodge. When triggered, the value of this ability will rise to the value specified in the skill itself

Dodges of Octavia's allies will work not only on basic attacks but also on any skill that deals magic damage (for example, Amira's Careless Wish or Xe'Sha's Deadly Ray).

If the skill has been learned, its passive effect will work permanently during the battle for each of the allies, including Octavia herself.

Magic dodge works the same way as a regular dodge, and will not work for heroes who do not have this ability (or if it hasn't been increased, for example, by an active artifact, or any skills).

3rd Moment of concentration

Applies a buff to all allies, increasing their Armor Penetration for 3.0 seconds. The same buff is also applied to an ally every time that they dodge any damage.
Bonus Armor Penetration: 8720 (15% Phys. atk. + 2400) Keywords: Buff.

"Moment of Concentration"

The armor penetration effect of this skill is stackable. Each triggered skill applies a separate effect with its own duration.

4th Imperial guard

Cannot be the target of skills and attacks, so long as the allies that Octavia started the battle with, are alive.
When allies dodge any damage, both the Guard and Octavia get extra energy. Extra Energy: 50.
