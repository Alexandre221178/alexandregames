Positive and Negative Points

Positive Points

  • Uses rage instead of energy
  • Lucas' increasing damage
  • Lucas Stuns Marked enemies
  • Lucas takes damage and loses rage in place of health

Negative Points

  • Only one skill uses physical attack
  • Low magic defense
  • Little Health



Stats Evolution Priority

Glyphs

In the glyphs of Astrid and Lucas prioritize armor penetration to deal more damage, Astrid's skills already have enough physical damage, then go up to health to have survivability, then agility to gain more armor and physical attack, and finally the glyphs of physical attack and armor.

  1. Armor Penetration
  2. Health
  3. Agility
  4. Physical Attack
  5. Armor

Artifacts

Artifacts In artifacts prioritize the book to gain armor penetration and physical attack, then the ring to gain armor and physical attack and finally the weapon to gain bonus armor for the whole team.

  1. Book
  2. Ring
  3. Weapon (Armor Bonus)

Skins

In the skins of Astrid and Lucas keep focusing on armor penetration to increase damage and agility to gain armor and physical attack, then magic attack, lastly evolve armor, because as Astrid positions herself in the bottom line she ends up receiving less damage .

  1. Armor Penetration
  2. Agility
  3. Physical Attack
  4. Physical Attack
  5. Armor

Hero stats at max level 2023





Max level Skills

1st Go, Lucas

Lucas, gains 25% Rage at the start of the battle and gains 25% more with each Astrid attack. At 100% rage, Astrid can activate the ability and transform Lucas into his reach form. Lucas moves to the frontline to attack enemies and takes their hits, losing 7% rage with each hit instead of taking damage. When the rage meter empties, Lucas reverts to his passive kitten form.
Pet Attack Damage: Max (28486) - (20% Physical Attack + 135 * Skill Level) Keywords: Knock back, Cannot be copied

2nd Unrestrained Temperament

Astrid increases your attack speed and skill speed by 9 seconds. If Lucas is in rage form, the attack and speed bonus affects Lucas instead of Astrid. Lucas also gets this buff when transforming into his rage form.
Attack speed and skill increased by Max (200%) - (70% * Level + 116) Keywords: buff

3rd Predator Attack

During the time that Lucas (cat) is in rage form, his attack damage increases with each attack he makes on enemies. (Passive Skill).
Increased damage with each attack: Max(6%) - (5% * (Skill Level + 20) + 0.5)

4th Mark of the Tracker

Astrid's basic attacks apply Mark of the Tracker to the enemy. The mark remains on target over the target's head for four seconds. Lucas (cat) stuns marked enemies when he deals damage to them. (Passive Skill).
The chance to stun is reduced if the target's level is above skill level Max(120) = (1 * Skill level) + 40) Keywords: Stun, Mark.



Strategy on how to use Astrid and Lucas

Astrid and Lucas are among the 2023 Meta teams. Astrid is the only hero of the mobile, with a PET, Lucas, this beautiful and cute cat, Astrid and Lucas are a good combination if you want to take advantage of passive skills, and even, in general, since you basically have two heroes in one, the main hero (Astrid) and her pet (Lucas) together leaves your team with 6 allies.

After the Revamp Buff Lucas does much more damage to enemy tanks and frontline heroes and even Ishmael blocking their vampirism with his passive ability that marks enemies and stuns, plus Astrid and Lucas is a good one counter to Jorgen, Phobos, and Tristan, as Astrid is the only hero in the game that doesn't use energy.

His first skill is special because it does not use energy being different and unique in the game, instead of gaining energy Astrid and Lucas gains Fury, when the fury bar is full Lucas turns into an angry cat and advances in the front line taking damage for himself, and with each damage he loses fury and prevents his team from taking damage, and still gains increased damage and speed, and increases his damage each time he causes damage until his fury ends.
