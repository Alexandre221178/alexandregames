Strategy on how to use K'arkh

K'arkh is one of the most Overpower characters in Hero Wars Alliance, but at the same time, he is little used in defense, as he has a great counter, Andvari, that protects allies against K'arkh's Ultimate.

He is little seen in defense but is greatly feared in attack, being even used to counter Artemis teams. If the enemy team does not have Andvari in its composition, the chances of K'arkh teams winning increase greatly.

K'arkh is usually seen with Faceless, because when Faceless throws enemies into the air, K'arkh has a passive that will also do damage. Unlike Kai, Faceless uses the ability first and stuns enemies, and for this reason, Kai is not seen as much in K'arkh teams.

For 2024, K'arkh will often be seen in Teams with Octavia and Dante, as these allies form a very strong combo with their abilities together. Dante giving a dodge buff to all allies, Octavia giving physical and magical dodge to the entire team, and K'arkh receiving more energy when dodging attacks, physical or magical.

Talisman of Astaroth

With the talisman, K'arkh gained more armor, physical attack, and a lot of magical defense, becoming very strong, even against low-power Andvari teams.

Evolution Priorities

Glyphs

In the glyphs of K'arkh, prioritize armor penetration to cause more damage, then agility to increase physical attack and armor. Third, go up physical attack, and lastly, armor and health.

Priority Glyphs
1st Armor Penetration
2nd Agility
3rd Physical Attack
4th Armor
5th Health

Artifacts Priority

In K'arkh artifacts, first level up the book to gain more armor penetration and physical attack, then the ring to gain more physical attack and armor and finally the weapon to gain physical attack for the whole team.

Priority Artifacts
1st Book
2nd Ring
3rd Weapon

Skins Priority

K'arkh's skins follow the same logic as glyphs, prioritizing armor penetration, the more armor penetration K'arkh has, the more damage he will cause to enemies.

K'arkh vs Hydra

K'arkh is rarely used against hydras, but it can do some damage in combination with the right team. Below is a suggestion for a K'arkh team for Earth Hydra.

K'arkh's team suggestion for Earth's Hydra:

K'arkh Counters

Counters What happens

Andvari
Andvari's Living Earth ability protects allies behind him from displacement, so K'arkh cannot launch allies behind Andvari into the air.

Satori
K'arkh's second ability causes him to gain extra energy and in this way Satori applies his marks.

Celeste
In the fourth skill, K'arkh can recover health, and Celeste has a skill that transforms 40% of enemies' healing into magical damage.

Arachne
Arachne applies toxins and stuns enemies in the front line.

Hero stats at max level 2023



K'arkh with Celestial Skin in Hero Wars Alliance

Max level Skills

1st Nexus of Horror

Karkh throws into the air three enemies with the lowest health. When the affected enemies fall back down, they get stunned for 2.0 seconds.
Chance to stun is lowered if the target's level is above 120 Keywords: Knock Up, Stun.

2nd Negator of Laws

Karkh opens an interdimensional rift that can block enemy physical attacks for 8.0 seconds. The hero gets extra energy for each attack blocked.
Block chance for physical attacks: 50%
Extra energy for a blocked attack: 15%.

3rd Deadly Tendrils

While Karkh is alive, he strikes every enemy who was thrown into the air. If the enemy level is equal to or less than 120, Karkh deals double damage.
Damage from attack: 56896((100% Physical attack)) Chance to deal double damage is lowered if the target's level is above 120 Keywords: Knock Up.

4th Survival Factor

Attacks against enemies thrown into the air heal the hero for a fraction of the damage dealt.
Health recovered: 25% of damage dealt Keywords: Knock Up.
