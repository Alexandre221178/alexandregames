Strategies for using Alvanor

Alvanor is one of the best Nature faction supports. Alvanor's skills have characteristics that increase the resistance for his team. However, managing a full team of Nature Heroes (Aurora, Mushy, Alvanor, Kai, Dark Star, and Thea...) is very hard to level up, but are great heroes to level up together.

Alvanor is also a good counter for Satori marks and progress teams.

Positive and Negative Points

    Positive Points

  • Heal Allies
  • Double Healing for nature allies
  • Reduces basic damage

    Negative Points

  • Not a Great Healer
  • Not a great support
  • It is not a tank, but it has position in front-line

Stats Evolution Priority

Glyphs Priority

In Alvanor's glyphs prioritize Life because Alvanor fights on the front lines and will need a lot of health, as he has no armor glyph. Then prioritize magic attacks to strengthen skills.

  1. Life
  2. Magic attack
  3. Intelligence
  4. Magic defense
  5. Magic penetration

Artifacts Priority

In Alvanor's Artifacts prioritize the intelligence ring to gain more magic attacks and magic defense, and then the book as it increases magic attack and magic piercing, and lastly the weapon.

  1. Ring
  2. Book
  3. Weapon(Magic Attack)

Skins Priority

In the skins, prioritize life first and then the weapon to gain more magic attacks, then the intelligence skin to gain more magic attacks and magic defense, and lastly the armor skin.

  1. Health
  2. Magic Attack
  3. Armor
  4. Magic Defense
