Oya History


Hero Wars Alliance History: Oya is the last chief of a forgotten tribe who joined the guardians of the domain to fight evil and avenge his people. Oya will strengthen any team as his powerful strikes can break all enemy shields.

What are Oya's negative and positive points?

Oya Positive Points

  • Attack low health killers, Keira, Artemis, Ginger, Xe'sha
  • Counter to defeat shield teams
  • Restores health to allies when they deal critical damage
  • Increases critical damage to allies
  • Receives energy when eliminating a marked enemy using her Ultimate

Oya Negative Points

  • Weak against Marks of Satori
  • Weak against Iris
  • There is still little resistance on the front line

Oya is not an excellent damage dealer for Hydras because Hydras are immune to marks, but he can help the team with critical damage to artifacts.

Which Allies have Best Synergy with Oya?

Ally Skill Synergy

Yasmine
Yasmine is a character with a high chance of critical damage and whenever an ally of Oya deals critical damage they will receive a healing bonus and immediately an increased critical damage bonus.

Elmir
Elmir starts the battle on the front line and when he creates his clones he immediately jumps to the back line. Positioning himself like this and dealing ranged damage on the front line and quickly increasing armor penetration for Oya with the weapon artifact. Furthermore, when used with Dorian, Elmir will receive bonus critical damage with vampirism.

Oya Max level Skills

1st Shield Breaker

Deals damage to the nearest enemy. All marked enemies also take additional damage. Oya gains energy if she kills an enemy with this ability.
Passive effect. Shielded enemies take more damage from any of oya's abilities.

2nd Hunting

Marks an enemy as the "hunter's prey" and lures them toward her. Any attack on the marked target burns its energy.
Passive effect. Allied heroes who are healing themselves or are being healed by Oya temporarily increase their critical strike chances. Allied nature heroes receive an increased bonus.

3rd Overwhelming

Oya performs a series of 3 quick strikes. With each successive hit, the damage to the enemy increases.
Enemies behind the main target take part of the skill's damage.

4th Brutal Onslaught

When an allied hero deals critical damage or breaks an enemy shield, he restores health.
Allied nature heroes receive an increased bonus.

Oya tips from the Hero Wars devs!

How long does her mark stay on?

Until the next time the ability is used or until it is removed.

I notice that if Oya's 2nd skill targets Iris, Iris won't put her 3rd skill's debuff on Oya even though there's a keyword "Mark". Why is that?

It happens this way because Iris debuffs opponents because of the tags on her allies, not herself.

Can Oya hit several targets on the field at once?

Yes, she can.

What is this ancient prophecy that Oya wants to fulfil?

The life of the tribe Oya lived in was based on rituals and prophecies. One of these prophecies was about Oya receiving Ancestor's Sword and becoming the last chief of the tribe. And what exactly this prophecy is about Oya is yet to find out.
