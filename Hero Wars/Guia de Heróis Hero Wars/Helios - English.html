Strategy on how to use Helios

Helios is a strong Hero and one of the best supports in Hero Wars and you definitely need to have a Helios in your team. Helios has the ability Flaming Retribution which is very useful against crit damage teams.

Helios is a Hero that can be used for both defense and offense, but I consider Helios a better hero for offense, once you know it's a critical defense team and you have Helios to attack you will be at an advantage, on defense it may not be very useful, as it is weak against mage teams.

Helios is worth investing in and can be used as a great counter to critical damage teams.

Positive and Negative Points

Positive Points

  • Critical Hit Counter
  • Absorbs physical damage taken by allies
  • Strong against critical and physical attack teams
  • Armor Artifact

Negative Points

  • No magic penetration
  • Only deals high damage on critical hit teams,
  • It is weak against teams of mages, even though having a lot of magic defense

Helios vs Hydra

Helios is a reasonable hero to use on physical damage hydras as a defense, but don't expect too much from Helios on hydra teams, as his strength is against critical damage and hydras do not deal critical damage.

Max level Skills

1st Star Birth

Helios directs 3 new and all existing solar spheres to the front line of an enemy team, where they explode, dealing damage in an area of effect. By summoning any sphere, Helios heals the Honor Heroes for 2% health.
Damage per sphere: Max(43486) - (45% Magic attack + 50 * Skill Level)

2nd Solar Wind

Summons an invulnerable flaming sphere, dealing damage to nearby enemies over time for 14 seconds. The sphere deals 50% more damage for each Honor Hero other than Helios.
Damage over time: Max(9530) - (10% Magic attack + 10 * Skill Level)

3rd Celestial Guardians

Summons 2 protection spheres at the center of your team. The spheres protect nearby allies by absorbing physical damage dealt to them.
Each sphere can absorb: Max(89972) - (90% Magic attack + 125 * (Skill Level + 20)) damage.

4th Flaming Retribution

Each time Helios' allies suffer a critical hit, the flaming sphere will strike the damaging attacker with a beam of fire.
Retaliation damage: Max(80477) - (75% Magic attack + 150 * (Skill Level + 40))
