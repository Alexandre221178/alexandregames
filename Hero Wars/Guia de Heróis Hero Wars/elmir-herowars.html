Maximizing Elmir's Potential in Hero Wars Mobile

Elmir is a unique hero in Hero Wars Mobile, known for his ninja style and ability to create sand clones. Although he may initially seem like a promising hero for beginners, some players may be disappointed to find that he is not among the best heroes in the game. However, with the right knowledge and smart strategies, it is possible to maximize Elmir's potential and use him effectively in your teams. In this tutorial, we will explore how to use Elmir in the best possible way in Hero Wars Mobile.

True Heroes and the Blades of Various Truths

In Hero Wars, only true heroes are worthy of wielding legendary weapons. The Blades of Various Truths have their own minds and only obey those who are worthy to be their partners. Elmir is on the right path by recognizing this. Among the various truths, one reigns supreme!

Understanding Elmir

Elmir is known for his unique abilities to create clones and his ninja agility. However, his clones, made of sand, are weak in terms of resistance, as they depend solely on Elmir's total health, which is not very high compared to other heroes. This can lead to disappointment for some players who expect more from him initially.

Elmir Usage Strategies

  1. Teams with Daredevil and Dorian: One of the most common strategies is to include Elmir in teams that also contain Daredevil and Dorian. These three heroes have strong synergy and can be difficult to defeat. However, it is important to note that this composition may have lost some strength with the introduction of new shield heroes in Hero Wars Alliance.
  2. Applying Clone Strategies: Although Elmir's clones are fragile, they can still be useful when used correctly. By strategically positioning Elmir and making the most of his clones, it is possible to confuse and distract enemies during battles.
  3. Using Skills at Strategic Moments: Elmir's skills should be used wisely and at the right moment during battles. The clone creation skill can be especially useful for diverting enemy attention or protecting other team members.
  4. Team Balancing: Although Elmir has his own distinct abilities, it is important to balance your team with other heroes that complement his weaknesses. Choosing heroes that can compensate for Elmir's lack of resistance or provide additional support can be crucial for team success.
  5. Upgrade and Enhancement: Like any hero in Hero Wars, Elmir can benefit from continuous upgrades and enhancements. Investing in his skills and equipment can help him reach his full potential and make him more effective in battles.

The Ninja's Path

Although Elmir may not be one of the best heroes in Hero Wars Mobile, he still has a valuable place in many teams. With strategy, patience, and a clear understanding of his unique abilities, it is possible to maximize his potential and use him effectively in battles.

Elmir is known for creating clones that inherit 70% of his health stats. His two talismans, the Talisman of Illusions and the Talisman of the Sandstorm, boost both his attack power and the survivability of his clones.

Which Allies have Best Synergy with Elmir?

Ally Skill Synergy

Aidan
Aidan can increase the maximum health of his allies, and in this way, increase the maximum health of Elmir's clones.

Dorian
Elmir starts the battle on the front line and when he creates his clones he immediately jumps to the back line. Thus positioning himself over Dorian's aura and benefiting from vampirism, recovering all his life and causing damage from a distance.

Octavia
The buff Octavia applies to her allies increases dodge and armor penetration, and her allies can also dodge magical damage, and when they dodge, they get even more armor penetration. Elmir has little magical defense and with this buff, he becomes very strong against teams of wizards.

Oya
If Elmir receives health, Oya has a passive that deals critical damage to Elmir, and if nature's ally deals critical damage or breaks a shield, Oya heals them and provides increased critical damage.

Who is Elmir Strong against in Battle?

Defeated Enemies How Elmir Beats Enemies

Rufus
Rufus has little armor and when Elmir jumps to the backline, he activates his armor-penetration artifact and deals a lot of physical damage to the enemy frontline.

Rufus

How to Counter Elmir?

Counters
 How enemies defeat Elmir

K'arkh
K'arkh has the ability to absorb physical damage from enemies, in addition, when he uses his Ultimate he launches the three enemies with the lowest health into the air. As Elmir has little defense and health, he is eliminated quickly.

Krista
Elmir's clones will improve Krista's Ultimate, as normally Krista's Ultimate explodes 5 times, but with the 4 clones activated, Krista's Ultimate will explode 7 to 9 times. This way, Krista naturally becomes a good Counter for Elmir's teams, because her clones will give a buff to Krista. Additionally, Lars can also eliminate Elmir with just a single beam when combined with Krista in battle.

Epic Conclusion of the Elmir Guide in Hero Wars

In the heat of the Hero Wars battlefield, few heroes stand out with the versatility and cunning of Elmir. His second skill, "Mirage," defies norms, allowing him to create clones that not only confuse but also absorb damage, adding a layer of tactical depth to his arsenal.

As Elmir hones his skills, his prowess grows exponentially. With "Perfect Blades," his clones become an offensive force, attacking enemies and increasing his presence on the battlefield. And when the crucial moment arrives, his ultimate skill, "Many Truths," grants him the power to multiply his clones, generating unpredictable and overwhelming chaos.

In summary, Elmir transcends the boundaries of the ordinary, emerging as one of the most valuable and versatile heroes in Hero Wars. His ability to turn the tide of battle and adapt to any situation makes him an indispensable ally on any team. With Elmir by your side, victory is not just a possibility; it's an inevitable certainty.
