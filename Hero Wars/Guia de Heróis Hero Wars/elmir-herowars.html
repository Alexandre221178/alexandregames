Elmir History


Hero Wars Alliance History: Only true heroes are worthy of using the legendary weapons. The Blades of Diverse Truths have their own mind, and only those worthy of being their partners obey. Elmir is following the right path and knows that among the different truths, there is one that reigns supreme!

How to use Elmir in Hero Wars Alliance

Elmir is the favorite hero of many beginners, his ninja style with clones makes our eyes flutter, but then we realize that he is not among the top heroes and disappointment soon sets in.

His clones are made of sand and weak as paper, as the clones' resistance only depends on Elmir's total amount of life, which is not much.

Elmir is normally seen in Daredevil teams with Dorian, where these three heroes have a lot of synergy and are difficult to beat. However, they lost a lot of strength after the addition of new heroes with shields in Hero Wars Alliance.

Elmir's Talisman

With Talisman, Elmir receives more Armor and Physical Attack from the Agility statistic, improving his damage and durability.

Elmir's talisman at maximum level will increase his total statistics by +18,000 physical attack points, and an additional 2,000 armor points. This leaves him with 74,500 physical attack points combined with his 27,950 points of armor penetration, causing a lot of damage to enemies.

Which Allies have Best Synergy with Elmir?

Ally Skill Synergy

Aidan
Aidan can increase the maximum health of his allies, and in this way, increase the maximum health of Elmir's clones.

Dorian
Elmir starts the battle on the front line and when he creates his clones he immediately jumps to the back line. Thus positioning himself over Dorian's aura and benefiting from vampirism, recovering all his life and causing damage from a distance.

Octavia
The buff Octavia applies to her allies increases dodge and armor penetration, and her allies can also dodge magical damage, and when they dodge, they get even more armor penetration. Elmir has little magical defense and with this buff, he becomes very strong against teams of wizards.

Oya
If Elmir receives health, Oya has a passive that deals critical damage to Elmir, and if nature's ally deals critical damage or breaks a shield, Oya heals them and provides increased critical damage.

Who is Elmir Strong against in Battle?

Defeated Enemies How Elmir Beats Enemies

Rufus
Rufus has little armor and when Elmir jumps to the backline, he activates his armor-penetration artifact and deals a lot of physical damage to the enemy frontline.

Rufus
When Elmir's clones deal damage to enemies, the Altar of Corvus reciprocates the damage to the clones and loses health, and thus Elmir's allies receive less damage from the Corvus Altar.

How to Counter Elmir?

Counters
 How enemies defeat Elmir

K'arkh
K'arkh has the ability to absorb physical damage from enemies, in addition, when he uses his Ultimate he launches the three enemies with the lowest health into the air. As Elmir has little defense and health, he is eliminated quickly.

Krista
Elmir's clones will improve Krista's Ultimate, as normally Krista's Ultimate explodes 5 times, but with the 4 clones activated, Krista's Ultimate will explode 7 to 9 times. This way, Krista naturally becomes a good Counter for Elmir's teams, because her clones will give a buff to Krista. Additionally, Lars can also eliminate Elmir with just a single beam when combined with Krista in battle.

Elmir Max level Skills

1st Shifting Sands

Elmir moves to the far line, uses his second skill (if available), and carries out ranged attacks for the next 10 seconds. The hero gets a physical attack bonus while the skill is active.
Physical attack bonus: Max(30014) - (38% Physical attack + 75 * Skill Level)
Keywords: Buff

2nd Mirage

Elmir creates a sand clone nearby. The clone cannot regenerate health or receive help from allies. The clone loses health over time, and will live for 12 seconds if it receives no damage from other sources.
Clone health: Max(70%) - (0.4 * Skill Level + 22) of hero's maximum health

3rd Perfect Blades

Sand clones get the ability to attack enemies. Clones that were created with the first skill continue attacking enemies from afar.
Clone attack damage: Max(13295%) - (15% Physical attack + 50 * (Skill Level + 20) - 1000)

4th Many Truths

When using his second skill, Elmir gets a chance to make two or four clones at once.
Max chance(80%) - (0.3 * (Skill Level + 40) + 44) chance of creating two copies
Max chance(40%) - (0.2 * (Skill Level + 40) + 16) chance of creating four copies
