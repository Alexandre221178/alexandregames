Strategy on how to use Rufus

Rufus is the best tank to use against magic teams. Rakashi Barrier is great against heavy magic damage teams. His abilities allow him to stay in front of enemies for a long time, making Rufus a great frontline hero against mages.

He can definitely replace Galahad or Judge in the later stages of the game, as Rufus' soulstones can be found in the Grand Arena shop. Rufus can be seen in the main teams and is worth investing in, you don't need to spend a lot of resources for Rufus to get strong and show results.

Against teams of mages, Rufus is definitely the best tank in Hero Wars.

Positive and Negative Points

Positive Points

  • Protect allies with the Rakashi barrier by absorbing magic damage
  • Invincible against magic damage and pure damage
  • Tough tank against mage teams
  • Recover part of health
  • Deals pure damage

Negative Points

  • Weak against physical damage teams
  • The resurrect skill does not work against the Morrigan
  • The Rakashi shield only absorbs magic damage

Rufus vs Hydra

Rufus is a great hero against magic hydras, helping his team by absorbing magic damage with Rakashi barrier.

Hero stats at max level 2023




Max level Skills

1st Rakashi's Barrier

Rufus covers the team with a shield that absorbs all magic damage.
Shield Capacity: Max(342034) - (215% Magic Attack + 2,500 * Skill Level) damage

2nd Rakashi's Mockery

Strikes the enemy with the greatest Magic Attack, dealing damage over time.
Damage: Max(53300) - (75% Magic Attack + 165 * Skill Level) for 4.0 seconds.

3rd Devourer of Magic

Part of the damage absorbed by Rakashi's Barrier is converted to hero's health.
Health Recovered: Max(84) - (70% * (Skill Level + 20) of damage absorbed by shield)

4th Rakashi's Oath

Rufus can now only be killed with physical damage. If the last attack against the hero dealt magic damage or pure damage, they resurrect with a portion of health restored.
Recovered Health: Max(494308) - (35% * Health + 1400 * (Skill Level + 40)) Keywords: Resurrection
