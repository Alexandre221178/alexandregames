Chabba vs Hydra

Chabba is not a great tank against hydras and is not among the best Hydra teams, but because he has a lot of armor he can be useful on secondary teams for physical damage hydras: earth, water and fire, and can be great if used on Artemis teams on Hydras.

Strategy on how to use Chabba

Chabba used to be one of the hardest heroes to find on top teams, but after Artemis's rework he's used heavily on Artemis teams on top teams. He is quite fun to use, due to his skill set related to food.

Currently Chabba has been successful in Artemis teams and it is very difficult to defeat them, every hero has his team, and for that reason, Chabba ended up joining the 2023 META teams.

All heroes have their place, in my humble opinion he is also a good counter to Andvari teams and a good option for those who have a strong Karkh (example 6th team above), since Chabba swallows Andvari Karkh will be free to defeat the enemy team; Chabba is also useful against Corvus teams.
