Strategy on how to use Thea

Thea is not a hero often seen in the end-game, she has good skills, but it is preferable to invest in Martha. Even with the recent buff Thea is still not better than Martha. She has good skills, but as a healer, she is not the best.


  • Advertisement
     

Thea's best qualities are the ability to silence and increase the speed of allied heroes by 50% every time she heals them. That is, every time she uses her ultimate rage accelerates the entire team by 50% making her an excellent combo to use with Jhu on the hydras.


Positive and Negative Points

Positive Points

  • Healing
  • Silence enemies for 4 seconds
  • Heals and increases ally's skill speed by 50% for 4 seconds

Negative Points

  • Healing is not enough
  • Needs to level a lot to heal enough

Thea vs Hydra

Thea is a great hero against hydras, her fourth purple ability causes Thea to accelerate her allies every time she heals them, so it is a good combination with Jhu and Mojo to deal more damage to Hero Wars hydras; this combo is one of the best against fire hydras; every 11 seconds or every time she recharges energy Thea will heal Jhu and speed up by 50%.

  • Thea's team suggestion for Hydra: Thea, Mojo, Jhu, Tristan, Astaroth
