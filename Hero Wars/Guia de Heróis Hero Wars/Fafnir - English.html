Strategy on how to use Fafnir

Fafnir is one of the heroes of the Honor faction and manages to protect agility allies from all types of damage through his shield ability, and, in addition, he can increase physical damage.

Fafnir if used with Keira and Jet can protect Keira from Helios' flaming shot, it's also a good idea to use it with Yasmine and Artemis and other agility heroes.

Fafnir's Talisman Guide

Fafnir's Talisman in Hero Wars Alliance is a valuable item that plays a crucial role in strengthening your team's defensive and offensive abilities. This talisman, named after the legendary blacksmith Fafnir, confers significant benefits, especially when combined with certain hero-specific abilities.

One of the distinguishing features of the Fafnir Talisman is its ability to enhance the performance of the “Rune Shield” skill. When equipping this talisman on Fafnir, the team will experience an increase in the shield duration granted by this ability. This means that Fafnir's Talisman not only provides an additional layer of protection but also extends the shield's durability, allowing your team to better withstand enemy attacks.

Furthermore, Fafnir's Talisman also interacts synergistically with the “Rune Blade” skill. When equipped by Fafnir, the talisman amplifies the physical attack bonus granted to allies through the Runic Blade. This results in a significant increase in your team's attack power, making them more formidable on the battlefield.

It is important to highlight that the strategic choice of which heroes will benefit from Fafnir's Talisman will depend on the composition of your team and the synergy between each character's specific skills. By incorporating this talisman into your game strategy, you will be able to strengthen both your team's defenses and attacks, providing a valuable advantage during clashes in Hero Wars Alliance.

Fafnir Skins Priority

In Fafnir's skins, priority is given to Super Skin, as it has double the physical attack and helps a lot with the shield's protective power for allies. Fafnir vs Hydra

Fafnir Best Counters

Counters What happens

Oya
Oya marks and attracts the enemy with the least health towards her, and deals 100% more damage to enemies that have a shield. Meanwhile, Fafnir places the shield on an ally with agility, heroes with little health in their statistics.

Heidi
Heidi's abilities release toxins on opponents who use shields as a defense. Additionally, the third skill releases a serpent that removes toxins from opponents in the front and transfers them to opponents in the rear, transferring all the toxins to Fafnir. All toxins deal pure damage and cannot be blocked by shields.

Jorgen
Jorgen Lepra's third skill curses the enemy team and redirects all physical or critical damage to the back line for 10 seconds, thus making Fafnir the target.

Cleaver
When Cleaver uses his Ultimate he uses his hook and pulls the furthest enemy toward the front line, this way Fafnir is unprotected from allied tanks and takes a lot of damage.

Fafnir Skills with Super Skin

1st Blacksmith Mastery

Fafnir uses his ultimate and stuns everyone on the enemy team for 1s. If Fafnir is currently forging a Runic Shield for an allied hero, then allies on Fafnir's team receive Shield. Also casts the 3 Runic Blade skill on allied heroes.
Keywords: Stun, Buff, Cannot be copied

2nd Runic Shield

Fafnir targets a random ally and uses basic attacks to forge a Runic Shield for the ally. The shield activates if the chosen ally drops below 25% health. The ally does not die when receiving this attack. This shield absorbs all incoming damage while activated. Fafnir will always choose an allied hero that has the main Agility stat first to provide the rune shield.
Shield: Durability with each hit is max: (30717) - (60% * physical attack + 50 * skill level)

3rd Runeblade

Fafnir casts an enchantment on the ally hero's weapon he is forging the Runic Shield on, and increases damage dealt by physical attack for 8s. Runeblade's buff is twice as strong if the enemy target has Strength as its primary stat.
Extra Damage: Max(29777) - (65% * Physical Attack + 25 * Skill Level + 20) Keywords: buff

4th Fault Awareness

Fafnir's allies who have armor piercing in their stats are able to fully stun enemies for 1s. This stun buff can trigger once every 8s.
Stunned Enemies: The chance to stun an enemy is reduced if the target's level is above Max-120 - (1 * skill level + 40) Keywords: stun
