Maximizing Fafnir's Potential in Hero Wars

Fafnir, a stalwart hero of the Honor faction, possesses unique abilities that make him a valuable asset in any team composition. In this comprehensive guide, we'll delve into strategies on how to effectively utilize Fafnir's skills to protect and empower your team members, focusing on his shield ability and synergies with other heroes.

Understanding Fafnir

Fafnir is renowned for his ability to shield agility allies from all types of damage, making him a crucial protector on the battlefield. Additionally, he can augment physical damage, further enhancing his utility in combat situations. To harness Fafnir's full potential, it's essential to comprehend the intricacies of his abilities and how they can be leveraged strategically.

Shield Ability: A Key Defensive Tool

Fafnir's shield ability is his primary means of safeguarding agility allies from harm. This protective barrier not only mitigates damage but also provides crucial support to fragile heroes who rely on agility for survival. Understanding when and how to deploy Fafnir's shield is paramount to maximizing its effectiveness in battles.

Synergies with Other Heroes

One of the most potent aspects of Fafnir's kit lies in his synergies with other heroes, particularly those of the agility class. When paired with heroes like Keira and Jet, Fafnir can effectively shield Keira from devastating attacks, such as Helios' flaming shot. Furthermore, teaming up Fafnir with Yasmine and Artemis, along with other agility heroes, can create formidable combinations that capitalize on Fafnir's protective capabilities.

Optimizing Fafnir's Usage

To make the most out of Fafnir in your team compositions, consider the following strategies:

  1. Formation Composition: Build your team around Fafnir's protective abilities, ensuring that agility heroes are positioned strategically to benefit from his shields. Experiment with different formations to find the optimal setup that maximizes Fafnir's defensive contributions.
  2. Target Prioritization: Identify priority targets that pose the greatest threat to your team, such as high-damage enemies or heroes with crowd control abilities. Use Fafnir's shields to protect vulnerable allies from incoming damage and disrupt the enemy's game plan.
  3. Guild battles and Coordination: In guild battles, coordinate Fafnir's abilities with other heroes' skills effectively. Timely deployment of shields and coordinated attacks can turn the tide of battle in your favor.
  4. Continuous Improvement: Invest in Fafnir's skills and equipment to enhance his effectiveness on the battlefield. As you progress in the game, prioritize upgrades that amplify Fafnir's defensive capabilities and augment his supportive role within your team.

Fafnir Best Counters

Counters What happens

Oya
Oya marks and attracts the enemy with the least health towards her, and deals 100% more damage to enemies that have a shield. Meanwhile, Fafnir places the shield on an ally with agility, heroes with little health in their statistics.

Heidi
Heidi's abilities release toxins on opponents who use shields as a defense. Additionally, the third skill releases a serpent that removes toxins from opponents in the front and transfers them to opponents in the rear, transferring all the toxins to Fafnir. All toxins deal pure damage and cannot be blocked by shields.

Jorgen
Jorgen Lepra's third skill curses the enemy team and redirects all physical or critical damage to the back line for 10 seconds, thus making Fafnir the target.

Cleaver
When Cleaver uses his Ultimate he uses his hook and pulls the furthest enemy toward the front line, this way Fafnir is unprotected from allied tanks and takes a lot of damage.
