Positive and Negative Points

Positive Points

  • Physical and Magic attack buff for 2 allies with Equilibrium skill
  • Clears negative effects and heals
  • Focuses on the enemy with the lowest health, and deals damage
  • Disperses energy from enemies (can be manually controlled)

Negative Points

  • Low magic defense
  • Doesn't have basic damage
  • Take a long time to activate weapon artifact

Max level Skills

1st Astral Projection

Nebula launches a slowly flying projectile. Activation deals damage and disperses the energy of enemies in the area of effect. The total damage and the dispersed energy are split between enemies evenly. The projectile can be activated manually, otherwise, it is activated when it reaches the furthest enemy.
Total Damage: Max(92385) - (120% Physical Attack + 200 * Skill Level) Total energy lost by enemies: Max(100%) - (0.5 * Skill Level + 40)

2nd Serenity

Nebula restores the health of two nearby allies and removes negative effects from them. Nebula can benefit from the skill if there is only one ally left.
Recovered Health: Max(31795) - (40% Physical Attack + 75 * Skill Level) Chance: The chance of removing a negative effect is reduced if the level of the enemy that applied the effects higher than skill level Max(120 = Skill Level)

3rd Disharmony

Deals damage to the enemy with the lowest health.
Damage: Max(72488) - (100% Physical Attack + 150 * (Skill Level + 20) - 2500)

4th Equilibrium

Now, Nebula's basic attacks will be switched to support mode. With every activation, Nebula increases the Physical and Magical Attack of one nearby ally by 5 seconds.
Temporary Physical Attack boost: Max(69988) - (100% Physical Attack + 150 * Skill Level + 1,000) Temporary Magical Attack boost: Max(112482) - (150% Physical Attack + 225 * (Skill Level +40)) Keywords: Buff

Strategy on how to use Nebula

Nebula is a hero from the progress faction, and is one of the best heroes to invest in with no regrets, from beginning to end of the game.

It will always be a useful hero, because its fourth skill, balance greatly increases the physical and magical damage of two closest allies, this makes it good for campaign, for the tower, for war, for hydra, and it makes great combo with Isaac, Judge, Orion, Keira, Karkh, Satori, Krista and Lars, Jorgen, Galahad, and etc.

Nebula is currently usually found on strong late game teams on progress faction teams giving physical attack buff to Isaac and magic attack to Judge, and is well worth investing in.
