Welcome to Alexandre Games' Hero Wars Best Heroes Rank exclusive Tier List December, 2025! This carefully curated list stands out by specifically focusing on battles within the Arena, Grand Arena, and Global Championship.

Our goal is to provide you with detailed insights and strategic advice tailored for these competitive environments, helping you optimize your team compositions and dominate your opponents.

S+ PvP Tier List Hero Wars 2025

  • PvP: "Player vesus Player"

    • S+ Tier Heroes are considered the top-performing, and they are the Hero Wars best characters. They have excellent synergy with most team compositions, offer significant advantages in battles, and often possess unique abilities that can turn the tide of a fight. However, they may have some minor drawbacks that are worth noting.

    Heroes are listed alphabetically for each Tier

    Aidan

    Tier List: S+

    Pros:

    • High damage output.
    • Excellent sacrifice abilities with healing and shield.
    • Strong synergy with other high-damage heroes.

    Cons:

    • Relatively low defense.
    • Vulnerable to burst damage.

    Amira

    Tier List: S+

    Pros:

    • Powerful magical attacks (Over-Power).
    • Can debuff enemies, reducing their effectiveness.

    Cons:

    • Requires careful positioning to maximize effectiveness.
    • High cooldowns on key abilities.

    Astaroth

    Tier List: S+

    Pros:

    • Strong tank with high defense.
    • Can shield allies, providing additional protection.
    • Effective at absorbing physical damage.

    Cons:

    • Limited damage output.
    • Dependent on team support for maximum effectiveness.

    Corvus

    Tier List: S+

    Pros:

    • High damage with skill Altar of Souls.
    • Can reduce all enemies defenses.
    • Strong against enemies who make area damage (AOE).

    Cons:

    • Slow attack speed.
    • Vulnerable to single-target attacks in front-line.

    Dante

    Tier List: S+

    Pros:

    • High physical damage output.
    • Can dodge attacks, increasing survivability.
    • Good at disrupting enemy front-line.

    Cons:

    • Vulnerable to crowd control abilities.

    Fafnir

    Tier List: S+

    Pros:

    • Increase allied's physical attack damage potential.
    • Can disable enemy abilities with stun.
    • Strong in both offense and defense, providing shield for allies.

    Cons:

    • Requires precise timing for maximum damage.
    • Vulnerable to crowd control.

    Iris

    Tier List: S+

    Pros:

    • Over-Power skill that deals damage to all enemies.
    • Reduces physical and magical attack from enemies that apply debuffs.
    • Immune from debuffs.

    Cons:

    • Requires protection from allies due to low defense.

    Julius

    Tier List: S+

    Pros:

    • High defense shield and survivability.
    • Can remove debuffs from allies.
    • Tank with Healer skill.

    Cons:

    • Low armor and magic defense.

    Kayla

    Pros:

    • Kayla deals significant pure damage, bypassing enemy defenses.
    • he has abilities that allow her to heal herself, increasing her survivability in battles.
    • Kayla can resurrect her brother Aidan, providing a significant advantage during tough fights.

    Cons:

    • Vulnerable to crowd control.
    • Requires precise timing for maximum damage.

    Nebula

    Tier List: S+

    Pros:

    • Can boost the attack power of allies.
    • Provides some healing support.
    • Good synergy with high-damage heroes.

    Cons:

    • Low personal damage and defense.
    • Requires protection from allies.

    Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles

    Tier List: S+

    Pros:

    • Can remove control effects.
    • Can heal themselves.
    • Good synergy with high physical damage heroes.

    Cons:

    • Low magic defense.
    • Do not receive the passive buffs granted at the start of the battle, such as Dorian's vampirism.

    Octavia

    Tier List: S+

    Pros:

    • Strong magical damage output with her mirror.
    • Can buff allies with extra dodge and armor penetration.

    Cons:

    • Low defense.

    Oya

    Tier List: S+

    Pros:

    • High mobility and attack speed.
    • Heal allies that destroy shields or makes crit hit.
    • Strong in team battles against shield teams.

    Cons:

    • Vulnerable to burst damage.

    Tristan

    Tier List: S+

    Pros:

    • High physical damage output.
    • Can boost allies' attack power with his skill and armor penetration artifact.
    • Effective against high magic penetration targets.

    Cons:

    • Vulnerable to magical attacks.

    Tempus

    Tier List: S+

    Pros:

    • High buff damage output to Dante and Iris.
    • Can boost the debuffs that allies apply to enemies.
    • Can prolong the debuffs that allies apply to enemies.

    Cons:

    • Vulnerable to physical attacks.

    Yasmine

    Tier List: S+

    Pros:

    • High critical hit chance.
    • Can evade enemy attacks, increasing survivability.
    • Strong against single targets.

    Cons:

    • Vulnerable to crowd control abilities.

    S Tier List Heroes

    S Tier Heroes are highly effective and versatile characters that excel in various team compositions and perform well in most battles. While they may not be as universally dominant as S+ heroes, they are still top choices and bring significant value to any team.

    Artemis

    Pros:

    • High Critical damage in area (AOE).
    • Strong critical hit chance.

    Cons:

    • Requires protection from allies due to low defense.

    Astrid and Lucas

    Pros:

    • High physical damage output.
    • Can summon Lucas for additional damage and tanking.
    • Strong synergy with other physical damage dealers.

    Cons:

    • Vulnerable to magical damage.

    Cascade

    Pros:

    • Strong AoE damage.
    • Can reduce enemies armor and magic defense.
    • Double weapon artifact with armor penetration and magic penetration.

    Cons:

    • Requires support to maximize effectiveness.

    Cleaver

    Pros:

    • High tanking ability with strong defense.
    • Can pull enemies towards him, disrupting their formation.
    • Good crowd control.

    Cons:

    • Limited damage output.
    • Slow movement and attack speed.

    Faceless

    Pros:

    • Can copy enemy abilities, turning them against their team.
    • Strong magical damage.
    • Versatile in various team compositions.

    Cons:

    • Requires careful timing and positioning.
    • Vulnerable to physical damage.

    Ginger

    Pros:

    • High ranged damage output.
    • Synergy with progress allies, increasing their physical attack.

    Cons:

    • Low defense and health.

    Isaac

    Pros:

    • High physical damage output.
    • Can disable enemy abilities and defense, reducing their effectiveness.
    • Good survivability.

    Cons:

    • Requires good team support, specially progress faction.

    Jorgen

    Pros:

    • Can steal energy from enemies, delaying their ultimate abilities.
    • Provides good support and buffs to allies.
    • Strong against teams that rely on energy-based abilities.

    Cons:

    • Limited damage output.

    Luther

    Pros:

    • High tanking ability with strong defense.
    • Can jump into the backline, disrupting enemy formations.
    • Good crowd control.

    Cons:

    • Vulnerable to Cascade and Faceless.
    • Requires precise control to maximize effectiveness.

    Soleil

    Pros:

    • Can boost the attack power and Healing of allies.
    • Decrease enemies defenses.
    • Good against heroes with control skills.

    Cons:

    • Low damage and defense.
    • Very expensive and hard to farming.
    • Not enough skins to increase power.

    A+ Tier Heroes

    A+ Tier Heroes are strong and versatile characters that perform exceptionally well in most situations. They might not be as universally powerful as S+ or S heroes, but they offer unique abilities and synergies that make them valuable assets in many team compositions.

    Folio

    Pros:

    • Summons a stationary Folio copy, boosting allies' Energy for faster ultimate abilities.
    • Deals massive AoE magical damage to all enemies.
    • Steals Intelligence from the enemy hero with the highest stat.

    Cons:

    • Low defense.
    • Very expensive and hard to farming.
    • Not enough skins to increase power.

    Alvanor

    Pros:

    • Strong magical damage and crowd control.
    • Can shield allies, providing additional protection.
    • Good synergy with other magical heroes.

    Cons:

    • Requires support of nature allies to become more powerful.
    • Vulnerable to high physical damage.

    Judge

    Pros:

    • Strong crowd control abilities.
    • Can buff allies' defenses.
    • Good against both single targets and groups.

    Cons:

    • Limited damage output.
    • Dependent on team support for maximum effectiveness.

    Morrigan

    Pros:

    • Can summon undead minions, adding extra attackers to the team.
    • Excellent healer with strong support abilities.
    • Good synergy with other undead heroes.

    Cons:

    • Low personal damage output.
    • Vulnerable to burst damage and crowd control.

    Mushy and Shroom

    Pros:

    • High burst damage capabilities.

    Cons:

    • Vulnerable to sustained damage.

    Phobos

    Pros:

    Cons:

    • Low defense and health.

    Sebastian

    Pros:

    • Protect allies from stun and curse.
    • Boosts critical hit chance for the team.
    • Provides strong support to critical hit allies.

    Cons:

    • Low personal damage and defense.
    • Requires good team synergy to maximize effectiveness.

    Xe'Sha

    Pros:

    • Strong magical damage and Over Power attacks.

    Cons:

    • Low defense and health.
    • Requires protection from allies.

    Ziri

    Pros:

    • High tanking ability with strong armor defense.
    • Can burrow underground to avoid damage and heal.

    Cons:

    • Limited damage output.
    • Vulnerable when not burrowed.
