Analysis of Octavia's Celestial Skin+ in Hero Wars Alliance

Explore the impact of Octavia's new Celestial Skin+ in Hero Wars Alliance. This analysis highlights the enhancements to her physical attack and the significant boosts to her key skills.

  • By: Alexandre Domingos. .

Copy Link

The new Celestial Skin for Octavia provides a significant boost of +7,095 physical attack points. When upgraded to the Celestial Skin+, this bonus doubles, granting a total of +14,190 physical attack points. This enhancement greatly impacts Octavia's performance, particularly improving her second and third skills, which rely on physical attack.

2nd Skill: Favorite of the Empress

Description:

Octavia's second skill, Favorite of the Empress, buffs the furthest forward ally she began the battle with, increasing their dodge for 6.0 seconds. Eternity Heroes receive a stronger dodge buff.

Insights into the Buff:

  • Active Effect: This skill boosts the dodge rate for allies, even those who don't naturally possess dodge abilities. It also offers protection against magical damage, shielding allies from powerful skills like Amira's Careless Wish or Xe'Sha's Deadly Ray.
  • Passive Effect: The dodge buff provided by this skill persists throughout the battle for all allies, including Octavia herself, ensuring a consistent defensive boost.

Impact of the Celestial Skin:

The increase in physical attack points enhances the effectiveness of Favorite of the Empress, as the skill's power scales with Octavia's physical attack. This results in a more substantial dodge buff, providing greater survivability for the team, particularly for Eternity Heroes who receive an even stronger dodge increase.

3rd Skill: Moment of Concentration

Description:

Moment of Concentration, Octavia's third skill, grants a buff to all allies, boosting their armor penetration for 3.0 seconds. Additionally, each time an ally dodges damage, they receive the same armor penetration buff.

Analysis of the Skill:

  • Stackable Buffs: The armor penetration buffs provided by this skill stack, significantly improving the team's ability to penetrate enemy defenses. This is particularly advantageous in increasing the overall damage output against tanky opponents.
  • Synergy with Dodge: Each dodge by an ally triggers an additional armor penetration buff, creating a cycle where increased dodge rates (boosted by Favorite of the Empress) lead to more frequent armor penetration buffs.

Octavia Super Skin Synergy with Dante, Iris, Aurora, and Corvus

Octavia's Celestial Skin+ enhance team compositions involving heroes like Dante, Iris, Aurora, and Corvus in Hero Wars Alliance, creating synergies that maximize their effectiveness in battles:

Dante

Octavia's dodge buffs significantly benefit Dante, especially if he's used in tank compositions within meta teams like Royal League. Dante's role as a frontline tank is strengthened by Octavia's ability to enhance dodge rates across the team, improving survivability against both physical and magical attacks.

Iris

Iris, known for her high damage capabilities, can capitalize on Octavia's buffs to increase the team's overall survivability. With improved dodge rates from Dante's skills with Octavia, Iris can focus more on damage and providing buffs without worrying as much about her own survivability.

Aurora

Aurora, another frontline tank with innate dodge abilities, synergizes well with Octavia. The combined dodge buffs ensure that Aurora remains a formidable defender, effectively mitigating incoming damage while disrupting enemy formations.

Corvus

Corvus benefits from Octavia's enhancements by gaining increased survivability and damage potential. As a versatile hero with both offensive and defensive capabilities, Corvus can capitalize on the dodge and armor penetration buffs to deal significant damage to enemies while remaining resilient in battles.

Octavia Super Skin Conclusion

Octavia's new Celestial Skin and Celestial Skin+ provide a substantial boost to her physical attack, significantly enhancing her second and third skills. Favorite of the Empress benefits from a stronger dodge buff, improving team survivability against both physical and magical attacks. Moment of Concentration gains more potent armor penetration buffs, allowing Octavia's team to deal increased damage to enemies. The synergy between these skills and the boosted physical attack makes Octavia a more formidable hero in battles, particularly in teams with Eternity Heroes and allies who benefit from increased dodge and armor penetration.
