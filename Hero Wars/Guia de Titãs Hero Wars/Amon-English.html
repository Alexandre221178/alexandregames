Strategy on how to use Amon

Amon is a great titan and the first titan of Light to be added in the game, and can be used in combination with other titans in guild wars and dungeons, his abilities to counterattack enemies that receive stun makes him a great ally for use in combination with Moloch, Eden and Nova.

Amon is the only titan in the game with an exclusive ability to damage stunned titans, he also has great synergy with the new titan Iyari and is worth investing to form a strong combo.


Titan Amon in Dungeon

Amon is not a strong titan for the dungeon, but he can help in 5X5 titan battles in the dungeon when your other titans are already out of health.

Stats Evolution Priority

Artifacts Priority

On Amon's artifacts focus on leveling up Seal of Attack first to increase damage and health, then Crown of Light to gain extra defense and damage against all earth, water and fire titans. Lastly, upgrade the Crown of Belav weapon to gain more extra damage for Amon and the whole team.

Maximum Level Skills

1st Stellar Destruction

One by one, Amon shoots the closest, middle, and farthest enemies, dealing 1,704,511.2 (210% ATK) damage to each of them. Each consecutive hit on the same target deals 35% less damage.
If there are any stunned enemies on the battlefield, all projectiles are launched at the nearest stunned enemy once activated, dealing 1,623,344 (200% Physical Attack) extra area damage.
