Guide for Rigel's "Vengeful Barrier" in Hero Wars Alliance

Vengeful Barrier

Description:

Rigel casts a shield on themselves that decreases the damage from the next 5 attacks by (200% ATK). When the shield is destroyed, it stuns enemies in front of Rigel for 2.0 seconds.

Analysis:

Damage Mitigation:
  • Effectiveness: The shield significantly reduces incoming damage from the next five attacks, providing substantial protection.
  • ATK-Based Scaling: The shield’s strength scales with Rigel's attack power (ATK), making it more potent as Rigel’s offensive stats increase.
  • Strategic Use: Ideal for absorbing heavy-hitting or multi-hit abilities from enemies, allowing Rigel to stay in the fight longer.
Crowd Control:
  • Stun Duration: Upon breaking, the shield stuns enemies in front of Rigel for 2.0 seconds.
  • Positioning: Effective positioning of Rigel ensures that multiple enemies can be stunned simultaneously, disrupting their attacks and abilities.
Dual Functionality:
  • Survivability: By reducing damage, Rigel can maintain their presence on the battlefield.
  • Control: The stun effect provides an opportunity to counter-attack or reposition, making Rigel's shield a versatile tool in combat.

Rigel Synergy with Amon's "Stellar Destruction"

Amon Stellar Destruction Skill

Description:

Amon shoots the nearest, middle, and farthest enemies one by one, dealing (210% ATK) damage to each. Consecutive hits to the same target deal 35% less damage. If there are any stunned enemies on the battlefield, all projectiles are launched at the nearest stunned enemy once activated, dealing (200% ATK) extra area damage.

Analysis:

Damage Output:
  • Initial Attack: High initial damage (210% ATK) to three different targets.
  • Diminishing Returns: Successive hits on the same target deal reduced damage, necessitating strategic targeting.
Enhanced Synergy with Stuns:
  • Optimal Targeting: If enemies are stunned, Amon's projectiles target the nearest stunned enemy, maximizing damage efficiency.
  • Area Damage: Additional area damage (200% ATK) is inflicted, making it highly effective against clustered enemies.
Combined Effectiveness:
  • Setup and Execution:
    • Initial Shielding: Rigel's "Vengeful Barrier" reduces incoming damage and sets up a potential stun.
    • Stun Activation: When the shield breaks, enemies in front of Rigel are stunned for 2.0 seconds.
    • Amon’s Follow-up: Amon's "Stellar Destruction" then capitalizes on the stunned enemies, targeting them with all projectiles and dealing significant extra area damage.
  • Strategic Timing:
    • Coordination: Synchronize Rigel’s shield breaking with Amon’s attack to ensure the stun effect is active, allowing Amon to unleash maximum damage.
    • Enemy Disruption: This combination can effectively disrupt enemy formations and eliminate high-priority targets quickly.

Synergies Overall Impact

Rigel's "Vengeful Barrier" not only provides substantial defensive benefits but also creates an opportunity for powerful offensive synergy with Amon's "Stellar Destruction." Proper timing and positioning can turn the tide of battle, leveraging both characters' abilities to their fullest potential.

Pros and Cons of Rigel in Hero Wars Alliance

Pros:

  • Strong Damage Mitigation: "Vengeful Barrier" reduces damage from the next five attacks by (200% ATK).
  • Effective Crowd Control: Shield breaking stuns enemies in front of Rigel for 2.0 seconds.
  • Scalable Protection: Shield strength increases with Rigel's ATK.
  • Versatile in Combat: Provides both defensive and offensive advantages.
  • Synergy with Amon: Stun enhances Amon’s "Stellar Destruction," maximizing team damage.

Cons:

  • Shield Reliance: Rigel is vulnerable without the shield.
  • Positioning Required: Stun only affects enemies in front of Rigel.
  • Limited Stun Duration: 2.0 seconds stun requires precise timing.
  • Cooldown Dependency: Effectiveness is limited by cooldown periods.
  • Vulnerability After Shield Break: Rigel can be exposed once the shield is down.

Best Teams for Rigel in Hero Wars Alliance

Team 1: Mort, Amon, Solaris, Iyari, Rigel

Iyari: Light support titan who heals and restores energy and health to Light titans and any ally with the lowest health.

Solaris: Super Light titan with area healing and damage, and protection against stun and slow effects by 20%.

Mort: Curses the enemy with the highest physical attack, channeling part of it to the nearest non-Darkness ally, which in this case is Amon.

Amon: Synergizes with Rigel’s stuns for enhanced damage.

Strengths: Excellent healing and protection, powerful area damage, and effective debuffs. This team excels in sustainability and damage output, with Mort providing additional protection by redirecting enemy power.

Team 2: Amon, Solaris, Eden, Iyari, Rigel

Eden: Curses a random enemy, burying them for 6 seconds and stunning them, making this composition strong for defense.

Iyari: Provides consistent healing and energy restoration.

Solaris: Offers area healing and damage along with protection against crowd control.

Amon: Benefits from Rigel’s stuns to focus damage on key targets.

Strengths: Strong defensive capabilities with Eden’s stun and bury ability, combined with Solaris and Iyari’s healing. This team is resilient and can control enemy movements effectively.

Team 3: Keros, Amon, Solaris, Iyari, Rigel

Keros: Deals damage to enemies in the center.

Iyari: Heals and restores energy and health.

Solaris: Provides area healing, damage, and protection against stuns.

Amon: Works with Rigel’s stuns for enhanced damage.

Strengths: Balanced in terms of damage and control, with Keros targeting central enemies and Rigel providing stuns. This team can disrupt enemy formations effectively.

Team 4: Amon, Solaris, Iyari, Rigel, Brustar

Brustar: Creates a shield and redirects received damage to enemies.

Iyari: Provides healing and energy restoration.

Solaris: Offers area healing and damage, and crowd control protection.

Amon: Capitalizes on Rigel’s stuns for increased damage.

Strengths: High damage redirection and protection from Brustar, combined with healing and control from Solaris and Iyari. This team is durable and can turn enemy attacks against them.

Team 5: Amon, Solaris, Iyari, Nova, Rigel

Nova: Stuns enemies at the back, synergizing well with Amon.

Iyari: Consistent healing and energy restoration.

Solaris: Provides area healing and damage, and crowd control protection.

Amon: Uses Rigel’s stuns for focused damage.

Strengths: Effective backline disruption with Nova’s stuns, enhanced by Amon’s targeted damage. This team excels at controlling the battlefield and neutralizing threats.

Team 6: Amon, Solaris, Iyari, Araji, Rigel

Araji: High damage output and boosts ally speed by 30%.

Iyari: Healing and energy restoration.

Solaris: Area healing and damage, with crowd control protection.

Amon: Benefits from Rigel’s stuns for increased damage.

Strengths: High damage and speed boost from Araji, combined with healing and control from Iyari and Solaris. This team is fast and powerful, able to deal significant damage quickly.

Team 7: Amon, Solaris, Iyari, Mairi, Rigel

Mairi: Reduces enemy attack by 40%.

Iyari: Provides healing and energy restoration.

Solaris: Offers area healing and damage, and protection against stuns.

Amon: Capitalizes on Rigel’s stuns for enhanced damage.

Strengths: Significant enemy attack reduction from Mairi, combined with healing and control from Iyari and Solaris. This team is defensively strong and can mitigate enemy damage effectively.

Team 8: Amon, Solaris, Hyperion, Iyari, Rigel

Hyperion: Heals the ally with the lowest health and deals damage to the furthest enemies.

Iyari: Consistent healing and energy restoration.

Solaris: Provides area healing and damage, with crowd control protection.

Amon: Benefits from Rigel’s stuns for focused damage.

Strengths: Enhanced healing from Hyperion and Iyari ensures survivability, while Hyperion’s damage to the furthest enemies disrupts backline threats. This team is strong in both offense and defense, maintaining pressure on opponents throughout the battle.

Best Rigel Teams Overview

In summary, Rigel shines in various team compositions thanks to his "Vengeful Barrier" ability. Each team leverages different strengths, from healing and damage output to crowd control and defensive capabilities, making Rigel a versatile character who enhances team performance in multiple scenarios.

Rigel Guide Conclusion

In summary, Rigel’s blend of strong defensive capabilities, effective crowd control, and potential for synergistic attacks makes him a valuable asset on the battlefield. Mastering his shield management and coordinating with team abilities are key to leveraging his full potential and overcoming his vulnerabilities.
