Strategy on how to use Mort

Mort is the second Darkness Titan to be added to the Hero Wars Alliance. With super powerful abilities, he promises to make titan battles more difficult in PvP wars.

The Shadow Rampage ability curses the enemy titan with the highest physical attack in battle. For a period of time, it channels part of the cursed titan's life and physical attack to all allies of the Darkness element and also to the ally of any other element that is closest to Mort.

Below I have included an updated table of physical attack statistics for Titans, and we can see that the most damaged titans will be snipers and supports, while supers and tanks are in a safer position. The problem is that the most used teams can become Mort's targets, especially Araji, who has more physical attack than Nova.

Titan Mort in Dungeon

Mort is best used in the dungeon with Titan Hyperion positioned as a nearby ally. Increasing Hyperion's physical attack also increases healing and damage especially against more distant enemies.

It can also be used in the dungeon with the titan Keros, reducing the damage to enemy titans and helping dark allies with life gain, as well as channeling life to the closest ally of another element.


Positive and Negative Points

Positive Points

  • Reduces health and physical attack
  • Weakens enemy titan with the highest physical attack
  • Transfers health and physical attack from enemies to allies
  • Synergy with Keros

Negative Points

  • Curses only one enemy titan at once
  • You need to charge energy to use Shadow Rampage


Stats Evolution Priority

Artifacts Priority

In Mort's artifacts, first level up the Balance Seal to gain more health and physical attack, then the Weapon artifact to gain more physical attack for him and the entire team. Finally, level up the Crown of Darkness artifact to increase resistance and damage against titans of the fire, earth, and water elements.

Titan Mort in Battles

Strong Against

  • Ignis, Keros, Sylva, Vulcan, Amon, Araji

Mort is strong against the Titans with high physical attack stats. Each time Mort uses his special ability, he will focus on the enemy titan with the highest physical attack stat, reducing their attack and health, and will channel this reduction to his allies.

Titan Mort Counters

  • Mairi, Nova, Hyperion, Iyari, Eden, Avalon

Mort is weak against titans with less physical attack stats, such as Eden and Hyperion. Mairi would be his main counter, reducing his physical attack by 40%. This way, he will channel less life and physical attack. Nova is also a good counter, especially if you play manual, as it stuns backline enemies, preventing Mort from using his Ultimate.
