Strategy on how to use Nova

Nova is an excellent titan, she's just not better than a super titan because she doesn't have two abilities.

When Nova uses her ability she causes damage to enemy Titans that are positioned on the back line, and depending on the formation of the enemy team she can also stun 3 to 4 Titans for 4 seconds at once.


Titan Nova in Dungeon

In the dungeon, Nova can be strategically used against Araji's ability in water vs. fire titan battles or in 5v5 battles. You should save Nova's special ability to use as soon as Araji uses his Ultimate, and this way, he is stunned for 4s and does not attack your team.

Always have the Nova ability charged with energy to interrupt enemy heroes' Ultimate.


Positive and Negative Points

Positive Points

  • Stun backline enemies for 4s
  • Can interrupt Araji's Ultimate
  • Can interrupt Eden's Ultimate

Negative Points

  • Marksman positioned on the front line


Stats Evolution Priority

Artifacts Priority

In Nova's artifacts, prioritize Attack Seal to gain more life and physical attack, as she is positioned on the front line she needs a lot of health. Then level up Water Crown to increase damage and resistance against fire titans.

Lastly, level up Siungur's Maelstrom weapon artifact to provide extra damage against fire titans for the entire team.

Priority Artifacts
1st Attack Seal
2nd Water Crown
3rd Siungur's Maelstrom


Titan Nova Artifacts in Hero Wars

Skins Priority

In Nova's skin, prioritize physical attacks to increase damage against all titans. Finally, level up the primordial skin to increase damage against fire element titans.
