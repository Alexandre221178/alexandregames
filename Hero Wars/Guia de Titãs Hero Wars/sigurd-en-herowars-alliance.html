Guide to Using Sigurd in Hero Wars Alliance

Sigurd is a Water element tank in Hero Wars Alliance, known for his top-notch defensive abilities. Even with new tank Titans like Rigel (Light element) and Brustar (Darkness element), Sigurd is still a great choice for both PvP battles and dungeon exploration.

Skill Overview: Icy Aegis

Sigurd’s Icy Aegis skill is one of the best in Hero Wars Alliance because it makes him completely immune to damage for 5 seconds. Even with new Titans, Sigurd remains a top tank for PvP and dungeons.

Icy Aegis Effect: Lifts the shield and becomes immune to any damage for 5.0 seconds.

When to Use Sigurd's Skill

Best used at key moments to block heavy damage or during enemy’s ultimate skills.

Manual Battles with Sigurd in PvP

  • Watch Enemy Skills: Pay attention to the enemy Titans’ big attacks. Use Icy Aegis just before they hit you with their strongest skill.
  • Absorb Damage: Use Icy Aegis to absorb damage to the fight, giving your other Titans time to get ready for their attacks or buffs.
  • Block Burst Damage: If the enemy team has high burst damage, Sigurd’s immunity can block their major damage, giving your team an advantage.

Sigurd Dungeon Exploration

Sigurd is a must-have in dungeons, especially for farming infinite gold. Here’s why:

  • Staying Power: Icy Aegis gives 5 seconds of invincibility, letting your team heal and attack without worrying about Sigurd taking hits.
  • Healing Combo: Pair Sigurd with Hyperion Water Titan who can heal or other Healer Titans. The invincibility period lets healers do their job without interruption.
  • Artifact Boost: Prioritize Sigurd with healing and health artifacts to boost his durability, making him the best tank for long dungeon runs.

Sigurd Team Composition for Dungeon

For the best results in Dungeon with Sigurd, consider this team setup:

  • Frontline: Sigurd (Water) – Main tank with Icy Aegis.
  • Healer: Hyperion (Water) - Super Titan with great skill of healing.
  • Support: Mairi (Water) – Provides enemies debuffs.
  • Damage Dealers: Nova (Water) – Deals steady damage while stun enemies being protected by Sigurd.
  • Flex Slot: Healing Titans Iyari and Avalon or another Titan that complements the team’s strategy.

Guide to Upgrading Sigurd's Artifacts in Hero Wars Alliance

Sigurd, the Water element tank, is a critical Titan in Hero Wars Alliance. Upgrading his artifacts in the right order can make a big difference in your battles. Here’s the best strategy for prioritizing Sigurd’s artifact upgrades.

Priority for Artifact Upgrades

  1. Defense Seal

    Reason: Boosting Physical Attack and Health is essential for Sigurd to deal more damage and survive longer in battles.

    Max Level Stats:

    • Physical Attack: +82,500
    • Health: +4,575,000
  2. Siungur's Aegis (Weapon Artifact)

    Reason: This artifact not only boosts Sigurd’s health regeneration but also triggers an effect when Sigurd uses his Icy Aegis skill, giving bonus stats to the whole team for 9 seconds.

    Max Level Stats:

    • Health Regen: +100,050
    • Activation Chance: 100%
  3. Water Crown

    Reason: Provides extra damage to Fire Titans and extra defense from Fire Titans, making Sigurd more effective against Fire element enemies.

    Max Level Stats:

    • Extra Damage to Fire Titans: +255,000
    • Extra Defense from Fire Titans: +100,500

Step-by-Step Strategy

  1. Upgrade Defense Seal: Start by maxing out the Defense Seal. The massive boost in health and physical attack will make Sigurd a much stronger tank and help him dish out more damage.
  2. Upgrade Siungur's Aegis: Next, focus on Siungur’s Aegis. The health regeneration is crucial for survivability, and the bonus stats effect is invaluable in battles.
  3. Upgrade Water Crown: Finally, upgrade the Water Crown. This will help Sigurd perform better against Fire element Titans, adding both offensive and defensive benefits.

Sigurd Guide Conclusion Hero Wars Alliance

For newcomers to Hero Wars Alliance, Sigurd is a crucial tank to focus on. Prioritizing his Health Skin and Extra Defense from Fire Titans ensures he can withstand significant damage. Upgrading his Defense Seal boosts his health and attack, making him a stronger tank. Siungur's Aegis improves his health regeneration and provides a team-wide buff, while the Water Crown enhances his effectiveness against Fire Titans.

Sigurd’s durability and team support abilities make him indispensable for new players, helping you progress faster and tackle tougher challenges. Focus on upgrading Sigurd, and you'll have a reliable tank leading your team to victory. Happy gaming!
