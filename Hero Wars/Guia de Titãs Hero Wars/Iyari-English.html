Strategy on how to use Iyari

Of the new titans added to the game, Iyari is the best, and will undoubtedly be the best support titan for a long time to come, her ability to heal and restore energy makes her an excellent battle titan.

Iyari when using his ultimate heals all allies of the Light element and also heals the ally with less health, which can be of another element and, at the same time, restores 100 points of energy.


Titan Iyari in Dungeon

Well, from the above strategies it is clear that Iyari is one of the best investments for the dungeon, mostly for use with Sigurd, because in addition to providing quick healing for the ally with less life than the tank in this case, it also restores energy by making Sigurd use his artifact and more healing for the team.

It's a great idea to fit Iyari into your 5v5 dungeon team to restore health to your earth, fire and water tanks.

Positive and Negative Points

Positive Points

  • Heals all Light element allies
  • Heals ally with less health
  • Extra energy for healed allies
  • Good for defense
  • Synergy with Amon

Negative Points

  • Does little damage
  • Healing focused on Titans of Light
  • Heals allies from other elements if they have less health


Stats Evolution Priority

Artifacts Priority

In Iyari's artifacts prioritize Seal of Balance to gain more life and physical attack, as Iyari's healing skills require a lot of physical attack to heal more, then level up Crown of Light for extra damage and extra defense against fire, earth, and water titans. Lastly, raise the Face of Belav weapon to give extra defense to the whole team when it uses the Ultimate


Titan Iyari Artifacts in Hero Wars


  1. Seal of Balance
  2. Crown of Light
  3. Face of Belav

Skins Priority

Iyari only has the default physical attack skin and it's good to prioritize, because the more physical attack the more health points she will restore to her allies.
