Strategy on how to use Hyperion

Hyperion is the Super Titan of the water element and is currently the best Super Titan in Hero Wars. Having him on your Titans team makes a big difference. His first ability deals a lot of damage to enemies positioned on the back line, and the further away, the greater the damage received.

Hyperion can also heal the ally with less health and, at the same time, heal the other allies on the team with his weapon artifact.

All these qualities make him one of the most important titans in Hero Wars. And without a doubt, it is one of the most used titans in PvP battles and is worth investing in.


Titan Hyperion in Dungeon

In the dungeon, Hyperion becomes even better, with the Sigurd tank, they make an excellent healing combo, helping your titans recover life in 5v5 battles or, becoming infinite, recovering all their life after reaching a high level of power in battles of water against fire.

In the 5v5 battles of the dungeon, Hyperion must be used strategically with Sigurd, Avalon, and Iyari to heal Moloch and Angus. This way, your team of titans goes deeper into the dungeon and gets a lot of gold, sparks of power, and titan potion.

Negative Points

Stats Evolution Priority

Artifacts Priority

In Hyperion's artifacts, prioritize leveling up the Seal of Balance first to increase damage and healing ability, as the more physical damage, the more it will heal the ally with less health, which will normally be your tank.

Then level up the Siungur's Staff weapon artifact to be able to heal the entire team when activated. And finally, go up the Crown of Water to increase damage and defense against fire element titans.

Priority Artifacts
1st Balance Seal
2nd Siungur's Staff (weapon)
3rd Water Crown
