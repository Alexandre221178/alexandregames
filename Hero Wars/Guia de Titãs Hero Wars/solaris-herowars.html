History of Solaris


Hero Wars Alliance Stories: Solaris - A brilliant Super Titan whose light can heat and burn to the ground. In battle, she can protect allies as well as attack and weaken opponents.

Strategy on how to use Solaris

Solaris is the new Super Titan of Light and the first Super Titan of new elements to be added in Hero Wars Alliance. She has synergy with Amon and Iyari, two excellent Titans, who already have a strong synergy, imagine the synergy of the three Titans of light in battles.

Solaris has a lot of synergy with the titan Iyari. Iyari summons a beacon of light that heals and gives extra energy to light allies every second, this way, Solaris activates her ultimate Over-Power more often, causing a lot of area damage to enemy teams.


Titan Solaris in Dungeon

Solaris is very strong in the dungeon in combination with Sigurd, Hyperion and Iyari protecting the team and the lowest health ally tank (Moloch and Angus) against stun, while the rest of the team regenerates their health with healing skills.

Stats Evolution Priority

Artifacts Priority

In Solaris artifacts, priority is given to Seal of Balance to increase life and physical attack, as the more physical attack, the more area-wide Over-Power damage it will cause. Then ascend Crown of Light to increase Solaris' defense and damage against the old titans of Earth, Fire, and Water. And finally, upgrade the weapon to increase health regeneration for the entire Solaris team.

How to Counter Solaris

Solaris
Best Counters		 What happens

Mort
Mort redirects part of the health and physical attack of the titan with the highest physical attack in the enemy team, this way he can reduce Solaris' Over-Power damage, even so, Amon has more physical attack statistics than Solaris. Solaris could be Mort's second or third target.

Mairi
Mairi's Ultimate reduces enemies' attack by 40%, for 8 seconds. This way, Solaris deals less damage to the enemies in area.
