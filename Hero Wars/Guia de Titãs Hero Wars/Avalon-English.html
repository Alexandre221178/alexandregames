Strategy on how to use Avalon

Avalon is the earth element support titan, with an ability that provides a shield that protects the entire team, especially against water element titans. Avalon is not used much for PvP war battles, but it is an excellent titan for the dungeon.

She also has the weapon artifact with the ability to heal the entire team, and for this reason, she is strategically used for dungeons.

Titan Avalon in Dungeon

Avalon can really help your team go deeper into daily dungeon missions by helping to regenerate your titans with the healing artifact and with your shield, which reduces damage.

Furthermore, with each battle, she regenerates the health of the Earth titans in Earth versus Water element battles in the dungeon.

Having Avalon with high power is essential for completing the dungeon missions, and in this way, you get more benefits to level up both your heroes and your titans.


Positive and Negative Points

Positive Points

  • Shield that absorbs all types of damage
  • Shield for all allies
  • Weapon artifact with healing for the team

Negative Points

  • Weak Shield for PvP Wars
  • It causes little damage.

Stats Evolution Priority

Avalon Artifacts Priority

In the Avalon titan artifacts, prioritize the Weapon so that when you activate the artifact, it can heal the entire team, then prioritize the Seal of Balance so that Avalon has more life and more physical attack to increase the power of the shield; and finally, prioritize the Crown of the Earth to improve defense and damage against Water element titans.

Priority Artifacts
1st Andvari's Bulwark (Weapon)
2nd Seal of Balance
3rd Crown of the Earth

Skins Priority

In Avalon's skins, first level up the default skin to increase the damage absorbed by Avalon's shield, then level up the primordial skin to have more health and increase survivability.
