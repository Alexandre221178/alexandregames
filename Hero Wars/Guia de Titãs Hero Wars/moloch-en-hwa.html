Introduction Moloch Guide (Blazing Ravager)

This guide will delve into Moloch's strengths, his role as a tank, artifact priorities, skins, and his strategic placement in different battles. Whether you're a seasoned player or a beginner looking to enhance your Titan squad, this guide will offer insights on maximizing Moloch’s potential.

Moloch’s Role in Battle

1. Guild Wars and Dungeon Battles

Moloch's primary role is that of a front-line tank, excelling in protecting his allies by absorbing enemy damage and disrupting their formations. Moloch is particularly effective in Guild Wars and Dungeon battles due to his high health pool, defensive abilities, and crowd control skills. His presence in the front line is invaluable as he buys time for his team to recover and counterattack. The most significant aspect of Moloch’s gameplay is his ability to stun the front-line tanks of the enemy team, often preventing them from activating their powerful abilities, which can be a game-changer in battles.

Skill Highlight:

Smashing Blow: Moloch’s signature ability, Smashing Blow, strikes the nearest enemies, dealing 250% of his attack damage and stunning them for 5 seconds. This ability is vital in breaking the rhythm of enemy tanks, especially newer titans like Rigel and Brustar. By stunning these high-damage titans, Moloch allows his allies to either counterattack or regain health.

2. Combating New Titans (Rigel and Brustar)

The arrival of newer titans like Rigel and Brustar poses a challenge for older titans, including Moloch, due to their enhanced damage against older generations of titans. Rigel and Brustar have high burst damage, and their synergy in battles can overwhelm traditional tanks. This makes Moloch's role more nuanced; success depends not only on his individual strength but also on the composition and synergy of the team he is placed with. Moloch must be paired with titans that complement his strengths and cover his weaknesses, especially in the face of such powerful opponents.

Strategy Tip:

To counter the extra damage from newer titans like Rigel and Brustar, it's essential to focus on Moloch’s defensive skills and prioritize formations that can mitigate their burst damage. Pairing Moloch with titans that provide healing or additional crowd control can help extend his survival time in battle, allowing him to remain a reliable tank for the team.

Artifact Priorities

Artifacts play a crucial role in determining a titan’s overall effectiveness, especially for tanks like Moloch. The correct artifact build can significantly enhance Moloch’s ability to withstand damage and control the battlefield.

Artifact 1: Defense Seal

Priority Level: High (1st Priority)

  • Attributes:
  • Physical Attack: +82,500
  • Health: +4,575,000

The Defense Seal should be the primary focus for Moloch. This artifact boosts Moloch's health and physical attack, making him more durable in battle. The health increase ensures Moloch can soak up as much damage as possible, which is essential for his role as a tank. Prioritizing this artifact will improve Moloch’s survivability in both Guild Wars and Dungeon battles, where maintaining a strong frontline is vital.

Artifact 2: Fire Crown

Priority Level: Medium (2nd Priority)

  • Attributes:
  • Extra Damage to Earth Titans: +255,000
  • Extra Defense from Earth Titans: +100,500

The Fire Crown is the next priority. This artifact enhances Moloch’s effectiveness against Earth Titans, which he frequently encounters in dungeons and PvP battles. Increasing his extra defense from Earth Titans will allow him to endure more hits, while the extra damage to Earth Titans boosts his offensive capability when facing Earth-element enemies. In battles against Earth Titans, particularly in the dungeon, this artifact proves crucial for both survival and damage output.

Artifact 3: Ragni’s Hammer

Priority Level: Low (3rd Priority)

  • Attributes:
  • Activation Chance: 100%
  • Extra Damage to Earth Titans: +150,000

While Ragni's Hammer provides extra damage to Earth Titans, it is the least prioritized artifact for Moloch. As a tank, Moloch's primary job is to soak up damage and provide crowd control for his team, rather than dealing significant damage. Although this artifact can increase his damage output, prioritizing Moloch’s defensive capabilities is more beneficial, especially considering the high burst damage from newer titans like Rigel and Brustar.

Skin Guide for Moloch

Skins offer essential stat bonuses that can greatly affect a titan's performance in battle. For Moloch, his skins are vital for boosting his health, which is crucial for his role as a front-line tank. Both available skins focus on increasing Moloch's health, making them indispensable for his survival in battles against high-damage opponents.

Skin 1: Default Skin

Health Bonus: +2,627,975

Priority Level: High

The Default Skin should be your first focus as it provides a substantial health boost. This increase in health is essential for Moloch’s role in battles, particularly in PvP and against newer titans who deal extra damage to older titans. By maximizing this skin, you ensure that Moloch can withstand more hits and continue to serve as a reliable tank for your team.

Skin 2: Primordial Skin

Health Bonus: +2,627,975

Priority Level: High

The Primordial Skin offers the same health bonus as the Default Skin. This skin should also be prioritized to maximize Moloch's health, further improving his durability in combat. Since newer titans like Rigel and Brustar deal extra damage to older titans, having the maximum health possible through skins is critical for Moloch’s longevity in battles. Together with the Default Skin, the Primordial Skin ensures Moloch remains competitive against these newer threats.

Conclusion on Skins: The two skins for Moloch are equally important and should be prioritized for survivability. Given the threat posed by newer titans, maximizing Moloch’s health is crucial for both PvP battles and dungeon encounters.

Combining Moloch with Other Titans

1. Synergy in Team Composition

As a tank, Moloch thrives when paired with titans that can complement his defensive abilities and crowd control. He works best with allies of Darkness and Light who can provide sustained damage and healing, as well as those who can capitalize on the opportunities created by his stun ability. Building a well-balanced team around Moloch is key to his success in both PvP and PvE encounters.

Recommended Allies:

  • Light Titans: Titans like Iyari and Solaris are excellent choices for Moloch. Iyari offers healing and buffs that boost the team's survivability, while Solaris provides additional burst damage to complement Moloch's tanking and crowd control abilities.
  • Darkness Titans: Titans like Brustar and Mort synergize well with Moloch. Brustar has high burst damage potential and can disrupt enemy formations, while Mort offers additional utility with debuffs that weaken enemies, allowing Moloch to maintain dominance on the battlefield.
  • Mixed Team Composition: A combination of Light and Dark Titans, such as Rigel from the Light element and Tenebris from the Dark element, can create a balanced team. Rigel’s utility in disabling enemies and Tenebris’ crowd control can complement Moloch's ability to stun and tank, creating a powerful synergy that is effective in both PvP and Dungeon encounters.

2. Moloch's Role Against Rigel and Brustar

The challenge presented by Rigel and Brustar is their high burst damage, which can quickly overwhelm older titans like Moloch. To counter this, Moloch must be supported by titans who can either heal or provide additional crowd control, ensuring that Rigel and Brustar do not get free reign to target Moloch or his allies. By strategically using Moloch’s stun abilities and supporting him with complementary titans, you can mitigate the threat posed by these powerful opponents.

Conclusion

Moloch remains a powerful and versatile titan in Hero Wars Alliance, capable of holding the front line and providing crucial crowd control for his team. While the introduction of newer titans like Rigel and Brustar presents a challenge, Moloch’s tanking capabilities, combined with the right artifacts and skins, ensure that he remains a valuable asset in both PvP and PvE encounters. By understanding Moloch’s strengths and weaknesses, and building a synergistic team around him, you can maximize his effectiveness and dominate the battlefield.
