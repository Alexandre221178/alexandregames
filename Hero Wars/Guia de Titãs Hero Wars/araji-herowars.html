Strategy on how to use Araji

Araji is the Super Titan of the fire element and has two skills. Araji's passive skill speeds up allies by 30% for eight seconds, which causes your team to gain more energy and attack enemies. Araji's Ultimate launches a beam of fire towards enemies on the front line, dealing a lot of damage.

Having Araji on your team of Titans makes a big difference. He can be combined with several teams of Titans in wars and is very popular, especially with his fire element ally Ignis, which increases the damage to allied Titans.

Araji's weak point is the water element titan Sigurd, which activates his shield, and makes him invincible for a few seconds, or the new titans, with a lot of extra defense against the old titans.


Titan Araji in Dungeon

Araji is very strong in the dungeon. When he reaches the maximum level, he can practically carry the fire team alone for a few floors.

The idea is to use Araji in 5 versus 5 teams in the dungeon with Hyperion, Avalon, and Sigurd to recover the health of the tank titans, especially the tank, Angus. To carry Moloch's life, I suggest replacing Araji with Iyari.

Stats Evolution Priority

Artifacts Priority

In Araji's artifacts, prioritize leveling up the attack seal because it increases physical attack and health, as Araji positions himself from the middle to the front line and can take a lot of damage. Then level up the crown of fire to increase defense and damage against earth titans.

Lastly, level up Araji's weapon to increase damage against Earth titans for all fire element allies.

Araji Best Counters

Counters What happens

Sigurdo
Sigurd Ultimate uses a shield that makes him invincible for 5 seconds, thus blocking Araji's Ultimate attack.

Moloch
If Araji is positioned on the front line next to the allied tank, he can be hit by Moloch's Ultimate and be stunned for 5 seconds.

Mairi
Mairi's Ultimate reduces enemies' attack by 40%, for 8 seconds. This way, Araji deals less damage to tank enemies on the front line.

Death
Mort curses the enemy with the highest physical attack, reducing health and physical attack attacks, as Araji has a lot of physical attacks, Araji has a chance of being Mort's first target in almost every battle.
