Strategy on how to use Eden

Eden is the super titan of the earth element, and his role is as a Marksman, that's right, he throws a huge stone at enemies, and his control skills make him very strong, especially against titans of the water element.

Eden has the ability to stun and deal area damage, plus he can pull a random enemy under the ground, leaving the enemy team one less ally.

Titan Eden in Dungeon

Eden is very strong in the dungeon and can help his team go deeper in the dungeon, and get more gold, even though he is strong it is good to keep the tank titan Angus of the strongest earth element.

Remember for the dungeon, the ideal is that the land titans are stronger than the water titans. So, you can invest in Eden without fear.
