Guide to Angus the Nature Protector in Hero Wars Alliance

Angus, the Nature Protector, is a formidable Earth Titan in Hero Wars Alliance. As a tank, his primary role is to absorb damage and protect his team, while also dealing significant damage, especially against Water Titans. This guide will delve into his skills, stats, evolution priorities, artifact management, and strategic use in battles. Whether you're new to the game or a seasoned player, this guide will help you maximize Angus's potential.

Overview of Angus's Abilities

Wrath of the Earth

  • Description: Angus summons roots from the ground, striking all enemies and causing damage over time.
  • Effect: Deals 293,809 damage per second (90% of his physical attack) for 8 seconds.
  • Additional Info: While this ability is active, Angus cannot attack.

Wrath of the Earth is Angus's signature ability, making him a potent damage dealer in prolonged battles. The roots not only deal continuous damage but also keep the enemies under constant pressure, making it difficult for them to recover.

Stat Analysis

  • Health: As a tank, Angus's health is his most crucial stat. With over 14 million health points, he can withstand substantial damage, making him a reliable frontline protector.
  • Physical Attack: His physical attack is not as high as some other Titans, but it is sufficient when combined with his abilities that scale with this stat.
  • Extra Damage to Water Titans: This stat makes Angus particularly deadly against Water Titans, dealing an additional 673,195 damage.
  • Extra Defense Against Water Titans: With 100,500 extra defense, Angus is incredibly resilient against Water Titans, allowing him to survive longer in these matchups.

Prioritizing Angus's Skin Evolution

Skins in Hero Wars provide essential stat boosts that can significantly impact a Titan's performance. Angus has two primary skins:

  • Standard Skin (Health): +2,627,975 Health (Level: 60/60)
  • Primordial Skin (Extra Damage to Water Titans): +418,195 Extra Damage to Water Titans (Level: 60/60)

Skin Evolution Priority

  1. Standard Skin (Health):

    Reasoning: Health is the most critical stat for a tank like Angus. Prioritizing this skin ensures that he can sustain more damage in battles, making him a more effective tank.

    Usage: This skin is universally beneficial, increasing Angus's survivability in all types of battles, regardless of the enemy's elemental type.

  2. Primordial Skin (Extra Damage to Water Titans):

    Reasoning: This skin is highly specialized, providing significant advantages against Water Titans. However, it offers no benefits against Titans of other elements.

    Usage: This skin is particularly useful in the Dungeon, where Earth Titans often face Water Titans. In these scenarios, the extra damage can be the difference between victory and defeat.

Conclusion on Skins: Focus on maxing out the Standard Skin first to ensure Angus is as durable as possible in all situations. The Primordial Skin should be developed afterward for specific use cases against Water Titans.

Prioritizing Artifact Management for Angus

Artifacts play a crucial role in enhancing a Titan's stats and abilities. Angus's artifacts are vital for maximizing his effectiveness on the battlefield.

Artifact Priorities

  1. Seal of Defense (Defensive Artifact):

    Boost: +82,500 Physical Attack, +4,575,000 Health

    Priority: High

    Reasoning: The Seal of Defense significantly increases Angus's survivability with a massive health boost while also adding some physical attack power. This should be your primary focus as it enhances Angus's core tanking capabilities.

  2. Andvari's Fists (Weapon Artifact):

    Boost: +120,000 Physical Attack

    Activation Chance: 100%

    Priority: Medium

    Reasoning: This artifact improves Angus's physical attack, making his abilities more potent. While it’s essential for increasing damage output, it’s secondary to enhancing his survivability.

  3. Crown of Earth (Support Artifact):

    Boost: +255,000 Extra Damage to Water Titans, +100,500 Extra Defense Against Water Titans

    Priority: Low

    Reasoning: The Crown of Earth is highly specialized, like the Primordial Skin, offering significant benefits in battles against Water Titans. However, it should be a lower priority unless you frequently engage in these specific battles.

Artifact Management Strategy

  • Initial Focus: Max out the Seal of Defense first to maximize Angus's health and durability.
  • Secondary Focus: Once the Seal of Defense is fully upgraded, work on Andvari's Fists to boost Angus's attack power.
  • Final Touch: The Crown of Earth should be upgraded last, and only if you are focusing on battles against Water Titans.

Strategies for Using Angus in Battle

Angus excels in specific scenarios, particularly when facing Water Titans, thanks to his ability to deal extra damage and resist their attacks. However, his role as a tank means that he needs to be used strategically to ensure your team's success.

1. Guild Wars and Global Championship:

  • Role: Frontline Defender
  • Focus: In Guild Wars and Global Championship, where the enemy composition can vary, prioritize using Angus's ability to sustain damage and disrupt enemies with Anger of the Earth makes him a solid choice. This ensures that he remains effective against all types of Titans, not just Water Titans.
  • Strategy: Pair Angus with Titans that can capitalize on the damage and disruption caused by Anger of the Earth. This could include damage-dealing Titans or support Titans that can heal or shield Angus.

By following these tips, you’ll be well on your way to building the best Titan Angus teams, optimized for Guild Wars and Global Championship success and beyond.

2. Dungeon Battles:

  • Role: Primary Tank
  • Focus: When fighting Water Titans in the Dungeon, Angus is your go-to tank. His extra damage and defense against Water Titans give him a distinct advantage, allowing your team to outlast and overpower the enemy.
  • Strategy: Position Angus at the front to absorb damage, while other Earth Titans or support units focus on dealing damage. Use his Wrath of the Earth ability strategically to disrupt the enemy and deal continuous damage.

Angus's Utility in the Dungeon

Angus is highly useful in the Dungeon, especially in the early game. With his high area damage, he can carry your Titan team further into the Dungeon, compensating for the lack of sufficient healing from your support Titans. This makes him an invaluable asset as you progress through the tougher stages.

Conclusion: Maximizing Angus's Potential

Angus, the Nature Protector, is a versatile and durable Earth Titan with a focus on tanking and dealing sustained damage, particularly against Water Titans. To maximize his potential:

  • Prioritize health through the Standard Skin and the Seal of Defense artifact.
  • Use his abilities strategically in battles, focusing on disrupting the enemy and absorbing damage.
  • Develop his specialized skills (Primordial Skin and Crown of Earth) for Water Titan-specific scenarios.

With this guide, you’ll be able to effectively use Angus in various battle scenarios, ensuring that he stands strong as the protector of your Titan team.
