Strategy on how to use Keros

Keros is a great titan and the first titan of darkness to be added in the game, and can be used in combination with other titans in guild warfare and dungeon, his attack and defense buff skills against all current elements make him very strong.

Keros' artifact when activated gives extra bonus damage to all allies against enemy titans of all elements, earth, fire and water.


Titan Keros in Dungeon

Keros isn't a strong titan for the dungeon, but he can help out in 5v5 titan battles in the dungeon when your other titans are out of health.


Positive and Negative Points

Positive Points

  • Has artifact of increased damage to all enemies "Team Buff"
  • Area damage AoE
  • Marksman

Negative Points

  • Has excellent defense but plays on the back line
  • It needs a lot of investment to stay strong

Stats Evolution Priority

Artifacts Priority

In Keros artifacts, prioritize Seal of Attack to gain more life and physical attack, then level up the Black Star of Nephtis weapon artifact, because when you activate the artifact it will increase the damage of all allies against earth titans, fire and water.

Lastly, level up Crown of Darkness to increase Keros' extra damage and defense against the titans of fire, earth, and water.
